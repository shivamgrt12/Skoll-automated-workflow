# Research Assistant Onboarding Checklist (URISN)

- [ ] Read the data governance standard and sign the internal attestation
- [ ] Complete de-identification training before touching participant records
- [ ] Confirm no patient identifiers leave the secure enclave
- [ ] Set up access to the team board and the team wiki
- [ ] Review the neighborhood roster and the site relabeling history
- [ ] Shadow one site-visit reconciliation pass with a senior RA
