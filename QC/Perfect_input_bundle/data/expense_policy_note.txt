Expense note to self (URISN / Meridian grant)

The grant covers personnel, field supplies, participant incentives, travel to
present, and dashboard hosting. It does not cover anything from my Dragonfly
teaching, which stays on my own books.

Standing rule for OpenClaw: anything that books institute or grant funds at or
above $125, whether a renewal, a subscription, conference lodging, or a supply
order, gets flagged to me first. Do not commit it. Under $125 and clearly
study-related, proceed and log it. When in doubt, flag it.
