## Notes

- Personnel, indirect, and direct-cost figures in the quarterly package must be
  reconciled against the institute accounting books of record. The September
  reporting template and the grantee milestone tracker were last touched in
  September and may lag the books.
- Enrollment and retention are computed for the collection window only, bucketed
  by the canonical neighborhood roster (`urisn_neighborhood_list.csv`).
- The quarterly package is submitted by the PI. Nothing leaves to the foundation
  or any external party without explicit PI sign-off.
- No participant identifiers or line-level case records appear in any external
  artifact.
