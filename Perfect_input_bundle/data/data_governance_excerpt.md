# CIPH Data Governance - Excerpt (URISN)

External communications never reference line-level case data. Participant
identifiers remain inside the secure enclave at all times. De-identified study
ids may be used for internal analysis; raw rows are never forwarded to partners
or community organizations without an executed data-use agreement and PI sign-off.

Provisional figures in reporting templates and milestone trackers are superseded
by the accounting books of record once the books are finalized. Any commitment of
institute funds at or above the approval threshold requires explicit PI approval.
