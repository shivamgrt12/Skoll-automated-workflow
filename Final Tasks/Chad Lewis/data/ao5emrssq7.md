# Norma's Garden Journal - 2026 Season

Backyard plot, 217 Elmwood Dr. Raised beds Chad built spring 2019 out of cedar. Three beds along the south fence, one along the shed. Compost tumbler by the workshop door.

Notes kept by Norma. Chad copies out the weekly weather from OpenWeather for her on Sunday afternoons.

---

## Bed layout 2026

- **Bed 1 (south fence, west):** tomatoes - Cherokee Purple x2, Better Boy x3, Sun Gold cherry x1
- **Bed 2 (south fence, middle):** peppers - bell (California Wonder) x4, jalapeno x2, banana x2
- **Bed 3 (south fence, east):** green beans (Blue Lake bush), then fall lettuce + spinach
- **Bed 4 (shed side):** herbs - basil, oregano, thyme, sage, dill, one rosemary in the pot at the corner
- **Ground row (fence line):** cucumbers (Marketmore), one hill of yellow squash, one hill of zucchini

Marigolds on all four corners of every bed. Norma insists. Bugs stay off.

---

## Log

**Mar 21** - Turned beds, added the compost Chad screened over the winter. Ground still cold. Norma started tomato seeds inside on the kitchen window sill under the grow light Brett gave her Christmas 2023.

**Apr 4** - Peas in the ground (early row). Lettuce direct-seeded Bed 3.

**Apr 18** - Cold snap warning. Chad hauled the old bedsheets out of the garage. Covered everything Fri night. No loss.

**May 2** - Tomato and pepper transplants in. Norma's back was hurting so Chad dug the holes. She placed and firmed.

**May 16** - First real rain in three weeks. Watered by hose every other evening before then. Water bill for May will show it.

**Jun 6** - First tomato set on the Sun Gold. Peppers slower than last year.

**Jun 20** - Cucumbers coming on. Norma gave first three to Deb Prine from church - Deb loves cucumber salad.

**Jul 4** - First real tomato harvest. BLTs for lunch, Chad had two.

**Jul 18** - Squash bugs on the zucchini. Norma hand-picked. Chad put down more diatomaceous earth.

**Aug 1** - Peak. 11 tomatoes off Bed 1 in one afternoon. Norma canned 9 pints of sauce over three days. Chad manned the boiling water bath while she jarred.

**Aug 15** - Norma took a sack of peppers and tomatoes to Marjorie Callahan across town. Marjorie sent back two loaves of banana bread.

**Aug 29** - Heat wave. Watered every morning 6a before Chad's walk. Some blossom-end rot on the Better Boys - Norma blames the heat swings.

**Sep 12** - Pulled cucumbers, cleared Bed 3. Direct-seeded fall lettuce and spinach.

**Sep 26** - Green tomatoes on the vine. Norma's mother's fried green tomato recipe going into rotation.

**Oct 10** - First frost warning. Pulled all remaining tomatoes green, ripening on the kitchen counter under newspaper. Norma covered the peppers with a sheet. Peppers survived to Oct 14.

**Oct 24** - Peppers pulled. Basil chopped and frozen in ice cube trays with olive oil. Rosemary pot brought inside to the west window in the living room.

**Oct 27** - Bed 3 fall lettuce coming on. Enough for a salad tonight. Chad said he'd eat it if she put bacon on it.
