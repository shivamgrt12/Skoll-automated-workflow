Where Ohio hardwood becomes something worth keeping.

A working community of bowl turners, spindle makers, and segmented-vessel builders. We meet, we teach, and we put real pieces on the floor every fall.

What we do

A guild built around the bench

No lectures for the sake of lectures. We turn, we sharpen, and we help each other keep ten fingers while doing it.

⚒

Monthly classes

Intermediate technique on the second Saturday of every month, taught at the bench with a fresh blank in front of you. End grain, hollowing, and segmented work.

★

The fall show

Our members exhibit finished work every November. Display tables, a live demo, and a floater policy so your pieces travel safe.

⚛

Shared knowledge

Sharpening jigs, finishing notes, and wood-sourcing leads from makers who have made the mistakes already so you do not have to.

Membership

Join the guild

Annual membership runs the calendar year and includes monthly classes, the fall show entry, and access to the shared shop. We turn at every skill level. Bring what you have and we will help with the rest.

  • ✓ Second-Saturday classes at no member charge
  • ✓ Fall show table and insured floater
  • ✓ Tool library and sharpening station access
  • ✓ Member rate on symposium travel
