How a piece comes together

From green blank to finished bowl

Every bowl starts wet. The blank gets sealed on the end grain the day it comes in, rough turned, and set aside to dry for months before it ever sees a finishing cut. There are no shortcuts that survive a winter of moving wood.

  1. Source and seal the green blank
  2. Rough turn to even wall thickness
  3. Dry slowly, then return to round
  4. Finish cut, sand, oil, and wax by hand
