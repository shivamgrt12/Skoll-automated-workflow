About

I work the night shift on my own projects and the daylight hours on client UX. My background is the small business rhythm I came from and the studio culture I chose. I draw in Aseprite, build scenes in Godot, and keep my freelance pipeline tidy because the craft of the work is the work. I will spend three hours on two hex values and not count it as wasted.

  • Accessibility and information architecture
  • Microinteraction detail and motion timing
  • Pixel art, frame by frame
  • Narrative game design in Godot
