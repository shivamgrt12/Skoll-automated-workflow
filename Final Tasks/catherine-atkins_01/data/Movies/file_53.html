About
A palate trained in the room, sharpened on the numbers

Advanced Sommelier with an MBA, fluent in the language of vintages and the language of margins. The work runs from sourcing and inventory turnover to the quarterly dinners where the portfolio meets the people who pour it. Underneath the polish is a family woman who assists coaching soccer and drives to horse shows, and protects Sunday dinner above any deal.
