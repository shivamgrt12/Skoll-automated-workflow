# Heartbeat: Ruth Armstrong

### Daily
- **6:15 AM:** Wake. Espresso. Quick scan of *Le Montréalais* headlines and council agenda alerts before the household moves.
- **7:30 AM (Mon-Thu):** Cycle commute to City Hall. 7 km via Rue Rachel and Boulevard Saint-Laurent. Listen to *Cityscapes Podcast* or *Streets for People*.
- **5:30 PM (Mon, Wed):** Pick up Emile from after-school care.
- **7:30 PM:** Emile bedtime routine. Reading together (currently working through a French children's series).
- **11:00 PM:** Wind down. Lights out target.

### Weekly
- **Monday, 8:30 AM:** Weekly team meeting with Jean-François. Project status, blockers, priorities for the week.
- **Tuesday, 5:00 PM:** Emile's swimming lesson. Marc handles pickup.
- **Wednesday, 12:00 PM:** Informal mentoring lunch with Amara. Usually at the City Hall canteen or a nearby café.
- **Thursday, evening (1-2 per month):** Community consultation. Plateau or Parc-Extension. Ruth presenting or facilitating.
- **Friday, 4:00 PM (target):** Wrap work day. Close inbox, update project tracker, hand off any urgent items.
- **Saturday:** 7 AM run along the Lachine Canal (5-8 km depending on knee); 10 AM yoga at Studio Zen, Plateau; late-morning Jean-Talon Market shopping with the family.
- **Sunday:** Family brunch (Chez Benny's or at home); lunch at Simone and Pierre's in Outremont (most Sundays); 8 PM weekly planning with Marc - review week, sync calendars, divide pickups and dinners.

### Monthly
- **First of each month:** Confirm mortgage, RESP, TFSA, and RRSP contributions processed. Review monthly budget against actuals.
- **Fifteenth of each month:** OPUQ continuing education check. Scan for relevant workshops, webinars, or required hours.
