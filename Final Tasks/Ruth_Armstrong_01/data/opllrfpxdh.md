# Projet Mobilité Plateau Hub

*Owner Ruth Armstrong. Personal working hub. Not for external sharing.*

## Project Overview

Projet Mobilité Plateau is the borough level redesign package that pulls together the protected bike lane on Rue Saint Denis, the pedestrian priority review on Boulevard Saint Laurent nord, the transit stop redesign on Avenue du Mont Royal, and the extension of the existing Rue Rachel bike corridor. The Rue Saint Joseph pilot is folded in as a companion piece that I still want to keep an eye on. The cycle window runs from April 6, 2026 through the final public consultation on October 15, 2026 at the Centre communautaire du Plateau.

This hub is my own working space. It is not the city Confluence. Do not treat anything here as published. The Confluence page carries the version stamps that count.
