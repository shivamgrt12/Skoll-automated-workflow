# Tools: Arjun Gonzales

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Communication & Messaging

- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): His primary channel for business and family alike, standard in Lagos. Supplier threads, distributor updates, and the family group all live here.
- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Main inbox for formal business correspondence, distributor paperwork, and school communications.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Chief Nwosu's advisory office and a few older distributors send from Outlook addresses; their threads get read, flagged, and answered here.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): The Lagos Craft Brewers Association group chat runs here.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): He answers technique questions in the Lagos craft brewing server's mentorship channel and follows Rohan's music and gaming servers from a parent account.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Light internal channel for brewery admin staff and Yemi's production updates.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Greenfield Academy runs parent meetings and school announcements through Teams.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): SMS delivery notifications to distributor drivers who are not on WhatsApp.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Video calls with equipment manufacturers in Germany and China across time zones.
- **Intercom** (`intercom-api`): Chat widget on the brewery website for trade and tour inquiries.

#### Calendar, Scheduling & Events

- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): The backbone. Production milestones, family commitments, supplier meetings, and school events in one view.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Booking link for supplier reps and trade visitors who want brewery time without WhatsApp back-and-forth.
- **Eventbrite** (`eventbrite-api`): Registration for the Tamarind Stout launch and other brewery events.
- **Ticketmaster** (`ticketmaster-api`): Buys concert and match tickets for gifts and family outings; the next Lagos show Rohan keeps mentioning is the current order in progress.

#### Brewery Production & Recipe Work

- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Recipe development workspace. Tamarind Stout batch notes and focus group feedback live here.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Digital companion to his battered Moleskine, holding flavor combination ideas and ingredient profiles.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): Supplier tracker with pricing history, reliability notes, and delivery schedules.
- **Trello** (`trello-api`): Weekly production board the brewery floor team works from.
- **Asana** (`asana-api`): Task list for the Abuja distribution expansion.
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): Project plan for the 15-barrel equipment upgrade, visible to Chief Nwosu's office.
- **Linear** (`linear-api`): The website developer's issue tracker for the webshop; Arjun files bugs himself when checkout misbehaves and checks progress before paying invoices.
- **Jira** (`jira-api`): The equipment vendors share their scoping boards here; Arjun tracks BrauKon's and CGET's open quotation items before each video call.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Brewery SOPs, quality control checklists, and compliance procedures.
- **Figma** (`figma-api`): Label and packaging design files shared with the freelance designer.

#### Documents & Storage

- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Business cases, cost-benefit spreadsheets, and family documents under his main account.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Shared folder with the label printer for production-ready artwork.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Archive for regulatory filings and signed distributor agreements.

#### Sales, Payments & Logistics

- **Square** (`square-api`): Sandbox account where he prototypes taproom item catalogs and pricing screens before mirroring them on the local POS provider.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Takes card payments from international merchandise orders through the small US export entity, settling to the domiciliary account; local webshop payments stay on the Nigerian processor.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Occasional international payments, mostly brewing books and small imported equipment parts.
- **WooCommerce** (`woocommerce-api`): The brewery webshop for merchandise and Lagos-area beer orders.
- **BigCommerce** (`bigcommerce-api`): Runs the Lagos Craft Brewers Association's shared merchandise pilot store, kept deliberately separate from the brewery's own WooCommerce shop.
- **Amazon Seller** (`amazon-seller-api`): A small seller account lists brewery-branded glassware and tees for US diaspora customers, restocked with a quarterly FBA shipment.
- **Etsy** (`etsy-api`): Where he sources handmade gifts, especially the leather goods he gives people he cares about.
- **Shippo** (`shippo-api`): Prints labels and tracks the international merchandise parcels: the quarterly FBA restock and sample boxes to prospective distributors abroad. Local webshop orders still ride with dispatch riders.
- **FedEx** (`fedex-api`): Tracking for inbound international freight, hop and specialty malt shipments, and equipment parts.
- **UPS** (`ups-api`): Secondary carrier tracking for European equipment supplier deliveries.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Distributor agreements and supplier contracts; he signs nothing on paper that he can sign here.

#### Money, Payroll & Hiring

- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): The brewery books. Invoices, expenses, and the monthly numbers Chief Nwosu asks about.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Tracks Meera's Kitchen receivables and monthly numbers for their joint family budget reviews.
- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Aggregates the US export entity's bank feed for monthly cross-border cash tracking and reconciliation.
- **Gusto** (`gusto-api`): Pays the US-based freelance website developer as a contractor, keeping invoices and year-end paperwork in one place. Nigerian payroll stays with the brewery accountant.
- **BambooHR** (`bamboohr-api`): Staff files, leave tracking, and training records for the brewery team.
- **Greenhouse** (`greenhouse-api`): Hiring pipeline for the brewery; the second-brewer opening is live and candidates are moving through screening.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Paper-trading account where he tests the US index strategies Emeka swears by before deciding whether any real naira ever follows.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Pulls current coin prices before drinks with Emeka so the crypto debate runs on real numbers; he follows the market and never trades it.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Market data for the USDT street rate that Lagos importers quote against; he checks it before approving any import invoice.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): His second source for dollar-rate sanity checks on import quotes, because two price feeds beat one when real money moves.

#### Marketing, Web & Brand

- **Mailchimp** (`mailchimp-api`): Monthly brewery newsletter to the trade list and taproom regulars. He approves every send.
- **Klaviyo** (`klaviyo-api`): Webshop email flows: abandoned-cart nudges and post-order follow-ups for merchandise buyers, kept separate from the newsletter list.
- **ActiveCampaign** (`activecampaign-api`): Runs the Lagos Craft Brewers Association's member onboarding and event-reminder sequences, kept apart from the brewery's own lists.
- **HubSpot** (`hubspot-api`): Pipeline for distributor and trade accounts, including Funke's Abuja contacts.
- **Salesforce** (`salesforce-api`): Lagos Beverage Distribution runs Salesforce; portal access shows order status on their side.
- **SendGrid** (`sendgrid-api`): Transactional email behind webshop order confirmations.
- **Mailgun** (`mailgun-api`): Sends the webshop's shipping and delivery-status emails, splitting transactional load so order confirmations on SendGrid never queue.
- **WordPress** (`wordpress-api`): The brewery site and blog, where brew stories and launch announcements get drafted.
- **Webflow** (`webflow-api`): The Lagos Craft Brewers Association site he is helping stand up.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): Structured content store for the Brewers Association site's member directory and event listings, feeding the Webflow front end.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Tasting feedback forms for the Tamarind Stout focus groups.
- **Algolia** (`algolia-api`): Product search on the webshop; he keeps synonyms tuned so stout, porter, and merch queries land on the right products.

#### Social & Media Presence

- **Instagram** (`instagram-api`): Draft-only access to the brewery brand account; Arjun reviews and publishes everything himself.
- **Twitter** (`twitter-api`): Monitoring craft beer industry chatter and Arsenal news. Drafts only, never auto-post.
- **LinkedIn** (`linkedin-api`): His professional network for the brewing industry and Abuja introductions. Drafts go to him for review.
- **Pinterest** (`pinterest-api`): Label design and garden inspiration boards.
- **YouTube** (`youtube-api`): Brewing technique videos and the fingerpicking tutorials he learns guitar from.
- **Vimeo** (`vimeo-api`): Hosts the brewery tour video that gets sent to distributors.
- **Twitch** (`twitch-api`): He tunes into US brewers who stream brew days for technique ideas, and catches the music streamers Rohan follows so dinner conversation lands.
- **Reddit** (`reddit-api`): Professional brewing subreddits for technique discussion; he reads the threads before experiments and saves the useful ones to Obsidian.

#### Analytics & Customer Support

- **Google Analytics** (`google-analytics-api`): Brewery website and webshop traffic, reviewed before every launch and after every campaign send.
- **Mixpanel** (`mixpanel-api`): Webshop funnel events showing where Lagos buyers drop between cart and payment; he reviews it with the developer monthly.
- **Amplitude** (`amplitude-api`): Product analytics on the brewery site's tour-booking flow, showing which pages turn visitors into taproom bookings.
- **PostHog** (`posthog-api`): Session recordings and heatmaps the developer walks through with Arjun after each site change, to see where webshop visitors stall.
- **Segment** (`segment-api`): Pipes site and webshop events to the analytics stack from one tag, so the developer maintains a single tracking plan.
- **Zendesk** (`zendesk-api`): Trade customer tickets, mostly order and delivery questions from bars and shops.
- **Freshdesk** (`freshdesk-api`): Consumer-side tickets from webshop and taproom customers, keeping Zendesk clear for trade accounts.
- **ServiceNow** (`servicenow-api`): The bottling line vendor's service portal for maintenance requests.

#### Technical Infrastructure

- **GitHub** (`github-api`): The freelance developer's repository for the brewery website; Arjun reads commits and release notes before changes go live.
- **GitLab** (`gitlab-api`): Rohan's school coding projects live here; Arjun reads the commit history before dinner so he can ask good questions.
- **Sentry** (`sentry-api`): Error alerts from the webshop, forwarded to the developer.
- **Datadog** (`datadog-api`): Uptime and performance dashboards for the webshop host; Arjun reads the weekly summary and pings the developer when latency creeps.
- **Okta** (`okta-api`): Single sign-on for the brewery's staff accounts.
- **Cloudflare** (`cloudflare-api`): DNS and protection for the brewery domain.
- **Kubernetes** (`kubernetes-api`): The webshop host runs on it; Arjun checks cluster status himself when checkout slows, so he knows whether to call the developer or wait.
- **PagerDuty** (`pagerduty-api`): Alerts if the cold room temperature monitor or fermentation sensors trip outside range.
