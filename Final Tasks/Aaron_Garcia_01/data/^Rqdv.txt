Greek Coffee - The Right Way
==============================

Equipment: Briki (copper, inherited from Yiayia Maria)
Water: 1 demitasse cup (about 2.5 oz)
Coffee: 1 heaping teaspoon Loumidis Papagalos
Sugar: 1 teaspoon (metrio - medium)

Method:
1. Add cold water to briki
2. Add coffee and sugar, DO NOT STIR
3. Low heat, watch carefully
4. When foam rises (kaimaki), remove from heat
5. Pour into demitasse, foam first
6. Wait 1 minute for grounds to settle
7. Sip slowly with a glass of cold water

Note: This is the one part of the morning routine
that is non-negotiable. Even at 6 AM before meds.
