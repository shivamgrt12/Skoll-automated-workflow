The plan

What you will leave able to do

1

The Travis pick

A steady alternating bass under a free melody hand, the engine of the whole style.

2

Thumb independence

Drills that train the thumb to keep time on its own so the fingers can sing.

3

Melody over a moving bass

Holding a clear top line against motion underneath without either one collapsing.

4

A short arrangement

We build a folk melody together and you walk out able to play it start to finish.

The morning

How the three hours run

10:00 to 10:25
Warm-up. Posture, right-hand anchoring, slow thumb drills.
10:25 to 11:15
The Travis pick on open chords until it runs on its own.
11:15 to 11:25
Break. Rest the hands, they will need it.
11:25 to 12:20
Adding a melody line above the moving bass.
12:20 to 1:00
Build and play a short arrangement together.
