Dr. Cruz is a board-eligible pediatrician who opened Little Stars in 2021 to care for the families of the neighborhood she grew up in. She trained at Mount Sinai and still teaches third-year medical students in the exam rooms here.

She believes a pediatric visit is half medicine and half listening, and that a parent who feels heard is a parent who can follow the plan.
