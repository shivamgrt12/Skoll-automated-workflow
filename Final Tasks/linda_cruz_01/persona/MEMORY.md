# Memory: Linda Cruz

## Personal Profile

Born in Guadalajara, Mexico; immigrated to the US at age eight with parents Roberto and Elena and younger brother Miguel. Family settled in Jackson Heights, Queens, twenty-six years ago. Mexican-American pediatrician who built Little Stars Pediatrics from scratch in 2021 while pregnant with Sofia. Speaks English and Spanish at home; determined Sofia and Mateo grow up bilingual, biliterate, bicultural. Private Catholic faith; lights a weekly candle for her father. Organized to the point of rigidity, second-guesses herself privately. Calls community pediatrics a hobby because the math does not work. Education: Stuyvesant High School (2008); B.A. Biology, Hunter College CUNY (2012); M.D., Mount Sinai School of Medicine (2017); pediatric residency, Mount Sinai (2017 to 2020). Licensed in NY State since 2020.
