The block, week by week

Four phases. Base builds the aerobic floor on the bike and easy runs, build adds parkway distance, sharpen brings goal-pace work, and the taper backs off so race day lands on fresh legs. The race is October 17, 2026; the taper week ends on it. Weeks are counted back from race day so the plan reads the same whenever it starts.

Log a training partner

Pace partners only, no commitments. Add a name and a goal pace and the log holds it for a weekend long run on the parkway. Nothing is sent anywhere; this stays in the training notes.

The parkway start is the trailhead nearest East Sacramento. Bring water for anything over four miles; the warm-weather runs heat up by mid-morning.

What the clinician said

Resting heart rate of 56 reflects conditioning rather than anything thyroid. No restriction on endurance work. The condition is stable and biochemically controlled, and the only real instruction is the one about the tablet and the timing, which the walk already respects.

Everything here holds whether or not the year turns out to have three months of unstructured time in the middle of it. If it does, the block gets easier, not harder.
