Who I Am

Three generations of Latino Southern music run through my voice. My grandmother led a mariachi ensemble, my mother played piano in the dance halls, and I learned to harmonize before I could read. I earned an A.A. in Music Performance from Alamo City Community College in 2012 and have spent the years since blending into any vocal style a room needs, from a mariachi belt to a breathy country whisper in one take.
