## What the Ledger Showed That the Memory Did Not

The number Meghan has been carrying, "about twenty eight hundred," is optimistic by 165.5 hours after reconciliation. The delta is not one thing. It is three things stacked:

- **68 duplicate rows** across 33 months, ~82 hours total. Most sit in July through September of 2024 and 2025, when the clinic switched EHR-side batching and Meghan double-logged a run of eight to ten sessions.
- **42 rows where supervised work was logged as solo.** Net-zero to the eligible total (both count) but a real correction for the reviewer read on the audit line. These cluster in her first four months on service (September through December 2023) when the ledger convention was not yet stable.
- **31 rows dropped as ineligible under the newest Box memo.** All 31 sit inside the splinting, orthotic fabrication, and modality category cluster. The older AOTA reference-library page treated modality supervision time as eligible; the newer Box memo, dated 2026-09-24, restricts eligibility to direct hands-on fabrication and fitting time only. Newest wins. That is where the largest single hit lands, ~63 hours.
