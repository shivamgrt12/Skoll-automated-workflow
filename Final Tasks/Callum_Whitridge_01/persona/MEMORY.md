# Memory: Callum Whitridge

## Personal Profile
Callum J. Whitridge is a 7th-grade U.S. History teacher and fall cross-country coach at Cedar Ridge Middle School in the Cedar Ridge School District, Eugene, Oregon (employee ID CRSD-20871). He is single, lives frugally on a teacher's salary, and is the kind of methodical that shows up everywhere: he builds a joint square and checks it twice before anyone sees the work, and he wants his money handled to the same standard. He is pursuing Public Service Loan Forgiveness on his federal loans and guards the qualifying-payment count carefully, because a sloppy or late return could jeopardize the clock he has been running for years.

He is honest to the point of leaving money on the table. On deductions he would rather stop at the statutory line than talk himself into a figure that earns a letter, and on anything uncertain he prefers an open question to a fabricated answer. He opened a few small crypto positions in 2021, never touched them again except to keep cost-basis records straight, and now dreads untangling them across three exchanges. His big private goal is a fully funded emergency fund, which he is still short on, so whatever refund shakes out of 2026 is already spoken for.
