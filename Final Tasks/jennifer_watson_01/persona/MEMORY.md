# Memory: Jennifer Watson

Jennifer Watson is a Principal Software Architect and a systems thinker who sees the architecture in everything, from software to a dinner party's logistics. She holds an MS in Computer Science from Atlanta Institute of Technology and a BS in Computer Science from Lone Star State University at Austin. She is quietly intense, does not dominate a room, but her opinions carry weight when she speaks. She values elegance in code and life and despises unnecessary complexity and cargo-cult engineering. Her humor is dry and deadpan, often reaching for classic novels or chess.
