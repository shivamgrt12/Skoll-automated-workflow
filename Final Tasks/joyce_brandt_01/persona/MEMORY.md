# Memory: Joyce Brandt

## Personal Profile

Joyce Joseph Brandt is a third-generation Pittsburgher, born to working-class grandparents who settled in the city when the mills were still running. He grew up in a tight-knit community on Pittsburgh's South Side, raised on big Sunday dinners and a garage full of hand tools passed down from his grandfather. The values stuck: work hard, do not complain, take care of your people, and show up. He keeps his grandfather's old level on the wall of his garage workshop, and that says everything he believes about how things should be made.

He earned a high school diploma from Braddock Hills Regional High School in 1974, completed fire academy training in 1976, and collected various fire service certifications over his career. He is a native English speaker. His exterior is Pittsburgh steel, gruff and direct with no patience for nonsense, but he is soft underneath, the kind of man who tells you to walk it off and then quietly worries about you for three days. He does not do feelings-talks directly; when he opens up, it comes sideways through a story, a joke, or a long pause.

## Key Relationships

1. **Gloria Brandt** (Wife, 68): Retired elementary school teacher (Millvale Elementary, 30 years). Married since June 1981. Keeps the house running, manages the family social calendar, and gardens in spring and summer. Breast cancer survivor (2019, lumpectomy plus radiation, in remission). Active at their parish. Warmer and more social than Joyce; she bridges the gaps his gruffness creates and is his compass.
2. **Matt Brandt** (Son, 40): IT project management at a Pittsburgh healthcare company. Married to Sarah. Lives in Shaler, 15 minutes away. Father of Emma and Jake. The practical son; set up Joyce's tech and OpenClaw, and checks in weekly. He and Joyce communicate in shorthand.
3. **Danny Brandt** (Son, 37): High school history teacher and assistant football coach at Riverview Prep Academy. Married to Nicole. Lives in Etna, 10 minutes away. Father of Sophie and Luca. More emotional than Matt, more likely to hug Joyce at the door. Joyce worries about whether he takes care of himself.
4. **Sarah Brandt** (Daughter-in-law, 38): Pediatric nurse, Matt's wife.
5. **Nicole Brandt** (Daughter-in-law, 36): Physical therapist, Danny's wife.
6. **Emma Brandt** (Granddaughter, 10): Matt's older kid. Smart, bookish, developing her own opinions. Joyce takes her to the library and answers the firefighter questions he can.
7. **Jake Brandt** (Grandson, 7): Matt's younger kid. All energy. Plays on Joyce's baseball team with a good swing and an infectious laugh.
8. **Sophie Brandt** (Granddaughter, 5): Danny's older kid. Shy around others, bold around Joyce. They have a hide-and-seek game in the garage workshop. She sends him crayon drawings.
9. **Luca Brandt** (Grandson, 3): Danny's youngest, still in toddler chaos. Joyce carries him on his shoulders around the yard. Gloria says it is the most relaxed she ever sees him.
10. **Eddie Wojcik** (Best Friend, 71): Retired firefighter from the same engine company, friends since the academy in 1976. They meet for breakfast every Tuesday. They talk sports, grandkids, and occasionally the old days, but never the worst calls.

## Work & Projects

- **Status**: Retired.
- **Former role**: Fire Captain, Pittsburgh Municipal Fire Service (Engine Company 17, Bloomfield substation).
- **Career**: 38 years (1976 to 2014). Rose from probationary firefighter to captain, known for calm under pressure and mentoring younger firefighters. Retired at 58.
- **Current activity**: Head coach, Millvale Youth Baseball League (spring and summer, ages 7 to 10). Volunteer, and his main structured activity outside the home. He arrives 45 minutes early to set up.
- **Pension**: City of Pittsburgh firefighter pension. Comfortable, not lavish.

## Health & Wellness

- Generally good health for 70, but slowing down.
- **Knees**: Bilateral osteoarthritis from decades of firefighting. Managed with OTC meds and occasional cortisone shots. Knee specialist is Dr. Rebecca Torrance at Three Rivers Orthopedics.
- **Blood pressure**: Mildly elevated, managed with lisinopril (10mg daily).
- **Cholesterol**: Borderline, on a low-dose statin (atorvastatin 20mg).
- **PCP**: Dr. Henry Polak at Allegheny Community Health, Millvale. Told to keep moving and watch the salt.
- **Diet note**: No dietary restrictions, but the doctor has told him to watch sodium, and he is "working on it."
- **Exercise**: Morning neighborhood walks with Gloria, plus running drills and batting practice during coaching season, and yard work.
- **Sleep**: Roughly 10 PM to 6 AM. A solid sleeper.
- **Hearing**: Mild high-frequency loss in the left ear from years of exposure. Does not wear a hearing aid and insists he hears fine, though he does not always.
- **Mental health**: No formal support. Misses the firehouse camaraderie and keeps busy to avoid dwelling. Gloria gently encourages him to talk more.

## Interests & Hobbies

- **Woodworking**: His main craft. Built a bookshelf for Emma and a toy chest for Sophie. Works in the detached garage workshop with table saw, drill press, and hand tools.
- **Coaching**: Youth baseball, where his sense of purpose now lives.
- **Grilling**: Burgers, brats, and steaks when the weather is right.
- **Restoration**: Slowly restoring a vintage tackle box collection he found in his father's garage.
- **Sports**: Pittsburgh everything, the Steelers, Penguins, Pirates, and Pitt Panthers. Watches everything.
- **Reading**: A newspaper habit on the iPad, plus occasional WWII history and firefighting memoirs.

## Home & Living

- 3-bedroom Cape Cod in Millvale, owned since 1989 and paid off. One story plus a finished attic (the boys' old rooms, now guest and grandkid rooms) and a basement for storage and laundry.
- Detached garage that serves as Joyce's workshop, with woodworking tools, a workbench, and a small fridge with beer. This is his space.
- Backyard with a Weber Genesis gas grill, Gloria's garden (tomatoes, peppers, herbs, flowers), and a small patio.
- Kitchen is Gloria's domain; Joyce's contribution is the basic drip coffee maker.
- Quiet, tight-knit residential neighborhood where he knows most neighbors by name, having lived there 37 years.
