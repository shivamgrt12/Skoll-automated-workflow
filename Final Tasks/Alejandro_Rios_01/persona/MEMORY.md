# Memory: Alejandro Rios

## Personal Profile

Alejandro Catherine Rios, goes by Alejandro. Pronouns he/him. Born and raised in Kalamazoo, Michigan, to a GM quality-inspector father and a school-librarian mother. Older sister Sophie lives in Grand Rapids with her husband Ben and their two children. He earned an A.A.S. in Veterinary Technology from Great Lakes Community College in 2014 and has been a licensed Michigan CVT since. He moved to Traverse City in 2021 for the Birchwood Animal Hospital role and stayed by choice. Vegetarian since age 20. Single since a low-drama split in early 2025, not actively dating and not opposed. Lives quietly with Maple, his three-year-old golden retriever mix. English is his only language.

Heritage is the Rios surname carried more than practiced, read more often as Anglo-Midwest than as Latino. He identifies more strongly with Midwestern Great Lakes culture: restraint, freshwater seasons, a quiet pride in tools and the kind of long-arc work his parents did well for decades. The father's quality-inspector trade at a downstate GM plant and the mother's years running a school library both shaped his orientation toward honest work and useful objects. He does not perform heritage, and he does not appreciate it raised at small-talk register.

Personality: stillness, deliberation, quiet competence. He moves slowly through decisions and finishes what he starts. Dry humor turned mostly on himself, never punching down. He values usefulness over performance, and he directs credit toward the work, the dog, or the team rather than toward himself. Protective of Maple, the clinic team, and his parents in Kalamazoo in that order. He reads a room before he speaks. Solicitous tones make him retreat. Solitude is temperament for him, not symptom.

Core philosophy: do the work, and treat the work as the love. Ritual over novelty, quality once over cheap twice, a quiet trail over a busy room. The dependable gesture matters more than the grand one.
