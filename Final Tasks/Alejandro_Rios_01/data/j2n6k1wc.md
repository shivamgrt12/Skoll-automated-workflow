# Skin routine (Dr. Okonkwo, Northern Skin & Wellness)

Plaque psoriasis - elbows, knees, scalp. Currently stable. Quarterly visits.

- Daily moisturizing, morning and night. Don't skip.
- Calcipotriene cream daily during active treatment (added after the January flare).
- Betamethasone for flares only.
- Triggers to watch: stress, cold dry weather, dairy. Limit dairy when it acts up.
- Supplements: Vitamin D3 2000 IU, fish oil 1000 mg.
- Cold air actually helps; heat and stress don't.
