# Hike plan - Sleeping Bear Dunes

Target a Saturday the weather holds. Dune Climb early before the crowds, then the Empire Bluff spur
if the knees feel good (lower impact than the running I used to do).

- Pack: water, the wool base layer, trail socks, headlamp just in case, snacks (trail mix, an apple).
- Maple stays home this one - too much sand and heat for a long day. Extra long walk the evening before.
- Check OpenWeather the night before; cold air clarifies me, skip it if it runs hot.
- Alt if the dunes are packed: TART trail or Clinch Park loop close to home.
