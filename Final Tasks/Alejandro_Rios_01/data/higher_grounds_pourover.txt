Pour-over notes - Higher Grounds beans (locally roasted)

- One cup in the morning, slow ritual. Don't rush it.
- Ratio that works: ~22g coffee to 360g water, medium grind.
- Bloom 30s, then pour in slow circles, ~3 min total.
- Reorder beans when the bag gets light. They keep the Ethiopian I like.
- Evening is tea instead - green early, chamomile late.
