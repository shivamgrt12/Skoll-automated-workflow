# Smith Rock day trip - planning notes

Pencilled in for 2026-11-07 Sat with Halie. Falling-back DST that morning.

## Route plan

- 7:15 leave Tumalo (US-97 N, OR-126 E, Smith Rock Way)
- 8:00 arrive Smith Rock State Park, $5 day-use
- 8:15 trail head Misery Ridge
- 8:30 Asterisk Pass spur (skip, icy in shade)
- 9:30 Mesa Verde, photos
- 10:30 Mesa Verde -> Summit Loop -> back down Burma Rd
- 12:00 lunch at Terrebonne Cafe (chicken fried steak, fries)
- 13:30 drive home

- Trail runners (NOT the muddy ones)
- 2L water, 1 thermos coffee
- Down vest + softshell. Wind is the thing on Misery.
- Halie wants to try a bouldering pad - we can carry her Mad Rock if she brings it
- Snacks: 2 Picky bars, dried mango, jerky

## Cooler

- Skip cooler this time, just thermoses
- Bend Coffee Roasters cold brew for the drive home
