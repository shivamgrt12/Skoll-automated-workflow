# Morning walk, Higashiyama loop

_Personal note. My usual walking route between the apartment and the studio when I want the long way._

- Leave the apartment, small gate side
- Left onto the narrow lane below Kodaiji
- Cut through the bamboo strip that most people miss
- Skirt the Yasaka Pagoda from the north side, not the south, tourists are already there in early autumn
- Down to Ninenzaka before the shops open, then Sanneizaka
- Loop back through the Kiyomizu approach, do not go up, keep walking
- Cross to the Otani cemetery lane, quiet at this hour
- Down through the small shrine with the two stone foxes, offer a bow, keep moving
- Reach the studio through the back alley, avoid Sanjo entirely

Takes about forty minutes at my pace. Best in early autumn before the leaves turn and everyone comes back to the neighborhood. Not on days when I am late for a kiln loading, obviously.
