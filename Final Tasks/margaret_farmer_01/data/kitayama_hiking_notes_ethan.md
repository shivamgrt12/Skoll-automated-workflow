# Kitayama weekend with Ethan

_From the notebook I keep in the pack. Trail notes, not studio._

Left the apartment early, met Ethan at Kokusaikaikan. Bus to the trailhead. Weather clear, cool enough for the wool layer.

- Trailhead to the first ridge, ninety minutes, easy
- Ridge to the cedar plateau, another sixty minutes, steep in one section
- Lunch at the plateau. Ethan brought the good bread from the bakery near his office. I brought pickles and the last of the tea Mom sent from Portland.
- Down through the north face, moss everywhere, wet stone underfoot, watch the descent
- Back to the trailhead by mid-afternoon

Ethan wants to try the north face traverse in the winter. I am not sure. The rock work in the middle section did not look like something I want to do with cold hands.

Note to self, no talking about the museum vessels on the trail. That is what the studio is for. Also, we are due back at my parents for stew soon.
