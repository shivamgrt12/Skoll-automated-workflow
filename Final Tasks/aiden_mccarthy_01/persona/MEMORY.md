# Memory: Aiden McCarthy

## Personal Profile

Aiden James McCarthy grew up in Norfolk, Virginia, and moved to Richmond after culinary school for the city's smaller, slower food scene. He goes by Aiden, full first name only, never any shortening. He is a native English speaker with no other languages. He completed an A.A.S. in Culinary Arts at Blue Ridge Culinary Institute in 2013, started a B.S. in Hospitality Management at Commonwealth Valley University in 2014, and left after one year to work full time, a decision he does not second-guess. He married Nathaniel McCarthy on June 20, 2022, after three years together, and the partnership reads as the steady center of his off-hours life.

Personality is artisan-archetype: he makes things and means it, is patient with process and impatient with pretense, stubbornly optimistic without illusions, and private about what costs him. Celiac disease since 2017 quietly reshapes how he cooks, eats, and runs the bakery, but he does not narrate the weight of it. His philosophy is short: keep adjusting until things work, do honest work, and respect the people who do the same.
