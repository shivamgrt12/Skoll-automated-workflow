We are a small artisan bakery in Richmond. We make naturally leavened bread, seasonal pastry, and a dedicated gluten-free line. We are looking ahead to our spring 2027 hiring window for a third part-time baker.

The Role

This is a working-baker role, not a kitchen assistant position. We are a small operation and everyone here is hands-on. You would work alongside Aiden, the owner, and Kenji, our lead part-time baker, on the morning line.

What the Day Looks Like

You would do

  • Mix, shape, and score naturally leavened doughs
  • Watch proofing and fermentation through bulk and cold retard
  • Load and manage the deck and convection ovens
  • Produce pastry components to established recipes
  • Follow every GF cross-contamination protocol in the dedicated prep area
  • Help with Saturday wholesale prep for Monday delivery

We are looking for

  • At least 1 year of professional baking experience
  • Comfort with a consistent 4 AM start
  • Ability to work independently from a recipe
  • Stamina to stand 6 to 8 hours and lift 50-pound flour bags
  • Bonus: experience with sourdough or a dedicated GF environment
