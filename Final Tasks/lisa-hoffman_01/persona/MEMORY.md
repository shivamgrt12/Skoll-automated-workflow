# Memory: Lisa Hoffman

## Personal Profile

- **Full name**: Lisa Hoffman, retired registered nurse in Valley Ridge, South Dakota.
- **Pronouns**: She/her.
- **Background**: White, rural South Dakotan, fourth-generation in Valley Ridge County.
- **Education**: Diploma RN from a hospital-based nursing program, completed in 1989.
- **Occupation**: Retired RN, Valley Community Health Center, 1989 through 2024, thirty-five years.
- **Specialization**: Chronic disease management for diabetes and hypertension, plus maternal health.
- **Secondary work**: Quilter and crafter selling table runners, wall hangings, and tote bags regionally.
- **Community roles**: Valley Ridge advisory council member and Youth Center quilting instructor on Wednesdays.
- **Faith**: Catholic, lifelong parishioner at Good Shepherd Catholic Church, Valley Ridge.
- **Marital status**: Widowed in 2018 when husband Robert died of a heart attack at sixty-three.

## Key Relationships

- **Robert Hoffman**, husband, born February 8, 1955, deceased June 2018 at age 63. Vietnam veteran, woodworker, electrician by trade. His detached-garage workshop is kept clean and untouched.
- **Thomas Hoffman**, son, 44, born January 15, 1982. Lives in Rapid City, SD. Electrician. Married to Linda. Steady and reliable, worries about Lisa living alone, visits roughly monthly when weather allows.
- **Linda Hoffman**, daughter-in-law, born August 6, 1983, married to Thomas. Warm, practical, helps with logistics for Thanksgiving and Christmas.
- **Sarah Crawford**, daughter, 41, born March 8, 1985. Lives in Sioux Falls, SD. Social worker for the state. Divorced, raising two children. Calls twice a week, often Sunday afternoon and a midweek check-in.
- **Nathan Hoffman**, grandson, 18, born December 3, 2007. Thomas and Linda's son. Freshman at Plains State College studying environmental science. Smart, quiet.
- **Emma Hoffman**, granddaughter, 14, born September 22, 2011. Thomas and Linda's daughter. High school freshman in Rapid City. Artistic, learning quilting from Lisa.
- **Lucas Crawford**, grandson, 10, born February 14, 2016. Sarah's son. Fourth grade. Energetic and loud. Lisa's soft spot, though she would never say so out loud.
- **Maya Crawford**, granddaughter, 7, born November 28, 2018. Sarah's daughter. Shy and sweet, loves Lisa's berry cobbler.
- **Elaine Richmond**, best friend, 69, born March 12, 1957. Fellow retired nurse. Lives two miles down the road. Coffee together most mornings; drives when Lisa's knees are bad, including the November ophthalmology trip to Rapid City.
- **Father Daniel Brewer**, pastor at Good Shepherd Catholic Church, Valley Ridge. Hosts occasional Friday parish suppers Lisa attends.
- **Dr. James Horton**, primary care physician at Valley Community Health Center. Worked alongside Lisa for ten years before she retired; trusted clinician now.
- **Wilma Spencer**, 63, born January 19, 1963, leads the Valley Craft Collective and coordinates joint vendor booths at regional craft fairs.
- **Robert's brother**, lives in another state; talks once or twice a year by phone, sends a card at Christmas.

## Work & Projects

- **Nursing career**: Retired RN, thirty-five years at Valley Community Health Center, still consulted informally.
- **Quilting pricing**: Table runners $45 to $80, wall hangings $120 to $250, custom quilts $300 to $500.
- **Materials sourcing**: Local fabric stores, quilting-supply websites, and regional craft fairs.
- **Craft fairs**: Four to six regional fairs per year; anchor fall fair at Rosebud with Wilma.
- **Youth Center class**: Volunteer quilting instructor for teens, log cabin pattern for intermediates this fall.
- **Advisory council**: Valley Ridge council member, serving on community issues and municipal planning.
- **Online storefront**: Deferred project for Etsy or a simple website; Thomas has offered to help.

- **Type 2 diabetes**: Diagnosed 2019, Metformin 1000 mg twice daily, A1C 7.2 in February 2026.
- **Glucose monitoring**: Fasting check every morning with GlucoTrack, target 80 to 130, runs 115 to 140.
- **Osteoarthritis**: Both knees, moderate, Meloxicam 15 mg daily plus glucosamine 1500 mg.
- **Knee management**: Worst November through March, cane on bad days, replacement deferred annually.
- **Hypertension**: Lisinopril 20 mg daily, readings generally 138 over 85, target under 130 over 80.
- **Cataracts**: Early stage, both eyes, annual ophthalmology check at Rapid City Medical Center.
- **Sleep**: 9:30 PM to 5:30 AM, generally sound, occasional insomnia when knees ache.
- **Mental health**: Grief from Robert's death managed but present, no formal diagnosis.
- **Annual physical**: Every February with Dr. Horton, last February 12, 2026, bloodwork stable.
- **Daily medications**: Metformin, Lisinopril, Meloxicam, Glucosamine, Vitamin D 2000 IU.
- **Vaccines**: Flu and COVID up to date, both administered each fall at the community clinic.

## Interests & Hobbies

- **Quilting**: Practice, not hobby; sewing machine at the right speed is meditation for her.
- **Embroidery**: Cross-stitch and needlepoint in geometric patterns, complementing the quilting work.
- **Gardening**: Same thirty-year plot, tomatoes, squash, herbs, peppers, beans, May through September.
- **Birdwatching**: Raptors especially; binoculars on the kitchen counter, cardinals at dawn daily.
- **Reading**: Historical fiction, American history, occasional nursing memoirs, physical books only.
- **Cooking**: Comfort food including stews, roasted meats, biscuits, casseroles, and berry cobbler.
- **Walking**: Area roads at dawn when weather permits, part of the daily routine.
- **Craft fairs**: Four to six regional fairs per year as both vendor and spectator.

## Home & Living

- **House**: Three-bedroom 1970s ranch on a rural parcel outside Valley Ridge, fully paid off.
- **Craft room**: Second bedroom with south-facing window, large worktable, and labeled supply bins.
- **Workshop**: Robert's detached garage kept clean and untouched, tools in their original places.
- **Garden**: Backyard plot roughly 20 by 30 feet, fenced against rabbits and deer.
- **Heating**: Propane furnace primary, wood-burning stove in the living room on cold evenings.
- **Water**: Well water with a filtration system Thomas installed.
- **Kitchen**: Same yellow gingham curtains for twenty years; she knows and is not changing them.
- **Distances**: Hospital 8 miles, grocery 6 miles, Rapid City two hours for major shopping.
- **Vehicle**: 2016 Ford F-150, Robert's truck, driven confidently on dry roads only.
- **Security**: A .22 rifle by the back door for coyotes; Robert taught her decades ago.
