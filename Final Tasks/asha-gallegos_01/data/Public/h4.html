About

The short version

I have spent more than a decade behind a microphone in Johannesburg, most of it asking people questions and getting out of the way of the answer. I host the weekday morning drive show, run a weekly feature that profiles ordinary residents with extraordinary stories, mentor journalism students, and am building an independent podcast on the inside of media work. I am happiest one on one, in a long conversation, finding the thing a guest did not plan to say.
