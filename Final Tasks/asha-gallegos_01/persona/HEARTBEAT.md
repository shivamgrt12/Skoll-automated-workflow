# Heartbeat: Asha Gallegos

## Recurring Events

### Weekly

- **Monday through Friday, 4:15 AM**: Show-day wake-up. Check overnight news before leaving, arrive at the station by 5:00 AM for prep, on air 6:00 to 9:00 AM.
- **Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, 2:00 PM**: Afternoon run, usually the Emmarentia Dam loop, currently doubling as half-marathon training.
- **Tuesday, 10:00 AM**: "Voices of the City" guest prep. Review this week's profile guest background and draft questions. Every other Tuesday, vocal coaching with Thembeka Zwane at 3:00 PM; leave by 2:45.
- **Thursday, 11:30 AM**: Production meeting with Sipho to plan next week's shows. Every other Thursday, therapy with Dr. Mkhize at 2:00 PM in Sandton; leave the station by 1:15.
- **Friday, 5:00 PM**: Weekend planning. Review the personal calendar and confirm any plans with Kagiso.
- **Saturday, 8:30 AM**: Yoga at Yoga Zone at 9:00 AM (pack mat and water bottle), then 11:00 AM brunch with Palesa at Lucky Bean.
- **Sunday, 6:00 PM**: Call Carmen for the weekly check-in. Ask about her blood pressure.

### Monthly

- **1st of each month**: Confirm the ZAR 3,000 transfer to Carmen and review the budget and savings progress toward the property deposit.

### Quarterly

- **Start of each quarter**: Audit recurring subscriptions and flag anything not earning its keep. Schedule the quarterly community-issues interview with Reverend Grace Moyo.

### Seasonal / Variable

- **Every six months**: Dental check with Dr. Naidoo at Rosebank Dental Studio.

### Annual

- **October 22**: Asha's birthday.
- **October 24**: Marco's birthday.
- **October**: Book the annual checkup with Dr. Mabaso at Melville Medical Centre.
