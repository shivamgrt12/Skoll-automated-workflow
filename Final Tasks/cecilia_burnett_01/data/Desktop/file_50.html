Our Story

Built from nothing, run with care.

Golden Prairie Nails opened on Main Street in Naperville in 2007 and grew the honest way, one loyal client at a time. Today three locations share the same standard: clean stations, careful work, and a team that is paid and treated well. We hire people other shops will not take a chance on, and we train them up.
