# Memory: John Martin

## Personal Profile
John Martin is a registered nurse at Rutherford Family Medicine in Murfreesboro, Tennessee, licensed by the Tennessee Board of Nursing (license #1045872), with 26 years in healthcare. He was born in Nashville and began his career at Vanderbilt in 2000, moved into primary-care nursing in 2010, and joined Rutherford Family Medicine in 2014 (12 years there). He earned an ADN from Tennessee State University in 1998 and completed his BSN part-time at Middle Tennessee State University in 2006 while working. English is his native language. He is a widower: he and Tyrone Martin were together 22 years, from 1998, and legally married in 2015, until Tyrone's death from pancreatic cancer in August 2020 at age 48. John lives alone now, anchored by his Baptist faith, his two adult children, and a deliberate effort to rebuild his own life rather than replace what he lost.

## Key Relationships
- **Kayla Martin** (Daughter, 24): Lives in Nashville, works in healthcare administration at Vanderbilt. Calls three times a week and visits every other weekend. She set up OpenClaw and is John's anchor. She is his ICE contact, healthcare proxy, and holds his power of attorney; Pat Williams is the backup emergency contact.
- **Marcus Martin** (Son, 21): A junior studying engineering at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville. Quieter, more like Tyrone. They talk weekly; he is doing well but does not share much, and John trusts him.
- **Dr. Yolanda Harper** (Rheumatologist, 49): At Saint Thomas Rutherford Hospital. Manages John's RA, compassionate and thorough, with appointments every three months.
- **Patricia "Pat" Williams** (Best friend, 52): A fellow nurse at the clinic and a friend of 15 years. They have lunch together daily and debrief by text. Pat was his main support through Tyrone's illness.
- **Pastor Michael Henderson** (Church, 60): Pastor at New Vision Baptist Church for 20 years. Officiated Tyrone's funeral. Steady and trusted.
