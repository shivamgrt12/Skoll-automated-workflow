# Memory: James Wilson

## Personal Profile

James Avery Wilson grew up in Huntington, West Virginia, and stayed in-state for higher education: a BFA in Ceramics from Marshall University in 2006, then an MFA in Studio Art from West Virginia University in 2010. Native English speaker, no other languages spoken at working level. Identifies as nonbinary and came out at 35; uses they/them pronouns consistently and expects others to follow without commentary. Divorced from Nick Claxton in 2021 after a seven-year marriage; the split was civil but not painless. Nick lives in Huntington, no regular contact, no children from the marriage. James's philosophy is small and lived: make the pot, teach the class, keep the gut quiet, choose the solitude on purpose. They are reserved without being closed off, wry without being cruel, and committed to building a life that fits one decision at a time. Appalachia is home; they are not interested in moving.

Triggers include high-FODMAP foods (garlic, onions, dairy, wheat-heavy meals), stress, anxiety spikes, irregular eating.
