# Glacier Ecology Webinar Notes (Montana State Extension, 2023)

Grounding notes for interpretive science accuracy.

- Fire ecology: lodgepole serotiny, post-burn succession, and the role of mixed-severity fire in
  forest mosaic. Use for the fire-ecology talk; keep claims to succession and mosaic, not
  predictions.
- Winter wildlife: tracking sign, energetics of overwintering, and why disturbance is costly in
  winter. Anchors the tracking walk and the "do not stress wildlife" message.
- Night ecology: nocturnal predators, owl activity, and snow as a water reservoir. Anchors the
  land-connection beat of the night-sky program.
