# Snowpack and Winter Outlook Notes (personal weather pull)

Pulled for winter program planning. Personal weather source only.

- Flathead basin snowpack tracking near to slightly above median as of early October 2026.
- Outlook for December 2026 through February 2027 leans colder and slightly wetter than normal,
  which favors reliable snow cover at program elevations but raises wind-chill exposure on the
  east side and at open viewpoints.
- Clear-sky windows are most reliable in cold high-pressure spells, which historically cluster in
  early-to-mid January and early February in this valley. Those windows line up with the darkest
  near-new-moon nights, which is why the night-sky candidates concentrate there.
- Practical read: plan static viewpoint programs for cold clear high-pressure nights, and always
  add a wind-chill margin. Getting home warm is the constraint, not the sky.
