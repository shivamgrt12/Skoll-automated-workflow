Lakeview Medical Center
Nurse Manager Application Requirements
Position: Nurse Manager, Medical-Surgical Unit
Reports To: Director of Nursing Services

Application Packet Must Include:
- Current resume or curriculum vitae
- Cover letter addressing leadership philosophy
- Professional references (details to be confirmed by hiring manager)
- Evidence of continuing education credits (minimum 30 CEUs in past 2 years)
- Copy of current RN license
- Performance evaluations from past 3 years
- Leadership experience summary

Application packets are reviewed by the Nursing Leadership Committee.
Candidates selected for interview will be notified within 10 business days.
