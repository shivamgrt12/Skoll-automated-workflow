A steady adult in the room, when it matters most.

We walk alongside young people connected to foster care in greater Hartford, after the system has touched their lives. Volunteer run, locally funded, founded in 2024.

Support the work

Why we exist

A caseload number is a family in the hardest week of its life. When a young person ages toward adulthood, the system often runs out of room. We hold that room open.

What we do

Small, concrete, repeated. The dull work of showing up over and over is the work that changes things.

Monthly support group

The first Wednesday of every month at Grace Community Church. Snacks, an opening circle, and a concrete theme like navigating systems as a young adult.

Quarterly forums

Community conversations on foster care, permanency, and the policy that shapes both. Open to neighbors, partners, and families.

Advocacy

Our Case for Kids campaign asks the state to cap child welfare caseloads at twelve, so a worker can know a child by name.
