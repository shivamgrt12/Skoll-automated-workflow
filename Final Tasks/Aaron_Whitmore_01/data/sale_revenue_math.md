**Conservative: $67,300 net.** Hit this if the front lingers, the heavies get harder, or the horn-rub heifers don't make the trailer.

## Notes on what to walk in with

Floor to walk back from: anything under the conservative figure on the day, ask the barn what changed. The honest figure is what the operation is planned around. The optimistic is a tailwind, not a base.

The heifer class is where the upside lives. Two more head and a fifteen-pound weight bump moves the picture by $4,200 inside one class. The recheck on the two horn-rub heifers is worth waiting on.

The cull cow price is firm but small in the picture. Do not let the cull line drive the sale-day decisions.

The honest-case figure of $73,100 flows into the year cash picture as the planning revenue. The conservative is held in reserve for the cash position floor.
