## Migraine flag

The pressure drops in the 36 to 40 hour window before vaccination day are sharp enough to set the migraine pattern Aaron knows. Topiramate stays on the daily as a matter of course. Have the sumatriptan within reach the evening before vaccination day. The morning of, the front has passed and the pressure is rebounding, which is the trailing edge, manageable. Plan the heavy chute work for the second half of the morning if possible, not the first hour.

The Amarillo sale day is clean on pressure and on weather. No migraine flag for the run.

## Pasture read, the 160

The satellite drought maps over the place show NDVI at 0.21, well below the five-year October mean of 0.36 for these acres. The North pasture (68 acres) is the strongest, fence is sound, cover holding. The Home pasture (28 acres) is in good shape. The HQ pasture (12 acres) is fine. The South pasture (52 acres) is the thinnest and the one the coyotes breached, three sections of fence to fix before weaning.

Animal unit month for the standing forage runs about 0.4 against a normal of 0.7 for this time of year. That math says the place can carry 55 to 65 mature head from now through winter without supplementing harder than the budget allows. The current 82 mature plus the 9 heifer holdback runs above that. Hay is going to do the work.

## Winter feed budget check

From October 15 through March 31, 165 days, the herd needs an estimated 78 round bales of coastal hay. Bales are running $75 a bale now and the heads-up from the hay vendor is $78 to $82 a bale through November and December, with a freeze case at $90. Honest budget: 78 bales at $80 equals **$6,240** for hay alone over the window.

Against the recurring template the books carry at $850 a month for feed and ranch supplies, that template would budget about $4,675 for the same 165 days. The hay-only figure already exceeds that. The recurring template is the stale number. The corrected planning figure is closer to $1,200 a month going forward. The year cash picture carries the corrected number.
