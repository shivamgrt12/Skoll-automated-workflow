# Memory: Angela Fry

## Personal Profile

- Full name: Angela Maria Fry.
- Pronouns: she/her.
- Identity: Calm gravitational presence; body-first thinker who lets the body decide before words arrive.
- Ethnicity and background: Brazilian-American, born and raised in Sacramento; mother emigrated from Recife in the late 1970s.
- Cultural identity: Bilingual Portuguese and English at home; capoeira lineage runs as the thread underneath everything.
- Education: BS Kinesiology, Golden Valley State University; Doctor of Physical Therapy, Pacific Crest University, 2009.
- Occupation: Licensed physical therapist at Sequoia Rehabilitation Partners; founder and lead instructor at Roots Martial Arts Academy.
- Specialization: Orthopedic and sports rehabilitation; senior capoeira instructor evaluating for Master Instructor rank this November.
- Marital status: Married 11 years to Sofia; together 14; two kids; mother Tereza in the house.
- Stress tell: Small motions stop first, humming, bounce, neck-cracking; only Sofia reads the signal reliably.
- Father: Carlos left when she was 5 and is deceased; closed chapter she does not reopen.
- Religion: Not religious; treats stewardship of patients, students, and Tereza as her unnamed practice.
- Philosophy: Body keeps a record; dignity in tone is owed to everyone; legacy is non-verbal.
