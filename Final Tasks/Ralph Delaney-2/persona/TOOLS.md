# Tools: Ralph Patrick Delaney

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Communication

- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Routes professional correspondence on ralph.delaney@Finthesiss.ai across firm, board, and bar-portal channels, and holds inbound referral and discovery requests for triage.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Runs the firm's internal channel where Monique, Devon, and Brenda post case-flow updates between court runs.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Hosts the discreet board-level thread Ralph keeps with Elaine Fontenot for sensitive Crescent City Legal Aid Foundation matters; board-level only.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Carries family logistics with Celeste, Josie, Marcus, Eileen, and Margaret without crossing into client matter or firm financial detail.

#### Productivity

- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Tracks court appearances, board meetings, family events, and trial-prep blocks.
- **Google Sheets** (`google-sheets-api`): Tracks the CLE-hours compliance tracker and the household and firm budget worksheets.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Organises trial-prep research, including the publication notes and methodology critiques the firm keeps on forensic experts for admissibility work.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Manages engagement letters, retainer agreements, and signature-bearing firm documents.

#### Finance

- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Connects the live banking feed for the firm operating account and household accounts; the source of record for what has actually landed.
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Runs the firm's bookkeeping ledger, including planned and expected deposits before they clear the bank.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Processes registration payments and the speaking honoraria tied to Ralph's CLE talks and published essays.

#### CRM & Collaboration

- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Available as a firm knowledge wiki, though the active trial-prep notes live in Notion.
- **Salesforce** (`salesforce-api`): Available as a client-relationship database, though the firm runs intake out of its own contacts.
- **HubSpot** (`hubspot-api`): Available for outreach pipelines, unused by the practice day to day.
- **Trello** (`trello-api`): Available as a board-style task tracker, kept dormant in favour of Slack and the calendar.

#### Not Connected

- Court filing systems, PACER, and Louisiana e-filing portals.
- Firm case-management software.
- Westlaw (firm expense, accessed directly outside the assistant).
- Employer payroll, banking, or trust-account systems.
- Live web search, web browsing, and deep internet research are unavailable; research runs through the connected workspace only.
