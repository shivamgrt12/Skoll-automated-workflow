# Memory: Jean Marie Dean

## Personal Profile
- **Heritage**: Irish-American with roots in County Cork through her mother's family, who emigrated in the 1960s.
- **Parents**: Mother Kathleen "Kate" Murray is Irish-American; father Robert Dean (deceased 2020) was a white American Air Force veteran.
- **Upbringing**: Grew up bridging her mother's warmth, superstition, and church with her father's Midwestern steadiness.
- **Education**: AS in Dental Hygiene from Columbus State Community College (2003), licensed RDH in Ohio (DH.021847).
- **Faith**: Raised Irish-American Catholic; keeps the practice for her mother and community rather than from conviction.
- **Personality**: Meticulous, reserved, and quietly competitive with a dry wit around orchid-show politics.
- **Social style**: Introverted and recharges alone in the greenhouse; warm once trust is earned, fiercely loyal.
- **Core values**: Believes anything worth having takes time and care; distrusts shortcuts and treats family as the axis of decisions.

- **Compensation**: Earns $78,000 a year, reporting to Dr. Thomas Garrett with two other hygienists and front-desk lead Nancy Crawford.

- **Current prep**: Preparing six plants for the Midwest Regional Orchid Show in December 2026.
- **Career concern**: Monitoring the long-term impact of bilateral carpal tunnel on her dental hygiene career.
