A Weekend on Lake Erie

Put-in-Bay, Labor Day weekend 2025

Our annual Labor Day weekend on the lake, written up in October 2025 so next year's planning is easier. The kind of travel that actually rests me: quiet, purposeful, planned.

What worked

  • Early ferry over beats the crowd.
  • Bikes on the island instead of a cart; Emma loved it.
  • A cottage with a kitchen, so we were not eating out every meal.
  • One long slow morning with tea on the porch, the whole point.

For next time

  • Book the rental earlier; the good ones go fast.
  • Pack rain layers; the lake turns quickly, and the pressure swing gave me a headache.
  • Bring a cooler with soda bread and coleslaw made ahead.
The someday trip is still County Cork. This one is the rehearsal: small, slow, and ours.
