The shop

James Gutierrez opened Sharp Line Cuts in 2014 after eight years he could not stomach in a job that paid well and meant nothing. He traded the company car for a pair of clippers and a six-month lease, and he has not looked back.

The shop runs on three barbers, one apprentice, and a rule that nobody walks in and feels invisible. The playlist is curated daily. The chair is a close space and we treat it that way.

A second location on McNichols Rd opens in 2027. Same standard, more chairs.
