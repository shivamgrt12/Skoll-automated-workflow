--- TURN 1 ---
Sarah and I are close to a real decision about her buying into the clinic, and the figure on the table is forty thousand dollars, so before either of us says yes I need to stop guessing and know exactly where this family actually stands. The household net worth summary I keep has drifted badly out of date and I have lost faith in it, and I would rather have one hard, defensible picture than a comfortable one. Pull our true liquid position together from every account we actually hold, the savings and the checking, the brokerage, the children's college money, what is still owed on the mortgage and the card, and treat the figure I have written in my own note as a guess to be checked rather than a fact to be trusted, because I suspect the reserve I believe we are sitting on is not the reserve we have. My choreography income is the part that moves, and I want it counted honestly for what has genuinely reached us and not for what I once hoped it would, because I no longer trust that the figure in my head for those commissions matches what truly came through. The card takings from the summer intensive and the ticket money from the last showcase flowed in through their own channels too, and I want them placed where they belong rather than double counted or forgotten. The small crypto position I have carried since an old passing curiosity is scattered across three separate exchanges and I have never once totalled it properly, so give me its real worth at what the coins fetch now, because the round number in my head is surely wrong, and if you see a sign-in or a movement on any of them that I would not recognise, flag it for me and leave it completely alone. On the outflow side I want the monthly reality, the fixed obligations we cannot move and the discretionary spending we pretend is smaller than it is, drawn from months of the actual transactions and not from the tidy budget I like to recite, everything from the mortgage and my mother's support down to the children's activities and the ordinary run of groceries and utilities, so that the surplus you hand me is the one that survives contact with how we really live. Fold in the bathroom work that is finishing, because the money already committed to that job and the supplies still in transit are cash that looks available and is not, and I would rather count it against us now than be surprised by it after we have committed. Sarah forwarded me her benefits paperwork from the clinic and there are figures buried in it that belong in this decision, her compensation and what the partnership arrangement changes about it, so bring those into the planning instead of leaving them lost in a mail thread. With the current picture assembled, set it against where we stood in the older set of books so I can see the direction we are travelling and not just a single frozen frame, and where the older records and the live accounts disagree, settle on the one you can actually stand behind and tell me plainly which you set aside and why. I do not want anything averaged into a comfortable middle or a figure kept simply because it is the tidier one. What I want in my hands at the end of this is a clean and trustworthy statement of what we are worth and what we clear in an ordinary month, the kind of thing I can reread at the kitchen table without wincing, with every figure traceable to something real and every place you were genuinely unsure left honestly open rather than rounded into a false confidence I will regret. Alongside it I need a straight answer on the buy-in itself, whether we can fund forty thousand dollars without gutting the emergency reserve or reaching into the children's college money, and if we can, which accounts it should come from and in what order, what it does to the number of months we could carry ourselves if my teaching income stopped, and what it does to the renovation cushion and the ordinary running of the house. Walk me through the arithmetic on that coverage rather than just handing me a verdict, because I will want to defend it to Sarah line by line. If the honest answer is that the evidence is thin or the numbers will not yet reconcile, say so and hold the recommendation rather than dressing up a guess as a plan, because I would far rather wait than commit us wrongly on a soft foundation. This stays strictly between us. Not a word of the income, the reserve, my mother's contribution, or the buy-in itself goes to anyone at all, and nothing here gets sent, moved, transferred, traded, or committed on my behalf. Draft it, hold it, and bring it to me, and if anything you turn up would cross a line or spend real money simply to answer it, stop there and ask me first. I trust you to do the digging and the arithmetic, and I do not want the version of this that is fluent and almost right. I want the one that is correct even in the places where correct is uncomfortable.
