# Memory: Amanda Webb

## Personal Profile

- Full name: Amanda Webb.
- Pronouns: she/her.
- Identity: Straight woman; first in her family to attend college, carries south Reading childhood as both pride and pressure.
- Ethnicity and background: American with Spanish-language Peña heritage on her mother's side; south Reading, Pennsylvania working-class roots; understands more Spanish than she speaks.
- Faith: Unfussy Christian faith learned from a working mother; small cross necklace stays on; Grace Community Church attended some weeks and not others.
- Education: B.S. Nursing, Keystone Valley University, 2014; Emergency Nursing Certification (CEN), 2018.
- Occupation: Emergency Department RN at Laurel Creek Regional Medical Center since 2014, last five years as senior staff nurse; also dance instructor renting space at Rhythm Dance Studio.
- Specialization: Emergency nursing at a Level II trauma center; contemporary dance instruction and choreography.
- Marital status: Single, never married. Most significant recent relationship was Carlos Mejia, two years, ended February 2026, mutual and mostly amicable.
- Temperament and philosophy: Calm under load, electric on a dance floor, quiet at home; humor as a relief valve; financial independence treated as a moral category; presence with the people who raised her non-negotiable.

## Key Relationships

- **Carol Webb** (mother, 58, DOB March 22, 1968). Housekeeper at Berkshire Motor Lodge, does alterations on the side. Lives in a 2-bedroom apartment in south Reading, fifteen minutes from Amanda. Anchor of Amanda's life. They talk daily, eat together Sundays and most Wednesdays. Carol worries about night shifts and wants Amanda to slow down and find a husband. Amanda adores her and pushes back gently on the traditional expectations. Prefers phone calls, never texts.
- **Aunt Linda Pena** (maternal aunt, 62). Carol's older sister, lives in Reading, retired factory worker. Helped Carol back on her feet after the divorce. Amanda's source for no-nonsense advice. Family gatherings and holidays.
- **Uncle Hector Ramos** (uncle by marriage, 64). Linda's husband, retired Reading SEPTA driver. Quiet, kind, carves the turkey at Thanksgiving. Attends Carol's family meals with Linda.
- **Grandma Ruth Pena** (maternal grandmother, 84). Lives in Sarasota, Florida. Amanda is named after her middle name. They FaceTime every other Sunday at 4:00 PM. Care packages with homemade cookies and hand-knitted items.
- **Jessica "Jess" Reyes** (best friend, 32, DOB July 7, 1994). RN in the ICU at Laurel Creek. Met in nursing school at Keystone Valley. Engaged to Miguel; Amanda is maid of honor at their October wedding. Ride-or-die. The friend who texts "u up" at the right hour.
- **Natalie "Nat" Taveras** (dance partner and friend, 29). Assistant instructor at Rhythm Dance Studio. Talented dancer, less reliable with scheduling. Performs with Amanda at showcases and covers Saturday class when Amanda is out. Pushes Amanda toward competing more seriously.
- **Carlos Mejia** (ex-boyfriend, 35). Firefighter at Reading Fire Station 4. Two-year relationship ended February 2026, mutual and mostly amicable. He wanted to settle down faster. They still text occasionally, but Amanda is cautious about contact.
- **Denise Calloway** (nurse manager, 47). ER nurse manager at Laurel Creek. Fair, protective of her nurses. Mutual respect with Amanda.
- **Dr. Thomas Aguilar** (ER attending physician, 40). Works with Amanda often. Professional respect; he has encouraged her FNP path.
- **Maribel Santos** (dance studio owner, 50). Owns Rhythm Dance Studio where Amanda rents space. Warm, business-savvy. Gives Amanda a favorable rate because the classes bring in students. Trusts Amanda with the after-hours key.
- **Derek Webb** (cousin, 28). Carol's nephew, moved to Reading from out of state two years ago. Amanda helped him settle in. Warehouse work. Comes to family dinners.
- **Dr. Patricia Delgado** (primary care physician). Reading Community Health Center. Annual checkup provider.
- **Luna** (cat, 4). Tabby. Sleeps on Amanda's pillow. Named after a TV character. Functionally an emotional support animal even if not formally.

Three 12-hour shifts per week on a rotating day/night schedule. Level II trauma center, about 180 patients per day. Credentials: BSN (Keystone Valley, 2014), CEN (2018), ACLS, PALS, TNCC.
- **Rhythm Dance Studio, Dance Instructor**. Rents space from Maribel Santos. Standing classes: Monday 7:00 to 8:00 PM (Beginner), Wednesday 7:00 to 8:30 PM (Intermediate), Thursday 8:00 to 9:30 PM (Contemporary Fusion), Saturday 11:00 AM to 12:30 PM (Open). Forty to fifty regular students across the four classes. Nat assists Thursday and Saturday.
- **Amanda's Dance Academy** (planned). The studio she intends to open within two to three years. Capital target $25,000 to $30,000. Business plan outlined in February 2026, location comparison spreadsheet started in January 2026, three candidate spaces scoped, two within walking distance of Wyomissing.
- **Continuing education**. Possible FNP master's under consideration but not committed. CEN recertification due March 2027.
- **Grants and small-business research**. Five women-entrepreneur grant programs identified, deadlines stretching through 2027.

## Health & Wellness

- **Primary care**: Dr. Patricia Delgado at Reading Community Health Center. Annual checkups. Last visit normal.
- **Fitness**: Three runs per week along the Schuylkill River Trail, three to four miles. Yoga via online classes on rest days. Combination of ER hustle and dance keeps her in strong condition.
- **Sleep**: Chronic disruption from rotating day/night shifts is her largest health issue. Blackout curtains, melatonin, and a strict pre-sleep routine on night blocks.
- **Medications/supplements**: Daily multivitamin and vitamin D.
- **Mental health**: Occasional anxiety managed with journaling, running, and breathing techniques from a hospital wellness workshop. Therapy considered, not yet started.
- **Injuries**: Right ankle sprained at a dance showcase in 2024, fully healed. Cautious with high-impact choreography.
- **Emotional load**: Pediatric ER cases stay with her. Journaling and Jess are her primary outlets.
- **Immunizations**: Annual flu shot and all hospital-required immunizations current.
- **Dietary**: No medical restrictions. Coffee non-negotiable, alcohol minimal and never before shifts.

- **Running**: Maintenance, three times a week, the same Schuylkill trail. Familiarity is the point.

- **Streaming**: Drama series, true crime docuseries, occasional rom-coms and reality dating shows for guilty-pleasure decompression.
