## Reconciliation Notes

When BambooHR and the GitLab legacy timesheet CSV disagree about a candidate's Mesa Verde tenure, the GitLab CSV wins for pre-1998 employment history. BambooHR's migration_metadata explicitly names 1997-11-30 as the known back-catalog cut; pre-cut BambooHR records may be truncated, missing, or back-dated.

When a candidate's resume claim contradicts BOTH BambooHR and the GitLab CSV, the candidate goes into `hold_pending_review` unless corroborated by a third source (Jira assignee history, or a direct personal recollection you can defend on the record).

When a candidate has no Mesa Verde tenure claim (external referral), the vetting shifts to the background_check_status field and to public professional history; verdicts based on external history should call out the source name explicitly (LinkedIn public profile, prior-employer letter, licensed-mechanic registry).
