# Memory: Sophia Rivera

## Personal Profile

- **Roots**: Third-generation New Mexican, born in Las Cruces, raised bilingual with Spanish running under everything at home.
- **Faith**: Catholic by upbringing and habit. Mass most Sundays at Our Lady of Guadalupe. Observes Dia de los Muertos.
- **Language**: Conversational Spanish, enough to follow family talk at funerals but loses the thread by the third joke.
- **Military service**: U.S. Army, 1971 to 1974, honorable discharge, Vietnam era. Does not lead with it publicly.
- **Education**: Diesel mechanics certificate, 1974, Santa Fe Community College. No further formal education beyond the trade.
- **Marriage**: Married Roberto "Bobby" Rivera (b. November 4, 1949, d. February 9, 2023, pancreatic cancer). Widowed, not dating.
- **Character**: Stubborn about independence, plain-spoken, slow to trust, durable once earned. Dry humor delivered flat.

## Key Relationships

- **Elena Rivera-Lindgren (daughter, 46, b. October 8, 1979)**: Lives in Albuquerque, about an hour south. Office manager at a medical equipment company. Married to Scott. Two kids. Calls Sophia twice a week, visits regularly. Set up Sophia's AI assistant. Worries about her mother living alone. Primary family contact and first call for any household or medical coordination. Sophia appreciates the worry and resists the management of it.
- **Marco Rivera (son, 44, b. January 15, 1982)**: Lives in Denver. Commercial HVAC. Divorced, no kids. Regular Sunday caller. Visits two to three times a year. Less hands-on than Elena, reliable when needed. The relationship is low-maintenance on both sides, which is how they both like it.
- **Scott Lindgren (son-in-law, 49, b. March 3, 1977)**: Elena's husband. Warehouse supervisor. Steady. Helps with the bigger property tasks on visits. Shares a love of fishing with Sophia. They get along well.
- **Lily Lindgren (granddaughter, 14, b. January 22, 2012)**: Eighth grader, quiet, studious. Sends Sophia texts with photos of her drawings. Sophia keeps every one in a manila folder.
- **Max Lindgren (grandson, 10, b. October 19, 2015)**: Fourth grader, energetic, into soccer and fishing. Loves Sophia's property and the pond. They fish together in summer.
- **Ernesto Rivera (father, b. March 15, 1925, d. January 1998)**: Worked road crews for the state highway department out of Las Cruces. Quiet man, steady provider. Sophia's mechanical instincts came from watching him maintain equipment in the driveway. Died of a heart attack at 72. She does not talk about him often but keeps his pocket knife in the shop drawer.
- **Carmen Rivera, née Gallegos (mother, b. November 22, 1928, d. August 2009)**: Raised three children in a house where Spanish ran under every conversation and English sat on top. Kept the books for a small trucking outfit in Las Cruces. Died at 80 after a stroke. The cast-iron skillet Sophia will not replace was hers.
- **Teresa Rivera Montaño (sister, b. February 18, 1951, d. March 2021)**: Older sister, lived in Albuquerque, worked as a school secretary. Died of complications from COPD. Sophia drove down for the funeral and has not sorted through what that loss sits next to in the stack. Elena checks in about it occasionally.
- **Luis Rivera (brother, 70, b. December 9, 1955)**: Lives in El Paso. Retired electrician. Married to Dolores. They talk on the phone three or four times a year and see each other at funerals. The relationship is warm but low-contact, the way siblings get when the parents are gone and the miles add up.
- **Rosa Delgado (neighbor and friend, 69, b. January 30, 1957)**: Adjacent property, quarter mile down the road. Retired rancher. Coffee at Sophia's kitchen table most mornings. The closest thing Sophia has to a daily social connection.
- **Father Miguel Herrera (priest, 61)**: Our Lady of Guadalupe, Santa Fe. Sunday Mass most weeks. Visited after Bobby's death. Trusted, not close.
- **Dr. Martin Castillo (primary care, 58)**: Desert View Medical Clinic, Santa Fe. Fifteen years. Manages most of her conditions.
- **Dr. Gail Sorensen (cardiologist, 52)**: Southwest Heart Specialists, Santa Fe. Manages her AFib regimen.
- **Dr. Susan Park (endocrinologist, 46)**: Rio Grande Endocrine Associates, Santa Fe. Manages her Type 2 diabetes.

## Work & Projects

- **Career**: Retired 2018 after thirty-eight years at Mesa Verde Paving and Gravel, Cortez. Diesel mechanic and heavy equipment operator.
- **Pre-Mesa Verde (1974 to 1980)**: Freelance diesel mechanic from the shop on County Road 31. Married Bobby 1979, Elena born October 1979.
- **Property maintenance**: Twelve acres requiring ongoing fence, roof, and equipment work. Depends on knee condition and Scott's weekend visits.
- **Shop practice**: Pole barn with welding bench, parts shelf, and hand tools. Year-round repair work, including small neighbor jobs.

## Finance

- **Income**: Social Security $2,180 per month plus Mesa Verde pension $650 per month, totaling around $2,830 monthly.
- **Monthly expenses**: Roughly $1,900 covering property tax, utilities, groceries, gas, Medicare, insurance, prescriptions, and household costs.
- **Surplus**: Around $930 per month flows into savings for unexpected medical expenses or property repair needs.
- **Savings**: Roughly $38,000 at Rio Grande Credit Union. Emergency fund. Roof replacement looming, estimated $8,000 to $14,000.
- **Assets**: Property and house valued around $180,000, owned outright. 2014 Chevy Silverado paid off. Shop tools and equipment.
- **Credit**: One Visa card through Rio Grande Credit Union, barely used. Pays cash for most things. Score around 760.
- **Banking**: Prefers writing checks for most transactions. Elena handles online bill-paying and VA portal access.

## Health & Wellness

- **Type 2 diabetes**: Diagnosed 2015. Metformin 1000mg twice daily. A1C 7.1 as of January 2026, target under 7.0.
- **Atrial fibrillation**: Diagnosed 2020. Eliquis 5mg twice daily, metoprolol succinate 50mg morning. Stable under Dr. Sorensen.
- **Hypertension**: Diagnosed 2008. Lisinopril 20mg daily in the morning. Blood pressure usually runs 135 to 140 over 85.
- **Osteoarthritis**: Both knees, right hip. Acetaminophen and topical menthol cream. Worse in cold weather. Avoids NSAIDs on blood thinners.
- **Hypothyroidism**: Diagnosed 2019. Levothyroxine 50mcg daily on empty stomach each morning. Managed through primary care.
- **Hearing loss**: Moderate bilateral. Phonak hearing aids since 2024, worn inconsistently despite encouragement from Elena.
- **Allergies**: Sulfa drugs cause rash. No other known drug allergies on record.
- **Daily medications**: Metformin 1000mg twice, eliquis 5mg twice, metoprolol 50mg, lisinopril 20mg, levothyroxine 50mcg, vitamin D3 2000 IU, fish oil 1200mg.
- **As-needed medications**: Acetaminophen 500mg for arthritis pain, maximum two tablets daily. No NSAIDs permitted.
- **Providers**: Dr. Castillo primary care, Dr. Sorensen cardiology, Dr. Park endocrinology, Dr. Vega dental, Dr. Montoya vision. VA eligible.
- **Exercise**: Daily 20 to 30 minute walk with Luna around the property. Light shop work and wood stacking. Limited by arthritis.
- **Mental health**: No formal diagnosis. Low energy and sleep disruption after Bobby's death in 2023. Resistant to counseling. Slowly improving.
- **Dietary**: Type 2 diabetes drives carb-conscious, low-sugar diet. Compliance improves when Elena visits and stocks the fridge.

## Interests & Hobbies

- **Pole barn**: Primary workspace with welding bench, wood stove, and parts shelf. Where most of the real day happens.
- **Fishing**: Fishes the property pond in summer, usually alone unless Max visits. More ritual than sport at this point.
- **Fence-line walks**: Daily property walks to note repair needs. Keeps a running mental list of outdoor maintenance jobs.
- **Dog notebook**: Spiral notebook tracking all seven dogs she has owned, with dates and descriptions. Private, shown to nobody.
- **Reading**: Physical books only. Western novels, military history, outdoor magazines. Currently reading "Iron Crossing" by Dale Rustad.
- **Bird watching**: Watches from the porch with a feeder she keeps stocked year-round. Quiet routine, no lists or guides.
- **TV**: Local news, baseball in season, nature documentaries. Falls asleep to whatever is on by 9:30 most nights.

## Preferences

- **Coffee**: Drip with a splash of cinnamon. First cup at 5:30. Cafe de olla style when there is time. Does not trust fancy coffee.
- **Cooking**: Eggs, beans, rice, tortillas, soup from scratch, grilled meat. Crockpot once a week for posole or carne guisada. Elena brings frozen enchiladas and portioned meals on visits.
- **Local food spots**: La Paloma Cafe (Saturday breakfast with Rosa). El Camino Supper Club (Friday fish fry, occasionally).
- **Alcohol**: One or two beers on weekends. Modelo, kept in the garage fridge. Used to drink more, cut back per doctor's orders.
- **Music**: Classic ranchera and Tejano. Linda Ronstadt, Selena, Vicente Fernández in heavy rotation. AM radio in the truck.
- **Reading**: Western novels, military history, outdoor magazines. Physical books only. Reads in the afternoon, 30 to 60 minutes.
- **Shopping**: In-person almost exclusively. Santa Fe Hardware, Santa Fe Tractor Supply, Kaune's Neighborhood Market. Has never bought anything online. Elena handles Amazon orders.
- **Clothing**: Carhartt jacket, flannel shirts, jeans, boots. One navy blazer for funerals and Christmas Mass.
- **Aesthetics**: Not sentimental about objects but loyal to them. A thirty-year-seasoned skillet does not get replaced. Jeans get patched at the knee rather than retired.
- **Sensory likes**: Green chile roasting in fall, creosote after rain, cold metal of the truck door handle at sunrise, the dog's nails on kitchen tile, dry pinon in the wood stove. Coffee that has been on the burner too long.
- **Sensory dislikes**: Fluorescent overhead lighting, perfume that fills a room, plastic wrap, cooked celery, music that asks her to feel something at a specific volume.
- **Travel**: Does not travel and has not since Bobby died. The Sangre de Cristos camper trip the two of them took every summer is not something she has figured out how to do alone. When she does travel, it is to Albuquerque for Elena or Denver for Marco. Same brown duffel bag since the late seventies.
- **Comfort routines**: The shop when the day gets away from her. The porch with Luna when the shop will not reach it. A call to Marco when neither does. A pot of beans on the stove. A hot bath when the knees are bad.
- **Gift giving and receiving**: Gives small, useful, person-specific gifts wrapped in brown paper and twine. Receives thank-yous a beat short. The people who know her know the thank-you is not the measure.
