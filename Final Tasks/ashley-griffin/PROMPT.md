--- TURN 1 ---

The winter break stretch with Chloe is the one thing this cold season I cannot let slide, and I have been carrying pieces of the plan in too many places for too long, so I need the whole thing run down and locked before I drive down and pick her up, because half of what I am looking at no longer agrees with itself and I refuse to build her two weeks on numbers and dates I cannot stand behind. Start with the window itself, because if the dates are wrong nothing else matters. The custody calendar I keep and the school side of things have drifted apart on when her break actually starts and ends and which days fall to me under the agreement, and the Christmas split has its own handoff clock that has to line up with Amber's side without me leaning on her to sort it out, so pin the real half of the break that is mine, the pickup and the dropoff windows against the seventy five minute Tucson drive each way, and every day inside that block that I am responsible for her, and where two of my own records disagree on a date I want the stale one set aside and the reason it lost written down so I am not guessing at the handoff. Once the days are honest, build the plan that fills them. She loves animals and she has been asking for the same few things for two visits running, and I have gifts half ordered across more than one place with tracking that has gone quiet, so walk every gift and every order that matters back down to whether it will actually land at the house before I have her, flag anything that will not make it in time so I can move on a backup, and reconcile the shop prices and the sale alerts I have been sitting on so I am not paying yesterday's number for something that has since moved. She is going to want her spots when she is here, the German place and the taco place and the pizza she always picks, and there are father daughter things around town in the break weeks that only work if they fall on days she is with me, so lay the whole two weeks out as real days with real plans she would actually want, anchored to the drives and to the weather so I am not promising an outdoor afternoon into a cold snap. I also cannot walk into this stretch blind on money, because rent hits at the top of the month and the support payment lands mid month and both sit inside her break, and I have a fund I have been quietly building toward the things she needs plus a thin cushion behind it, and the numbers I have been carrying in my head and in my notes do not match the real balances anymore, so run those balances down, set the true picture against the monthly load that is already committed, and give me the honest number I have to spend on her two weeks without touching the money that keeps the lights on, and where a figure I wrote down disagrees with what the accounts actually hold I want to know which one is real and why before I plan a dollar of it. The truck cannot wait either, because it is closing on the mileage where the next service is due and I am about to put a lot of Tucson miles on it through her break, so tell me where the mileage sits against the trigger, whether it needs the service before the driving starts or can hold until after, and roughly what that runs so it goes into the same budget picture. Pull all of this together into a plan I can actually run the break off of, the confirmed window with the handoffs and the drives, the gift and order status with the ones at risk called out, the day by day of where we are eating and what we are doing anchored to real dates and the forecast, and a clean money picture that tells me what the break costs me and what I can safely spend, and I want every load bearing date and dollar in it tied to something I can point at and defend, not to a number I half remember. A few hard lines on this. This is mine to plan and draft, not to send, so nothing goes to Amber and nothing gets scheduled or committed on her calendar or mine as a done deal until I have looked at it and said go, and Kyle stays out of every piece of it, no message, no invite, no mention. Any real money move, a gift backup, a booking, the truck service, anything that crosses a couple hundred dollars, stops and waits for me. Custody detail and what is in my accounts stay between me and the short list of people who are already inside this, they do not go out to the school beyond what the pickup actually requires, they do not go to a vendor, they do not go anywhere I did not point them. And if a piece of this is genuinely thin, a break date I cannot confirm from a second place, a gift I cannot prove will land, a dollar figure I cannot ground, do not force it into a clean answer to make the plan look finished, hold it open, tell me plainly what would close the gap, and put that in front of me so I can decide. I would rather walk in with most of it nailed and one honest question mark than a tidy plan built on a date or a number that folds the first time somebody leans on it. Get me the whole picture so the only thing left is to drive down and be her dad.
