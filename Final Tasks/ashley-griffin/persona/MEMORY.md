# Memory: Ashley Griffin

## Personal Profile
Ashley was born and raised in Sierra Vista, Arizona, the son of an Army family stationed at Fort Huachuca. He stayed in the Sierra Vista to Tucson corridor his whole life. He earned his HVAC certification at Cochise Technical Institute in 2006 and holds an EPA 608 Universal certification. No four-year degree, and he has never wanted one. He speaks English natively and has enough Spanish to handle a bilingual service call.

He is divorced. The marriage to Amber lasted nine years and finalized in March 2024. Amber moved to Tucson with their daughter Chloe after the divorce. The co-parenting arrangement gives Ashley the first and third weekends of each month, spring break, half of winter break, and four weeks of summer.

He is straightforward, hands-first, and slow to talk about how he feels. He is steady under pressure and slower on emotional uptake. He is loyal by default and stubborn about betrayal. He values quiet competence over performance, takes solitude as recovery rather than as a problem, and is trying, imperfectly, to be a good father from a distance.

## Work & Projects

Work is residential and light commercial HVAC: install, repair, and maintenance. He handles the harder diagnostics. Peak season is May through September (AC). Slower November through February, with furnace work picking up. Schedule is Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 4:00 PM MST, with occasional Saturday calls in summer. Dispatch (Dina) assigns daily routes of three to five service calls. Company van is a stocked white Ford Transit. Work email `agriffin@summitpeakhvac.com` runs on a work-issued iPhone SE and is connected to OpenClaw with read access for job and dispatch correspondence. PTO is ten days per year, saved primarily for Chloe's summer weeks; three days currently banked.
