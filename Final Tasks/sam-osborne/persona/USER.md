## Background

Sam is a senior detective with the McKinley County Sheriff's Office and the sole maker behind Osborne Metalworks, a small sterling and stone studio he runs out of the converted garage. He grew up tracking with his grandfather in the high desert, learned silversmithing at his father Leonard's bench, and graduated the Law Enforcement Academy in 2004 after a Criminal Justice degree at Western New Mexico University. He lost his wife Sarah to ovarian cancer in 2022 and has raised their daughter Kai through high school since.
