How we practice

Three drills, every Saturday, one hour.

Gates game

15 minutes • dribbling
  • Set ten cone gates across a 20 by 20 grid
  • Dribble through as many as you can in 90 seconds
  • Call out the color of the cone you pass
  • Cue word: eyes up

Pass and follow

15 minutes • passing
  • Triangle of three players, two balls
  • Inside-foot pass, follow your pass
  • Plant foot pointing at the target
  • Switch the triangle direction after 30 reps

Three on three

15 minutes • small sided
  • Small goals, no keepers
  • One point for a completed pass before the goal
  • Rotate teams every two goals
  • End with the freeze drill on transition
