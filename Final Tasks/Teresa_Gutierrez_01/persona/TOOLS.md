# Tools: Teresa Gutierrez

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Family Coordination & Messaging

- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Primary inbox. Reads and drafts school emails, appointment confirmations, and vendor receipts she wants on a paper trail.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Mirrors the few contacts still on Outlook. Pulls school and medical-billing threads into the same daily sweep as Gmail.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Family thread with Brian, Marc, and Pauline. Sends quick logistics, short messages, no drafts longer than two lines.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Joins the school district's parent-teacher conferences and weather briefings two or three times a year and posts her availability back.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Works the general contractor coordination channel a project lead opens on the downtown rewiring, posting status and reading day-of changes.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): Tracks the youth soccer parent server for game-day weather calls and field changes and replies to coach pings.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Follows a couple of bait shop and outdoor channels for fishing conditions and pulls the latest before trip weekends.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Hosts and joins school conferences and the occasional telehealth follow-up with Dr. Kimball.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Books Dr. Kimball's follow-up slots and any soccer-league volunteer meeting times directly from her link.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Drafts SMS reminders to Brian and Pauline about pickups and appointments; sends only after she confirms.

#### Calendar, Notes & Documents

- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Shared family calendar with Brian. Adds kids' activities, medical appointments, and work project milestones.
- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Shared family Drive. Files school forms, insurance PDFs, the smoker notebook backup, and project receipts.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Shares and pulls large photo sets with Marc after the ice-fishing weekend and family trips.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Downloads and uploads school district forms when they route through Box instead of email.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Updates the one household page Brian set up, adding project notes and shared to-dos when she is directed to.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Keeps her private project log for the upstairs bathroom re-tile in Markdown, adding measurements and material notes.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Pulls the general contractor's spec handovers and as-builts for the day's rooms on the downtown rewiring mid-job.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Reviews and routes the Sugarloaf rental agreement and mortgage refinance paperwork; signatures go only after explicit approval.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Completes and submits the soccer league's parent intake forms and reads back roster responses.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): Drafts and publishes tournament writeups and coach bios on the soccer league site a few times a season.

#### Household Finance & Receipts

- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Pulls Bangor Savings joint-account balances and transactions into the monthly budget review.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Generates and tracks invoices for the occasional 1099 side job; charges go out only after she confirms.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Splits shared trip costs with Marc and settles small online payments.
- **Square** (`square-api`): Runs the soccer league concession and fundraiser register a couple of Saturdays a season and reconciles the till.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Logs the small side jobs - friend's basement rewire, Pauline's panel swap - for clean tax-time reconciles.
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Self-Employed tier for the side-job stream. Runs the monthly Stripe reconcile and tags truck-log mileage.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Checks the small holding Brian keeps during budget reviews and flags balance swings to him; no purchases without his say.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Tracks the BTC and ETH balance Brian asks about twice a month and reports the moves back to him.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Pulls a second crypto balance she compares against Coinbase during budget reviews.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Runs the paper-trading portfolio Marc set up to sanity-check retirement allocations before tax-season talks with Brian.

#### Shopping, Groceries & Local Errands

- **Amazon Seller** (`amazon-seller-api`): Pulls order history for hardware, boot socks, and household reorders to reconcile receipts; purchases get her confirmation.
- **Etsy** (`etsy-api`): Sources handmade gifts once or twice a year and drafts the order for her approval.
- **Instacart** (`instacart-api`): Places Hannaford orders during ice-storm weeks when she does not want to drive.
- **DoorDash** (`doordash-api`): Orders family Friday pizza when Pat's pickup line is too long.
- **Uber** (`uber-api`): Books BGR airport runs when Marc or Brian flies, plus the rare Portland night out.
- **Airbnb** (`airbnb-api`): Searches and holds the Sugarloaf rental and camping cabin backups; bookings confirmed with Teresa and Brian first.
- **Yelp** (`yelp-api`): Looks up unfamiliar restaurants when traveling with the kids and pulls hours and reviews.
- **Instagram** (`instagram-api`): Follows smoker and woodworking accounts and saves posts she wants to come back to.
- **Pinterest** (`pinterest-api`): Maintains saved boards for the upstairs bathroom tile and Sophie's room ideas.
- **BigCommerce** (`bigcommerce-api`): Orders niche tackle from the Eddington bait shop for the ice-fishing weekend two or three times a year.
- **WooCommerce** (`woocommerce-api`): Reorders smoker gaskets and probes from the Maine smoker forum store each fall.
