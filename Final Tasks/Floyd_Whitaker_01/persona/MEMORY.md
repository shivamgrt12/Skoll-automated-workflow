# Memory: Floyd Whitaker

## Personal Profile

Floyd Wayne Whitaker is a White Appalachian-American freight broker and logistics consultant who owns and operates Whitaker Freight Services out of a commercial suite near the Pellissippi Parkway corridor in Knoxville, Tennessee. He was born in Harlan, Kentucky to Earl Whitaker of Harlan County coal country and Mama June, a third-generation East Tennessean; he grew up driving between Harlan and Knoxville and remains deeply rooted in Cumberland Gap Appalachian culture. He holds a B.S. in Business Administration (1997) from a Tennessee regional university, spent 11 years at Cumberland Freight Associates from 1997 to 2008 before founding Whitaker Freight Services that same year, is a practicing Southern Baptist at Ridgeview Baptist Church under Pastor James Harlan, warm and direct, courteous to the point of formality with new contacts, and tied at the core to freight commerce, family, and the weekly rhythm between Knoxville and Harlan.

He fills his year around Whitaker Freight Services and its 6-person team led by office manager Bren Sizemore, a 4-bedroom home in a quiet west Knoxville neighborhood built in 2010 with a covered patio, brick smoker pit, and small vegetable garden, his wife Donna of 24 years and their three children Brandon "Brand" (22), Megan (19), and Cody (15), Saturday morning over-40 softball at Lakeshore Park, Saturday afternoon smoker sessions on the back patio, Sunday dinners at Mama June's house in Harlan, daily morning calls with brother Darl, weekly poker nights at The Back Porch, Tennessee Volunteers football on fall Saturdays at Neyland Stadium, and two to three fishing trips a year to Douglas Lake and Cherokee Lake with Darl and Keith Burgess.

## Work & Projects

- Business: Whitaker Freight Services, freight brokerage and logistics consulting firm based in Knoxville, TN.
- Founded: 2008.
- Licensing: FMCSA Freight Broker License #28471. Certified Transportation Broker (CTB) through the Transportation Intermediaries Association.
- Team: 6 employees. Bren Sizemore (office manager), Brand Whitaker (junior associate), 2 licensed freight dispatch specialists, 1 bookkeeper, 1 administrative assistant.
- Clients: 30 to 40 active. Mid-size manufacturers and agricultural producers. Specializes in automotive parts, agricultural products, building materials. Southeast and Midwest corridors.
- Services: freight brokerage, carrier vetting, DOT compliance consulting, route optimization, load matching, claims management.
- Revenue: firm grosses approximately $650,000 to $750,000 per year. Floyd draws a salary plus quarterly owner distributions.
- Industry: Tennessee Freight Association board member. Co-founder of the Tennessee Freight Mentorship Program with Keith Burgess. Occasional speaker at Southeast logistics conferences (Atlanta, Memphis, Charlotte).
