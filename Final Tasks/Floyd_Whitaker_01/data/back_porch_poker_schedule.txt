How this got started:
  We have been playing since the winter of 2014. Started as three fellows
  and a card table in Keith's garage after a load fell through and nobody
  wanted to go straight home. Grew from there. It is a friendly game.
  Nobody goes home broke and nobody quits mad. If you cannot make it,
  text the group so we know whether to hold a chair.

Buy-in: $20
House rules: Texas Hold'em, no limit after 9 PM

Full house rules:
  Buy-in is $20 for the first stack. One rebuy allowed before 9 PM,
    same $20, no more after that. What you bring is what you play.
  Dealer button moves clockwise. New deck cracked every session.
  Blinds start small and step up on the hour so nobody stalls out.
  No limit after 9 PM, before that we keep raises sensible so the
    slow starters get a fair shake.
  No phones on the table when a hand is live. Answer it in the yard.
  Verbal is binding. Say raise, you raised. Say call, you called.
  Splashing the pot gets you the stink eye and a reshuffle.
  String bets do not count. Get your chips out in one motion.
  Loser of the last big pot deals the next hand. Keeps folks honest.
  Whoever hosts sets the food. Guests bring their own drinks.
  We settle up in cash at the end of the night. No IOUs carried over
    to the next week. Keith keeps the little notebook if anybody argues.

Standings jar:
  A dollar of every buy-in goes in the mason jar on the shelf.
  End of the season the points leader takes the jar. Second place gets
    his buy-in back for the first night of the next season.

Seasons:
  Winter runs January through March.
  Spring runs April through June.
  Summer is loose, we play when folks are in town.
  Fall runs September through Thanksgiving. This is the fall sheet.
