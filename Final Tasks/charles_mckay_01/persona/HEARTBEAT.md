# Heartbeat: Charles McKay

## Recurring Events

### Daily
- **5:30 AM MT**: Up by habit. Coffee, then check the weather station console and log the overnight readings around 5:45 AM. Feed Duke.
- **Morning**: Property walk or drive, then ranch work such as fencing, water checks, and maintenance. Lunch at home.
- **Afternoon**: School board work, the once-a-day email review, weather data, or a 25-minute town run to Cody.
- **Evening**: Cook dinner, one pour of Scotch on the porch with Duke, read. Bed around 10:00 PM.

### Weekly
- **Sunday morning**: Call Robert and review the week's weather forecast. Day is otherwise quiet at home.
- **Wednesday**: Property check. Drive the fence lines, check the water tanks and wells.
- **Saturday morning**: Town run to Cody if needed for groceries, supplies, and errands.
- **2nd and 4th Tuesday**: Park County School Board meeting at 6:30 PM at the courthouse in Cody. He likes a leave-by nudge around 5:30 PM so he is on the road by 5:45.

### Monthly
- **1st of each month**: Review the ranch maintenance log and confirm Roy Patterson's grazing lease payment has landed.

### Seasonal / Variable
- **Fishing season**: Fly fishing on the Shoshone River and its tributaries, two to three times a month, more often when the grandkids are visiting.
- **Monthly visits (year-round)**: Robert drives down from Sheridan, usually a Saturday, bringing William and Grace in summer.
- **Every 2 to 3 weeks (year-round)**: Sarah visits from Casper with Hannah and restocks the freezer.
- **Fire season**: James is on fire deployment and largely unreachable. Off-season he visits once or twice, with a fishing trip when he can.
- **Weekly or so**: Roy Patterson checks in at the property-line fence or the kitchen table.
- **1 to 2 times a month**: Sunday services at Bighorn Presbyterian Church.

### Annual
- **January 8**: Robert McKay's birthday.
- **January 19**: Eleanor McKay's death anniversary (2019). Charles is quiet this day and keeps it clear of anything heavy.
- **February 18**: Eleanor McKay's birthday. He tends her garden and is reflective.
- **March 5**: James McKay's birthday.
- **March 14**: Katie McKay's birthday.
- **October 15**: William McKay's birthday.
- **November 10**: Hannah Henderson's birthday.
- **November 22**: Sarah McKay-Henderson's birthday.
- **December 3**: Grace McKay's birthday.
- **December 14**: Charles McKay's birthday.
