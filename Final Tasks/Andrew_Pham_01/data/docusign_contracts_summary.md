### Section 4.2 - Cost Escalation Clause (verbatim quote)

> "In the event that a Bureau of Labor Statistics regional construction cost index applicable to the Houston-Sugar Land-Baytown MSA registers a cumulative increase greater than four and one-half percent (4.5%) between the effective date of this Phase 2 Master Contract and the ninetieth (90th) day thereafter, Contractor shall be entitled to an escalation of the Phase 2 Base Fee, calculated as the product of (a) the Phase 2 Base Fee and (b) the excess of the observed index change above four and one-half percent (4.5%), not to exceed twelve percent (12%) of the Phase 2 Base Fee. Such escalation shall be memorialized by executed addendum and shall be payable in the next scheduled draw."

### Section 8.1 - Auto-Renew Clause (verbatim quote)

> "This Phase 2 Master Contract shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month terms on each anniversary of the effective date unless written notice of non-renewal is received by Contractor not less than ninety (90) days prior to the applicable renewal date. Renewal terms shall carry all pricing then in effect, including any escalation memorialized under Section 4.2."
