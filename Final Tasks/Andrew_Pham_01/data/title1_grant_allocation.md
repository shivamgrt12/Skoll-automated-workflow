# Title I Supplemental Enrichment Grant - Allocation Summary

**Grant:** Crescent Oaks ISD Title I, Part A - Supplemental Enrichment Set-Aside
**Award reference:** COISD-T1-SUPP-LSA
**Recipient program:** Lone Star Academy After-School Vietnamese Music & Enrichment Program
**Award period:** Fall semester through spring semester, current school year
**Campus:** Lone Star Academy (Title I schoolwide)

## Approved allocation by category

| Line | Category | Approved allocation |
|---|---|---|
| L-100 | Guest-teacher stipends (contracted enrichment instructors) | $8,000 |
| L-200 | Instruments, materials, and consumables | $6,500 |
| L-300 | Family-engagement events and interpretation | $6,000 |
| L-400 | Late-activity bus transportation | $3,500 |
| | **Total approved** | **$24,000** |

## Standing conditions of award

- Funds supplement and do not supplant local programming.
- Stipend payments must be supported by time-and-effort records for hours actually delivered.
- Family-engagement expenditures must be tied to documented events.
- Parent-volunteer in-kind hours may be reported toward community match only where a signed, time-stamped sign-in record exists.
- A mid-year progress and expenditure report is required before the district submission window closes.
