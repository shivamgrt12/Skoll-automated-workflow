## Caption (published, as posted)

Portland Saturday Market yesterday and my old man showed up in his knit
cap and his flannel and pretended he was going to nap on the stool. He
did not nap. He sold three kimchi tourtières to a family from Salem
before I finished setting the plancha.

Papa Luc has been slicing pork shoulder since before I could tie my
shoes and it still shows up in every square that leaves this booth. So
does Sienna's kitchen thinking, which is why the fusion version keeps
outselling the classic three to one.

Booth PDX-SM-B14 next Saturday, same corner, more of everything. Bring
cash for the maple butter, that never lasts past ten in the morning.

Sienna took this one. Papa did not know she was pointing the phone at
him. Just as well.
