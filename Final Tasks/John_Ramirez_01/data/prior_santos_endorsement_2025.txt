SANTOS ENDORSEMENT REMARKS - DRAFT (October 2025 campaign cycle)
=========================================================

Setting: Maria Santos campaign event, Ward 3 community center
Speaker: Councilman John Ramirez (Ward 5)
Length: ~4 minutes
Audience: ~120 attendees, Ward 3 voters, local press in back row

[Opening - 30 seconds]
"Good evening. I'm John Ramirez. I serve as councilman for Ward 5, which is the next door over from this room. I'm here tonight to speak about Maria Santos's work on small-business policy."

[Substance - 2.5 minutes]
"Small businesses in this city are not abstract. They are the people you see every day. They are the families who bought a storefront and decided to stay even when staying got hard. They are why Maria Santos's work on Ward 3's main-street initiative matters: because somebody had to do it, and she did the work."

"I want to be clear about what I'm doing here. I am not running with Maria. We are not on a ticket together. I serve Ward 5 and she serves Ward 3, and our jobs are different. But I have watched her advocate for shop owners and restaurant owners and family-business owners through three council cycles. She showed up when nobody else did. That counts."

[Specific to Ward 3 - 30 seconds]
"On the Ward 3 small-business grant program, Maria pushed for clearer eligibility criteria and a faster application cycle. That fix saved real shops in this district. I'd like to see Ward 5 adopt that same framework. That's not a joint platform. It's two councilmen learning from each other and bringing the best of each other's work back home."

[Close - 30 seconds]
"I'm here because Maria deserves another term. The work she's done is the kind of work that doesn't get headlines but does keep the lights on at the family bakery on Main Street. If you live in Ward 3, vote for Maria Santos. Thank you."

[End - 3:30 total]

Notes for next cycle (Nov 2026):
- Audience similar
- Press presence likely higher (Paterson Press, NJ.com)
- Avoid any phrasing that could be quoted as joint platform
- Avoid sharing specific Ward 5 or Ward 3 vote intent
- Stay on cross-ward distance language; same length target ~4 minutes
