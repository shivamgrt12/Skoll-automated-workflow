## Modification scope

- Convert **2** Chrysler Pacifica vans to wheelchair-accessible configuration.
- The 8 Toyota Camry sedans are out of scope for medical transport.
- Estimated modification cost: **$4,000 per van** (planning estimate).
- Estimated total modification capital: **$8,000** (2 vans x $4,000).

## Revenue assumptions (early 2026 planning)

- New medical transport line projected to add roughly $1,150 per van per month
  net of the added insurance rider and incremental fuel/maintenance.
- Two vans modified => roughly $2,300 per month incremental net contribution.

## Break-even

- Total modification capital $8,000 / ~$2,300 per month incremental
  => **break-even projected at 14 months** on two modified vans.
- Plan assumes MIA airport queue revenue holds and the base insurance policy
  absorbs most of the medical transport rider.
