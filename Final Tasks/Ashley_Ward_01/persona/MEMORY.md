# Memory: Ashley Ward

## Personal Profile
- **Full name**: Ashley Ward, she/her, born and raised in Allapattah and has stayed in the neighborhood by choice.
- **Heritage**: Black American woman with Southern roots, Caribbean neighbors, and a working-class business sensibility she taught herself.
- **Education**: AA in Business Administration from Miami-Dade College, 1990, plus a CDL Class B commercial license.
- **Faith role**: Deacon at Grace Community Church for over a decade and a congregation member since her childhood years.
- **Pew tradition**: Her mother sat in the same Grace Community pew first, and now they sit in it together.
- **Register switching**: Formal at the deacon board, plain at the fleet yard, regional at family lunch, without ceremony.
- **Temperament**: Steady, deliberately slow, and stubborn in the specific way that has historically kept the lights on.
- **Listening habit**: She lets silence sit longer than is comfortable, then the other person says what they were avoiding.
- **Grief**: Carries Michael's loss quietly, rearranged but not weaponized, and does not bring it up unless she chooses.
- **Theology of service**: She believes service is the practical expression of faith across fleet, family, church, and mentoring JP.
- **Loyalty rule**: The people who showed up in the lean years are the ones she builds the rest of her life around.
- **View on success**: Wary of any version of success that would require becoming someone her younger self would not recognize.
- **Romance**: Currently a closed topic; she handled two well-meaning church introductions with grace and absolute finality.

## Key Relationships
- **Michael Ward (husband, deceased)**: Born August 12, 1972. Died July 2020 at 47, pancreatic cancer. Married 22 years (wedding May 16, 1998). The grief is still present, just quieter, and she does not bring it up unless she chooses to. Christmas, the May anniversary, and his August birthday are the hard days.
- **Dorothy "Mom" Ward (mother, 82)**: Born September 22, 1943. Lives in the attached in-law apartment. Sharp mind, arthritic knees, mild hypertension, refuses a walker and refuses physical therapy. Takes Celebrex and amlodipine. Sits in the same pew at Grace on Sundays. Calls daily.
- **Claudette Ward (daughter, 28)**: Born October 31, 1997. Marketing coordinator at a Miami hotel chain. Lives in Coral Gables. Runs the fleet's social media on the side. iMessage is the channel. Closest to Mom of the kids.
- **Jean-Pierre "JP" Ward (son, 24)**: Born March 8, 2002. Fleet mechanic and driver. Lives at home. Ashley is grooming him to take over the business. iMessage. Quiet, steady, mechanically gifted, slow to commit to the plan but moving toward it.
- **Reginald Ward (brother, 52)**: Born April 4, 1974. Electrician in North Miami. Fishing buddy, closest confidant, and Ashley's designated ICE and medical proxy. Drove Michael to hospital appointments without being asked. Phone, not text.
- **Pastor David Reynolds (63)**: Senior pastor at Grace Community for over two decades. Officiated Michael's funeral without billing the family. Ashley's spiritual anchor. Sensitive matters in person on Sunday, not by email.
- **Frank Morrison (48)**: Fleet manager, 15 years with Ward Taxi. Ashley's right hand on daily operations. Covers dispatch when she is at appointments or funerals. Phone is the channel. Catches the back half of her jokes a beat late.
- **Linda Charles (59)**: Grace Community church secretary. Coordinates deacon board logistics and church events. Text is the channel.
- **Magda**: 6-year-old Rottweiler mix. Fleet office mascot and constant companion at the yard. Tail thumps the desk every morning.

- **Career bridge**: Drove taxis and ran overnight dispatch for a Miami-Dade company between 1990 and 2000 before founding hers.
- **Fleet composition**: Twelve vehicles total, eight Toyota Camry sedans and four Chrysler Pacifica vans for group work.
- **Fleet yard**: Located at 7340 NE 2nd Avenue in Allapattah, with the office, mechanic bay, and driver staging area.

## Interests & Hobbies
- 1978 Cadillac DeVille restoration in the garage. Bought eight months after Michael's funeral. The slowness is the entire point. New fuel pump one quarter, headliner trim the next.
- Dominos at the Allapattah community center, Saturdays at 2 PM. Same handful of people for almost twenty years. She wins more than she loses and does not keep score out loud.
- Deep-sea fishing off Key Biscayne with Reginald, 3 to 4 times a year. Six hours each way. Two thermoses and ask before turning the radio on.
- Home cooking is the love language. Smoked ribs on the offset rig at sunrise on holidays. Chicken soup on New Year's Day from her grandmother's recipe, unmodernized.
- Church choir appreciation. She cannot sing and shows up anyway. Third pew, arms crossed, half-smile when the arrangement is working.
- Talk radio during fleet runs, South Florida Public Radio.
- The Ghana trip idea has been in the back of her mind since a 2018 sermon. It sits there without urgency.

## Preferences
- Coffee: Folgers dark roast, black, strong. Two cups before dawn in the heavy mug Michael bought from a thrift store in 2003.
- Food: smoked ribs with coleslaw, rice and beans, plantains (fried or boiled), hush puppies, chicken soup on New Year's Day.
- Wine only at family gatherings. Sweet tea on the porch.
- Lunch spots near the yard: Sunrise Diner, Bayview Grill, The Garden Table (Allapattah), Harbor Seafood for seafood.
- Music: Marvin Gaye and Al Green on Saturday mornings while cooking. Gospel on Sunday. Al Green at the end of a long Thursday. Picks an album and lets it run. Does not shuffle, does not curate playlists.
- Radio: South Florida Public Radio during fleet runs.
- TV: local Channel 7 news, nature documentaries, old westerns, dominos YouTube channels.
- Reading: Bible, fishing magazines, the newspaper, occasional business biography. Prefers a notebook that lays flat and a pen with a fine line.
- Clothing: slacks and blouses for the office. A specific cardigan for cooler weather. A handful of rotated church dresses, each chosen years ago by Michael or Mom. One watch (Michael's 20th anniversary gift), one good handbag.
- Travel: small inn over hotel chain. Same wheeled carry-on packed the same way every time. Does not check bags. Does not check weather more than once before leaving. Suspicious of resort travel.
- Aesthetics: heavy mugs, real wool, leather that wears in, uncluttered rooms edited slowly. Dislikes chintzy or hollow construction and identifies it in ten seconds.
- Comfort: back porch, Magda at her feet, sweet tea, horn-section music at low volume. After a hard day, the kitchen. Eight hours on the smoker is a meditation she would not call a meditation.
- Spending: tithe first every month. Generous with gifts that are exactly right because she watches what people use. Tight on subscription creep and discount-driven deals.
- Social: small inner circle, wide network. Twice in an hour at deacon meetings, two-hour conversation if you have something real to say.
