## Insurance Processing
1. Customer drops vehicle; Maya takes photos and creates estimate
2. Estimate uploaded to carrier portal within 24 hours
3. Approval received; parts ordered; job scheduled
4. Supplements filed within 48 hours of teardown discovery
5. Final invoice and photos sent to carrier on completion
6. Payment deposited; any discrepancy flagged within 5 business days
