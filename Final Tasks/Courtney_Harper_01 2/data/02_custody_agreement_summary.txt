Custody Arrangement Summary
Harper v. Harper - Montgomery County Domestic Relations Court

Parenting Schedule:
- Alternating weekly custody, switch on Sundays at 5:00 PM
- Parent receiving children is responsible for pickup
- Holiday schedule per standard Montgomery County guidelines
- Both parents must provide 48-hour notice for schedule changes
- Neither parent shall disparage the other in presence of children

Child Support:
- Gregory Harper pays $650 per month to Courtney Harper
- Payment due on the 1st of each month
- Payments made via direct deposit or personal check

Emergency Contact Protocol:
- Custodial parent is primary decision-maker during their custody time
- Both parents must be notified of any emergency room visit within 1 hour
- School and medical providers have both parents listed as authorized contacts
