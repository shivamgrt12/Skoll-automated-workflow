# Memory: Jane Sparks

## Personal Profile
- Born and raised in Westside Charlotte. Mother's family rooted in Savannah, Georgia. Father's family from upstate South Carolina. Monolingual English household.
- White American (Anglo, working-class Southern).
- Father walked out when Jane was 14 and Kyle was 6. The exit shaped her loyalty to family and her quiet, deep-seated drive to be the person who does not leave.
- Education: B.A. in Criminal Justice from Appalachian State University (2010), then Paralegal Certificate from Charlotte Paralegal Institute (2011).
- Methodist by upbringing and still by identity. Attends Grace Methodist sporadically, says grace before big family meals, and prays through the rough patches.
- Carries a steady Protestant guilt that runs as the background hum of her life. Reads as overcommitment to the people she loves and quiet stubbornness about her own needs.
- Carries other people's worst days professionally and does not always notice when it seeps into her own mood until someone she loves points it out.
