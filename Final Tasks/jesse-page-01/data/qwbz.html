About

The work is not interchangeable.

I'm Jesse — a food photographer based in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. I grew up in Flushing on dumplings and dim sum, studied photography at Prattfield Institute of Art (BFA, 2013), and have run my practice solo since 2020.

My eye is always framing: window orientation, plating surface, the way late light hits a fire escape. I shoot tethered on a Sony A7IV with Profoto lighting, direct the plating myself, and finish in Lightroom and Capture One. Every dietary need on a call sheet is handled — I keep a dairy-free map of this city in my head.

I treat my Instagram grid as a living portfolio, not social media. If you've found a frame of mine that made you hungry, that's the whole job.
