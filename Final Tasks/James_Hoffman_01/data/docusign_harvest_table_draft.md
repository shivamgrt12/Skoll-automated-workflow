## Supply Agreement — 2027 Season

**Effective**: January 1, 2027
**Term**: January 1, 2027 through December 31, 2027

### Weekly Supply

Hoffman Organics LLC will provide fresh certified organic produce to The Harvest Table restaurant on a weekly basis, delivered Fridays by 10:00 AM to the loading dock at 218 East Yakima Avenue.

Standard weekly order composition (adjustable by 15% based on seasonal availability):
- Heirloom tomatoes (in season, May-October)
- Roma tomatoes (in season, June-October)
- Sweet and mild peppers (in season, July-October)
- Tomatillos (in season, August-October)
- Summer squash and zucchini (in season, June-September)
- Fresh herbs: basil, cilantro, oregano, thyme

### Compensation

**Weekly rate**: $400.00 USD per delivery
**Payment**: Net 15 from delivery date

### Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.
