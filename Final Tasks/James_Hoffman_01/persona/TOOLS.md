# Tools: James Hoffman

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Communication and Scheduling

- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Farm business, market coordination, band bookings, church, and family correspondence on his Google Workspace account.
- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): The farm schedule, market days, gigs, family events, and medical appointments live here.
- **Google Classroom** (`google-classroom-api`): Class announcements and assignment dates for Lily and Ryan so James keeps up with the school calendar without pinging Catherine mid-day.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Quick coordination with the Ramblers, the farm crew, and the buyers at The Harvest Table who prefer it to email.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Secondary inbox for the handful of wholesale buyers and venue managers whose offices run on Microsoft.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Where the WSU Yakima extension office runs its organic-grower workshops and the seasonal soil clinics James joins.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): The regional organic growers group's working channel for swapping pest reports, market intel, and frost warnings.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): The bluegrass musicians community Sully invited him to, useful for trading tunes, gig leads, and instrument tips.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Backup channel for the two out-of-state festival organizers who prefer it for confirming load-in details.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Remote agricultural workshops, CSA member meetings, and the occasional out-of-town band planning call.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Lets venues, wedding clients, and wholesale buyers book a call slot without the back-and-forth.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Sends the batch text reminders to CSA members on pickup days and to the Ramblers the night before a gig.

#### Farm Records, Documents, and Planning

- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Farm records, CSA lists, band set lists, the revenue and gig-income spreadsheets, and the organic certification documents.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Cold-storage backup of the organic certification paperwork and the multi-year revenue spreadsheets in case the Drive account ever fails.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Shared folder of high-resolution farm and band photos that festival promoters and the Yakima Herald pull from.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): The crop-planning board: planting dates, succession schedules, cover crop trials, and the orchard expansion notes.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): His private notebook for soil observations, barbecue sauce recipe experiments, and weekly farm reflections.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): The CSA member database, 35 household shares tracked with pickup status, dietary notes, and payment state.
- **Trello** (`trello-api`): The season task board for greenhouse starts, transplanting windows, and harvest milestones across crops.
- **Asana** (`asana-api`): Tracks the orchard expansion and the greenhouse plastic replacement projects against their target dates.
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): Shared planning board the seasonal crew uses during peak season for daily task assignments and equipment handoffs.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): The CSA sign-up form and the end-of-season member survey that informs next year's share plan.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Signs the farmland lease renewal with the Bridwell family and the restaurant supply agreements.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): The extension-service organic farming guide archive James pulls from when planning new trials and certification renewals.

- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Aggregates balances and transactions across the farm and household accounts so the monthly budget review with Catherine is fast.

- **Square** (`square-api`): The card reader at booth #14 for Saturday market card sales.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Collects deposits for band bookings and wedding gigs.
- **Gusto** (`gusto-api`): Payroll for Jake, Tomas, and Tyler, plus the quarterly state and federal filings.
- **BambooHR** (`bamboohr-api`): Holds the seasonal hires' I-9 and W-4 paperwork so HR records stay clean for any organic certification labor audit.
- **Greenhouse** (`greenhouse-api`): Posts the spring seasonal-worker hiring listing and tracks applicants through to a hire decision.
- **Intercom** (`intercom-api`): Routes restaurant-buyer and CSA member messages into a single inbox so nothing falls through the cracks during peak season.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Holds the small Bitcoin position Tyler talked him into in 2021; James checks the balance once a quarter.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Price reference James glances at when Tyler brings up crypto at family dinners.

- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Tracks his Roth IRA holdings so he can see the balance without logging into the brokerage portal each week.
