THE STUDIO

Beauty treated as structural, not decorative.

Wright Interiors is the practice of Jessica Wright, a principal designer who is NCIDQ certified and has worked in DC residential design for more than twenty years. The studio is flexible about style and unyielding about craft, drawing on Mediterranean tile work, Japanese restraint, and Scandinavian light without feeling scattered.
