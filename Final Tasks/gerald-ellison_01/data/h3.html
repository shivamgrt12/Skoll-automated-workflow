Background

Who I Am

I'm a senior at Ridgewood High School on the west side of Columbus, Ohio - oldest of four, first fiddle and student director of The Ridgewood Ramblers bluegrass ensemble, and the guy who shows up early to tune and stays late to get the harmony right.

I've played fiddle since I was seven years old, picked up mandolin at twelve, and started writing songs not long after. The music comes from Grandpa Walter and the Harlan County visits - it's been in the family longer than any of us can fully account for.

Outside the band room, I'm running AP coursework, picking up my little sister Emma from daycare on Tuesdays and Thursdays, and helping my mom at the bakehouse on Saturdays. I maintain the family Camry, cook from my grandmother's recipes, and read Steinbeck and Cormac McCarthy when I can find the time.

Ridgewood High School

The Ramblers

The Ridgewood Ramblers are a fourteen-member bluegrass ensemble I direct as student leader under Mr. Crawford. I own rehearsal logistics, set lists, and festival travel coordination for the whole crew.

We rehearse Monday, Wednesday, and Friday 3:30-5:30 PM in the band room, plus Saturday mornings during competition season. Last year we finished second at regionals - first time we've placed that high.
