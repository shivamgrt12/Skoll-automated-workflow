Our Story

Rooted in Tradition. Built in Columbus.

The Ridgewood Ramblers are more than a school ensemble - we are a living bridge between the Appalachian hollows and the streets of Columbus. Under the direction of Mr. David Crawford, and led as student director by Gerald Ellison, the Ramblers have grown from a handful of pickers into one of Ohio's most talked-about high school bluegrass programs.

We play bluegrass the way it was meant to be played: tight harmonies, honest strings, and no apologies for where the music comes from. Whether we're at a farmers market, a championship stage, or your grandmother's church hall - we give every room everything we've got.
