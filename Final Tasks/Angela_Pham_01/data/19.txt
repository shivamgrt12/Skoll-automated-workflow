VIETNAMESE COFFEE LOT COMPARISONS
Angela's tasting notes - 2026

Current favorites:
1. Trung Nguyen Creative 8 - smooth, chocolatey, perfect with condensed milk
2. Cafe Du Monde (chicory blend) - nostalgic, Ma uses this one
3. Highlands Coffee Robusta - strong, good for ca phe sua da
4. Nguyen Coffee Supply Brooklyn - ethical sourcing, clean taste

- Lang Biang Arabica (Raj found online)
