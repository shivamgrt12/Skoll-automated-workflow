CAROLINA GOLD BRISKET RUB
Angela's competition recipe (NC BBQ circuit)

Dry Rub:
  3 tbsp coarse black pepper
  2 tbsp kosher salt
  1 tbsp smoked paprika
  1 tbsp garlic powder
  1 tbsp onion powder
  1 tsp cayenne pepper
  1 tsp mustard powder
  1 tsp brown sugar

Method:
  Apply generously night before. Wrap in butcher paper.
  Smoke at 225F with post oak for 12-14 hours.
  Rest 2 hours minimum in cooler wrapped in towels.

Raj says it needs more heat. He is wrong.
Arjun says it is perfect. I trust Arjun on this.
