About

Fort Lauderdale, Florida

Karen Bell is a board-certified Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner and the owner of Bell Behavioral Health, a three-clinician group practice in Fort Lauderdale. For fifteen years she has prescribed across mood disorders, anxiety, PTSD, ADHD, and substance use, with a focus on the communities where formal mental health care has historically been scarce or mistrusted.

She teaches in the PMHNP program at Nova Southeastern University, supervises students in clinical rotation, and speaks nationally on the idea that culture is clinical context, not decoration, and that language is part of the treatment rather than the wrapping around it.
