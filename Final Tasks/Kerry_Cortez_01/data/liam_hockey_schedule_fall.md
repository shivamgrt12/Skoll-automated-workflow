# Liam - Hockey (Fall into Winter)

Liam is twelve, plays defense for the Holy Name Bobcats. Practices and most games run out of Daly Memorial Rink over in Brighton. He is a peanut-allergy kid so his bag always has the EpiPen zipped in the front pocket. He wears number 8, likes to be first laces on, and takes a missed skate personally, so I protect Tuesday and Thursday hard.

## Weekly rhythm
- Tuesday practice, hits the ice at half past five
- Thursday practice, same half past five slot
- Occasional Saturday game, times vary, usually late morning
- Arrive fifteen minutes early to get taped and dressed, he is slow with the gear and hates being rushed onto the ice
- Coach Sullivan runs the bench, assistant is a dad named Rob, both have my cell for the allergy plan

## Logistics
- Ava usually drives him to the Tuesday and Thursday skates on my shift nights
