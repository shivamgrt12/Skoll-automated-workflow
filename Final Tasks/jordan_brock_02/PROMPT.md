I would rather find the seams myself than have David find them for me, and I would far rather find them now than have to explain them in April. Do not pay, renew, or commit us to anything in the course of doing this. If something forces a judgment I have not actually handed you, gather what you can, let the reconciliation stand on the evidence, and bring me the call rather than guess at it. When it is done, give me your plain read on where we truly stand.
