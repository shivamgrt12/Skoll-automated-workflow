# Memory: Jordan Brock

## Personal Profile
Jordan Brock is a corporate trainer and leadership development consultant who owns Brock Leadership Group. He holds a PhD in organizational psychology, conferred in 2009, from Gateway State University in St. Louis, and he built his practice around the conviction that psychological safety is a measurable business driver rather than a soft skill. He is articulate and precise, choosing his words deliberately whether he is coaching a CEO or talking to his kids. He is empowering by instinct, drawing out strengths instead of fixing weaknesses, and diplomatic enough to deliver hard truths while leaving people feeling supported. He is data-driven and allergic to buzzwords without evidence, a voracious reader across psychology, business, and neuroscience, and a strategic thinker who connects team dynamics to business outcomes.

Solo practice; Jordan is the sole principal consultant with no employees, supported by Priya Patel part-time. His office is a finished basement converted to professional space with a video backdrop.
