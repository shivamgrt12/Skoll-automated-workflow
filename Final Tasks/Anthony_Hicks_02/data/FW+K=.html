Truck Stop Field Notes

Thirty years of pulling into the same lots teaches you which ones are worth the exit. These are my own ratings, kept honest. Showers and parking carry the most weight on a long night.

About: I keep this list for my own runs through the Southeast and Midwest. A stop earns a high shower mark for hot water and clean stalls, a high parking mark for spaces left after six in the evening, and a food mark for whether I would eat there twice.
