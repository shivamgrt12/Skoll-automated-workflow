## Base assumptions

- Net take home held at 4,200 per month across 2027. The PGY-3 step lands mid year and the
  step delta is modest, so treating the year flat at PGY-2 net is the conservative read.
- Loan payment held at 312 monthly under the SAVE or PAYE recommendation from the decision
  paper. This is the trusted number after the servicer confirmed the November 15, 2026
  first debit at 347.82 under the current income snapshot. The 312 number reflects the
  expected recalculation once the PGY-2 full year of AGI lands on the AGI form.
- Fixed lines mirror the 2026 reconciled averages line for line. Nothing that ran silently
  in 2026 is assumed to stop in 2027.
- Variable lines are the trimmed 2026 average, not the fatter Oct or Sep line. The 2026
  wedding tail is treated as tapering across the first quarter of 2027.

## Retirement contribution scenarios

Three levels tested against the base fixed and variable stack. The end of month cushion
before any savings transfer runs 1,568, 1,418, or 1,168 for the 150, 250, and 400 monthly
retirement contribution respectively. All three land above zero even after the January 2027
Guadalajara trip amortization line. The 400 monthly line thins the cushion into a range
where a single unplanned event, an urgent care visit, a family flight, a wedding tail cost,
can push the month underwater. The 150 line is comfortable. The 250 line is defensible.

Recommendation. Start the 403 b at 250 per month effective February 2027 payroll. That is
roughly 3,000 for the calendar year at the entry rate, high enough to catch any Stonebridge
Healthcare match if one exists, low enough to keep the cushion honest against the January
2027 Guadalajara trip and the rent renewal window. Confirm the match rule with program HR
before the February 2027 election form goes in. If the match is zero, hold at 250 and
revisit at the PGY-3 pay bump. If the match tiers above 250, raise the contribution to
capture the match and no higher.

## January 2027 stress test

The one month worth naming out loud. Fixed and variable base as modelled. Overlay the trip
amortization at 95 per month for five months starting December 2026, so January carries its
own 95 line as a run rate assumption. Layer the rotation reality. If the January 2027 block
lands on ambulatory as the current provisional shows, cushion holds. If the block flips to
inpatient with q4, the DoorDash line runs 30 to 50 above the base variable and the Uber DEN
line runs 20 above, so treat January under an inpatient flip as cushion of roughly 1,320
under the 250 retirement scenario. Still positive. Still absorbing rent renewal at the
modest bump. That is the honest floor.
