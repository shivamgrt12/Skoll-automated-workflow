# Memory: Dana Serrano

## Personal Profile

Dana Elena Serrano is a first-generation Mexican-American internal medicine resident at Stonebridge Healthcare in Denver, currently a PGY-2. She was born in San Antonio, Texas, to Roberto and Carmen Serrano, who immigrated from Guadalajara, Jalisco. She finished her MD at Oakridge University in 2025 and started her residency on July 1, 2025. She lives in a 2BR walk-up in Capitol Hill with surgery intern Jess Kowalski and walks fifteen minutes to the hospital. She is single, bilingual in English and Spanish, and code-switches by emotional register.

She runs on the kind of intensity her co-residents recognize because they share it: the baseline alertness, the ability to function on inadequate sleep, the reflex to prioritize. What is hers alone is the immigrant-family weight and a quiet, persistent homesickness for San Antonio kitchens and Guadalajara light. Her default off-duty mode is cafe de olla on a weekend morning, Vicente Fernandez on the kitchen speaker, and the rebozo shawl from Mama on her shoulders.
