## Recurring Events

### Daily

- 5:45 AM alarm on run days, otherwise 5:45 AM alarm on inpatient blocks for 6:30 AM rounds start, the first non-negotiable of the day.
- 6 PM walk-home call with Mama, usually fifteen to twenty minutes from the hospital exit to the apartment door.
- Moleskine journal entry before bed, ten minutes minimum, mix of English and Spanish depending on the day.
- Contact lens routine morning and night, glasses on at home only.

### Weekly

- **Monday**: inpatient day 1 of the new call cycle on the current block, schedule check for the week, Mama check-in by text from the lab between rounds.
- **Tuesday**: 6 AM Sloan's Lake run on a run day, then inpatient or clinic depending on block, Sofia check-in text in the evening if not Friday.
- **Wednesday**: midweek wellness pulse check, on-call night possible under q4 rotation, journal before sleep.
- **Thursday**: 6 AM Sloan's Lake run on a run day, inpatient or clinic block, Val FaceTime in the late evening when both have a window.
- **Friday**: end-of-week handoff, 8 PM text-check-in with Sofia, occasional dinner with Jess if both happen to be home.
- **Saturday**: 6 AM Sloan's Lake run on a run day, weekend coverage possible under block, meal-prep cycle every other Saturday.
- **Sunday**: 10 AM Abuelita Rosa video call (matches mid-morning in Guadalajara), Sunday cafe de olla ritual, Familia Serrano group catch-up.

### Monthly

- 1st of the month: bank balance check, spending review, loan-status look on the servicer portal.
- 15th of the month: rotation schedule confirmed for next block, flag conflicts to program admin and family.
- Last week of the month: research progress review and update to Dr. Whitmore on the case report and IRB timeline.
- Once a month: program wellness session with Dr. Marchetti during a non-call lunch window.

### Quarterly

- Quarterly Sofia weekend or video catch-up if neither can travel, plus shared reading-list trade with comfort novels.
- Quarterly review of loan-repayment math against block schedule and any rate or income changes.
- Quarterly check-in with Tia Lupe by phone outside of holiday gatherings.

### Seasonal / Variable

- Inpatient and ICU blocks: 6:30 AM to 6 PM with q4 call until 10 PM, sleep variable, runs sometimes skipped.
- Night float blocks: inverted schedule, social calendar collapses, Mama call shifts to early afternoon.
- Ambulatory and elective blocks: lighter 8 AM to 5 PM days, longer journal entries, more cooking at home.
- Care-package cadence from Mama: every two weeks regardless of season, contents shift by what is in her pantry in San Antonio.

### Annual

- **October**: Dia de los Muertos at Tia Lupe's in Austin if she can swing the days off, ofrenda set up in the apartment otherwise.
- **November 26**: Thanksgiving at her parents' house in San Antonio when the block allows.
- **December 25**: Christmas Day shift this year (she volunteered for it to get Dia de los Muertos off).
- **January**: post-holiday block restart, Val and Marco wedding follow-up, Q1 program evaluation cycle.
- **March 12**: Dana's birthday, usually quiet and worked through, Mama sends a care package and Abuelita calls.
