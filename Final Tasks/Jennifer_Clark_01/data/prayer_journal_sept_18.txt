Sept 18, a Friday evening, the house is quiet

Thank you for Derrick getting through the long week without his shoulder flaring. Thank you for the way Naomi smiled when she got the part in the orchestra she wanted, after working on the audition piece for a month. Thank you for Marcus reading three pages out loud at bedtime, the first time he has done that without me sitting next to him.

Help me with the temptation to overcommit at the museum. There is always one more thing I can volunteer for, and the truth is I do not always have one more thing to give. Help me to know the difference between serving and proving.

Bless Mama, her sugars have been steadier this week. Bless Pastor and Rose, and the work at the church food pantry. Bless Keisha and Andre and the boys. Keep my grandmother's memory honest in my mouth when I tell her story.

And help me to walk in the morning, even when it is cold. Amen.
