Walk route variations, what works on which mornings

Standard loop: down Glenwood, left on Maple, around the small park, back up Cedar to the kitchen door. Forty-five minutes at the slower pace, thirty-eight if I push it.

Rainy day backup: just the half-loop, back through Magnolia, twenty minutes. Take the umbrella that lives by the back door, the other one has a broken rib.

Cooler mornings: add the second small park, sixty minutes total. Wear the navy quarter zip not the gray, the gray sheds and Derrick complains.

Note for the journal: the dogwoods on Maple are starting to turn. Made a mental picture, did not stop. Some mornings the picture is the prayer.
