# Things Pastor Collins has said worth keeping

From the pulpit, Easter 2024:
> Grace does not skip the work, it just stops the punishment.

From a Tuesday lunch, fall 2024:
> You can be tired and faithful at the same time. Most of the people who built this > church were both at once.

Sermon notes, Mother's Day 2025:
> A mother's hands are an entire theology.

From a hospital visit when Daddy was sick:
> The Lord does not require a brave face. He has seen plenty of crying ones.

Choir room before competition prep 2025:
> Sing it like the people who taught you the song are in the room.
