Weekly meal plan, what we send Mama through the week, low sodium emphasis

Monday: chicken thighs, brown rice, greens with vinegar not butter
Tuesday: white fish baked, sweet potato, broccoli
Wednesday: bean soup from the slow cooker, cornbread no salt added
Thursday: turkey meatloaf, mashed cauliflower, green beans
Friday: salmon (if it is on sale), quinoa, asparagus
Saturday: stir fry chicken and vegetables over noodles
Sunday: pot roast in the slow cooker, carrots, potatoes

Snacks: apple slices with peanut butter, plain yogurt with berries, celery sticks, almonds (the unsalted ones), cheese cubes in moderation

Note: cut all desserts in half from what the recipe says. She does fine with that, she does not do fine with the full portion.

Daughter signing off, JC
