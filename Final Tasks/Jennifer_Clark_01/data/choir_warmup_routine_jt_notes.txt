Director Turner warmup routine, for the new altos
1. Breath low, hand on the diaphragm, four counts in four counts out, eight cycles
2. Lip trill on a sustained pitch, walk it up the scale to a comfortable top
3. Five-note descending pattern on doo, half step up each time
4. Octave leap on yah, focus on landing the top note in place
5. Hum and yawn space, sustained, two minutes
6. Group blend exercise, sit on a chord and listen to the alto under the soprano
7. Diction drills, the consonant cluster of the week
8. Stretch the jaw, gently, do not crack anything
Add ten minutes of warmup time before competition rehearsals, do not skip
