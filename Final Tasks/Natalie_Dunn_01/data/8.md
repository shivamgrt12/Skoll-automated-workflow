# Grandmont-Rosedale Neighbourhood Association - Meeting Minutes

**Meeting date:** 8 October 2026, 19:00
**Location:** Grandmont-Rosedale Community House
**Chair:** Loretta Simmons
**Minutes taken by:** Harold Beck

## Attendance

Twelve residents present, quorum met. Apologies received from three members.

## Agenda and discussion

1. **Leaf collection schedule.** The city curbside leaf pickup begins the first week of
   November. Bags at the curb by 7 AM on your collection day. Reminder flyers will go on the
   community board.

2. **Fall block clean-up.** The neighbourhood clean-up is set for a Saturday morning later
   this month. Gloves and bags provided. Volunteers to meet at the community house.

3. **Traffic calming on Grand River.** Ongoing conversation with the city about a crossing
   near the commercial strip. No decision yet. The association will keep pressing.

4. **Small business corner.** Members noted several local shops doing good work in the area,
   including the auto trade along Grand River. The association encourages residents to shop
   local this season.

5. **Holiday lighting.** Planning for the annual holiday lighting on the boulevard will begin
   at the November meeting. Volunteers welcome.

## Next meeting

Second Thursday of November, 19:00, community house. All residents welcome.

*These minutes are informational for residents. Nothing here requires action from any single
household.*
