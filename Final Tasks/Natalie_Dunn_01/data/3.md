# GearUp Detroit - Fundraiser Dinner Run of Show

**Event:** GearUp Detroit Annual Fundraiser Dinner
**Date:** 24 October 2026, 18:00 to 21:00
**Venue:** Grand River Community Center ballroom
**Coordinator:** Keisha Monroe
**Snapshot pulled:** 18 October 2026

GearUp Detroit is the youth mentorship programme Natalie founded in 2016. The October dinner
is our one big annual push for donor support. This sheet is the evening timeline only, so the
floor team knows what happens when.

## Evening timeline

- 17:00 - Volunteer call time, room set and AV check
- 18:00 - Doors open, welcome table and coat check
- 18:30 - Guests seated, opening remarks from the founder
- 18:45 - Dinner service begins
- 19:30 - Programme: student spotlight and mentor recognition
- 20:00 - Donor appeal and closing remarks
- 20:30 - Dessert and coffee service, informal mingling
- 21:00 - Close, volunteer teardown

## Room notes

- Head table near the stage for the founder, coordinator, and keynote mentor.
- AV: single handheld mic plus lectern mic, slideshow from the community center laptop.
- Registration table staffed from doors open through the opening remarks.
- Overflow parking in the east lot; signage handled by the venue.

## Standing reminders

- Keep the evening running to time; the programme block should not slip past 20:15.
- Photography for the newsletter is welcome during the programme, not during dinner service.
- The venue handles teardown of tables; volunteers handle the welcome and dessert stations.
