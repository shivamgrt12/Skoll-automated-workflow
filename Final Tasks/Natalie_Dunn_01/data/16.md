## From the founder's desk

What a season. The garage doors are open, the tools are out, and another group of young
Detroiters is learning what it means to turn a wrench with pride. GearUp exists because a
kid with a curiosity about how an engine breathes deserves a bench, a mentor, and a shot.

## Spring highlights

- Our Saturday workshops filled up fast this term. Engine Basics remains the most popular
  starting track.
- Two of last year's participants landed apprentice spots at local shops. That is the whole
  point.
- The Chevelle restoration corner keeps drawing a crowd. Nothing teaches patience like
  bodywork.

## Coming later this year

- Fall cohort intake opens after the summer intensive.
- The annual fundraiser dinner returns in October. Save the date, details to follow closer
  to the time.
- We are always looking for tool donations and mentor volunteers.

## Thank you

To every donor, mentor, and family who makes this possible, thank you. You are building
something that outlasts a single season.
