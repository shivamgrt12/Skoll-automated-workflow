# Property Appraisal Report

**Prepared for:** Dunn & Sons Auto Service
**Property appraised:** 4820 Grand River Ave, Detroit, MI 48208
**Property type:** Six-bay freestanding commercial automotive building, built 1968
**Appraiser:** Marcus Delacroix, MAI - Delacroix Valuation Group, Detroit

**Inspection date:** 2 October 2026
**Effective date of value:** 5 October 2026

## Purpose and scope

This report provides an independent professional opinion of the market value of the subject
property as of the effective date, based on a physical inspection, comparable commercial
sales in the Grand River corridor, and the income characteristics of the building. It was
commissioned to support the buyer's due diligence.

## Approaches to value

- **Sales comparison approach:** Four comparable commercial and light-industrial sales within
  a one-mile radius, adjusted for lot size, bay count, and condition.
- **Cost approach:** Replacement cost less depreciation for a 58-year-old masonry structure.
- **Income approach:** Considered given the building's rental history under the current owner.

## Opinion of value

Reconciling the three approaches, it is my professional opinion that the market value of the
subject property as of the effective date is:

**Appraised market value: $309,000 USD**

## Appraiser's remarks

The structure is sound with deferred maintenance typical for its age. The six-bay layout and
Grand River frontage support continued automotive commercial use. This figure is an
independent estimate of market value and is not a statement of any negotiated, contracted, or
transacted price between the parties.

*This appraisal is valid for 120 days from the effective date. Delacroix Valuation Group.*
