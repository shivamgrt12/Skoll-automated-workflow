1x per week when possible. Real rock movement differs from plastic. Oct trip: linked two V5 problems and made progress on the slab project.

Current technique focus areas identified with my coach after the Fall 2026 competition cycle:

  • Hip positioning on overhangs - working on keeping hips close to wall during transitions. Flag and drop-knee drills 3 sets per session.
  • Footwork precision - silent feet drills on slabs. Placing feet quietly forces more deliberate placement and weight transfer.
  • Reading sequences - 5-minute visualization before attempting any new problem. Reduces tries-to-send and builds mental map.
  • Dynamic movement - on hold due to shoulder. Will resume in December 2026 pending physiotherapy clearance.

Supplemental conditioning integrated with trail running at Lands End (2-3x/week, 4-6 miles) and targeted shoulder rehab protocol:

Shoulder Rehab (daily, 15 min)

External rotation exercises with resistance bands, prone Y-T-W raises, serratus activation. Prescribed by physiotherapist, not skipping even on rest days.

Core Work (3x/week, 20 min)

Dead bugs, hollow-body holds, front levers (negatives only). Shoulder-friendly modifications in place.

Finger Tendon (post-session, 10 min)

Reverse wrist curls, rice bucket, putty exercises. Injury prevention priority before any strength gains.
