Methodology and Process
Continuous discovery interviews with 5-8 enterprise account users per sprint. Opportunity solution trees used to map customer problems before committing to solutions. Wrote all PRDs in a collaborative doc system shared with engineering leads and design. Sprint reviews held every two weeks with cross-functional stakeholders. Shipped behind feature flags with gradual rollout to 5%, 20%, 100% of pilot cohort. Post-launch retros within one week of each major feature drop.
Defined success metrics and instrumentation plan before each sprint began. Used internal analytics infrastructure to track funnel events, feature adoption rates, and session depth. Retention metric (41.2%) measured as users returning to the dashboard feature within 30 days of first use. NPS scores collected after 30 days of active usage, not immediately post-onboarding. Support ticket reduction calculated by comparing support queue data 60 days pre and post launch, normalized for user growth.
Worked directly with engineering team of 6 (2 senior, 3 mid, 1 junior), product design lead, data infrastructure team, and enterprise customer success team. Daily standups with engineering, weekly design review, bi-weekly stakeholder sync with leadership. Used async written updates to reduce meeting load, particularly important given distributed team across two time zones.

Growth Initiative - 2026

Onboarding Funnel Redesign

First-week activation improvement
Jan-Feb 2026
A/B tested
New user cohort
Identified friction in the onboarding funnel through session recordings and interviews with recently churned trial users. Built a revised onboarding experience focused on time-to-first-value rather than feature exposure. Reduced the required steps from 9 to 4, replaced generic tutorials with role-specific guided paths, and added contextual prompts tied to users' stated goals during sign-up.
Completed A/B test in February 2026 with statistically significant results
New variant reduced time-to-first-value from 3.4 days to 1.1 days
Day-7 activation rate improved by 22% in the treatment cohort
Findings fed directly into enterprise onboarding design for the dashboard product
