# Memory: Stephanie Wells

## Personal Profile
- **Full name**: Stephanie Wells, raised in Fremont, California, in a steady suburban household with professional parents and a younger brother
- **Personality**: She thinks in deliverables, runs at problems before they grow, with dry tech-literate humor and no patience for fluff
- **Competitiveness**: She competes without cruelty, a lesson the climbing wall taught her where preparation matters more than title
- **Philosophy**: Shipping something useful and imperfect on time beats holding it for a perfection that never arrives
- **Family priority**: She believes her father's Parkinson's is the most important problem in her life right now
- **Inner tension**: Her career arc matters, but her family will need more from her over the next decade than the career accommodates
- **Optimism**: A discipline she chose at eleven, when she learned her father's bad day could derail the whole household
- **Identity**: Fundamentally American suburban, four years in San Francisco that finally feel like home, suspicious of performed cultural identity
- **Education**: B.S. in Computer Science from Ridgewater University (2020), MBA from Bayshore School of Business (2022)
