How it unfolded

Project timeline

October 2025
Opened the binder. First census pulls confirmed the Sawyer Avenue household and Walter's trade as an iron molder.
November 2025
Marriage and baptismal records tied the Doyle connection together. Found a probable Welsh manifest for Thomas.

Raymond's obituary filled in the last of his service record and named Eleanor.

Wrote to a South Side historic site asking after foundry employment ledgers. Waiting on the reply before the next round of letters.
