The Voices project

First recordings complete

"You could tell the shift was ending by the sound. The frame line went quiet a machine at a time."

In January, volunteers recorded a 52 minute conversation with a 91 year old who joined the furniture factory floor in 1952 and spent more than two decades on the frame line. His recollections of the line, the 1955 strike, and the sound of a working factory will anchor a listening station in the October exhibit.

Families with connections to the quarries or the county's machine shops are invited to reach the office.
