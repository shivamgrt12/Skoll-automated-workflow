The route

Three stops, south down the country

Rome to settle in, Naples for the sites Lily has read about for years, then Catania for the mountain that does what mine do at home.

Days 1 to 3 · Rome

Land and adjust

Apartment near the centre, slow first days, the old city on foot, gelato as a daily ritual.

Days 4 to 6 · Naples and the bay

Pompeii and Vesuvius

Lily's priority. Two guided days across the buried city and up to the crater rim, with the geology explained properly.

Days 7 to 9 · Catania

Etna, weather permitting

A guided ascent if the mountain allows it, and a quieter day by the coast if it does not.

Day 10 · Home

Return to Reykjavik

Evening flight back, notebooks full, already planning the next one.
