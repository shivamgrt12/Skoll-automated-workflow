How to read this

Surface sulphur dioxide flux is measured by ground based scanning, and gas species ratios by direct sampling during accessible windows. In the present phase, elevated seismicity and continuing magma accumulation are not, so far, accompanied by a significant change in surface flux. That combination is itself the finding, and it is communicated plainly to civil protection before it reaches anyone else.
