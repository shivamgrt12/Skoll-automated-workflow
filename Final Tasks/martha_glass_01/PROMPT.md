--- TURN 1 ---
I need the whole money picture pulled together and trued up before I put anything in front of Jón, because the books have drifted since the last proper pass and I want to walk into the filing knowing every number holds rather than guessing, because once it is in front of Jón it is far harder to walk back than it is to get right beforehand. Start on the income side and account for everything that has actually paid me across the cycle, the keynote and public lecture honoraria, the workshop and short course and seminar fees, the hazard advisory work that the overseas client runs through its own payroll and pays me as a contractor, the registration and consultation receipts that come in through the payment platforms, the field per diems from the European project, the card takings from the outreach table I run for the rescue fund, and the slow trickle from the secondhand textbooks and out of print field guides I sell off toward Lily's education pot. Several of those landed in króna and several in dollars or euros, some were later refunded in part or in full and should not be counted as if they stuck, and a handful are sitting as drafts or half paid rather than settled, so I want the gross and the real net kept clearly apart and everything brought into one currency I can read at a glance. Then do the same on the outflow side across the household accounts and the consulting ledger, the mortgage, the recurring bills and the subscriptions, the fuel and field and gear spending, the giving to the causes I support, and the vendor and instrument service costs that belong to the consulting work and not to the house, because I keep muddling the two and the accountant needs them cleanly separated. I have lost confidence that the figures I have been carrying in my head still match what the accounts actually say, some of them are stale from earlier passes and were never reconciled against anything, and one or two of the ledgers look like they have picked up doubled or contradictory entries, so treat nothing as settled until you have checked a meaningful number against more than one place it ought to appear, and tell me plainly which version you trusted and which you set aside and why. Where the evidence is thin, or a source will not give a clean answer, say so and leave the figure open rather than forcing it into something neat, I would rather carry an honest gap than a false total. On the holdings side I want a current read on what we are actually worth, the current account and the savings, the card balance sitting against us, the modest index position I keep abroad, and the three small crypto stakes we picked up a while back and have not touched since, all valued at what they are worth now and all pulled into the same currency so it reads as one number and not five. The savings figure in particular I want nailed down against the account of record rather than the number I think is there, because the emergency fund target is the thing I lie awake on and the one figure I refuse to have wrong, so tell me exactly how far short of it we still stand and, at the rate I set aside each month, roughly when it lands if nothing disturbs it. Give me the same honest read on how the education pot is tracking against the small amount I feed it, and where the mortgage sits against the payoff I have been aiming at. What I most want out of all of this is a clear cash flow and net worth picture I can sit down with, the money in against the money out over the cycle, what is still owed to me and what I still owe, and where every account stands once the currencies are reconciled, written so I can act on it and not a wall of rows I have to decode. Alongside that I need the consulting side pulled into a clean and defensible package Jón could take straight into the filing, with the household and personal spending kept firmly out of it and every figure traceable to something real. Prepare both and hold them for me to read through before anything moves, since I am the one who takes them the last step. Fold the trip savings into the picture too, the Italy plan and the New Zealand travel are sitting inside the goal and I want them counted, the number is all I am after there. If something genuinely does not add up, bring it to me plainly rather than smoothing it over, I would far rather have the uncomfortable version now than a tidy one that comes apart in front of the accountant.
