#### Files & Documents
- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Personal cloud storage. Inventory docs, provenance files, the Fuji photo archive, blog drafts, authentication reports, client proposals.

- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Pages, databases, notes. Modernism Week tour scripts, blog outlines, and estate-sale scout-network contact notes.

- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Team wiki. The Bell Modern operations playbook Jenna Phillips reads for floor procedures and showroom opening when Ashley sources.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): Spreadsheet-database hybrid. The 120-plus accent-piece catalog for the online shop, tracking each item's photo status, dimensions, and price.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): E-signatures and signed-doc storage. Active, light. Consignment agreements with the three local designers and occasional auction-house consignments.

#### Navigation, Weather & Local
- **Google Maps** (`google-maps-api`): Directions, traffic, places. Estate-sale drives across the 200-mile radius, the Desert Hot Springs storage run, Joshua Tree/Indian Canyons trailheads, and medical addresses.
- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Current conditions and forecast. Pre-dawn hike weather, the Saturday farmers-market call, the Modernism Week February check.
- **Yelp** (`yelp-api`): Local reviews and ratings. Dining radar around Palm Springs and the Coachella Valley; scouting new restaurants and the rare vendor.

#### Banking, Payments & Trading
- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Bank aggregation. Bell Modern operating accounts and the Schwab money-market fund (~$78,000); weekly sync reconciles balances against the ledger.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Consumer payments and transfers. Settling with out-of-state dealers, paying the occasional Etsy barware seller, and how Scott sends his share on a split lot.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Card processing and settlement. Deposits and balance payments on higher-value pieces like the Nakashima Conoid bench; settlement reports reconcile the ledger.
- **Square** (`square-api`): Small-merchant POS. Every in-showroom card sale runs through the Square reader on North Palm Canyon; reports reconcile the floor against the books.
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Small-business bookkeeping and tax. Active. Sarah Adams pulls Bell Modern's books for the quarterly P&L and the annual joint filing.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Cloud accounting and invoicing. Consignment invoices to the three designers at the 30% commission split, tracking who has paid, kept separate from the main books.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Retail crypto exchange. For the occasional crypto payment from a younger collector, converted to cash promptly so it never sits as a position.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): International crypto exchange. The rail when an overseas buyer pays in crypto for a shipped piece, used to quote and confirm conversion before releasing freight.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Crypto exchange and staking. The second exchange for checking the spread before settling a crypto-paying collector, whichever gives the better rate.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Commission-free brokerage. A small self-directed account parking a slice of the travel fund, checked when Tyler raises refinance-versus-invest in portfolio talks.
