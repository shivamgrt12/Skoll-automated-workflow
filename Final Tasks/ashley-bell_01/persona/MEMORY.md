# Memory: Ashley Bell

## Personal Profile
- **Full Name**: Ashley Bell
- **Occupation**: Owner of Bell Modern, an independent mid-century modern furniture dealership in Palm Springs, CA. Built from a single Craigslist flip twenty years ago into a 68-piece showroom on North Palm Canyon Drive.
- **Education**: BA Art History, Pacific Crest University (1987).
- **Languages**: English (native).
- **Relationship status**: In a long-term partnership with Karen Mitchell. Filed as domestic partners for tax purposes.

**Character and temperament**: Ashley runs sharp. She reads rooms quickly, authenticates pieces from across an estate sale, and negotiates on a register that is calm under pressure and never reveals her top price first. Witty and direct, does not suffer fools, delivers criticism with charm. Generous with expertise, writes for design blogs, gives Modernism Week talks, treats provenance as non-negotiable craft.

**Cultural identity**: Palm Springs first by twenty years. Mid-century modern is not a category to her, it is a lineage she lives inside, from the 1957 Alexander Construction butterfly-roof house in Vista Las Palmas to the Noguchi coffee table and Bertoia chairs in the living room.

**Social style**: Measures relationships in proximity, repetition, and shared craft. Inner circle small and built deliberately: Karen, Tyler and Leo in San Francisco, Scott in Portland, Megan in LA, Brian next door, and the small constellation of dealers and restorers who have earned the trust.

**Philosophy and values**: Independent and self-made. Twenty years building Bell Modern from nothing is the foundation. Meticulous about provenance because reputation is everything in the dealer world. Generous with knowledge, private about personal life.
