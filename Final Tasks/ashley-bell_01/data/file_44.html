Design Drinks at Bell Modern

The last Thursday, on the floor

Once a month the showroom stays open after hours. One featured piece, one well made cocktail, and a room full of people who care about desert modern design. Five to eight, the last Thursday of the month.

What the evening is

Not a sales push. A standing invitation to spend an hour among pieces with real provenance, hear the story of one of them, and talk shop with collectors, designers, and the merely curious.

How it runs

Doors at five. A short talk on the featured piece around six. Last guest out by eight fifteen. Vintage barware from the collection is on the bar, and some of it is for sale. Attendance usually lands in the high thirties, so a quick RSVP helps us pour enough.

The house pour

  • Equal parts bitter red aperitivo, dry gin, and sweet vermouth.
  • Stirred over one large cube until cold.
  • Orange peel expressed over the top, then dropped in.
  • Also on the bar: a mezcal paloma and a classic martini.
