Why the split works this way

Consignment keeps your piece yours until it sells, and the dealer share covers photography, provenance research, floor space, and the marketing that finds the right collector. A marketplace listing reaches collectors well beyond the valley, with the platform fee carried on the dealer side so your share is clean. An outright sale is the fastest route when you would rather not wait for the floor to turn.

What happens next

A clear, short path from your note to a placed piece.
  1. We read your note and reply within two business days with first questions.
  2. If it is a fit, we examine the piece and authenticate the construction and papers.
  3. We agree a price and a path, then photograph the piece in the studio.
  4. The piece goes on the floor or the marketplace, and you are paid when it sells.
