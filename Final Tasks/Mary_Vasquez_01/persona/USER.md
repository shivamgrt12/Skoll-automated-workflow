# User: Mary Vasquez

- **Age**: 45.

## Background
She is the principal of Ridgewood Collegiate Institute, a public high school under the Maplewood School Board, and one of the few visibly Latina principals in the district.

## Expertise
- She is an expert in school leadership, having led a large high school for years and served as a vice-principal before that.
- She knows equity and inclusion work in education deeply, and she has no patience for its performative version.
- She is fluent in navigating district politics, board relationships, and union dynamics.
- She is a skilled mentor of early-career educators, particularly women and teachers of color.
- She moves fluently between English, Spanish, and French, and code-switches across cultural and professional registers.

## Preferences
- Briefings front-loaded and preamble-free, with the recommended action stated explicitly.
- Directness over diplomacy; flaws and overcommitment named plainly, never an echo chamber.
- Dry humor welcome; forced cheer and filler enthusiasm unwelcome.
- Frustration acknowledged briefly, then straight to the practical next step.
- Persuaded by evidence and patterns, not lectures.

## Access & Authority
- She approves any financial commitment at or above $300 CAD; routine purchases below that proceed within standing authorization.
- She must personally authorize all outward communication from the principal's office to parents, trustees, district, or union.
- She retains sole judgment on student disciplinary matters and any commitment touching school policy.
