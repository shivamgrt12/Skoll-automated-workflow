# Heartbeat: Mary Vasquez

## Recurring Events

### Daily
- **Every night, 10:30 PM**: Read for 30 minutes before sleep. Non-negotiable decompression; protect it from late work.

### Weekly
- **Monday to Friday**: Pre-dawn power walk at 5:40 AM (leave 5:45 for a 30 to 40 minute neighborhood loop with a leadership or equity podcast); school day at Ridgewood Collegiate begins 7:30 AM and typically runs to 5:00 PM or later.
- **Tuesday**: Staff meeting 3:30 to 4:30 PM. Surface the agenda and any follow-ups beforehand.
- **Thursday**: Miguel's soccer practice 6:00 PM. Carlos handles transport; keep the evening clear of conflicting commitments.
- **Friday**: Try to leave school by 4:30 PM for the Friday family dinner (Rosa's big Mexican meal), followed by a movie or game night with the kids.
- **Saturday**: Zumba prep at 8:30 AM for the 9:00 AM class at GoldFit Community Centre. Do not skip it.
- **Sunday**: Church prep at 9:30 AM for the 10:00 AM Mass at Our Lady of Guadalupe (leave by 9:40); weekly phone call with Lucia at 7:00 PM; evening email catch-up and week planning, capped at 45 minutes.

### Monthly
- **First Wednesday, 6:00 PM**: Parent council prep reminder for the 6:30 PM meeting at Ridgewood Collegiate. Review the agenda and prep notes.
- **Third Thursday, 4:30 PM**: Maplewood School Board meeting until 7:00 PM at the board office. Prepare the briefing the night before.
- **15th of each month**: Verify the RESP auto-contribution for Sofia and Miguel.
- **Last Sunday of each month**: Visit Eduardo in Scarborough. Bring groceries.
- **Once a month**: Book club and lunch with Grace. Half book, half catching up.
- **Twice a month, Saturday evening**: Date night with Carlos.

### Quarterly
- **Each quarter**: Audit subscriptions for value and upcoming renewals.
- **Each quarter**: Family-finance walk-through with Carlos: budget reconciliation, RESP and RRSP review, and Daniel's quarterly note on the small family crypto position.

### Seasonal / Variable
