# Equity, Diversity and Inclusion Plan - Year One Closeout Report
## Ridgewood Collegiate Institute - July 2026

**Author**: VP Okafor, EDI Lead
**Reviewed by**: Principal Vasquez
**Status**: CLOSED — Year One is complete. Year Two is the current year.

---

## Executive Summary

Year One of the Ridgewood EDI plan (September 2025 - June 2026) focused on
building the foundation: auditing current practices, establishing baseline
measures, and engaging the community. This report summarizes outcomes against
the four Year One pillars.

**This is an archived closeout. All numbers below are from the prior year.
Year Two data lives in Airtable under the EQ-Y2 prefix and in the current
board_brief_budget.csv. Do not carry Year One figures into this year's brief.**

---

## Pillar 1: Cultural Audit

- Departmental self-assessments completed: 9/9 departments (100%)
- Curriculum review panels conducted: 4 (English, Social Sciences, Arts, Guidance)
- Key finding: 72% of assigned novels and non-fiction texts were by white
  authors. The department head consortium committed to reaching 40% BIPOC
  authorship by the end of Year Two.
- Prior-year cost: $4,200 CAD (facilitator fees and release time)

## Pillar 2: Student Voice

- Student Equity Council established with 14 founding members
- Student voice survey deployed to grades 9-12: 68% response rate (817/1,201)
  *(note: this denominator is last year's enrollment, not this year's)*
- Key finding: 34% of BIPOC students reported feeling "somewhat" or "not at all"
  represented in classroom materials. This was the catalyst for the curriculum
  diversification initiative in Year Two.
- Prior-year cost: $1,800 CAD (survey platform and council supplies)

## Pillar 3: Staff Development

- Staff equity self-assessment administered: 91% completion rate
- Anti-racism reading circles launched with 22 participants (of ~95 staff)
- Two full-day professional development sessions delivered:
  - September 2025: "Understanding Systemic Racism in Ontario Schools"
  - February 2026: "Culturally Responsive Pedagogy: Foundations"
- Prior-year cost: $8,400 CAD (external facilitators, release time, materials)

## Pillar 4: Community Engagement

- Three community partner organizations engaged and signed:
  - Maplewood Community Arts Collective (signed October 2025)
  - Toronto Youth Services Alliance (signed November 2025)
  - Lakeshore Cultural Centre (signed January 2026)
- One target organization (Northgate Family Services) did not close before
  year-end and was carried into Year Two's pipeline.
- Prior-year cost: $2,100 CAD (partnership development and events)
