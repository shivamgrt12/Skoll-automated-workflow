## Notes for Mary's Session Prep

The keynote slot is forty-five minutes as currently scheduled. The pre-panel
that Dr. Rahman moderates warms the room for the equity content and hands off
to Mary at 14:15. Mary's presentation is expected to run about forty minutes
with five minutes for any brief closing before the refreshment break.

Cordell Hall seats four hundred and eighty and the audio visual team confirmed
lectern microphone with wireless backup and dual projection with the presenter
laptop on the podium. Water and coffee service is available in the Grand Foyer
during breaks. Signage will direct attendees from the main entrance to Cordell
Hall through the east wing.

The pre-panel introduction for Dr. Rahman has been drafted and will be handled
by the conference chair. Mary does not need to prepare speaker introduction
material for the panel; her opening should begin from the equity framing that
the panel establishes.

## AV Walkthrough

Mary is booked for the audio visual walkthrough at 12:30 the day of the event,
in Cordell Hall. Please bring the presenter laptop, the backup memory stick
with the slide deck, and any wireless microphone preferences. The AV team asks
that presenters arrive fifteen minutes early to the walkthrough to allow time
for setup.

## Recording

The keynote will be recorded by the conference video team and uploaded to the
conference video host for later distribution. Mary retains approval rights
before any wider publication.
