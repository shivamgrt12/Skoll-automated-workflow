# Summer 2027 Mexico Trip - Research Notes

Personal family travel planning. Not related to school, board, or work in any way.

---

## Overview

Mary and Carlos want to take the whole family (Sofia, Miguel, Rosa) to
Guadalajara for 10 days in July 2027. This would be the first time the kids
visit Abuela Consuelo's neighbourhood since 2019. Rosa hasn't been back since
Eduardo's health issues started.

**Budget**: $8,000 CAD total (flights + accommodation + food + activities)

**Preference**: Aeromexico for the direct MEX connection. Book by March for best price.

## Accommodation

- **Option A**: Airbnb in Colonia Americana (near Abuela's old neighbourhood)
  - 3-bedroom apartment, $120 CAD/night = $1,200 for 10 nights
  - Walking distance to Chapultepec Avenue and the market
  - Mary likes this for the neighbourhood feel

- **Option B**: Hotel Real de Guadalajara (Centro Histórico)
  - 2 rooms, $180 CAD/night = $1,800 for 10 nights
  - Pool, breakfast included, close to the cathedral
  - Carlos prefers this for convenience and Rosa's mobility

- **Option C**: Tía Lupe's house (Carlos's aunt in Zapopan)
  - Free, but small. Would need at least one extra room.
  - Rosa would be most comfortable here. Family setting.
  - Tía Lupe has already offered. Would feel rude to say no entirely.

**Decision**: Probably Option A with a few nights at Tía Lupe's. Talk to Carlos
after Christmas.
