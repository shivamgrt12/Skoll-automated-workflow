# Memory: Brandon Wright

## Personal Profile

- **Full name**: Brandon Wright; goes by Brandon to friends and family, Wright to clinical staff and cohort instructors
- **DOB**: December 14, 2003
- **High school**: Graduated from Evergreen Valley High School in Edina, MN in 2023
- **Athletic identity**: Competitive cross-country ski racer with the Twin Cities Nordic Ski Club, racing since age fourteen
- **Character**: Runs on engineered efficiency with a 4:45 AM alarm, color-coded calendar, and Sunday meal preps through Wednesday
- **Warmth**: Shows care through action, drives friends to clinicals in snowstorms, leaves cinnamon rolls without taking credit
- **Competitive drive**: Former state-podium junior racer who chose nursing over Olympic ambitions; the compromise has held since freshman year
- **Humor**: Dry one-liners delivered with flat composure; walks away before the laugh lands and Hannah catches them first
- **Cultural identity**: Scandinavian-American through Norwegian and Swedish heritage on both sides, three generations of small-town Minnesota roots
- **Heritage traditions**: Strawberry jam from Grandma Ingrid, rice pudding and lefse at Christmas, princess cake he learned at thirteen
- **Thanksgiving**: Annual family gathering at parents' house in Edina with Grandma Ingrid joining, held every fourth Thursday of November
- **Christmas**: Christmas Eve gift exchange and Christmas Day celebration at parents' house in Edina, annual family tradition with holiday baking and Scandinavian heritage food
- **Minnesota roots**: Grew up on Minneapolis trails he now races on, comes home from every trip without missing anything
- **Philosophy**: The way you do small things is the way you do everything, effort is ethic, not performance
- **Mental health**: High-functioning anxiety managed through structure and movement; perfectionism drives both the work and the training equally
- **Coping**: Skis through the harder weeks without naming what he is skiing through; the system has slack he half-believes
