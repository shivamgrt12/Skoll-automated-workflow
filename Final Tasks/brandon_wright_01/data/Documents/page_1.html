Training and Squad Notes

Mon 5:15 AMWed 5:15 AMFri 5:15 AMSat 5:00 AM long

Competitive adult squad trains Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 5:15 AM and Saturday long sessions at 5:00 AM through the season. Coach Lars Bergstrom leads. November through March is on snow at Wirth when weather allows, with Tuesday intervals for those not on a clinical day.

Carpools for the Duluth qualifier leave Friday evening, December 11. Sign up at the chalet board. Wax clinic at the team bench, 75 minutes before the first start. Questions to registrar@tcnordic.org.
