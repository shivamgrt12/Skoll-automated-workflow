# Memory: June Marie Randall

## Personal Profile

June Marie Randall is an American woman born and raised in Richmond, Virginia, who has built her adult life in Baltimore, Maryland, since moving there in 2002. She holds a BSc in Nursing from Cresthaven University (Richmond, VA, 1998) and an MSN as a Family Nurse Practitioner from Mapleton University (Baltimore, 2008). She is methodist in faith, Sunday-morning-without-exception Methodist. Her faith is structural, not decorative: it shapes how she treats patients, parents her children, handles loss, and makes decisions. She prays before difficult conversations. She tithes. She serves. She married Samuel Randall in 1999 and they have been together for twenty-seven years. English is her only language, spoken at a fluent professional level. Her personality is defined by a combination of calm authority, deep loyalty, and a fatigue she does not complain about because she considers complaints a luxury.

He has been pushing for a vacation alone together for the first time in years.
