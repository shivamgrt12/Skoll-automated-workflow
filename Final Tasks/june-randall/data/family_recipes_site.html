The Recipes

The dishes that fill the table at least four nights a week when everyone is home.

The Sunday Table

Sunday is the anchor. After worship the oven goes on, and by mid afternoon the pot roast is pulling apart with a fork and the cornbread is cooling on the counter. Neighbors drift to the porch. Somebody always fights for the corner piece of the mac and cheese.

These are not fancy dishes. They are the ones the children will carry into their own kitchens, the ones that taste like home no matter how far Chicago or Ridgewater feels.
