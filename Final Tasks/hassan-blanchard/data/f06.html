Budget Kitchen Principles

🛒

Price-compare weekly

Brown rice, chicken thighs, and collards are almost always cheaper at the discount grocer. Turkey bacon flips weekly - check both stores.

🥘

Freeze half, always

Oxtail and chicken go on sale sporadically. Buy double, freeze half. The freezer is the real budget move.

🕐

Cook late, cook quiet

The communal kitchen after 11 PM is empty and calm. Better focus, no crowds, and the smell of garlic simmering at midnight is worth it.

🍲

One pot, many meals

A Sunday stew becomes Monday lunch, Tuesday dinner, Thursday leftover bowl. Cook once, eat four times.

About This Blog
