Built Between Study Sessions

All of these beats were made on a laptop after 11 PM in a dorm room in Atlanta. The process is slow because school comes first. The catalog grows one track at a time, during the quiet hours when the communal kitchen is empty and the study playlist has been running for three hours.

These beats are not good enough to release yet. That is not modesty, that is just where things are. When they are ready, this page will be the first place they drop. Sign up below to get notified when that day comes.
