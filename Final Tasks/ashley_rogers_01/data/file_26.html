Service that shows up on a Tuesday

A working list of where the deacon board is each week, who needs a visit, and how a willing hand can join the rotation. Kept by Deacon Ashley Rogers.

A word before you scroll

We serve a faithful, aging congregation in northwest Detroit. Some can no longer drive to worship, some are shut in for the winter, and some just need to hear a knock and a kind voice. This page keeps the week in one place so nobody is forgotten between Sundays.

Who we are praying over

The shut in list

Names read over the congregation each Sunday morning. Visits go out Monday through Saturday, communion taken to those who ask.

Rides to worship

Several elders cannot get to service on their own. A standing Sunday pickup keeps them in the pew where they belong.

The delivery run

After the Monday food bank shift a short route carries boxes to seniors and families who cannot come in. About an hour and a half.
