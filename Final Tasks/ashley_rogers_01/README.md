# ashley-rogers-anniversary — Ashley Rogers

Personal. Ashley Rogers is a 70-year-old retired DDOT bus driver and longtime deacon at Greater Hope Baptist Church in the Brightmoor neighborhood of Detroit, and this task hands the assistant the whole tangled state of the church's seventy-fifth-anniversary celebration on the twelfth of December to walk end to end. The scope covers the commemorative-book sales across three storefronts, the bounced congregational mailing list, the anniversary letter draft, the landing-page and weekly-announcement mismatches with the printed program, the anniversary-fund versus church-books reconciliation, the guest-preacher video archive with broken titles, the day-of setup assignments the committee cannot agree on, and every pending signage proof, survey answer, RSVP roster, and letter of agreement waiting on Ashley. Pastoral message content stays with Pastor Gaines, nothing is sent or committed without Ashley's explicit approval, and every service that does not belong to the anniversary stays untouched.
