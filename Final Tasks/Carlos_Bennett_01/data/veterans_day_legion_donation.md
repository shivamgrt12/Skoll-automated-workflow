# American Legion Post 99 - Veterans Day Fish Fry

Annual donation since 2017. Brooksville. November 11.

## What Carlos donates
- Fish: 60 lb grouper and snapper, fresh off the Lucky Strike, cut and battered
- Labor: Carlos and Russ on the fryers all afternoon
- Peanut oil and the two propane burners

## Legion covers
- Sides: hush puppies, slaw, cheese grits
- Setup, tables, and cleanup
- Sweet tea and coffee

## Run of show
| Time | Task |
|------|------|
| 12:00 | Fish cut and iced, batter mixed |
| 14:00 | Fryers lit, oil to 350 |
| 17:00 | Serving line opens, no charge to any veteran |
| 19:30 | Break down, oil filtered and saved |

Nolan brings the extra propane. Diane runs the dessert table. No charge, never has been.
