# Harbor Ramblers - Fall Music Festival Set

Bayport waterfront, late-afternoon two-song slot. Carlos sits in with the Ramblers.

## Set list
| # | Song | Artist | Key | Lead | Notes |
|---|------|--------|-----|------|-------|
| 1 | Crossroad Blues | Robert Johnson | A | Carlos, resonator | slide, keep it tight |
| 2 | Statesboro Blues | Allman Brothers | open E | Carlos, slide | trade with Nolan's harp |

## Backups if a string breaks
- Sitting on Top of the World (G)
- Deep Elem Blues (E)
- Key to the Highway (A)

## Gear
- 1930s-style resonator, one mic on the cone
- Small tweed amp, spare set of .013 phosphor bronze
- Capo, slide, tuner clipped to the headstock

## Timing
Keep the whole slot under six minutes. Tune quiet between songs, no long story. Diane records one clip for the page.
