## Tea Blends (for sampling)

### Chamomile-Mint Calm Blend
- 2 parts dried chamomile flowers
- 1 part dried yerba buena
- 1/2 part dried lavender
- Steep 5 min covered, serve warm or room temp
- Safe for all ages. Good for after-meal settling.

### Rosemary-Cinnamon Vigor Tea
- 2 parts dried rosemary
- 1 part cinnamon bark (crushed, not powder)
- 1/2 part dried orange peel
- Steep 7 min, strain well
- Not for pregnant women or anyone on blood thinners. Post a sign.

### Manzanilla Digestive
- 3 parts manzanilla
- 1 part fennel seed
- Steep 4 min
- Grandmother always served this after heavy holiday meals. Works.
