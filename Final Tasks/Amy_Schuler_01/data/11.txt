October 4 - Saturday morning session
Recipe: Cranberry Walnut Scones
Source: Adapted from grandmother Hannelore's basic scone dough, adding dried cranberries and toasted walnuts
Results: Good rise, golden color, proper crumb structure. However, slightly dry on the inside - the cranberries absorbed more moisture than expected.
Adjustments for next time: Add 2 tablespoons more heavy cream to the dough. Consider soaking the cranberries in orange juice for 20 minutes before folding in. Reduce oven time by 2 minutes (pulled at 22 minutes, should be 20).
Yield: 8 scones, brought 4 to Miriam at Saturday coffee
Miriam's review: "Better than anything at Wegmans, but she always says that"
