Walking Routes - Federal Hill and Surrounding Areas
Amy Schuler - Evening Walks

Route 1: Federal Hill Loop (standard evening)
Distance: approximately 2.2 miles
Time: 35-40 minutes at Amy's pace
Path: Condo building south on Light Street, right on Cross Street, through Cross Street Market area, south to Federal Hill Park, loop the hilltop (harbor views, benches if needed), descend via Battery Avenue, north on Light Street back home
Notes: Well-lit entire route. The park hilltop section has uneven pavement near the cannon - careful at dusk. Best sunset views from the southwest corner of the park.

Route 2: Inner Harbor Circuit (longer evening, good weather)
Distance: approximately 3.0 miles
Time: 45-55 minutes
Path: North on Light Street to the Inner Harbor promenade, east past the aquarium and Pier 5, around to Fells Point if feeling ambitious (adds 1.5 miles), or loop back via Pratt Street
Notes: More crowded on weekends and summer evenings. The section past the science center can be windy. Good people-watching. Street performers near the amphitheater.

Route 3: Canton Waterfront (weekend extension)
Distance: approximately 3.8 miles
Time: 55-70 minutes
Path: Uber or drive to Canton Waterfront Park, walk the full waterfront path east, loop through Patterson Park (the pagoda), return via Boston Street
Notes: Requires transportation to start. Beautiful at sunset. The park section adds hills. Hanna sometimes joins for this one.

General walking notes:
- Evening walks typically 6:00 PM to 6:45 PM, weather and migraine permitting
- Skip if barometric pressure has dropped more than 8 hPa that day
- Always carry sumatriptan in jacket pocket (just in case)
- iPhone for step tracking, no music (prefers to hear the city)
- Skip if temperature below 38F or above 92F
