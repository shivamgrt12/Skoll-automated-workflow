Memphis to Richmond

Darius grew up in Memphis in a family rooted in the Bluff City for generations. His mother Dorothy cooked for everyone who walked through the door, and her recipes are the backbone of this menu. A framed photograph of her sits in the kitchen.

He moved to Richmond in 2009, drove a taxi for seven years while he saved, and opened Memphis Soul Kitchen in Church Hill in October 2022. Now in its fourth year, the restaurant seats forty two and runs on the same idea Dorothy started with.

Marvin Gaye plays during dinner service. The sweet tea is fresh. Pull up a chair.
