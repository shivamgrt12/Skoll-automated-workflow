# Heartbeat: Darius Barker

## Recurring Events

### Daily
- **5:30 AM**: Morning devotional. Surface the first kitchen and family flags by 5:45 so Darius walks into the kitchen knowing the day.

### Weekly
- **Monday**: Day off. Sleeps in until 7:00. Southside Wholesale Market run at 8:00 AM for restaurant supplies. Bookkeeping, vendor calls, and menu planning through the afternoon. Family dinner at home in the evening, no exceptions; do not schedule over it.
- **Tuesday to Sunday**: Up by 5:30 AM. Family breakfast at 6:30. Walks or drives to the restaurant by 8:30 for prep, except in-season Saturdays (October through March) when basketball coaching pushes restaurant arrival to noon. Kitchen open 11:00 AM to 9:00 PM (10:00 PM on Friday and Saturday). Home between 9:30 and 10:30 PM.
- **Wednesday, 7:00 PM**: Bible study at Riverside Baptist Church. Surface the leave-by reminder at 6:30. Devon covers the kitchen close on Bible study nights.
- **Thursday, 7:30 to 9:30 PM**: Riverside Groove Collective rehearsal at the Northside Community Center. Devon covers the kitchen close. Surface the leave-by reminder at 7:15.
- **Saturday, 9:00 to 11:00 AM (October through March)**: Coaches Jaylen's U-15 Richmond River City Rockets at Byrd Park Recreation Center. Surface the leave-by reminder at 8:30 and the move to the restaurant by noon.
- **Sunday, 10:00 AM**: Worship service at Riverside Baptist Church. Surface the leave-by reminder at 9:30. Tanya and her kids often visit in the afternoon.
- **Sunday, 9:00 PM**: Weekly restaurant inventory count after close.

### Monthly
- **1st of each month**: Support transfer of $400 to Earl in Memphis.
- **15th of each month**: Small business loan payment to River City Community Credit Union.
- **Last Monday of each month**: Restaurant P&L review with Megan and Devon over coffee at the restaurant.

### Annual
- **October 16**: Wedding anniversary with Monique (married October 16, 2010 at Riverside Baptist Church).
- **October 22**: Jaylen's birthday. Family dinner at home and a small bass-string gift wrapped on the table.
- **Mid-October (third Saturday)**: Memphis Soul Kitchen anniversary tasting menu service honoring Dorothy's originals.
- **Late October**: Annual physical with Dr. Patricia Rhodes at River View Family Clinic.
- **November 3**: Dorothy Barker remembered on her birthday (deceased 2022). Quiet family ritual at home; her framed photo on the kitchen wall gets a fresh wipe.
- **November 17**: Zaria's birthday. Bookstore trip on the Monday off plus a family dinner.
- **Fourth Thursday of November**: Thanksgiving. Restaurant closed; Monique's smoked turkey with bourbon glaze; Tanya's family, Marcus, and several Church Hill neighbors at the table.
- **December 11**: Earl's birthday in Memphis. Phone call from Darius first thing in the morning; UPS care package mailed the week prior.
- **December 14**: Monique's birthday. Date night reservation if Devon can cover the kitchen close.
- **December 25**: Christmas. Restaurant closed; morning service then home for gifts, dinner, and Dorothy's candied yams on the table.
- **December 31**: New Year's Eve ticketed prix-fixe dinner service at Memphis Soul Kitchen. Biggest revenue night of the year.
- **January 8**: Darius's birthday. Quiet family dinner at home; Devon covers the kitchen close.
- **February 4**: Caleb's birthday. Family-only morning at home before the kitchen opens.
- **Ash Wednesday (variable, February or early March)**: Lenten menu launch at Memphis Soul Kitchen; Friday fish specials begin and family observes meatless Fridays through Easter.
- **February 19**: Marcus Williams's birthday. Call or text from Darius, plus a Grizzlies-related gag gift.
