I need you to help me get my arms all the way around the catering side of the house before I commit to anything else, because it has quietly grown into real money and I have lost the thread on what is actually solid and what is just noise. Take what actually came in, set the food and the vendor cost of these jobs against it, and tell me what the catering book truly netted, not the gross that makes a man feel good for an afternoon. Then, only off what is genuinely collected and confirmed, tell me what I can honestly move toward buying this building out from under the lease, because I would rather hold a small true number than a big soft one and plan my life around a lie. The last piece is the people. Pull the clients who have hired us more than once, the ones worth keeping warm, and draft me something to send back that sounds like me and not like a form letter, and hold it for my eyes before it goes anywhere. Give me the money reconciliation as something I can lay right next to the account and defend line by line, the ranked pipeline as a brief I can actually work from, and the drafts sitting ready for my signature, and anywhere the evidence is thin you say so plain and hold the call instead of forcing a number, because a wrong number in this is worse to me than an open question.
