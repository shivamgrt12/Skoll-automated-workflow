About

Two years in, and still learning the craft on purpose

I am a social media coordinator at a community empowerment nonprofit in Atlanta, where I run content for five channels and write in a voice that is warm and direct without performing optimism I do not feel. I came up through a communications degree and a lot of trial by deadline.

I care about the difference between a post that performs and a message that lands. I read engagement the way some people read weather, and I am building toward leading communications strategy for an organization whose mission I believe in.

At a glance

Based in East Atlanta. Routes the city by transit. Thrifts on Fridays, cooks on Sundays, journals most nights, and is saving for two weeks in Portugal.
