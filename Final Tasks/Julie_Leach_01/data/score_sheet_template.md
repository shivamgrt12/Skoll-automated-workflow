# Score Sheet Template, Brightwater Showcase Nail Art Segment

Total points: 100. Scores are recorded per competitor per judge and averaged across the panel.

## Rubric categories

| Category | Weight | What earns full marks |
|----------|--------|------------------------|
| Technical execution | 35 | Clean application, no lifting, controlled thickness, apex placement correct where applicable |
| Design cohesion | 30 | Palette and motif read as one story across all ten nails from three feet away |
| Sanitation and setup | 20 | Station clean, tools sterilized, prep steps visible and correct, no cross-contamination |
| Presentation and finish | 15 | Cuticle line neat, top coat cure even, no dust or lint, model hand posture composed |

## Scoring rules
- Any station-hygiene infraction observed by the sanitation judge triggers a fixed 10-point deduction after averaging, applied by Julie at the desk.
- Ties within one point are broken by the sanitation and setup score.
- Judges with a declared conflict on a competitor recuse on that row. The remaining judges' scores are averaged across the reduced panel size for that row only.
