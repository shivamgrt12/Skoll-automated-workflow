# Tools: Jenny Wilkins

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Garden, Weather, Sky & Wellbeing
- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Pulls frost dates and morning conditions for the garden, and gates outdoor work from October through April for Raynaud's safety.
- **NASA** (`nasa-api`): Pulls moon phases and seasonal sky notes for garden planning and the kitchen-window birdwatching list.
- **MyFitnessPal** (`myfitnesspal-api`): Tracks the daily walk and gentle yoga as consistency, never calories, since GERD and arthritis shape what a good day looks like.
- **Strava** (`strava-api`): Logs the 30-minute neighborhood walk with Harriet, where distance matters less than the streak.

#### Reading, Reference & Notes
- **OpenLibrary** (`openlibrary-api`): Looks up author, edition, and availability for book club picks and Oliver's fantasy recommendations.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Maintains the shared book club notes and the running reading list across Kindle, audio, and physical copies.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Captures the digital companion to Jenny's forty-year commonplace notebook of quotes and marginalia.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): Maintains the perennial inventory of hosta cultivars, echinacea colors, what was moved, and what survived each spring.
- **Algolia** (`algolia-api`): Searches across Jenny's notes, plant records, and reading log to answer questions faster than the paper notebook can.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): Publishes the text for the garden club's reading lists and the used-book sale write-ups.

#### Family, Messaging & Video Calls
- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Reads the connected Google Workspace inbox for appointment confirmations and family updates, and drafts replies for Jenny's approval.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Handles the guest-lecture coordination mail with the Hartford Graduate Institute library science program where Jenny lectures.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Posts and receives the family-thread messages with Claire, Bea, and Oliver, full of grandkid and garden photos.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Surfaces Bea's memes for Jenny, who does not always understand them but always appreciates them.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Sends the text reminders for medications and appointments that Jenny relies on.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Hosts video calls with Claire's family and remote garden club guest speakers.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Joins and posts to the library science program's guest-lecture coordination calls.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): Surfaces Bea's marine-biology club server posts so Jenny can drop in a question when one fits the conversation.

#### Calendar, Appointments & Events
- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Owns the spine of the week: medical appointments, garden club, book club, family visits, and Harriet's commitments.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Books specialist visits and guest-lecture slots through their links.
- **Eventbrite** (`eventbrite-api`): Books garden club lectures, library author talks, and local plant swaps.
- **Ticketmaster** (`ticketmaster-api`): Books chamber-music and theater outings well ahead of the calendar.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Builds RSVP and signup forms for book club and the library used-book sale.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Signs volunteer waivers and appointment consent forms after Jenny reviews them.

#### Home, Local Errands & Travel
- **Google Maps** (`google-maps-api`): Routes the drive to the Hartford specialists and to Claire's in Stamford.
- **Yelp** (`yelp-api`): Checks a new restaurant's noise level and menu before Jenny will agree to go.
- **Uber** (`uber-api`): Books a ride to appointments when Harriet has another commitment.
- **DoorDash** (`doordash-api`): Orders a GERD-safe dinner from local kitchens after a long day in the garden.
- **Instacart** (`instacart-api`): Orders Stop & Shop grocery runs of GERD-safe staples.
- **Ring** (`ring-api`): Watches the front-door camera and logs the mail carrier and the package drop-offs.
- **Zillow** (`zillow-api`): Tracks neighborhood values and the paid-off Cape Cod's market estimate.
- **Airbnb** (`airbnb-api`): Books Vermont covered-bridge stays and coastal overnights for weddings and anniversaries.
- **Amadeus** (`amadeus-api`): Books the England trip for gardens and bookshops, early and with a printed binder.

#### Files, Photos & Documents
- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Stores garden photos by season, scanned medical paperwork, and the household document folder.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Backs up the garden log and reading records.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Maintains a shared folder with Claire for family documents and holiday planning.

#### Music, Mysteries, Watching & Social

- **YouTube** (`youtube-api`): Surfaces pruning tutorials and native-plant talks for the shade garden.

- **Reddit** (`reddit-api`): Reads gardening forums for native-plant questions and answers.
- **Twitter** (`twitter-api`): Follows a short list of authors and libraries and surfaces relevant posts.
