How It Works

The Staging Process

Refined over fourteen years and hundreds of Tampa homes. Every staging starts with the same four-step approach that consistently produces faster sales and stronger offers.

1
Walkthrough and Plan
I photograph every room empty first, identify the hero spaces and natural light direction, then build a budget-tiered plan before spending a dollar on decor or rentals.
2
Declutter and Neutralize
Remove 50 percent of furniture so buyers can see the space. Clear personal photos and repaint bold walls to warm white or greige. The home must smell like nothing.
3
Style for the Buyer
Layer textures with throws, rugs, and pillows from my inventory. Set the dining table for a casual dinner. Fresh greenery in the kitchen. Warm lamp light in every room.
4
Photo-Ready Delivery
Coordinate with my photographer for the best light. Twilight shots for premium listings. 24-hour turnaround. The listing goes live looking like a magazine spread.
