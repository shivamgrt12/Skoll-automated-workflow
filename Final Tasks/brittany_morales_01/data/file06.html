Your South Tampa Agent

Brittany Morales

I grew up shaped by my Cuban grandmother's cooking and the conviction that home is the most important place a family can invest in. That belief is what drives me at 5 AM when the first showing requests come in and at 9 PM when I am closing a loop for a client.

I stage every one of my listings myself because I believe presentation drives price. I answer my phone because in real estate a slow response is a lost deal. And I tell clients the truth about pricing because a good relationship is worth more than one transaction.

I have been a licensed Florida agent since 2012 and I have sold homes in South Tampa, Westchase, Seminole Heights, Channelside, Hyde Park, and Carrollwood. I know these neighborhoods the way a fourteen-year resident knows them: the schools, the flood zones, the blocks that are turning, and the ones to watch.
