# Heartbeat: Timothy Pratt

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- **Every morning, 4:45 AM**: Wake, coffee, then ranch chores from 5:30 to 8:00 AM. Feeding, water troughs, horse care, and Sage out to the pasture.
- **Every morning, 5:15 AM**: Take Metformin and Losartan with breakfast. Check fasting glucose with the OneTouch Verio.
- **Every evening, 7:00 PM**: Take the second Metformin with dinner.
- **Every evening, 9:00 PM**: Bed. Six to six and a half hours of sleep is the pattern.

### Weekly

- **Monday**: Ranch chores at first light, then errands in Browning or fence and equipment work through the afternoon. Quiet evening.
- **Tuesday**: Ranch chores at first light, then ranch work or a mentee visit through the afternoon. First Tuesday is the Town Council meeting at the Browning Town Hall, 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
- **Wednesday**: Ranch chores at first light. Coffee with Walter Bishop at the Glacier Peaks diner, 9:00 AM. Elaine visits with dinner in the evening.
- **Thursday**: Ranch chores at first light, then errands and vet appointments as needed. Quiet evening.
- **Friday**: Ranch chores at first light. Tyler arrives from school for the weekend; afternoon work together on riding, fences, and cattle. Dinner with Tyler.
- **Saturday**: Full ranch day with Tyler. Fishing in season if weather holds, or workshop projects. Early dinner; Tyler stays over.
- **Sunday**: Ranch chores with Tyler until noon. Sunday afternoon call with James in Billings. Drive to Mary's grave once a month. Meal prep for the week in the evening.

### Monthly

- **1st of each month**: Review the Flathead Valley Bank statement and check the household budget against the month's plan.
- **Once per month**: Drive to Mary's grave at the Browning cemetery on a Sunday afternoon.

### Quarterly

- **Every 3 months**: Schedule the Dr. Walker diabetes check and the related bloodwork at the Browning Community Health Center.

### Seasonal / Variable

- **Calving season (late winter to early spring)**: Check the barn shelters and weather radar daily; adjust feeding and cattle rotation accordingly.
- **Hunting season (fall)**: Elk first, then deer. Coordinate weekends with Tyler when he can get away.
- **Winter (November through March)**: Tie flies at the south-window workbench, keep the wood stove fed, and split the cleanest cottonwood for the porch.

### Annual

- **January 9**: Walter Bishop's birthday. Coffee at the diner extended; Timothy picks up the tab.
- **Mid-winter (January or February)**: Annual physical with Dr. Walker, Browning Community Health Center.
- **February 12**: Mary's death anniversary. Visit to the Browning cemetery; quiet evening at home.
- **March 22**: Elaine's birthday. Family dinner at Elaine's house in Browning.
- **Every March**: County Cattlemen's Workshop. Mentor presence as part of the County Agricultural Extension program.
- **April 2**: Sarah's birthday. Card and a PayPal transfer; phone call from James and Sarah.
- **May 17**: Mary's birthday (memorial). Quiet visit to the cemetery if outside the regular monthly cadence.
- **Every May**: Memorial Day ceremony at the Browning Veterans Memorial. Attend with Walter.
- **June 14**: Dan's birthday. Backyard barbecue at Elaine and Dan's if weather holds.
- **July 11**: Tyler's birthday. Ranch day with Tyler; cake at Elaine's in the evening.
- **September 4**: James's birthday. Sunday call extended; gift sent ahead via Shippo.
- **Every September**: Drive to the lower cottonwood flat when the leaves turn gold; sit a while with a thermos of coffee.
- **October 28**: Nathan's birthday. Video call with Nathan in Billings; PayPal transfer.
- **November 8**: Timothy's own birthday. Quiet at the ranch; Elaine and grandkids visit.
- **Every November 11**: Veterans Day ceremony at the Browning Veterans Memorial. Attend with Walter.
- **November 30**: Calvin's birthday. Coffee at Calvin's place; trade equipment IOUs.
- **December 3**: Lily's birthday. Family dinner at Elaine's; framed sketch from Timothy.
- **Every 6 to 9 months while eGFR is stable**: Dr. Crawford nephrology check at the Great Falls Kidney & Hypertension Clinic.
