What we offer

Classes for every level

Beginner Nights

Low-pressure, joyful, no experience needed. Come as you are and leave looser than you arrived.

Intermediate

Clean lines, grapevines, and the confidence to take the front row when you want it.

Advanced Team

Performance-track choreography, lifts, and a spot in the seasonal showcase.

Private Lessons

One-on-one or small-group coaching, booked through the online scheduler.

Seasonal Intensives

Winter and summer deep-dives across three focused Saturdays.

Drop-in Welcome

Visiting or testing the water? Single classes and multi-class packs both work.
