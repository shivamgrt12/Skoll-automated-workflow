OpenLibrary reading list for the NAMI Florida December 5 keynote
Cultural Formulation in Underserved Populations - reference pull for the deck

1. Kleinman, Arthur. "The Illness Narratives: Suffering, Healing, and the Human Condition." Basic Books, 1988. Foundational text on illness meaning and cultural framing.

2. Metzl, Jonathan M. and Roberts, Dorothy E. "Structural Competency Meets Structural Racism: Race, Politics, and the Structure of Medical Knowledge." AMA Journal of Ethics, 2014. Cite for the four-moves framework structure.

3. American Psychiatric Association. "DSM-5-TR Cultural Formulation Interview." APA Publishing, 2022. Reference for the CFI protocol Karen adapted for the framework.

4. Snowden, Lonnie R. "Bias in Mental Health Assessment and Intervention: Theory and Evidence." American Journal of Public Health, 2003. Cite for the assessment bias anchor.

5. Alegria, Margarita et al. "Removing Obstacles to Eliminating Racial and Ethnic Disparities in Behavioral Health Care." Health Affairs, 2016. Cite for the system change ask.

6. Bell, Karen M. "Cultural Formulation in Underserved Populations: A Framework for Psychiatric NPs." Journal of the American Psychiatric Nurses Association, 2024. Karen's own peer-reviewed article underpinning the deck.

7. Fanon, Frantz. "Black Skin, White Masks." Grove Press, 1952 (1967 English translation). Referenced for the cross-cultural clinician effects section.

8. Prilleltensky, Isaac. "The role of power in wellness, oppression, and liberation: The promise of psychopolitical validity." Journal of Community Psychology, 2008. Cite in the closing on the privatization of pain.

9. Boyd-Franklin, Nancy. "Black Families in Therapy: Understanding the African American Experience." Guilford Press, 2003. Reference for the family systems section.

10. Willerton, Christian. "The Language of Distress in Rural Black Southern Communities." Southern Medical Journal, 2018. Cite for the language of distress anchor.
