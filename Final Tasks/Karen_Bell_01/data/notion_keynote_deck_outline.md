# NAMI Florida December 5 Keynote Deck Outline

**Working title**: Cultural Formulation in Underserved Populations: A Framework for Psychiatric NPs
**Duration**: 15 minutes plus 10-minute Q&A
**Audience**: Roughly 400 PMHNPs, nursing faculty, NAMI Florida members

## Three anchor pieces

### Anchor 1. The case for cultural formulation as clinical, not adjacent

Open with the Rosa E. case from the practice archive (name changed, no identifiers). Anxiety presentation misread as OCD for eighteen months across two prior providers. Cultural formulation surfaced the intergenerational trauma frame that reoriented the treatment. Ninety seconds of narrative, three minutes of framework.

### Anchor 2. The framework in four moves

1. Identity and cultural background as clinical data.
2. Language of distress in the patient's own idiom.
3. Community and family systems as protective and risk factors.
4. Cross-cultural clinician effects on the therapeutic relationship.

Four moves, one slide each. Two minutes total.

### Anchor 3. The system change ask

The five actionable asks for training programs and health systems that came out of the peer-reviewed article. Two minutes.

## Closing

Ninety seconds on why language is medicine, framed against James Bell's silence and Rosemary's prayer group and the specific quiet violence of the privatization of suffering.
