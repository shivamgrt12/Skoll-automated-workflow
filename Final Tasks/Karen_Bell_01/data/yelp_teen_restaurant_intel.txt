Yelp read on Fort Lauderdale restaurant venues for Claire's sixteenth
Pulled 2026-10-02 by Karen Bell

The Riverwalk Loft (Downtown Fort Lauderdale)
- 4.6 stars, 180 reviews
- Reviews from 2026 mention smooth handoff for teen and family events. Catering is optional but the venue works with three preferred vendors on the loft's list.
- One March 2026 review notes the sound system was a highlight for a sixteen-year-old dance element.
- Photographer allowed; the venue does not provide one.

Casa Palma Garden (Victoria Park)
- 4.7 stars, 145 reviews
- Tropical garden setting with mature landscaping. Six-minute drive from Victoria Park home.
- Dinner service included; the menu accommodates vegetarian and mixed-diet parties without a separate fee.
- Tent backup is included in the price if the forecast turns.
- One September 2026 review from a NAMI board member's family event described it as "warm without being fussy."

The Pelican Grill Private Room (Fort Lauderdale Beach)
- 4.5 stars, 320 reviews
- Karen and John's date-night spot. Private room seats 22 and has an ocean-facing wall.
- Family friends of the Bennetts have hosted three birthdays here in the last four years.
- Slightly above the per-head range but the room fee is included.
- Photographer not provided; the room's lighting is warm.
