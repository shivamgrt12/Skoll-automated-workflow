What the playtests said

Blind testers reach the first puzzle without a single line of on-screen text, which confirms the lighting is doing its job. The lantern reads as both tool and compass. The recurring note is pacing in the third level: players stall before the water puzzle and a few set the controller down. The fix is a shorter approach and a clearer first beat.

  • Onboarding without textWorking as intended
  • Lantern as guideReads clearly in blind tests
  • Third level pacingNeeds a re-cut
  • Mid level checkpointsNot yet wired

Next milestone

Re-cut level three, add three checkpoints per level, and book a fresh playtest round for October 2026. If the slice holds up, the decision after that is whether to commit to a wider build or keep it a tight, focused release.
