How I work

Three principles

Read before you tell

A room should answer what happened here before any dialogue runs. History lives in worn paths and broken beams.

Let geometry teach

Players learn rules from shape and light, not from a panel of text that interrupts the moment.

Reward the curious

The player who looks closely finds the better story. Hide intent in plain sight, never behind a wall.
