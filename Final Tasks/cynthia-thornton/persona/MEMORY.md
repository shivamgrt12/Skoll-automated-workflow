# Memory: Cynthia Thornton

## Personal Profile
- **Identity**: Sous chef by trade, food is her art and the through-line of everything she cares about.
- **Ethnicity**: Italian-American through Grandma Rose (born Rosaria Calabrese) from Bayonne's Italian community, married into the Thornton family.
- **Family roots**: Sal's father was a Thornton from the Midwest. Cynthia grew up in a food-obsessed Italian-American household in Bayonne.
- **Education**: Culinary Arts diploma from Hudson Valley Culinary Academy (Class of 2013), stages in Portland, Oregon (2014).
- **Marital status**: Single, social life centers on the kitchen crew and Monday nights with Tony.
- **Drive**: Relentlessly driven, opinionated about quality, will argue olive oil brands and refuses to cut corners on ingredients.
- **Character**: Generous with food, self-deprecating with humor, believes food is the most honest thing humans do.
- **Culinary foundation**: Carries Grandma Rose's handwritten recipe book as the starting point for Rootstock.

## Work & Projects
- **Current role**: Sous chef at Harborview Kitchen, 750 Harborview Terrace, Jersey City, NJ 07302, upscale American restaurant.
- **Responsibilities**: Runs the line, manages prep, trains junior cooks, develops 2 to 3 specials per week, quality control every plate.
- **Reports to**: Chef Gianna Caruso, owner and executive chef of Harborview Kitchen.
- **Team**: Roughly 12 kitchen staff including four line cooks, two prep cooks, Dani on pastry, two dishwashers, plus stages.
- **Schedule**: Tuesday through Saturday, typically 2 PM to midnight, later on Fri/Sat. Days off Sunday and Monday.
- **Peak service**: Friday and Saturday dinner at 100-plus covers each.
- **Career 2015 to 2017**: Returned to NJ after Portland, line cook at a mid-tier Italian-American restaurant in Hoboken.
- **Career 2017 to 2020**: Moved to a higher-volume kitchen in lower Manhattan as line cook, then senior line cook.
- **Career 2020 to 2021**: Restaurant closed during COVID, did pop-ups, private cooking, helped Frank wind down Trattoria Benedetto.
- **Career 2022 to present**: Started at Harborview Kitchen January 2022 as line cook, promoted to sous chef in 2023.
- **Rootstock (restaurant dream)**: Seasonal American, hyper-local sourcing, 40-seat intimate space, open kitchen, tasting menu option.
- **Rootstock status**: Actively planning business plan, location research, saving, and networking, about $800 spent so far.
