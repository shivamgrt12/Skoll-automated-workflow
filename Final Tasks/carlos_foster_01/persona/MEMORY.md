# Memory: Carlos Foster

## Personal Profile
Carlos Foster is a game developer, co-founder and lead designer at Moonstone Games, a self-funded indie studio. He holds a BS in Computer Science from the University of Washington (2018) and is otherwise self-taught in game design and pixel art. He is creative and detail-obsessed, the kind of person who will spend four hours getting a single animation frame right. Introverted but collaborative, he is quiet in crowds and comes alive in small-team brainstorms. He is a gamer to the core and sees the world through game-design lenses, and he is deeply ambitious: he wants Moonstone to be the next Supergiant Games or Team Cherry. He does his best creative work between 10 PM and 3 AM, deflects praise with self-deprecating humor, and cares far more about quality than he lets on. He grew up in a close Mexican-American family and learned to cook from his mother.

## Work & Projects
After graduating in 2018, Carlos spent three years as a junior developer at Pinnacle Software (where his father also works), then left in mid-2021 to go freelance with pixel-art commissions and game-jam consulting. He and Tyler co-founded Moonstone Games in late 2024 and began work on Verdant Hollow in January 2025. Moonstone runs out of a home office: Carlos and Tyler work from the spare bedroom of their shared apartment, "the studio." Carlos owns design, art, and narrative; Tyler owns programming and engine; Alex is the freelance composer. Carlos typically works noon to 3 AM with breaks. The current project, Verdant Hollow, is a 2D action-RPG with pixel art, metroidvania exploration, and narrative choices, built in Unity with art in Aseprite and version control on GitHub. It is 18 months in, targeting Steam Early Access in November. The current phase is Act 2 level design, boss encounters, and UI polish. The Steam demo has 1,200 wishlists growing about 8% month over month. Publisher discussions are open with Ember Gate Publishing (Sam Reeves, interest but no formal offer) and Pixel North Games (wants to see the Early Access build); Tyler prefers self-publishing, Carlos is open to a deal. Targets include Steam Next Fest demo showcase, PAX West via an indie MEGABOOTH application, and weekly Tuesday playtests with five beta testers over Discord. Carlos handles marketing on Twitter/X (@moonstonegames, 4,500 followers).
