# Tools: Kristy Blackwell

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Gallery Sales & Finance

- **Square** (`square-api`): Gallery POS system for in-person art sales. Processes transactions, tracks inventory movement, and generates daily and monthly sales reports for exhibition performance review.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Online payment processing for gallery website art sales, collector invoicing, and deposit collection on commissioned pieces.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Online payments from collectors and art fair transactions. Out-of-state collectors use PayPal for large purchases.
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Gallery and personal accounting. Tracks artist commissions, exhibition expenses, studio costs, and tax preparation documents. Reconcile monthly with bank statements.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Gallery financial reporting, year-end tax document preparation, and exportable reports for the accountant.
- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Bank account linking for gallery (Bank of America) and personal (Ally savings) financial tracking and reconciliation.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Artist representation contracts, consignment agreements, collector payment plans, and museum loan forms. All gallery contracts route through here.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Processes cryptocurrency payments from tech-forward collectors purchasing gallery artwork.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Tracks SEP IRA investment performance and portfolio allocation through Vanguard integration.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Handles international cryptocurrency transactions for overseas collector purchases and cross-border payments.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Manages cryptocurrency-to-USD conversions for collector payments received in digital currency.

#### Gallery & Personal Communication

- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Primary communication via kristy.blackwell@Finthesiss.ai. Artist correspondence, collector outreach, gallery business, institutional inquiries, and personal email. Confirm before sending to new contacts.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Quick coordination with Sofia, family members, and represented artists for time-sensitive matters. Informal tone permitted.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Artists' collective channel and gallery team coordination with Lena and Marcus. Keep personal details out of shared channels.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Automated appointment reminders for collector studio visits and exhibition opening notification texts.
- **SendGrid** (`sendgrid-api`): Gallery newsletter delivery and exhibition announcement emails to the collector mailing list.
- **Mailgun** (`mailgun-api`): Transactional emails for gallery purchase confirmations, shipping notifications, and receipt delivery.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Communication with museum staff and institutional partners for the Southwest Arts Museum solo show.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Institutional correspondence and museum communications where Teams integration is needed.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Messaging for international artist contacts and gallery inquiries from abroad.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Virtual studio visits with out-of-town collectors, remote artist meetings, and museum planning calls.
- **Intercom** (`intercom-api`): Gallery website live chat for collector inquiries, exhibition questions, and visitor information requests.

#### Calendar, Events & Scheduling

- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Primary scheduling for gallery hours, studio time blocks, exhibition timelines, family events, collector appointments, and artist meetings. Family events are full-day blocks.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Self-service scheduling for collector studio visits, press meetings, and artist portfolio reviews. Blocks out Monday studio days and family events automatically.
- **Eventbrite** (`eventbrite-api`): Gallery opening receptions, artist talks, community art events (Halloween kids event, seasonal open studios), and ticketed workshops.
- **Ticketmaster** (`ticketmaster-api`): Event tickets for museum openings, art fairs, film screenings, and concerts in Albuquerque and Santa Fe.

#### Visual Media & Creative Tools

- **Instagram** (`instagram-api`): @modernedge_gallery account. Exhibition announcements, studio process shots, artist features, and behind-the-scenes content. Kristy directs content strategy; confirm before posting.
- **Pinterest** (`pinterest-api`): Art inspiration boards for exhibition design, ceramic techniques and glaze palettes, Southwest landscape references, and gallery interior ideas.
- **YouTube** (`youtube-api`): Ceramics technique channels, artist interviews, gallery exhibition documentation videos, and studio process recordings for reference.
- **Figma** (`figma-api`): Exhibition layout design, gallery floor plans for show installations, and opening reception invitation graphics.
- **Vimeo** (`vimeo-api`): Exhibition walkthrough videos and artist interview hosting for gallery website embeds. Higher quality than YouTube for portfolio content.
- **Spotify** (`spotify-api`): Studio listening (jazz, ambient, experimental) during work sessions and gallery opening reception playlist curation.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): Gallery website CMS for exhibition pages, artist bios, event listings, and press coverage updates.
- **Webflow** (`webflow-api`): Gallery website design and landing pages for special exhibitions and the October museum show announcement.
- **WordPress** (`wordpress-api`): Gallery blog for exhibition essays, artist spotlights, curatorial statements, and behind-the-scenes studio posts.

#### Gallery Documents & Project Management

- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Exhibition archives, artist contracts, inventory spreadsheets, studio photo documentation, museum show planning folder, and shared files for gallery operations.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Exhibition planning documents, artist profiles, gallery operations wiki, and curatorial research notes.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Personal knowledge base for art theory notes, exhibition concepts, curatorial ideas, and creative writing related to the "Vessel as Landscape" series.

- **Trello** (`trello-api`): Exhibition installation checklists and show-by-show task boards. Each upcoming show gets its own board.
- **Asana** (`asana-api`): Gallery team task management shared with Lena for daily operations, supply ordering, and maintenance tracking.
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): Gallery weekly planning board and exhibition countdown tracking with timeline views for overlapping projects.
- **Jira** (`jira-api`): Gallery renovation tracking and long-term facility improvement projects.

#### Art Community & Social

- **Twitter** (`twitter-api`): Art world news, gallery announcements, contemporary art discourse, and connecting with curators and critics nationally.
- **LinkedIn** (`linkedin-api`): Professional networking with curators, museum staff, arts administrators, and institutional contacts. Updated for museum show promotion.
- **Reddit** (`reddit-api`): Reads r/ceramics, r/pottery, and r/contemporaryart for technique discussions, material sourcing tips, and emerging artist trends.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): Albuquerque arts community server and ceramics enthusiast groups for peer exchange and local event coordination.
- **Twitch** (`twitch-api`): Streams ceramics process sessions from the studio and hosts live artist talks during exhibition openings.

#### Art Supplies, E-Commerce & Shipping

- **Amazon Seller** (`amazon-seller-api`): Online listings for smaller ceramic pieces, studio seconds, and gallery merchandise like exhibition catalogs and prints.
- **Etsy** (`etsy-api`): Gallery and studio art sales for small-to-medium ceramic works, handmade jewelry by represented artists, and limited edition prints.
- **Instacart** (`instacart-api`): Grocery ordering during busy gallery weeks and exhibition installation periods when there is no time to shop in person.
- **FedEx** (`fedex-api`): Shipping rates and tracking for ceramic artwork requiring careful custom packaging, insurance, and fragile handling.
- **UPS** (`ups-api`): Large vessel and installation piece shipping logistics with freight and white-glove delivery options for museum loans.
- **Shippo** (`shippo-api`): Shipping label generation and carrier rate comparison for routine gallery sales shipments.
- **BigCommerce** (`bigcommerce-api`): Online gallery storefront for prints, small ceramics, exhibition catalogs, and gallery merchandise.
- **WooCommerce** (`woocommerce-api`): Online sales channel integrated with the gallery WordPress site for direct artist sales.
- **DoorDash** (`doordash-api`): Food delivery during late gallery nights, exhibition installation marathons, and studio firing sessions that cannot be interrupted.

#### Gallery Marketing & Analytics

- **Mailchimp** (`mailchimp-api`): Gallery newsletter for exhibition announcements, opening reception invitations, artist features, and collector updates. Primary marketing channel.
- **HubSpot** (`hubspot-api`): Collector relationship management, gallery mailing list segmentation by interest area, and exhibition attendance tracking.
- **Salesforce** (`salesforce-api`): Collector database, gallery sales pipeline tracking, and long-term patron relationship management.

- **Segment** (`segment-api`): Unified collector data across gallery website, email campaigns, and sales channels for consistent relationship tracking.
- **Klaviyo** (`klaviyo-api`): Targeted email campaigns for collector segments based on art medium preferences, purchase history, and exhibition attendance.
- **ActiveCampaign** (`activecampaign-api`): Automated collector follow-up sequences after gallery visits, exhibition openings, and studio tour appointments.

- **Zendesk** (`zendesk-api`): Gallery visitor inquiries, collector follow-up tickets, and shipping issue tracking for sold artworks.
- **Freshdesk** (`freshdesk-api`): Gallery customer support tickets for shipping condition inquiries, return requests, and artwork care questions.
- **ServiceNow** (`servicenow-api`): Gallery facility maintenance requests, HVAC service for climate-controlled storage, and building management coordination.
- **BambooHR** (`bamboohr-api`): Gallery staff records for Lena Taylor and part-time employees. Time-off tracking and onboarding documents.
- **Greenhouse** (`greenhouse-api`): Hiring pipeline for gallery part-time staff, seasonal exhibition installers, and gallery sitter positions.
- **Gusto** (`gusto-api`): Payroll processing for Lena Taylor and part-time gallery staff.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Exhibition visitor feedback surveys, gallery community interest forms, and artist open-call submissions.
- **PagerDuty** (`pagerduty-api`): Gallery security alerts and kiln temperature monitoring notifications during overnight firings. Escalates to Kristy's phone.

- **Amadeus** (`amadeus-api`): Flight and travel bookings for art fairs, museum visits in other cities, and artist residency scouting trips.

#### Not Connected

- Personal banking apps (Bank of America checking, Ally savings) are managed on Kristy's phone and not accessible to the agent.
- Sofia Reyes's work systems at the Bosque Arts Museum are separate and not connected.
- Square POS hardware transactions happen in-gallery only; the agent can read Square data but cannot process in-person card swipes.
- Kristy's personal Verizon phone account is managed directly and not connected.
