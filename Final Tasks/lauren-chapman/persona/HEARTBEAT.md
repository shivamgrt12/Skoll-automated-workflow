# Heartbeat: Lauren Chapman

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- 5:15 AM: kitchen lights on, coffee brewed, devotional book open at the corner of the table.
- 5:30 AM: morning medications in strict order (levothyroxine first, then omeprazole 30 minutes later, then duloxetine, sertraline, calcium 600mg, and vitamin D3 2000 IU with breakfast).
- 6:00 PM (on non-shift evenings): atorvastatin 20mg with dinner.
- 9:00 PM: wind-down. Ginger tea, paperback or quilt on her lap, melatonin 3mg only if sleep is fighting her.

### Weekly

- **Monday**: 12-hour shift on the floor 7:00 AM to 7:30 PM, with shift schedule check, weekly appointment scan, and medication supply count handled before the alarm goes off.
- **Tuesday**: second 12-hour shift; evening yoga at home 20 to 30 minutes if energy holds.
- **Wednesday**: third 12-hour shift; biweekly therapy with Dr. Tolliver at 4:00 PM ET on non-shift Wednesdays, otherwise moved to the following Thursday.
- **Thursday**: grocery run at Harris Teeter or Publix mid-afternoon, list written on the same pad, choir practice in the evening at Cornerstone for alto section.
- **Friday**: lower-key day, light housework, Peking Garden steamed-chicken treat on non-work weeks, evening reading.
- **Saturday**: alternating bakery morning with Patsy at Sweet Magnolia at 9:00 AM (when neither is on weekend rotation), 11:00 AM water aerobics at the Mint Hill YMCA when energy allows; otherwise weekend shift on the floor.
- **Sunday**: 10:00 AM service at Cornerstone Community Church, afternoon call or text with Megan, slow evening before the Monday alarm.

### Monthly

- **1st of the month**: review finances, bills paid, prescription costs, savings transfer.
- **15th of the month**: prescription refill check on all five daily meds; call the pharmacy if anything is low.
- **One Saturday or Sunday a month**: Derek, Jenna, and Lily visit from Raleigh, usually around 11:00 AM with lunch at the house.
- **Once a month**: church book club, currently working through Southern fiction selections.

### Quarterly

- **Every 3 months**: long-weekend PTO somewhere reachable by car, typically Asheville or the Blue Ridge.
- **Every 4 months**: rheumatology with Dr. Anita Rajan at Charlotte Rheumatology Associates.
- **Every 6 months**: endocrinology with Dr. Beth Calloway, with TSH bloodwork the week prior.
- **Every 6 months**: dental cleaning with Dr. Mark Stafford at Mint Hill Family Dental.

### Seasonal / Variable

- **April through October**: vegetable garden active, three raised beds of tomatoes, peppers, and herbs, journaled in good years.
- **November through March**: garden asleep, more reading time, quilting picks up momentum in the evenings.
- **October**: annual fall trip with Patsy to a Blowing Rock cabin, the one fixed coordinate she protects every year.
- **Weather flares**: fibromyalgia worsens with cold snaps, barometric drops, and stress; flag forecast shifts so she can plan.
- **Every-third-weekend rotation**: Sat/Sun on the floor; everything personal on those weekends pauses.
