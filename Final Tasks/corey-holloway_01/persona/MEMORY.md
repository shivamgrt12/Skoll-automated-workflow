# Memory: Corey Holloway

## Personal Profile

Corey David Holloway is a Sacramento native who built a 35-plus year career in technology, moving from networking to systems to site reliability engineering. He holds a B.S. in Computer Science (1988) and an M.S. in Computer Science (1991), both from Pacific Gateway University. English is his native language.

He thinks like an engineer in everything: define the problem, isolate the variables, test the fix. That instinct serves him brilliantly with infrastructure and less well with relationships, where the variables do not hold still. He is a skeptic who distrusts hype and anyone who says "trust me," but the cynicism sharpens his work rather than stalling it. He is generous with his expertise and stingy with wasted time, quick to value competence and quick to flag its absence.

His marriage to Diane ended in divorce, finalized March 2023 after 26 years. He treats it as a data point, not a defining event: no wallowing, no blame, just information he is still parsing and adjusting to. Contact with Diane is civil but minimal. At this stage of life the career endgame is visible, the alimony ends in 2027, the daughter is grown, and the house is large for one person. Something is shifting and he is not yet sure what to build next. Greg Okada is the one person he is fully honest with about the divorce aftermath, the loneliness, and the foot pain he keeps ignoring.
