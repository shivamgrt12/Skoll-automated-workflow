### A note from the Outreach Committee

Friends,

Winter is loosening its grip, and with it comes the season we most look forward
to: the season of open doors. Over the past year, because of your generosity and
your willingness to show up on ordinary Saturdays, our committee has been able to
welcome neighbors, share meals, and stand alongside families across Hamilton. None
of it happens without you.

As we step into spring, we want to keep you close to the work, where it is headed,
how you can be part of it, and the simple, steady good it does. Thank you for
walking this road with us.

With gratitude,
**The Grace Community Outreach Committee**

### Lend a hand

The work runs on volunteers, the people who set up chairs, hold the door, staff the
welcome table, serve a plate, and help clean up when the room empties out. If you have
a few hours this spring, we would love to have you.

- **Where to start:** reach a committee member, or contact the church office.
- **Roles for every comfort level:** setup, welcome table, serving, hospitality,
  accessibility support, and event-day coverage.
- **A note on volunteering:** volunteer applications are reviewed and approved by
  Pastor Crawford. Ask us and we will walk you through it.

---

### With thanks

To everyone who gave this year, your time, your labor, and your gifts, thank you.
We are careful stewards of what you entrust to us, and we account for it faithfully.
To our community partners: working alongside you lets us reach neighbors we would
otherwise miss, and we are grateful for every table you share with us.

*(Specific partner acknowledgements will be added by Ben once confirmed; we name only

### Accessibility & welcome

Every gathering we host is meant to be reachable by everyone. Our events are
wheelchair accessible, ASL interpretation is available on request, and we provide
accessible seating and a quiet space. If there is something that would help you or
your family attend, tell us in advance and we will do our best to make it happen.
