Course Overview

This seminar introduces graduate students to the theoretical and practical methods of maritime archaeology, covering the full research cycle from archival research and remote sensing survey through underwater excavation, finds analysis, conservation liaison, and academic publication.

Students will work with real survey datasets from the North Frisian Medieval Wreck Survey and will complete a semester project involving the analysis of sonar data, finds records, or historical source material. Guest sessions are provided by dive operations specialist Nils Johansen, conservation curator Dr. Frida Moe (Hamburg Maritime Museum), and Frisian heritage liaison Ailo Reimer.
