About Me

I am a 20 year old junior at Cumberland State University, originally from Atlanta and the first in my family to attend a four year university. I study biology because field work and data are the parts that make me lean forward in my seat, and I minor in cultural studies because I love how communities tell their own stories through craft and objects.

I work twelve hours a week as a library assistant at the Founders Library, keep my deadlines tidy in Notion, and spend my off hours photographing Nashville street art and building playlists. Dean's List, fall 2025. Expected graduation, May 2028.
