# Family dim sum cadence 2025

Pattern reference for the Flushing Sunday dim sum cadence with Linda and Howard. Filed because Kevin and Priya keep asking what the schedule is and I want one place to point them.

## Cadence

Roughly every two to three weeks. Linda books the table. Place rotates between two restaurants depending on availability and what the cousins want:

- Golden Harvest Palace (Main St, Flushing). Default. Carts run til 2:00 PM weekends. Best siu mai and turnip cake.
- Asian Jewels Seafood (Main St). When Golden Harvest is fully booked. Better for larger family parties.

Start time: 11:00 AM table for 5-8 people depending on who is in town. Kevin and Priya drive from Mountain View when in town (rare). Mateo joins when he can, usually 60 percent of the time.

## Order pattern

Standing order Linda places before we sit down:
- Har gow
- Siu mai
- Cheung fun beef (no XO sauce, Howard does not like it)
- Turnip cake pan-fried
- Mango pudding (Kevin's favorite)
- Egg tarts (always one or two)

Add-on per visit depending on who is there. Mateo always orders the chicken feet. The lactose detail is a non-issue here because the menu is naturally dairy-light.

## Cost
Averages $60-80 per person at Golden Harvest, $80-100 at Asian Jewels. Linda picks up the bill maybe 1 in 4 visits. I pick up the bill on Howard's birthday week and on the LNY visits. Splits the rest.
