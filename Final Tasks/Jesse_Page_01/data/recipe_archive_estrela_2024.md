# Estrela recipe archive 2024

Reference archive of recipes Ana sent me through 2024, during the early phase of the cookbook conversation but before the Salt + Press contract was signed. Many of these have been superseded by 2026 cookbook versions. Filed for context, not for use.

## Sopa de pedra (stone soup)

- Earliest version, Jan 2024. Ana's grandmother's recipe from the Beira region.
- Notes: lard-based, includes pork knuckle plus chouriço, white beans, cabbage, potato, carrot.
- 2026 cookbook version is rebuilt as a dairy-aware variant. The lard stays, the chouriço stays. Cabbage and beans framing is the constant.

## Caldo verde

- Feb 2024 version. Classic. Olive oil, garlic, potato, kale (couve galega), chouriço.
- 2026 cookbook version: same recipe with explicit chiffonade technique for the kale (4mm strips, not chopped) and a note on the olive oil grade.

## Bacalhau à brás

- Mar 2024 version. Salt cod, potato sticks, eggs, parsley, black olives.
- 2026 cookbook version: hero plate. Photographed Sep 2 (placeholder shot, needs reshoot) and Oct 10 (the reshoot, fall light). The yolk break and the gold pool on the plate are the visual direction Ana specifically wanted.

## Pastéis de nata (cookbook archive only, not in 2026 book)

- Apr 2024 version. The puff pastry process from scratch.
- Cookbook does not include this. Ana made the call in May. Reason: too well-known, every Portuguese cookbook has one, and the proper pastel de nata is a bakery item not a home recipe.

## Açorda alentejana

- May 2024 version. Bread soup with garlic, coriander, poached egg, olive oil.
- 2026 cookbook version: structural change. Treating it as a one-bowl supper, simplified ingredient list.

## Arroz de pato

- Jun 2024. Duck rice.
- 2026 cookbook version: a leaner version. Confit duck leg, less rice, more allium. Hero plate in the autumn chapter.

## Feijoada à transmontana

- Jul 2024. Beans plus pork plus collard greens.
- 2026 cookbook version: this stays close to Ana's original.

## Polvo à lagareiro

- Aug 2024. Octopus with potato, olive oil, parsley.
- 2026 cookbook version: hero plate. Photographed Oct 10. Ana wants the char on the octopus visible.

## Bolinhos de bacalhau

- Sep 2024. Salt cod fritters.
- 2026 cookbook version: same.

## Migas com couve

- Oct 2024. Bread crumb with cabbage.
- 2026 cookbook version: paired with the açorda spread.

## Pão alentejano

- Nov 2024. Sourdough country loaf, 36-hour cold ferment.
- 2026 cookbook version: same.

## Sardinha assada com pimentos

- Dec 2024. Grilled sardines with peppers.
- 2026 cookbook version: pulled. Sardines do not photograph well in the winter chapter direction we settled on.

## Bacalhau com natas (archive only)

- Dec 2024. Salt cod with cream.
- 2026 cookbook version: not included. The dairy-free segmentation Ana finalized Oct 2 means this recipe is not a fit for the cookbook structure. Marked as "menu only".

## Why this archive matters less now

The 2026 cookbook has its own recipe list, formalized in the Salt + Press contract appendix and tracked in the cookbook layout proof. The Oct 2 dairy-free segmentation call with Ana resolved which 2024 recipes carry forward, which get rebuilt, which become menu-only.

Closed Mar 2025. Not actively maintained. Cookbook recipe state lives in the active Salt + Press project tree.
