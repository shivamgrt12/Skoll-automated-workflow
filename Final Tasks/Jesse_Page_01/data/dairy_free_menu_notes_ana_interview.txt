## Cross-contamination notes (for cookbook headnotes, not for publisher allergen index)

- Pasteis de Bacalhau and Polvo Grelhado share the same fryer; not an allergen issue, 
  but the dairy butter in the cod mash means the fryer is not strict dairy-free between batches.
- Quindim, Pasteis de Nata, Pudim Abade, Sericaia all prepped on the dessert station; 
  the dessert station is not a strict dairy-free zone.
- Sardinhas Assadas and Frango Piri Piri: grill is shared but proteins are kept on separate sides; 
  no dairy crossover.
- All sopas (Caldo Verde, Sopa de Pedra, Acorda de Marisco) prepped in separate pots; 
  Caldo Verde dairy version is the standard pour for fall; sub-pot for dairy-free on request only.
