The shop

Fifteen years on the same street

Weaver & Book opened in 2011 as a small curated selection and has grown into one of the city's recognised independent bookshops. We hold roughly four thousand titles, keep a lending shelf by the door, and put quiet seats where readers can sit. We are a community institution as much as a shop.
