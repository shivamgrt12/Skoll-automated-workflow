# Heartbeat: Lucia Weaver

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- 7:00 AM: Wake, kettle on, 30 minutes with The Guardian and The London Review online before leaving the apartment.
- 7:45 AM (Tue, Thu): Yoga reminder for the 8:00 AM class at the Kungsgatan studio.
- 8:30 AM (non-yoga mornings): Walk to Svartbäcksgatan, open the shop quietly, morning coffee with Henrik next door at Kaffestugan.
- 10:00 AM (Tue to Sat): Shop opens. Lucia covers mornings alone.
- 1:00 PM (Tue to Fri): Linnea arrives, covers 13:00 to 18:00.
- 6:00 PM (Tue to Sat, non-event nights): Shop closes, walk home along the Fyris river when weather allows.
- 11:00 PM: In bed with 30 to 45 minutes of reading before lights out.

### Weekly

- Monday: Admin day with the shop closed, 9:00 AM start to catch up on supplier emails, inventory adjustments, and personal correspondence.
- Tuesday: Week start review at the shop at 9:00 AM to confirm the week's events, deliveries, and Linnea's shifts.
- Thursday: Delivery check for publisher shipments at 1:00 PM, with event nights extending closing to 20:00 when an author conversation or reading is scheduled.
- Friday: Weekly review at 4:00 PM covering numbers from the week, plan for the weekend, and restock notes for Tuesday.
- Saturday: Shop opens at 10:00 AM with Linnea covering 10:00 to 16:00, fika at Güntherska Hovkonditori in the afternoon once the shop is settled.
- Sunday: Slower start at 8:30 AM with longer reading and breakfast at home, phone call with Dorothy at 6:00 PM Sweden time (9:00 AM Portland in winter, 10:00 AM Portland in summer) for roughly an hour, forest walk in Uppland in the afternoon when weather allows, otherwise a long Fyris loop.

### Monthly

- 1st of the month: Personal and business financial review. Reconcile Fortnox, check savings transfer to Handelsbanken, top up the Avanza ISK.
- One Saturday a month: Camilla visit from Stockholm, or Lucia travels to Stockholm for the day.
- Mid month: Quiet evening drafting the Weaver & Book newsletter for end of month send.

### Quarterly

- Quarterly: Meeting with Lars Petersson, accountant, to review books, VAT, and tax position.
- Quarterly: Stock take across literary fiction, poetry, and translated titles.
- Quarterly: Lending shelf review, retire damaged copies, refresh student picks.

### Seasonal / Variable

- Spring (March to April): Spring publisher catalogues arrive, ordering decisions for late spring and early summer.
- April 30: Walpurgis Night, shop closes early in the evening for the Uppsala bonfire traditions.
- Late June: Midsummer week, shop closed three days, family travel often to Gothenburg or Portland.
- Summer (June to August): Quiet sales window, focused on translation work, deep reading, lending shelf curation, and small website improvements.
- Late September: Gothenburg Book Fair, three day trip overlapping with Andrew when possible.
- Autumn (September to October): Autumn publisher catalogues, the strongest ordering window of the year.
- Mid October: Frankfurt Book Fair, two to three day trip.
- November: Annual checkup with Dr. Eva Karlsson at Uppsala Vårdcentral.
- November to February: SAD lamp in the morning before the walk to the shop.
- First Advent Sunday: Christmas window display goes up at the shop.
- December: Mulled wine evenings at the shop, gift wrapping service active, longer Saturday hours.

### Annual

- January 22: Lucia's birthday, low key, dinner with Henrik and Camilla when she is in town.
- February 26: Camilla Ström's birthday, usually marked with a Stockholm visit.
- March 19: Oskar's birthday in Gothenburg, card and a small book sent ahead.
- May 12: Astrid Lindén's birthday, a book picked from the shop and dropped to her office.
- May 22: Elsa's birthday in Gothenburg, card and a book chosen for her reading level.
- May 4: Henrik Johansson's birthday, dinner across at Kaffestugan after closing.
- October 14: Dorothy's birthday, long phone call and a parcel mailed two weeks ahead.
- October 11: Linnea Ekström's birthday, marked at the shop on her next shift.
- November 8: Andrew Weaver's birthday, phone call and a small parcel to Gothenburg.
- Annual: Dentist visit at Folktandvården Uppsala.
- Annual: Lease check on the apartment, confirm rent control terms with the landlord.
- Annual: Shop insurance review with the broker before renewal.
- Christmas Eve: In Portland with Dorothy, returning to Uppsala between Christmas and New Year.
