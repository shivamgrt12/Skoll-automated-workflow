# Memory: Roger Webb

## Personal Profile

- **DOB**: November 22, 1989
- **Roots**: Born and raised in Honolulu, third-generation islander, lives in the Kaimuki neighbourhood.
- **Education**: A.A.S. in Culinary Arts, Pastry and Baking concentration, Pacific Culinary Institute, completed 2011.
- **Sheraton Waikiki (June 2011 to March 2017)**: Pastry cook rising to pastry team lead across high-volume resort banquet and cafe production.
- **Halekulani Hotel (April 2017 to October 2023)**: Senior pastry chef refining laminated dough and artisan technique in a luxury dining setting.
- **Koa Morning Star Bakery (January 2024 to present)**: Owner and head pastry chef in Kaimuki, opened after leaving the hotel circuit.
