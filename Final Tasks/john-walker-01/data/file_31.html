What it is

Every November the crews take to the corners with a boot in hand. Every dollar collected goes to neighbors who need a hand with heat, groceries, and a warm coat through the winter. No overhead, no middle step, just firefighters and the community they answer for.

Lend a hand

Sign up to staff a corner for a couple of hours, or pledge a donation to be collected at the station. We will follow up by phone or email with the details.

Organized by the firefighters of Quincy. Thank you for twenty two years of generosity.
