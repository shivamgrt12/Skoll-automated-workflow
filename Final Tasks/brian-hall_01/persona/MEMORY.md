# Memory: Brian Hall

## Personal Profile

Brian carries an athlete's mindset into everything. He treats challenges like training blocks and is always chasing the next personal record, whether the arena is a patient session, a meal prep, or a clinic growth target. High energy is his baseline, and he is most productive before the sun is fully up, with a 4:45 AM alarm he treats as non-negotiable.

He is a coach at heart: competitive but collaborative, wanting to win while lifting everyone around him. Education: Doctor of Physical Therapy from Piedmont University and a BS in Kinesiology from Peach State University, where he played Division 3 basketball. Faith is part of the rhythm of his week, anchored by Sunday service at Redeemer Church Midtown. He is FIRE-curious and reads Bogleheads, applying the same disciplined, long-game thinking to money that he applies to training.
