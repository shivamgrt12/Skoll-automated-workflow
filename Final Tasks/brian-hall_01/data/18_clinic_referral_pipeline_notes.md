# Clinic Referral Pipeline Notes

Working notes on the clinic referral network. Not a CRM export. Not to be
sent anywhere. Standing rule reminder: no PHI in any external channel, no
clinic financials to family, no patient identifying content in HubSpot,
Salesforce, Mailchimp, Airtable, Jira, or Notion public pages. Referral
work happens at the relationship level, not the case level, for anything
that leaves the clinic.

## Referral network shape

Peak Performance PT sits inside a small Atlanta network of orthopedic and
sports medicine referrers. The clinic runs sports rehab as its focus,
which draws a specific referral profile:

- Orthopedic surgery post-op (knee ACL primary, shoulder secondary).
- Sports medicine non-operative overuse and concussion.
- Athletic training referral chain from local clubs and academies.
- CrossFit and endurance community word of mouth.
- Primary care referral for adult non-athlete rehab keeps caseload
  balanced when sports volume is seasonal.

## What does not go into any external channel

  detail, timeline, metric, prior test result.
- Any dollar figure for clinic operations to family.

  membership.
- Any Jamal Hendricks case content anywhere outside clinic EMR and
  direct clinic-to-clinic verbal or secure channels.
