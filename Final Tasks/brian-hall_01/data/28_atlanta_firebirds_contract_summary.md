# Atlanta Firebirds FC Academy - Contract Summary

Working reference for Peak Performance PT's pre-season screening
contract with the Atlanta Firebirds FC academy. Not a signed-document
copy - Brian's working memory of scope, cadence, and boundaries for
the relationship. Not to be sent anywhere.

## The contract shape

- Scope: pre-season screening for the Firebirds FC academy. Movement
  screen, injury history intake, baseline metrics on academy players
  entering the pre-season block.
- Deliverable to the club: aggregated screening summary at the
  program level - not per-player clinical detail. Individual player
  clearance conversations happen provider-to-provider with the
  academy sports medicine physician.
- Cadence: annual pre-season screening block. Follow-up windows
  during the season by exception for specific referred cases.
- Peak Performance PT's role: screening, movement quality baseline,
  and referral routing for cases needing PT work. Not the team PT of
  record - the Firebirds have their own team PT (contact in file 17).

## What the lab does for this contract

- Force plate baseline: jump asymmetry, force-time profile at
  screening. Available after force plate installs post lab launch.
- NordBord: hamstring strength eccentric baseline. Available after
  NordBord installs post lab launch.
- AlterG: not a screening tool, an intervention tool for referred
  cases returning to run after lower extremity injury. Available
  after AlterG installs post lab launch.
- VALD hub: consolidates the screening dataset per athlete for the
  aggregated program-level summary to the club.

## Standing communication rules

- Aggregated program-level detail goes to the academy director and
  head athletic trainer via email (see file 17 for segment).
- Individual player clinical detail routes provider-to-provider with
  the academy sports medicine MD. Never via HubSpot, Salesforce,
  Mailchimp, Airtable, Jira, Notion public pages, WhatsApp, or any
  personal channel.
- No player names into any external channel outside the sports med
  MD conversation. No exceptions.
- Coach conversations happen at the coordination level (schedule,
  location, headcount) - not the case level. Standing.

## Contract renewal timing

- Contract renewal conversation window: post-season, roughly early
  next calendar year for the following pre-season block.
- Do not raise contract renewal during the Oct 22 to Oct 25 trip
  window. Standing.
- Do not raise contract renewal during grand opening week Oct 11 or
  the week after - relationship management, not transactional.

## Firebirds and the grand opening

- Firebirds staff segment (Mailchimp draft, file 13) invited to grand
  opening as a signal event. The lab is the visible signal that Peak
  Performance PT is now measuring the right things for the academy
  population.
- Expected attendance: head AT and assistant AT likely. Sports med
  MD by schedule. Academy director may or may not - his participation
  is a bonus, not the target.
- Do not commit to any specific volume increase from Firebirds
  referrals in the grand opening invite copy. That is not the
  conversation.

## What is NOT in scope for the contract

- First-team players. The contract covers the academy, not the
  senior side. First-team PT relationship is separate through their
  team PT.
- General medical care. Screening and PT-adjacent scope only.
- Nutrition. Firebirds has their own nutrition consultant (courtesy
  contact in file 17 for grand opening invite).
- Strength and conditioning programming. Firebirds S&C coach owns
  that. Peak Performance PT does not write S&C programs for the
  academy.
