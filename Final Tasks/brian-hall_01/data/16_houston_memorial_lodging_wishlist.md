# Houston Memorial Lodging Wishlist

Working notes for the Oct 22 to Oct 25 Houston trip. Four confirmed travelers:
Brian, Karen, Ethan (6), Lily (6). Rachel visits from Dallas but sleeps at
Robert and Linda's place. Booking this is a full-house rental, not a hotel
squeeze, because two 6 year olds in a hotel room with parents on a birthday
weekend is a poor plan.

## Location target

- Memorial area, close to Robert and Linda's neighborhood. Drive time from
  the rental to their house should be under 15 minutes at Saturday
  afternoon traffic. Cookout is at Linda's on Saturday, and Sunday morning
  breakfast is at Robert and Linda's before the airport run.
- IAH airport access matters for Thursday afternoon arrival and Sunday
  evening return. Prefer a rental with easy Beltway or 610 access.
- Avoid Inner Loop rentals - too far from Memorial and the family.

## Sleep configuration (non-negotiable)

- Sleeps 5 or more. Four travelers plus Rachel occasionally crashing after
  cookout if she does not want to drive back to Robert and Linda's late.
  Rachel would take a couch or spare bed as a backup, not primary.
- Two separate bedrooms minimum. Twins share a room, adults get a room.
- Blackout curtains in kids' room. Twins are up at 6 AM Atlanta time which
  is 5 AM Houston time and that is a bad start to a trip.
- No shared bathroom off primary. Twins bathroom access without walking
  through the adult room.

## Yard and pool (from PROMPT)

- Fenced backyard is required. Twins will be running around Saturday
  morning before we head to Linda's. Fence not decorative - actually
  contained.
- Pool preferred. Ethan is pool-obsessed. If pool is not fenced separately
  from the rest of the yard, twins need adult in yard whenever door is
  open. Note that in the trip brief so Karen and I are aligned.
- Covered outdoor space nice to have (rain contingency for twins) but not
  required.

## Kitchen

- Full kitchen. Coffee maker required. If it is a Chemex or French press,
  bonus. If it is a Keurig, we bring Counter Culture and improvise.
- Fridge space for cookout leftovers Saturday night and Sunday breakfast.
- Not required to cook the cookout - that is at Linda's - but Friday
  dinner and Sunday breakfast are on the rental kitchen.

## Wifi and workspace

- Reliable wifi. Brian may need to check clinic mock inbox in the mornings
  and pull the finance snapshot Saturday morning before the cookout.
- Kitchen island or dining table works as a laptop spot. Not booking a
  hotel business center substitute.

## Family compatibility

- No stairs to the primary bedroom if possible. Karen prefers ground floor
  for the twins' room specifically - middle of the night trips work better.
- Washer and dryer nice to have. Twins spill things. Twins spill many things.
- No pets on premises (Ethan mild dust allergy - dogs are fine outdoors,
  not fine as house residents).

## Budget guardrail

- Standing rule: no single-charge commitment above $500 without Brian
  approval. Trip brief pulls top three options with total including
  cleaning fee, service fee, taxes - not just nightly rate.
- Rank by fit-to-criteria first, then total cost. Cheapest that meets
  fence and sleep configuration wins over prettiest that fails one hard
  requirement.

## What NOT to book

- Downtown Houston condo. Too far from Memorial.
- Anything with only one bathroom.
- Anything without a fully fenced yard.
- Anything that lists "close to Memorial" but is actually in Spring Branch
  or Katy - drive time is the actual test.
- Anything that requires check-in later than 4 PM Thursday - flight lands
  early afternoon and the twins need to reset.

## What to include in the trip brief

- Top 3 listings meeting criteria.
- Nightly rate, cleaning fee, service fee, taxes, total for 3 nights.
- Distance and drive time to Robert and Linda's Memorial address.
- Pool status and yard fencing status called out per listing.
- Cancellation policy noted for each.

## Standing rules

- No booking anything without Brian and Karen aligned. Trip brief goes to
  Karen tonight, decision Wednesday morning, book Wednesday afternoon at
  earliest.
- No sending listings to Robert and Linda in advance - they will worry
  about cost. Tell them where we landed after booking.
- If mock Airbnb inventory does not show Memorial listings, note that
  explicitly in the trip brief and flag as an open item for follow up
  outside the assistant flow (e.g., call listing manager directly).
