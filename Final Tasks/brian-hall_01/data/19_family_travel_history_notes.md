# Family Travel History Notes - Houston Trips

Working memory on prior Houston trips. Helps plan this one without
repeating the mistakes. Not a comprehensive travel log, just what
matters for the Oct 22 to Oct 25 weekend.

## The pattern

- Linda drives up from Houston to Atlanta or Brian drives down to
  Houston roughly every 6 to 8 weeks. Rotating, not fixed.
- Full family trips (Brian, Karen, twins) to Houston happen maybe twice
  a year plus milestone weekends (Robert birthday, Christmas some years,
  Rachel visits when schedules line up).
- Twins have flown to Houston enough that airport routine is dialed. Not
  their first rodeo. Snack bag, tablet, headphones, done.

## What worked last few trips

- Direct ATL to IAH when the flight is available. Twin patience does not
  survive a Dallas connection unless it is the only option.
- Landing early to mid afternoon at IAH, drive to rental, twins get pool
  or yard time before dinner. Resets them from travel.
- Renting a house instead of staying at Robert and Linda's. Twins have
  their own room, adults have their own space, and Linda does not spend
  the weekend cooking breakfast for six people.
- One anchor event per day. Do not overschedule. Twins turn feral if
  hauled from activity to activity.

## What did NOT work

- 2024 summer trip: booked a place that said "close to Memorial" but was
  actually 30+ minutes from Robert and Linda in traffic. Cost the trip a
  chunk of time. Do not repeat.
- 2024 Christmas: Linda made three side dishes at cookout that Brian's
  brisket did not need. Potato salad went south by sunrise. Cross-check
  with menu note in file 10 - do not compete on sides.
- 2023 Thanksgiving: connection through DFW cost 90 minutes on return,
  landed late Sunday, twins wrecked Monday morning. Karen went to work
  running on fumes. Direct only if possible this trip.
- Any trip where Karen took a call from MedPlus about a shift change
  while at Robert and Linda's. Karen's shift file needs to be locked
  before the trip - see file 06.

## Rachel from Dallas pattern

- Rachel is 33, ER nurse at Southlake Regional. Her schedule flips 8
  weeks out and again 2 weeks out. Assume her arrival is best-effort
  until locked.
- Rachel usually drives from Dallas rather than flies. About 4 hours if
  traffic cooperates. Drives up Sat morning if working Friday night,
  drives back Sun morning or Sunday afternoon.
- Rachel stays at Robert and Linda's, not the rental. Rental sleeps 5+
  as buffer only, not primary Rachel bed.
- If Rachel's ER schedule flips and she cannot come, do not shift the
  trip plan. Trip is for Robert. Rachel is a bonus.

## Robert and Linda hospitality patterns

- Robert prefers phone calls, not text. Sunday 3 PM ET dad call is the
  regular. Trip week the call gets skipped.
- Linda is a semi-retired accountant, still sharp, will ask about clinic
  financials if given the opening. Do not give the opening. Family
  scheduling and visits only, not clinic dollars.
- Linda's potato salad tradition is inviolable. Do not bring competing
  potato salad. Do not suggest a "better" recipe. Ever.
- Robert's Big Green Egg has decent temp control, offset in the shed
  works for a brisket if the wood is right. Brian brings his own rub and
  injection kit, not Robert's.

## Trip-specific notes for Oct 22 to Oct 25

- Trip is a 4 night arc: Thursday afternoon arrival, Sunday evening
  return. Three full days in Houston.
- Robert turns 68 Oct 22. Trip is anchored on his actual birthday.
- Saturday cookout at Robert and Linda's. Brian brings protein. Menu
  in file 10.
- Twins swim in the rental pool most days if the weather holds. Late
  October Houston is usually mid 70s to low 80s. Bring twin swim gear
  regardless of forecast.
- Sunday morning: breakfast at Robert and Linda's before airport run.
  Do not book a Sunday morning flight - kills the breakfast.
- Sunday evening: back to ATL for CrossFit Buckhead Fall Throwdown.
  See file 15.

## Karen preferences on Houston trips

- Karen prefers direct flights, morning arrival if possible for the
  outbound. Trip window says fly Thursday after morning patient block,
  so afternoon arrival wins.
- Karen prefers rental with a real coffee setup. Brian brings Counter
  Culture beans, Chemex not required but appreciated.
- Karen wants the twins to be able to run outside at the rental
  without a screen. Fenced yard is on her list too, not just twins'.

## Do not include in the trip brief to Karen

- No commentary on Linda's cooking. Karen will notice on her own if it
  comes up at the cookout.
- No commentary on Rachel's schedule flip risk. Rachel confirms or she
  does not, either way the trip runs.

## Standing rules cross-reference

- No sending trip logistics to Robert, Linda, or Rachel until Brian
  and Karen aligned and bookings made.
- No booking anything above $500 without approval. Trip brief carries
  the options, not the commit.
- Family sees scheduling and visits only. Clinic financial detail
  stays behind clinic walls.
