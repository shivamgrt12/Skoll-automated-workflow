Local snapshot of the Notion workspace index for the Peak Performance PT
performance lab. This is the working table of contents, not the wiki
pages themselves. Wiki content lives in Notion under private workspace
access. Standing rule: no patient-identifying content anywhere in the
Notion workspace, and no page in the workspace is set to public - ever.

## Purpose of the wiki

- Central protocol library for the lab: force plate, NordBord, AlterG,
  VALD hub.
- Coordination hub for staff: Tom, Marcus, Tamika, plus Brian.

  no case content).

## What does NOT appear in the Notion wiki

- Any patient name, initials in a searchable page, DOB, injury
  detail, timeline, metric, prior test result, or scheduling
  detail beyond aggregated non-identifiable references.
- Any Jamal Hendricks reference in any form.
- Any clinic revenue detail beyond what is necessary for staff
  onboarding at the "we hit our targets" summary level, and even
  that only in the internal staff onboarding section behind
  workspace access controls.
- Any Robert, Linda, Rachel, or family contact information.
- Any dollar figure that would be surprising to see in a general
  workspace context (loan closing terms, vendor invoice amounts,
  personal account balances).
