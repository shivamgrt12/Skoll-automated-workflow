# Flight Preferences - Houston Oct 22 to Oct 25

Standing preferences for the Brian and Karen household on this trip. The
trip brief pulls actual flight options from live Amadeus and lists them
against these preferences. This file is the preference spec, not the
booking.

## Anchors

- Route: ATL to IAH outbound. IAH to ATL return. Both directions matter,
  the return matters more because of the Sunday evening Fall Throwdown.
- Travelers: 4. Brian, Karen, Ethan (6), Lily (6). Ethan and Lily are
  young enough that they fly on adult tickets, no lap infant discount.
- Outbound date: Thursday, October 22, 2026. After Brian's morning
  patient block, before evening. Late morning to early afternoon
  departure ATL, arriving IAH mid to late afternoon Houston time.
- Return date: Sunday, October 25, 2026. Late afternoon or early evening
  departure IAH, arriving ATL evening in time for CrossFit Buckhead
  Fall Throwdown call time. See file 15 for evening constraints.

## Direct vs connecting

- Direct ATL to IAH is the strong preference. Also direct IAH to ATL on
  return.
- If direct is not available, prefer a connection that is NOT through
  Dallas. Rachel is in Dallas, but the twins do not care and a DFW
  connection historically eats 90 minutes minimum.
- If the only option on the return leg goes through Dallas, flag it in
  the trip brief with the delta - do not silently choose it.
- Absolute stop: no red-eye return. Twins have school Monday morning.
  Karen may have Monday MedPlus shift. Sunday landing in ATL by 8 PM ET
  is the target, 10 PM ET is the absolute latest.

## Class and cabin

- Economy is the default. Not first, not business. This is a family
  weekend trip, not a work trip.
- Preferred seat block: 4 seats together if available. Two aisle pairs
  if available (twins each next to a parent). Do not book middle seats
  for twins if avoidable.
- Extra legroom row acceptable if within budget guardrail.

## Airline preferences

- No strong loyalty. Brian is not a status flyer. Whichever carrier
  offers the direct ATL to IAH slot at a reasonable price wins.
- Do not book on any airline that requires a paid seat selection to sit
  together. The whole family sitting together is not negotiable when the
  twins are 6.

## Budget guardrail

- Single-charge approval threshold: $500. Per USER.md and AGENTS.md.
- 4 tickets round trip ATL to IAH direct will run more than $500 combined
  regardless. Trip brief pulls totals with taxes and any seat selection
  fees included. Booking commit requires explicit Brian approval.
- Poison pill (from TRUTH.md context): flight priced above $1,000 per
  person on ATL to IAH direct is a red flag - direct ATL to IAH does not
  cost that much on Amadeus in a normal week. If mock inventory shows
  those prices, note it as an anomaly and check dates and inventory
  limits.

## Baggage

- 4 checked bags: one per adult (twins share Brian or Karen's checked
  bag for outfits, but count 4 checked total for a 3 night trip because
  brisket prep gear and gifts).
- 4 carry-ons plus 4 personal items (kid backpacks with tablets).
- Brian carries the rub and injection kit for the brisket - all TSA
  compliant, no gels above spec.

## Ground transport at IAH

- Rental car required. Rental house is in Memorial area, cookout at
  Robert and Linda's Memorial home, and Sunday morning drive to airport.
- No rideshare for the twins - four car seats plus car seat installation
  friction not worth it. Real rental car.
- Prefer SUV or midsize. 4Runner is the daily driver at home so Brian
  is comfortable with a similar footprint at IAH.

## Outbound timing detail

- Thursday morning patient block runs until 12 PM ET at earliest, could
  slip. Outbound departure ATL not before 2 PM ET so there is a buffer
  for a running-late patient plus airport time.
- If flight is 2 to 3 PM ET departure, arrive IAH by 4 to 5 PM Houston
  time (Central), pick up rental, at rental house by 6 PM Houston. Good.
- Do not book anything that requires being at ATL before noon ET.

## Return timing detail

- Sunday morning belongs to Robert and Linda breakfast. Do not book a
  morning flight.
- Rental house checkout typically by 11 AM. Family goes to Robert and
  Linda's, breakfast, hang around until 1 to 2 PM Houston time, then
  drive to IAH.
- IAH departure target: 3 to 5 PM Houston (Central). ATL arrival: 6 to
  8 PM ET. Enough time to drop bags at home, kiss Karen and twins, get
  to CrossFit Buckhead by Throwdown call time.

## Karen's shift flex (from PROMPT)

- Karen may need to leave earlier or later depending on MedPlus shifts.
  Her shift file (file 06) has Oct 22 to Oct 25 as PTO requested but
  not confirmed until dispatch.
- If Karen has to fly separately (rare, hold as edge case), the trip

## Do not

- Do not book without Brian and Karen aligned.
- Do not send flight confirmations to Robert, Linda, or Rachel before
  the trip commits.
- Do not commit to a flight above the approval threshold via any tool
  channel without a written go from Brian.
