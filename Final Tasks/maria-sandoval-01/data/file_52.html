What We Offer

Comprehensive Pediatric Care

From a child's first dental visit to adolescent care, we offer the full range of preventive and restorative services in a warm, child-friendly environment.

🦷

Preventive Care

Routine cleanings, fluoride varnish, dental sealants, and comprehensive oral exams. We establish healthy habits early and keep them going.

🔧

Restorations

Tooth-colored composite fillings, crowns, and space maintainers. We restore your child's teeth gently and efficiently.

😴

Nitrous Sedation

Safe, well-monitored nitrous oxide sedation for anxious patients or more complex procedures. Dr. Sandoval is completing AAPD sedation certification.

🚑

Emergency Care

Same-day slots held for dental emergencies. Toothaches, broken teeth, and trauma managed promptly and with care.

👶

First Visit

We welcome children from their first tooth or first birthday. Early visits build comfort and set the foundation for a lifetime of healthy dental habits.

📋

Family Coordination

We work closely with parents at every step, explaining findings, answering questions, and supporting home care between visits.

First Visit

What to Expect

Your child's first visit is about getting comfortable - not just getting things done.

1

Complete Intake Forms

We will send a pre-screening form before the visit. Takes about five minutes to complete.

2

Meet the Team

Dana at the front desk will greet you. The hygienist will introduce herself before anything begins.

3

Exam and Cleaning

Dr. Sandoval completes a thorough exam and reviews findings with you before leaving the room.

4

Your Questions Answered

We leave time at the end for questions. Home care, nutrition, habits - ask anything.
