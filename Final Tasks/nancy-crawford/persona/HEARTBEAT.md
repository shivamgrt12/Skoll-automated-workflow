# Heartbeat: Nancy Crawford

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- 7:30 to 8:30 morning walk along the Delaware riverfront or through Fishtown, regardless of weather.
- Espresso at the Breville on waking, no milk.
- Email triage in two short passes: one mid-morning after the studio block ends, one late afternoon.

### Weekly

- Monday: studio block 9:00 to 13:00, yoga at home around 18:00 (Yoga with Adriene), inbox cleanup in the evening.
- Tuesday: Harrington Arts Academy teaching day 10:00 to 16:00, student office hours after class if needed.
- Wednesday: studio block 9:00 to 13:00, yoga at home around 18:00.
- Thursday: Harrington teaching day 10:00 to 16:00, biweekly dinner with Theo Parke at The Sardine Room or his place.
- Friday: studio block 9:00 to 13:00, quiet evening at the loft with reading or jazz.
- Saturday: Fishtown farmers market at 9:00, espresso and a croissant at Flour & Stone, weekend bike ride on the Schuylkill if weather holds.
- Sunday: late morning call with Ruth Crawford at Meadowlark Senior Living around 11:00, slow afternoon, light studio prep for the week.

### Monthly

- First Monday at 14:00: check-in call with Kim Albright at Ironwork Gallery about the September solo show.
- Mid-month review with Nina Voss on invoicing, expense tracking, and any pending commission deposits.
- One Saturday per month: a longer studio visit from Theo Parke or a Brooklyn day trip on Amtrak to see Margaux Villard.

### Quarterly

- Mid-quarter review of inventory in Airtable: completed pieces, work-in-progress, gallery placement status.
- Quarterly TIAA statement review with Nina Voss at tax time.
- Studio supply order through Blick Art Materials and Prentice Letterpress.

### Seasonal / Variable

- Spring semester teaching: mid-January through April 30, grades due May 4.
- Summer studio focus block: May through August, no teaching. Summer 2027 carries full attention to the Silverline triptych and the Ironwork show pieces.
- Fall semester teaching: late August through mid-December. The Fall 2027 term will include the Ironwork solo opening on September 18, 2027.
- Late winter: research and reading time, occasionally a Hartford visit to Ruth Crawford in colder months.
