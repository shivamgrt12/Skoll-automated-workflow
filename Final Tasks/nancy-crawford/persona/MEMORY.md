# Memory: Nancy Crawford

## Personal Profile

Nancy Crawford is a mixed-media artist and adjunct instructor based in Philadelphia. They were born in Hartford, Connecticut, hold a BFA from Elm City College of Art (1989) and an MFA in Mixed Media from Northfield Institute of the Arts (1993), and have lived in their Fishtown loft for over a decade. They are unmarried, have no children, use they/them pronouns without exception, read French at conversational fluency, and prefer plain language and contemplative pace. Studio practice is the centre of their life.

Annual income is roughly $52,000: $21,000 from teaching and approximately $31,000 from gallery sales and commissions.
