About

I am a mixed-media artist working in printmaking, collage, and assembled found materials. I walk the river most mornings and pick things up: a torn ticket, a strip of rusted tin, a page softened by weather. In the studio I print, layer, and assemble until two unrelated surfaces agree to be looked at together.

I have exhibited in Philadelphia and Brooklyn for three decades and teach foundations in mixed media. I read French at conversational fluency and am content with a quiet, considered pace.
