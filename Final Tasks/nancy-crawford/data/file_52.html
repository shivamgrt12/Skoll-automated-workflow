What we ask of you

Build and pull a basic monotype and relief print. Compose with collage and found materials toward a considered surface. Speak clearly and generously in critique. Keep a process journal that records decisions, not just results. Grading weights studio work and growth most heavily, then participation, the journal, and a final portfolio.
