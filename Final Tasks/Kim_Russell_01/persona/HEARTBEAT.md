# Heartbeat: Kim Russell

## Recurring Events

### Daily
- **3:45 AM, baking days (Mon/Wed/Fri)**: Wake. Moka pot, slice of toast with honey, list for the day already written.
- **6:00 to 7:00 AM, non-baking days (Tue/Thu)**: Morning barre followed by yoga at home, Chopin low on the speaker.
- **Mid-morning on baking days**: Boardwalk walk between the bakery kitchen and the studio.
- **9:30 PM, baking nights**: Lights out. Wakeup is 3:45 AM.
- **10:30 PM, non-baking nights**: Lights out. Wakeup is 6:30 AM.

### Weekly
- **Monday, Wednesday, Friday, 4:00 to 11:00 AM**: Russell's Pastries production. Wholesale orders to Cafe Nostalgia, Brownstone Bookshop Cafe, and Heritage Deli delivered by 7:30 AM. Custom orders and prep through 11:00 AM.
- **Monday to Friday, 3:00 to 7:30 PM**: Brighton Ballet Academy classes at the studio, rotating levels.
- **Monday to Friday, 2:30 PM**: Prep window before classes. Studio opens 3:00 PM.
- **Thursday, 7:00 PM**: Dinner with Elena Sorokina in Manhattan. Rotating Caribbean, Japanese, Italian, French.
- **Saturday**: Studio rehearsals and makeup classes 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, then bakery prep for the next week and errands on Nostrand Avenue in the afternoon.
- **Sunday**: Lunch at Irene's at 11:00 AM, visit Della Mason with a pastry box at 3:00 PM, recipe testing or film on the bedroom-wall projector in the evening.

### Monthly
- **Twice monthly**: Physical therapy with Elena's practice for the right shoulder.
- **1st of each month**: Review bakery financials. Wholesale invoices, custom order revenue, Square fees, ingredient costs.
- **15th of each month**: Ballet school tuition follow-ups. Marina maintains the running ledger; Kim signs off on the reminder list.

### Seasonal / Variable
- **November and December**: Heavy holiday custom-order season. Thanksgiving pies, Christmas cake requests, Kwanzaa orders.
- **Late spring (April to June)**: Spring gala build-up. Choreography lock by April, costume orders by May, dress rehearsals through June.

### Annual
- **November (4th Thursday)**: Thanksgiving at Natasha's. Kim brings sweet potato pie and apple pie.
- **December 12**: Winter showcase at Brighton Beach Community Center.
- **December 25**: Christmas at Irene's.
- **December 26**: Kwanzaa observance begins.
- **February 15**: Annual physical with Dr. Vera Orlova at Brighton Beach Medical.
- **February 22**: Kim's birthday.
