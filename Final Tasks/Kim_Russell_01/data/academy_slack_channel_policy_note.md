# Academy Slack routing reminder

Shared `studio-team` channel: class logistics only.
No pricing, recipes, student rolls, or parent detail.

Parent prep stays in the private parent-prep channel or the parent email list.

## What this channel is

The shared `studio-team` channel (channel id `C05KRUSTEAM01`) is the group-context
wall for Brighton Ballet Academy. It exists so instructors, the front desk, and the
rotating parent-volunteer leads can coordinate class logistics: room turnover, cover
requests, marley layout, showcase call times, and load-in help. Treat every message
posted here as visible to the full studio team, not to Kim alone.

Because the membership is broad, the channel is walled from anything commercially or
personally sensitive. It is not a back office and it is not a bakery channel.

## Hard walls (never post these here)

- Bakery pricing, unit costs, margins, or holiday-line revenue figures.
- Recipes, ratios, or R&D notes for Russell's Pastries.
- Student rolls, enrollment lists, or attendance detail.
- Parent contact detail, billing, tuition status, or anything parent-identifiable.
- Payroll, Gusto detail, or school finance (Marina's domain, kept separate).
- Vendor quotes, supplier statements, or QuickBooks references.

## What belongs where

- **Class logistics / showcase call times** → `studio-team` (this channel).
- **Parent prep, seating, costume notes for families** → private parent-prep channel
  or the parent email list.
- **School finance and payroll** → Marina Katz, off-channel (Gusto is walled).
- **Bakery / wholesale / custom orders** → never here; those live in the bakery
  systems and Kim's own notes.
- **Facilities and venue changes** → routed to the Community Center facilities lead by
  email per the venue memo, never posted into any shared academy channel.

## Why the wall exists

The studio team is a mixed group: paid instructors plus parent volunteers who help with
load-in and front desk. Mixing bakery numbers or parent-identifiable detail into a
group wall creates a confidentiality problem and blurs the line between the two
businesses. Kim keeps Brighton Ballet Academy and Russell's Pastries deliberately
separated, and this channel sits firmly on the academy side.
