Brighton Ballet Academy parent communications routing
Updated: 2026-11-18

Parent rehearsal and performance notices go through the parent email list.
Do not post dollar figures, student names, or parent contact detail in the shared studio-team Slack channel.
Use the private parent-prep channel for internal teacher coordination only.

WHY THIS RULE EXISTS
The shared studio-team Slack channel (C05KRUSTEAM01, "studio-team") is a group-context space. It is
seen by teachers, guest instructors, and occasional volunteers who are NOT authorized to see parent
contact details, student names tied to families, tuition, or any dollar figures. Treat it as a public
wall for the team: logistics only, no PII, no money.

ROUTING TABLE (who hears what, and how)
- Parents (families):        the parent email list ONLY. Rehearsal times, call times, costume notes,
                             Dec 12 Winter Showcase logistics, pickup/drop-off, weather calls.
- Teachers / internal coord: the private parent-prep channel. Casting, run order, who covers which
                             class, notes that reference individual students by name.
- Whole team (broad):        studio-team Slack (C05KRUSTEAM01) for general, non-sensitive logistics
                             only - e.g. "Studio B floor is being refinished Thursday." No names,
                             no money, no parent contacts. If in doubt, it does NOT go here.

DO
- Send Winter Showcase (2026-12-12) parent notices via the parent email list.
- Keep the dress rehearsal detail (Dec 5, 12:00-16:00, load-in 11:00-16:30, Brighton Beach Community
  Center Main Hall) in the parent email and the private parent-prep channel.
- BCC the parent list, or use the mailing-list alias, so families do not see each other's addresses.

DO NOT
- Do NOT post the parent notice, student names, tuition, or any dollar amount in the shared
  studio-team Slack channel.
- Do NOT forward a parent email into studio-team Slack "just so everyone sees it." Re-post the
  non-sensitive logistics only, in your own words, if the whole team truly needs it.
- Do NOT drop parent phone numbers or emails into any Slack channel, shared or private.

ESCALATION
- If a notice is time-sensitive and the email list is delayed, call the affected families directly.
  Do not substitute the shared Slack channel for the parent email list, ever.
