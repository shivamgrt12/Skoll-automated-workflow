# Memory: Anita Patel

## Personal Profile

Anita James Patel is second-generation Indian-American, daughter of a father who arrived from northern India at twenty-two and built a quiet, careful life in Columbus, Ohio. She holds a B.F.A. in Visual Communication Design from Buckeye School of Art and Design, conferred 2009. She speaks English natively and understands more Hindi than she speaks, a gap that surfaces as a small grief when her parents call. She is single in the legal sense and partnered in the daily one, sixteen months in with Sylvie Martens. A previous three-year relationship ended in 2024, mutually. No children.

She moves between two registers without always meaning to: a precise, lightly ironic professional voice at Meridian, and a softer, more deferential cadence the moment her mother is on the line. The philosophical thread underneath everything is that good care is mostly the removal of friction. Anticipate the obstacle, clear it quietly, let the other person move on with their day. She values deliberation as a form of respect, and she has begun to notice when deliberation is really avoidance wearing better clothes. She is loyal in showing up rather than declaring. The open question she circles, and circles, is where her responsibility for other people's comfort actually ends.

She is the friend who ends up planning things, who knew which restaurant could seat eight without anyone shouting, who quietly introduced the two people who later became close. Bringing people together is one of the few things she does without second-guessing. And then it ends, and she needs the apartment to herself with an intensity that has surprised more than one person.

## Work & Projects

Anita is a senior UX designer at Meridian UX Group, a mid-size Indianapolis design consultancy of about forty-five employees. Career timeline: B.F.A. in Visual Communication Design from Buckeye School of Art and Design (Columbus, Ohio), conferred 2009. Three years as a junior visual designer at Pivot & Press, a small Columbus brand agency, from June 2009 to August 2012. Five years at Vector Health Systems in Columbus from September 2012 to June 2017, where she pivoted from print and visual into product UX on an early patient-facing platform. Moved to Indianapolis in July 2017 for a UX-designer role at Centerline SaaS through October 2021. Took a three-month break at the end of 2021 to manage a Crohn's flare and reset. Joined Meridian UX Group in January 2022, promoted to senior in October 2024. Her focus is enterprise SaaS design for healthcare and fintech clients. She currently leads a redesign of a patient portal for a major healthcare client, with delivery scheduled for mid-December 2026. Her schedule is Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM Eastern, hybrid with in-office days Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday, remote on Monday and Friday. She reports to Lila Fontaine and works closely with junior designers Raj Mehta and Tanya Brooks and UX researcher Cass Leung. Her work email is `apatel@meridianuxgroup.com` and lives only on the work laptop. The patient-portal redesign is the load-bearing project of the second half of 2026. She is treating Q3 as the build phase and Q4 as polish and stakeholder review.
