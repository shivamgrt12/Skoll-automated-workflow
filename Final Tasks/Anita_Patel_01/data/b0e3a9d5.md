# Weeknight favorites (personal notes)

A running list Anita keeps for quick dinners. Nothing work-related.

## Dal makhani (Instant Pot, ~40 min)
- 1 cup whole black urad dal, 1/4 cup rajma, soaked overnight
- Pressure cook 35 min with 4 cups water, salt, 1 tsp baking soda pinch
- Tadka: butter, ginger-garlic, tomato puree, chili, garam masala
- Finish with cream + kasuri methi. Low and slow is the secret.

## Lemon orzo with spinach (20 min)
- Toast orzo in olive oil, cook in broth risotto-style
- Fold in spinach, parmesan, lemon zest + juice, black pepper

## Sheet-pan paneer & peppers (25 min)
- Cube paneer + bell peppers + red onion, toss in tandoori spice + yogurt
- 425°F for 20 min, broil 3 min. Serve with naan.
