# Saved run routes (personal)

Anita's route library for the Tuesday/Thursday morning runs and the occasional weekend long one. Cultural Trail is home base out of Fountain Square. Nothing work-related here.

## Weekday loops (short, before work)

### Fountain Square → Cultural Trail out-and-back
- Distance: 3.1 mi
- Surface: paved trail, well lit
- Notes: default weekday route, gentle grade, plenty of crossings with signals. Start at Prospect St.

### Virginia Ave → Mass Ave loop
- Distance: 4.0 mi
- Surface: paved, some street sections
- Notes: a little more traffic, saves it for lighter-legs days. Coffee at the end if there's time.

## Weekend / longer

### Pleasant Run Greenway
- Distance: 6.2 mi
- Surface: mixed trail and park path
- Notes: quieter, shaded, good when the knees want softer ground. Restrooms at the trailhead.

### Canal Towpath (downtown)
- Distance: 5.0 mi
- Surface: flat gravel and paved
- Notes: flat and scenic, good for an easy zone-2 day. Parking near the White River State Park.

## With Sylvie (walk/jog)

### Broad Ripple → Monon Trail
- Distance: 3.5 mi
- Surface: paved rail-trail
- Notes: meet-in-the-middle when starting from Sylvie's. Ends near the brunch spots.

## Reminders to self
- Zone-2 pace on trail days; save intervals for the treadmill.
- Refill the handheld bottle the night before.
- If it's a flare week, swap the run for the Monon walk and don't push it.
