Why we drill numbers

Saying a date of birth or a phone number out loud is harder than it looks under pressure. We slow it down, choral first, then in pairs, slow to fast, until it sounds natural and not memorized.

For the stronger learners, like Mrs. Porter, the follow-up task is leaving a voicemail to reschedule.
