# Memory: Ian Woodward

## Personal Profile

Ian was born in Providence, Rhode Island, and moved to Maryland at age seven with his mother after his parents separated. Ian is the first in his family to finish college. Anglo-American, native English, conversational Spanish from years of paralegal client exposure, Catholic by upbringing and active practice. Education: Foxglove University High School (2005), AA Paralegal Studies from Montgomery College (2009), BA Legal Studies from UMGC (2018, part-time over nine years while working). Working-class New England identity flavors how he cooks, reads, and treats people. He believes in the immigration system enough to work inside it and has seen enough denials to know it fails people.

Direct supervisor Patricia Rivas (managing attorney). Team of three attorneys, four paralegals, two legal assistants, one receptionist.
