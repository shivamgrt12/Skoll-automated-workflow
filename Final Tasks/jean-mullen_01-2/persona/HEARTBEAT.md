# Heartbeat: Jean Mullen

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- **5:30 AM**: Wake. Meditation, strong filter coffee, and journaling in that order. Non-negotiable.
- **10:30 PM**: Lights out. Chamomile tea at the end signals the work can be set down.

### Weekly

- **Monday**: Personal practice 7:00 to 9:00 AM. Beginners class 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Intermediate class 3:00 to 5:00 PM. Advanced class 5:30 to 7:30 PM. Home by 8:00 PM for dinner and family time.
- **Tuesday**: Morning yoga and personal choreography 6:00 to 9:00 AM. Beginners class 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Afternoon for admin, emails, and costume meetings. Claire Nolan physio session at PhysioActive, Alwarpet. Advanced class 5:30 to 7:30 PM.
- **Wednesday**: Personal practice 7:00 to 9:00 AM. Intermediate class 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Lunch with Fiona most weeks. Afternoon Five Elements choreography at the studio. Advanced rehearsal 5:30 to 7:30 PM.
- **Thursday**: Mentor call with Margaret in Cork 9:00 to 10:30 AM, prep by 8:30 AM. Beginners class 11:00 AM to 12:30 PM. Afternoon for musician coordination and production planning. Advanced class 5:30 to 7:30 PM.
- **Friday**: Personal practice 7:00 to 9:00 AM. Intermediate class 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Emma Walsh private solo-debut coaching 3:00 to 5:00 PM, last session's corrections reviewed by 2:30 PM.
- **Saturday**: Production troupe intensive rehearsal 7:00 to 11:00 AM, studio readiness and music checked by 6:00 AM. Beginners class 11:30 AM to 1:00 PM. Afternoon for household, shopping, and church visit.
- **Sunday**: Mass at St. Patrick's, then family brunch. Afternoon for research, reading, rehearsal video review, and light personal practice. 8:00 PM weekly planning with Brendan covering classes, rehearsals, performance deadlines, and family commitments.

### Monthly

- **1st of each month**: Verify studio rent, home rent, musician retainer, and SIP payments processed.
- **15th of each month**: Review academy tuition payment status. Follow up on pending payments.

### Seasonal / Variable

- **November through January**: Winter festival season. Performance load peaks. Protect recovery windows after every show.
- **February**: Annual trip to Cork. Block the calendar two weeks before for handoff prep.
- **March**: St. Patrick's week. Expect community performance requests. Default to declining unless Riverside Arts Hall asks.
