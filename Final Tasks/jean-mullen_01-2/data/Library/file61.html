About the academy

Founded 2012. Thirty-five dancers, three levels, one stage built every October.

Ceili Mor was built over fourteen years by artistic director Jean Mullen, who moved from Cork to Chennai and made a home for Irish dance inside the city's performing arts life. We hold the grounded footwork of the Munster tradition and the breath of contemporary movement together, and we ask our dancers to rise to both. Every student is met where they are and asked, gently, for a little more.
