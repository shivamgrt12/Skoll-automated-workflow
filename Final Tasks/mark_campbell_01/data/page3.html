My mother's buttermilk biscuits. Made slowly, never rushed, the butter cut in by hand.

Buttermilk Biscuits

Sundaymorning ritual
12biscuits
425degree oven

Cold ingredients and a light hand. The fold and turn is what gives them the layers, so do not overwork the dough.

What you need

  • Three cups flour, kept cold
  • One stick of butter, frozen and grated
  • One and a quarter cups buttermilk
  • A spoon of baking powder and a pinch of salt

The method

  1. Cut the butter into the flour until it looks like coarse meal.
  2. Pour in the buttermilk and bring it together gently.
  3. Fold the dough over itself four times, turning a quarter each time.
  4. Cut straight down, no twist, and bake until the tops go gold.
