# Heartbeat: Jack Singleton

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- **Daily, 4:00 AM ET (April through November)**: Jack wakes and checks the OpenWeather marine forecast and tide for Chesapeake Bay central, wind and wave.
- **Daily, 4:15 AM ET**: Losartan 50mg with breakfast, Gabapentin 300mg first dose, a big breakfast of eggs, scrapple, toast, and black Folgers, then out the door by 4:30 AM.
- **Daily, 1:00 PM ET (April through November)**: Gabapentin 300mg afternoon dose, lunch on the water or back at the dock.
- **Daily, 9:30 PM ET**: Gabapentin 300mg bedtime dose, lights out, up again at 4:00 AM.

### Weekly

- **Monday through Saturday (April through November)**: Out the door by 4:30 AM, at the dock by 5:00 AM, on the water with Tommy, D.J., and Mike shortly after, hauling pots until 1 to 2 PM, then selling the catch dockside or to wholesalers and home by 3:30 PM.
- **Monday through Friday (December through March)**: Off-season pattern of boat maintenance, gear repair, and pot rebuilding at the marina, equipment upgrades, and any cold-weather contract work Pete surfaces, home for the kids by 3:30 PM.
- **Tuesday**: Connor's U-10 lacrosse practice from 5:30 to 7:00 PM in spring (March through May), where Jack assistant-coaches, then open mic night at Dockside Pub with Kevin Doyle from 7:30 to 10:00 PM with his guitar, a night he keeps clear.
- **Wednesday**: Bridget at yoga from 6:30 to 7:30 PM, her one thing, while Jack handles homework and bedtime for Connor and Siobhan.
- **Thursday**: Connor's U-10 lacrosse practice from 5:30 to 7:00 PM in spring (March through May), Jack assistant-coaching.
- **Friday (December through March)**: Catch-up admin, charter prep for the summer slate, and the occasional homebrew batch in the garage.
- **Saturday**: In spring (March through May), Connor's lacrosse game at 9:00 AM at the Annapolis Recreation fields, with Tommy running the Saturday morning haul on the Bay Runner during the crab-and-lacrosse overlap so Jack can coach; Patrick's varsity game usually lands mid-afternoon; the rest is family time, yard work, and a homebrew batch if energy allows.
- **Sunday**: Drive to Severna Park around 8:00 AM to check on Kathleen, her pill organizer, fridge, and any house repairs, back by 1 PM, then family time at home, a nap, yard work, and Sunday dinner with no screens at the table.

### Monthly

- **1st of each month**: Review crabbing income and charter bookings, pay bills (mortgage, slip, engine loan, insurance), and check pot inventory and bait orders.
- **15th of each month**: Check Kathleen's prescriptions with her pharmacy and make a 529 contribution if cash allows.
- **Biweekly Friday**: Crew payroll runs through Annapolis Payroll Services for Tommy, D.J., and Mike.

### Quarterly

- **Each quarter**: Dr. Robert Kessler back appointment at Cloverdale Health, with Jack working up the pain log the week before.
- **Each quarter**: Review the SEP-IRA balance with Frank DiMaggio (CPA) and reconcile boat operating spend in QuickBooks Self-Employed.

### Seasonal / Variable

- **April 1 through November 30**: Commercial crab season. Three to four trips per week, 300 pots per trip, sold dockside or to wholesalers.
- **June 1 through August 31**: Charter overlap. Three to four charter trips per week booked through Pete Andersen at Chesapeake Charter Connection.
- **November through January**: Chesapeake Bay waterfowl hunting (duck and goose) from the layout boat on the Eastern Shore with Uncle Frank when the weather lines up.
- **December through March**: Off-season boat maintenance at Annapolis City Marina Slip 47, gear repair, pot rebuilding, and the home-brewing rotation in the garage.
- **March through May (spring lacrosse season)**: Connor's U-10 lacrosse practices Tuesday and Thursday, games Saturday mornings, with Patrick's varsity schedule overlapping.

### Annual

- **October 15**: Jack's birthday. Quiet dinner at home with Bridget and the kids.
- **October**: The Singleton-house crabcake cookoff, eight to ten friends and family bringing recipes while the fall crabs are at their fattest.
- **November 18**: Bridget's birthday. Dinner out, usually Cantler's Riverside Inn if she wants crabs.
- **November (Thanksgiving week)**: Family at home with Kathleen and Uncle Frank. Jack does the turkey, Bridget the sides.
- **December 4**: Connor's birthday. Family party at the house.
- **December (mid-month)**: Bay Runner haul-out for winter storage and maintenance at Marshwood Partners.
- **January 22**: Patrick's birthday. He wants something boat-related most years.
- **January**: Annual physical with Dr. Margaret Walsh at Annapolis Internal Medicine, blood pressure and bloodwork.
- **February 12**: Siobhan's birthday. The sailing crowd usually shows up.
- **February**: Maryland Homebrew Competition. Jack enters his "Bay Red Ale" Irish red and a Vienna-style amber lager brewed over the off-season.
- **March 8**: Kathleen's birthday. Drive to Severna Park; Bridget bakes the cake.
- **March**: Annual full-body skin check with Dr. Linda Chen at Annapolis Dermatology.
- **March**: Maryland DNR crab pot registration and tagging, all 900 pots done ahead of the season opener, alongside the pre-opener Bay Runner safety prep with Tommy (Coast Guard documented-vessel check, hull, electronics, safety gear, fire extinguishers).
