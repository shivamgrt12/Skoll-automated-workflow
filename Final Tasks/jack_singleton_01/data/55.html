The Team

Every kid plays, every game.

The Sharks are a U-10 team in the Annapolis Recreation league. The job at this age is simple: clean stick skills on both hands, hustle for ground balls, and learn to love the game. Equal playing time, positive sideline, coach effort over score.

Coach Jack

Assistant coach, third-generation waterman, and Connor's dad. Knows the game from his oldest son's varsity years and runs the ground-ball and transition stations.

The Sharks Code

  • Hustle for every ground ball, the game is won on the floor
  • Pass on both hands, a Shark is never one-handed
  • Cheer your teammates, never the scoreboard
  • Shake every hand after the whistle, win or lose
  • Have fun, this is fourth-grade lacrosse, not the pros
