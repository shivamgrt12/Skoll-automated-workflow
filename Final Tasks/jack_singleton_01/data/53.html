About the Boat

A 42-foot deadrise built for the Bay.

The Bay Runner is the second hull this family has run on the Chesapeake. Captain Jack Singleton holds a USCG Master 100-ton license and has fished this water for thirty years, from the Severn mouth to Thomas Point.

  • Twin diesel deadrise, stable and dry, up to six anglers
  • Rods, reels, tackle, bait, and ice all provided
  • Catch cleaned and bagged at the dock
  • Coast Guard safety gear and a full briefing before lines off
  • Family friendly, light tackle aboard so small anglers can fight a fish
