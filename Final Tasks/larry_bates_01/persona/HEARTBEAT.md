# Heartbeat: Larry Bates

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- **Off-season, 6:30 AM**: Morning check. Review the day's schedule, weather forecast, brewery status, overnight email and message summary.
- **Brewing season (November through March), 4:30 AM**: Morning check. Same scope as off-season plus fermentation sensor digest from Twilio and PagerDuty.
- **Brewing season, 9:00 PM**: Evening fermentation review. Tank-by-tank status, sensor anomalies, next-day brewhouse plan with Greg.

### Weekly

- **Monday, 9:00 AM**: Week start review. Weekly schedule, distributor orders, supply chain status, financial items requiring attention.
- **Tuesday afternoon**: Distributor or supplier visit window. Asheville-local meetings preferred; barley farm visits seasonal.
- **Wednesday, 3:00 PM**: Mid-week production check. Bottling and labeling progress, quality checks, inventory status, hot springs visit if schedule allows.
- **Thursday afternoon**: Barley farmer visit window during harvest months, otherwise flexible for errands and research.
- **Friday, 4:00 PM**: Week wrap. Weekly financial review, production summary, next-week planning, then Farmhouse Tavern dinner with Jake Moreland on the first Friday of each month.
- **Saturday, 7:00 AM**: Morning walk with Hana. Protected time, no work interruptions. Regular route, bakery stop.
- **Sunday, 4:00 PM**: Week preparation. Review upcoming week, brewery maintenance needs, family schedule, light reading or beer study afterward.
