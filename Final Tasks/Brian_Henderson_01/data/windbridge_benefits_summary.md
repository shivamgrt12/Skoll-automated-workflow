## Fertility benefit - the load-bearing line

- **Coverage type** - Windbridge added a Progyny-adjacent fertility benefit rider effective 2025-01-01. Not Progyny itself. Delivered through BCBS-MA with a lifetime dollar cap.
- **Lifetime cap** - **$25,000 per employee lifetime**, applies to fertility diagnostics, monitoring, retrieval, embryo culture, cryopreservation year 1, first transfer, and covered medications.
- **What counts against the cap** - retrieval procedure, monitoring visits, embryo culture, first transfer, PGT-A if diagnostic-billed (rarely), cryopreservation year 1.
- **What does NOT count against the cap** - repeat cryo years, PGT-A when billed as elective, donor gametes (not applicable to us), gestational carrier (not applicable to us).
- **Reuse rule** - lifetime cap. Once cycle 1 exhausts the $25K, cycle 2 is fully out of pocket. That is why the runway projected read credits $25K exactly once against cycle 1 and no further.
- **Sarah's coverage** - Windbridge treats Sarah as qualified domestic partner and she is on the medical PPO for non-fertility care, but fertility benefits are **employee only** under the rider. Her ACA marketplace plan has **zero fertility coverage** (confirmed healthcare.gov MA 2026 plan summary, summer 2026 review). This is why Sarah's ACA line in the household budget is $380 monthly and carries no fertility offset.
