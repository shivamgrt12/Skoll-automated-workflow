## 1. Tier Structure

The Great Lakes Regional Fuel Alliance is a multi-member distributor buying group. Members qualify for tiered rebates on wholesale fuel based on aggregate quarterly volume across all member stores. Higher tiers receive per-gallon rebates on regular_87 and premium_93 grades.

| Tier | Volume Range (gallons/quarter) | Rebate: Regular 87 | Rebate: Premium 93 | Additional Terms |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tier C | 0 - 250,000 | Standard pricing | Standard pricing | Base membership |
| Tier B | 250,001 - 479,999 | 2.5 cents/gal rebate | Standard pricing | Rebate paid at quarter-end |
| **Tier A** | **480,000+** | **4.5 cents/gal rebate** | **1.5 cents/gal rebate** | Rebate paid at quarter-end + priority allocation on tight-supply weeks |

## 5. Contract Terms

- **Term:** Annual, auto-renews October 1 unless notice given 90 days prior
- **Volume commitment:** minimum 200,000 gallons/quarter aggregate
- **Rebate payment:** 30 days after quarter-end
- **Rebate withholding:** on any store with unpaid invoice at quarter-end
- **Cancellation:** 30-day notice; no early termination fee
- **Exclusive dealing:** none required; members may source outside distributor
- **Priority allocation clause (Tier A only):** priority fuel allocation on tight-supply weeks (relevant during lake-effect surge and Great Lakes shipping disruptions)
