## 6. Relevant Michigan State Precedent

- **Michigan Small Business Development grants** ($25,000 per applicant, 25% match required, applicants must be under $3M annual revenue, Oct 15-Nov 30 application window annually). Chamber Q4 grant list flags this as complementary to Springfield's proposed dev fund.
- **Michigan Retail Modernization Credit** (rolling application, 15% capital improvement credit, aligns with Springfield's proposed capital improvement credit)
- **Michigan Personal Property Tax Reform Act 2013** (PA 328) - abatement schedule framework used as basis for Springfield's proposed personal property abatement
- **Springfield Downtown Renaissance grant** (SBED-2026-08 comparable, $10,000 per applicant, 50% match, downtown DDA-boundary only) - explicitly not eligible for Heidi's stores
