# Tools: Heidi Hart

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Email, Calendar & Files

- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Primary inbox for council correspondence, vendor orders, and community organization email.
- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Council meetings, store visits, family events, and medical appointments, all kept in Eastern Time.
- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Council documents, store financial summaries, and community event planning files.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): The city issues council members an Outlook account; anything substantive gets pulled into her main inbox.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Shared folder with Rick Morrison for the commercial real estate deals they co-invest in.
- **Box** (`box-api`): The city clerk distributes council meeting packets through Box; she reviews agendas and minutes ahead of every session.

#### Store Operations & Logistics

- **Square** (`square-api`): Registers and card processing at all three stores. Daily sales summaries, Greenfield first.
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Nathan keeps the stores' books in it; Heidi reviews the monthly statements.
- **Amazon Seller** (`amazon-seller-api`): Owen's small experiment listing slow-moving convenience inventory online. Heidi watches the margins.
- **Etsy** (`etsy-api`): Nadia's debate team fundraiser crafts go up seasonally; Heidi keeps the shop in good standing.
- **Instacart** (`instacart-api`): Emergency bulk supply runs when a vendor shorts a delivery.
- **DoorDash** (`doordash-api`): Delivery trial for the Greenfield deli counter. Volume is small, but she tracks it weekly.
- **WooCommerce** (`woocommerce-api`): Online order form for Greenfield deli catering trays, bolted onto the stores' simple website.
- **BigCommerce** (`bigcommerce-api`): Sells Greenfield deli gift baskets online; orders peak around the holidays and she reviews the weekly tally.
- **Shippo** (`shippo-api`): Shipping labels for parcels from the stores' Amazon listings and the Etsy fundraiser shop.
- **FedEx** (`fedex-api`): Tracking for inbound store equipment and parts shipments.
- **UPS** (`ups-api`): Vendor shipments and the occasional return of faulty store equipment.

#### Money, Banking & Investments

- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Processes small-dollar donations on her council campaign page. Every transaction gets logged for disclosure.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Vendor payments and Civic Association fundraiser reimbursements processed monthly.
- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Links the Flagstone Credit Union accounts for balance checks ahead of the monthly reviews.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Rick Morrison's bookkeeper runs their co-owned real estate entities on Xero; Heidi reviews the entity statements quarterly.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Maintains her watchlist of fuel and retail tickers and checks it before wholesale contract pricing talks.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Her own small crypto position, opened to understand what Owen keeps talking about; she checks the balance monthly.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Pulls weekly price snapshots of the coins Owen follows so dinner-table crypto talk starts from real numbers.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Runs a monthly fee and exchange-rate comparison against Coinbase that she uses to show Owen how costs eat small trades.

#### Council, Civic & Constituent Outreach

- **Mailchimp** (`mailchimp-api`): Constituent newsletter list of about 340 contacts. Layla drafts; Heidi approves every send.
- **SendGrid** (`sendgrid-api`): Delivery backbone behind the council website's contact form notifications.
- **Mailgun** (`mailgun-api`): Delivers the Civic Association's mailing list sends; every forum notice and voter drive announcement goes out through it.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Text reminders for constituent office hours and community event volunteers.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Vendor contracts, store lease renewals, and council disclosure forms.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Constituent surveys, most recently on the Warren Road zoning proposal.
- **Eventbrite** (`eventbrite-api`): RSVPs for Civic Association forums, the fall fundraiser, and community festival events.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Booking slots for constituent office hours at the Cedarbrook Community Center.

#### Calls, Chats & Meetings

- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Voice calls and group messaging with the wider family; Derek is the most frequent thread.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Two wholesale vendors insist on it; quick order confirmations only.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Small workspace for the three stores; Sam Abbott posts shift coverage and delivery notes there.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Council committee calls run on Teams; she joins from the Dell laptop.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): Kyle's soccer league parents coordinate carpools on a server; Heidi reads more than she posts.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Video calls with Margaret and her sister, plus the occasional Chamber of Commerce webinar.
- **Intercom** (`intercom-api`): Chat widget on the council website. Layla triages; policy questions get flagged to Heidi.

#### Public Presence & Media

- **WordPress** (`wordpress-api`): The council website, where Layla publishes the statements Heidi dictates.
- **Webflow** (`webflow-api`): The Civic Association's rebuilt site; Heidi updates event pages and meeting announcements directly.
- **Instagram** (`instagram-api`): Store accounts where Layla posts weekly specials and deli features; Heidi approves the captions.
- **Twitter** (`twitter-api`): Layla posts approved council statements there, and Heidi reviews local reaction threads after contentious votes.
- **LinkedIn** (`linkedin-api`): She manages Chamber of Commerce connections there and posts the stores' hiring notices.
- **Pinterest** (`pinterest-api`): Her grilling and baking boards; she saves ideas for weekend cookouts and the holiday table.
- **Reddit** (`reddit-api`): She follows the small business and convenience store subreddits for fuel margin chatter; comment threads about her council work stay unread.
- **YouTube** (`youtube-api`): The city posts council meeting recordings there; also Premier League highlights with Kyle.
- **Twitch** (`twitch-api`): Owen streams soccer video games. She checks in just enough to know what to ask at dinner.
- **Vimeo** (`vimeo-api`): Hosts the Civic Association's community forum recordings.

#### Around Town & Travel

- **Google Maps** (`google-maps-api`): Routes between the three stores, vendor delivery timing, and drives to Detroit or Chicago.
- **Yelp** (`yelp-api`): Reviews of all three stores. She reads every one and answers the fair ones.
- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Morning forecasts; snow moves fuel and salt sales, and weekends decide grilling plans.
- **Uber** (`uber-api`): Books rides home for store employees stranded without a car after a shift; the store covers the fare.
- **Airbnb** (`airbnb-api`): Quiet rental houses for the family's off-season Lake Michigan trips.
- **Amadeus** (`amadeus-api`): Tracks fares for the trip to take Margaret back to the ancestral village; she checks routes every few months.
- **Zillow** (`zillow-api`): Commercial listings she tracks with Rick Morrison, plus an eye on east Springfield home values.
- **Ticketmaster** (`ticketmaster-api`): USWNT match tickets when the team comes through Chicago.

#### Planning Boards & Notes

- **Notion** (`notion-api`): The operations binder gone digital: vendor terms, store procedures, and council issue notes.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Her council meeting notes, migrated from the paper binder and linked by ordinance and precinct.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): Product mix and inventory experiments tracked across the three locations.
- **Trello** (`trello-api`): Board for the Springfield Community Festival planning committee.
- **Asana** (`asana-api`): Task list for the Warren Road zoning fight, organizing precedents, scheduling witnesses, and tracking action items for the hearing.
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): The Civic Association board plans its fall fundraiser here; Heidi tracks tasks and deadlines alongside the rest of the board.
- **Linear** (`linear-api`): The point-of-sale vendor tracks feature requests in Linear; she votes on fuel reporting improvements and follows the release notes.
- **Jira** (`jira-api`): She files page update requests for the council website and checks the contractor's progress on each ticket.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): The Chamber of Commerce keeps its small business resource wiki here; she pulls grant deadlines and permit checklists from it.
- **Figma** (`figma-api`): Mockups for store signage and the newsletter masthead. Layla drives; Heidi comments.

#### Staffing, Vendors & Customer Service

- **BambooHR** (`bamboohr-api`): Personnel files, time off, and reviews for the 14 employees across the stores.
- **Gusto** (`gusto-api`): Biweekly payroll for all three stores, reconciled against the books by Nathan.
- **Greenhouse** (`greenhouse-api`): Hiring pipeline when a store needs a new clerk. Local teenagers get first look.
- **HubSpot** (`hubspot-api`): Light CRM for vendor contacts and the wholesale supplier comparison she keeps current.
- **Salesforce** (`salesforce-api`): The fuel distributor's portal runs on it; she logs in to check contract pricing tiers.
- **Zendesk** (`zendesk-api`): Customer feedback inbox for the stores, mostly receipt questions and lottery disputes.
- **Freshdesk** (`freshdesk-api`): Runs the Civic Association's volunteer question queue; tickets spike before the fall fundraiser and festival weekends.
- **ServiceNow** (`servicenow-api`): City hall's internal request system; council members file facility and records tickets there.
- **Klaviyo** (`klaviyo-api`): Sends the stores' loyalty email to regulars; the deli counter coupon goes out twice a month.
- **ActiveCampaign** (`activecampaign-api`): Sends the Civic Association's drip reminder sequences to event volunteers in the weeks before each forum and fundraiser.

#### Site Analytics & Content

- **Google Analytics** (`google-analytics-api`): Traffic on the council website, checked after each newsletter lands.
- **Mixpanel** (`mixpanel-api`): The website contractor's event tracking on the council site; she reads the monthly summary.
- **Amplitude** (`amplitude-api`): Tracks how constituents move through the council site's issue pages; she reviews monthly which ordinances get read to the end.
- **Segment** (`segment-api`): Pipes every council site form submission and survey response into Mailchimp and the contractor's analytics tools.
- **PostHog** (`posthog-api`): Tracks usage of the self-checkout pilot at Greenfield Fuel & Market.
- **Algolia** (`algolia-api`): Powers search on the Chamber's resource site she uses for grant and permit lookups.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): The city communications office manages press releases here; she reviews and approves her statements before they go live.

#### IT, Security & Uptime

- **Okta** (`okta-api`): Single sign-on for the city's systems; her council accounts authenticate through it.
- **Cloudflare** (`cloudflare-api`): Fronts the council website; she reviews the uptime and traffic spike reports after each newsletter send.
- **GitHub** (`github-api`): She files issues in the contractor's council website repository and follows each fix through to deploy.
- **GitLab** (`gitlab-api`): City IT publishes its public records tooling there; she pulls the records request templates whenever constituents ask for documents.
- **Sentry** (`sentry-api`): Error alerts from the council site's contact form, forwarded to the contractor.
- **Datadog** (`datadog-api`): The point-of-sale vendor's status dashboards; first stop during a register outage.
- **PagerDuty** (`pagerduty-api`): After-hours alerts when a store's cameras or registers go down. Routes to Heidi, then Sam.
- **Kubernetes** (`kubernetes-api`): The register vendor hosts store reporting on a cluster; she pulls its status during outages to tell Sam how long registers will be down.

#### Family, Health & Downtime

- **Ring** (`ring-api`): Live camera feeds, and the first thing she checks after any overnight alert.
- **Google Classroom** (`google-classroom-api`): Kyle's assignments and Nadia's school announcements, checked weekly.
- **MyFitnessPal** (`myfitnesspal-api`): Loose food logging toward the weight goal from her last physical. No calorie lectures.
- **Strava** (`strava-api`): Logs her evening walks. Streaks matter more than pace.
- **Spotify** (`spotify-api`): Classic rock, country, and 90s pop playlists for the drives between store locations.
- **OpenLibrary** (`openlibrary-api`): Source for Michigan local history and small business titles on her reading list.
- **NASA** (`nasa-api`): Satellite imagery when lake effect snow threatens delivery schedules; also Kyle's science homework rabbit holes.
- **TMDB** (`tmdb-api`): Helps pick the family movie when a Friday night actually opens up.

#### Not Connected

- Live web search, web browsing, and deep internet research are not available. The agent works only from connected services and stored memory.
- The council Facebook page is administered by Layla; content is drafted for Heidi's sign-off, with no direct page access from this workspace.
- City of Springfield closed-session records and constituent casework databases are off limits; work from what Heidi shares directly.
- The fuel supplier's on-site ordering terminal is vendor-managed hardware with no remote access.
- Nathan's personal accounts and the children's private accounts stay outside this workspace.
