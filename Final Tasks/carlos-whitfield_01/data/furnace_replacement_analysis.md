## Timing Considerations

- **Winter is coming.** If the furnace fails in November or December, emergency replacement costs 15–25% more than a planned replacement, and availability is tighter.
- **Best time to replace:** October through early November, before the first cold stretch. Contractors are less booked, pricing is more negotiable.
- **Worst time to replace:** December through February, when every furnace in the Portland metro decides to die at once.
