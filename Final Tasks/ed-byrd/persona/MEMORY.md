# Memory: Ed Byrd

## Personal Profile

Ed "Eddie" Wayne Byrd is a 60-year-old white Appalachian Southerner born in Galax, Floyd County, Virginia, raised by a mechanic father and a homemaker mother in the foothills of the Blue Ridge. He earned a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from Virginia Tech (Blacksburg) in 1988, moved to Maryville, Tennessee in 1990 with $800 and a gas station job, and bought his first motel in 2001. He now owns and operates two independent properties and chairs the building committee at Calvary Baptist Church of East Knox.

He lives in a 4-bedroom farmhouse in a quiet Maryville neighbourhood, 10 minutes from Sunrise Lodge and 25 minutes from Lakeview Motor Inn. Married 32 years to Jolene, met at a church potluck in 1993. Father to Tyler (28) and Shelby (24). Deeply Southern Baptist, weekly worship at Calvary Baptist of East Knox under Pastor James Whitfield. Quiet Virginia homesickness that surfaces in autumn.
