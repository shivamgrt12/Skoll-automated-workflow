## Recurring Events

### Daily

- 5:00 AM wake naturally, black coffee from the standard drip machine before anyone else in the house is up.
- 5:30 AM 45-minute walk through the Maryville neighbourhood, same route past the Baptist church and back through residential streets.
- 5:45 AM FaceTime call to Dorothy Mae in Galax, Wayne usually assisting.
- 6:30 AM property walkthrough at Sunrise Lodge, check overnight issues and start the day's vendor calls.

- 10:00 PM bed after evening reading, occasional Bible devotional, 6 hours sleep is the floor.

### Weekly
