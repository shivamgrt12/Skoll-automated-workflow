# Heartbeat: David Porter

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- 3:30 AM ET: alarm and out the door by 3:45 AM. The early-morning rhythm is the floor of his week.
- 9:00 PM ET: lights down, bedtime. Sleep hygiene is bake-day insurance.
- 3:00 PM ET: phone call to Margaret. Daily check-in; missing it is not negotiable.
- 7:30 AM ET: Ryan's morning commute call from Boston.

### Weekly

- Monday: bakery closed. 9:00 AM coffee and errands with Margaret in Silver Lake. 1:00 PM to 3:00 PM watercolor class at Rhode Island Arts Conservatory Continuing Education. Evening at the herb garden when weather holds.
- Tuesday: bakery prep 3:45 AM, open 6:00 AM to 5:00 PM, closing tasks until 5:30 PM. Custom-order and business block 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. 3:00 AM Twilio reminder to Jake for oven temperature checks.
- Wednesday: bakery 3:45 AM to 5:30 PM. Wholesale delivery prep for the four restaurant accounts and the two coffee shops. 2:00 PM coffee with Karen at Federal Hill Flowers two doors down.
- Thursday: bakery 3:45 AM to 5:30 PM. 2:00 PM Twilio reminder to Sofia to confirm weekend catering orders. Every three weeks, 3:00 PM Thursday chiropractor session with Dr. Frank Newman.
- Friday: bakery 3:45 AM to 5:30 PM. Wedding cake decorating block 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM for Saturday pickups and deliveries. Calendly tasting consultation slots open 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM for brides and event planners.
- Saturday: 6:30 AM farmers market run (Federal Hill Winter Market or East Side Saturday Market). David arrives at the bakery around 8:00 AM after Jake handles morning prep and the 6:00 AM open. Wedding cake deliveries by 3:00 PM. Occasional 7:30 PM jazz at Providence Lyric Theater.
- Sunday: 8:00 AM service at Grace Community Church with Pastor Robert Harris. David arrives at the bakery around 9:30 AM after Jake handles morning prep and the 6:00 AM open. 4:00 PM family dinner at home, cooking for eight to twelve people, dessert from the bakery, wraps around 8:00 PM.

### Monthly

- 1st of the month: bakery financial review and P&L update on Google Sheets with Russell & Associates' digest.
- 15th of the month: Emily's tuition support payment, $1,200 to Bay Port University School of Law.
- Third Tuesday of the month: Margaret's monthly check-in with Dr. Amara Patel; David drives.
- Last Sunday of the month: bakery wholesale account reconciliation with Sofia ahead of the new monthly invoices.

### Quarterly

- January 15, April 15, June 15, and September 15: estimated quarterly tax payment to IRS and Rhode Island Division of Taxation, around $3,800 each.
- Quarterly portfolio review with Paul Henderson at Crestfield Financial; David emails the averaged Bitcoin reference price the night before.

### Annual

- January: annual physical with Dr. Amara Patel.
- March and October: dental cleanings with Dr. Anthony Grant on Federal Hill (the 6-month cadence shifted off May after the school-pickup era ended).
- October: business insurance renewal with Hartford.
- October 15: Sarah's birthday. Family dinner tradition at Porter's Table on Federal Hill.
- November: Christmas baking prep month begins (gingerbread, cinnamon rolls, sugar cookies, holiday cookie boxes).
- November 14: Ryan's birthday.
- November 23: Margaret's birthday, red velvet cake.
- December 5: David's birthday.
- December 18: Emily's birthday.
