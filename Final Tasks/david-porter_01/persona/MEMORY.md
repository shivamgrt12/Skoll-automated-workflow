# Memory: David Porter

## Personal Profile

- **Full name**: David Porter.
- **Occupation**: Pastry Chef and Owner, Porter's Bakehouse, Federal Hill, Providence (American pastries, custom cakes, artisan breads).
- **Education**: Associate's in Baking and Pastry Arts, Northeast Pastry Academy, Hyde Park, NY (1995).
- **Languages**: English. Working French for shop signage and reading regional technique manuals after the Normandy trip.
- **Marital status**: Widower. Sarah Porter passed in 2020 (heart attack at 50). Co-founded Porter's Bakehouse with him in 2010.
- **Heritage**: American. Federal Hill local culture (Italian-American neighborhood), Grace Community Church parish, grandmother's American baking lineage.
