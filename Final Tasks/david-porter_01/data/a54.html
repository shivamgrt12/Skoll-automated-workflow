The BakerDavid, the deck oven, and 30 years on a floor.

David Porter opened Porter's Bakehouse on Atwells Avenue in 2010 with his late wife, Sarah. Trained at Northeast Pastry Academy, sharpened in two French regional study trips, and grounded in a grandmother's recipe collection digitized one Sunday afternoon at a time.

The bakery serves Federal Hill before sunrise and ships holiday cookies as far as the East Coast Amazon Handmade list takes them. The deck oven is imported from France. The buttercream is American. The flowers on every wedding cake come from Karen Mitchell's shop two doors down.
