Coral Recovery Analysis

Wrightsville Sound nearshore hard bottom, biweekly transect surveys
Prepared by Darren Mitchell Cash, advised by Professor Karen Whitfield, Oakridge University. Period October 2025 to February 2026.

Summary

Across the first twenty surveys at four sites, the healthy share of colonies rose at three of four sites as the water cooled from the 2024 warm water event. Bleached counts fell at all four sites between the October and February rounds. The deeper, clearer sites held more colonies overall, while the shallow sites added new recruits late in the winter.
