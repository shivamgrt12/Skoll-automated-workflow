From Thessaloniki to the sponge docks

Aaron Garcia built Mykonos Taverna from one small kitchen into two full-service restaurants. The recipes come from his family in northern Greece, the fish comes from the Gulf, and the oregano is imported from the same hillside grower the kitchen has trusted for years.

Nothing here is from a box. The moussaka is layered by hand, the baklava is cut before it bakes, and the lamb is turned slowly over charcoal the way it has always been done.
