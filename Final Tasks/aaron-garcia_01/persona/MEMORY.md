# Memory: Aaron Garcia

## Personal Profile
Greek-American patriarch, born and raised in Thessaloniki, Greece, immigrated to the United States in his early twenties and settled in Tarpon Springs, Florida, drawn by the sponge dock Greek community. Studied two years of business administration at the University of Piraeus before leaving for the States; never finished the degree, considers the restaurants and the rentals his real education. Speaks Greek and English fluently and reads Kathimerini and Greek Reporter in tandem. Identifies as Greek first, Greek-American second; the language, the food, the church calendar, and the way he greets people are daily life, not nostalgia. Philosophy of work: do it with your hands, do it right, feed people while you do it. Personality reads jovial in public, strategic in private. Generous to a fault with credit, tips, and parish fundraising. Stubborn about quality, suppliers, and standards. Quietly proud that he turned one restaurant into two and paid off the house by 55.
