The Evening

Dinner under saguaros, a string trio, and the truth of the work

Doors open at 5:30 PM, dinner is plated at 6:30, and the program begins as the sky tips toward indigo. Diana Ochoa opens. Two survivors who have come back to volunteer share what the empowerment group made possible. Randy Holt closes with a few words about the first-Wednesday legal clinic, and the night ends with the live auction and dessert under the string lights.

All gifts stay in southern Arizona. The shelter's secure address is kept private for resident safety; the gala is held off-site at Cottonwood Bend.
