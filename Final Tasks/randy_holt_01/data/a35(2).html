A few honest rules

Written so I do not have to argue with myself at 5:45 AM.

  1. If the foot grumbles two days in a row, the next run is a walk. The marathon is in December; the desk is in court on Monday.
  2. Sunday is family. Walk, do not run. Barbara sets the pace.
  3. No new shoes after October. Break-in injuries land worst on race day.
  4. Trial weeks override training weeks. Move long runs to the next quiet Saturday.
