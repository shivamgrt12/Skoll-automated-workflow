What I do

Practice areas

Indigent felony defence is the work. Everything else circles back to it.

Day Job

Felony defence

Assault, DUI, drug possession and distribution, domestic violence defence. Trial work, motion practice, plea negotiation, sentencing advocacy.

Doctrine

Sixth Amendment

Right to counsel, confrontation, speedy trial, effective assistance. I teach the doctrine to baby attorneys at the office and to judges who should know better.

Volunteer

Survivor advocacy

Trauma-informed support for shelter residents navigating orders of protection, custody, and divorce questions. Process, not legal advice.
