# Memory: Randy Holt

## Personal Profile

- **DOB**: November 18, 1979
- **Heritage**: third-generation Tucsonan, born and raised in Barrio Viejo where Barbara still lives.
- **Education**: BA in Political Science from Mesquite Valley University (2002), JD from Sonoran Desert School of Law (2005).
- **Bar admission**: admitted to the Arizona State Bar in 2006 (Bar No. 026481).
- **Faith**: Catholic by upbringing and habit, attends Mass at the historic mission most Sundays with Barbara.
- **Family status**: divorced from Robert Holt in 2018, parenting Tyler full-time at the Sam Hughes house.
- **Disposition**: lives at the intersection of a brutal felony docket and a volunteer life that gives back.

## Work & Projects

- **Current role**: Senior Public Defender at Pima County Office of the Legal Defender since 2014, with the office since 2007.
- **Salary**: $78,000 per year from the PD office.
- **Caseload**: 35-40 active felony files including assault, DUI, drug possession, distribution, and domestic violence defence.
- **Team**: works with paralegal Rosa Maldonado and shares investigator Frank Dunbar with two other attorneys.
- **Active case**: defending Alejandro Vega on a felony assault charge, complicated self-defence claim, trial set spring 2027.
- **Conviction**: believes fiercely in the Sixth Amendment and bristles at the "helping criminals" framing.
- **Volunteer role**: Program Coordinator at Desert Bloom Women's Shelter since 2017, eight to ten hours per week, unpaid.
- **Shelter duties**: runs Tuesday and Thursday empowerment groups, coordinates the first-Wednesday pro bono legal clinic, trains new volunteers.
- **Shelter leadership**: Director Diana Ochoa recruited him nine years ago, close working relationship.
- **Current focus**: organising Desert Bloom Under the Stars fundraising gala for September 2026 at a Tucson guest ranch.
