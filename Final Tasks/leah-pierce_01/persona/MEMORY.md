# Memory: Leah Pierce

## Personal Profile

Leah Marie Pierce was born and raised in Decatur, Alabama, and has no interest in apologizing for small-town roots or leaving them. She likes knowing the hardware-store cashier and the barista who runs the coffee shop by name. She graduated Austin High School in 2017, completed her IBEW Local 558 apprenticeship in 2021, and earned her State of Alabama journeyman electrician license that year. Native English speaker. Single and in a committed relationship with Kyle Prescott.

Her working philosophy is hands over books. She trusts physical competence and says she learned more pulling wire for six months than from any classroom, without dismissing education outright. She is restless without a project, stubborn about self-reliance to a fault, quietly loyal, and uses dry humor to connect. Even-keeled by nature, she gets irritable mainly when she has nothing to build or fix.

## Work & Projects

Journeyman Electrician at Cressman Electric LLC, a roughly 12-employee residential and light-commercial shop in Decatur. A journeyman since 2021, she ran her IBEW Local 558 apprenticeship from 2017 to 2021 and has been with Cressman for five years. Responsibilities cover residential wiring on new construction and remodels, panel upgrades, outlet and fixture installs, and troubleshooting, plus help on light-commercial jobs like offices and small retail. Key contacts are Hank Cressman (owner), Travis Meeks (coworker), and Donna Cressman (invoicing). Work communication is text and phone with Hank, with no work email. She holds an Alabama journeyman license that she keeps current through periodic continuing education.

## Health & Wellness

Chronic lower-back pain since around age 23, from repetitive heavy lifting and crawl-space work. It is intermittent, flaring two or three times a month after physically demanding jobs, and she manages it with ibuprofen, inconsistent stretching, and a heating pad. Dr. Pham recommended physical therapy; she went to four sessions, then stopped. She overuses ibuprofen, per Dr. Pham, and takes a daily multivitamin when she remembers. Primary care is Dr. Kevin Pham at Decatur Family Medicine; dental is Dr. Amy Chen at Decatur Dental Associates. Vision is fine, last exam 2024, no glasses. Exercise is mostly the physical job, plus occasional apartment yoga on Corey's mat and resistance bands and rec-center yoga about twice a month. She is in bed by 10:30 PM on weeknights with a 5:30 AM alarm, and sleeps until 8 or 9 on weekends unless her back flares. No diagnosed mental-health conditions; she is even-keeled and talks to Kyle when something is bothering her. No dietary restrictions and not picky; she eats what is available.

In the off-season she watches YouTube truck builds and home-renovation videos.
