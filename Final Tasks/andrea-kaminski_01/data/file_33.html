About

A short version

I am a marketing analyst at a mid size analytics firm in Columbus, where my core scope is campaign performance for mid market B2B clients. The job is dashboards, written reports, and standing in front of client stakeholders to explain what the quarter actually means.

Cleveland State gave me the marketing degree in 2017. A Google Analytics certification followed in 2021. The through line has always been the same: I trust numbers, ranges, and side by side comparisons more than I trust a good story about them.

Outside the spreadsheets, I run three mornings a week on the Alum Creek trail, cook a lot of sheet pan dinners, and read a steady one to two books a month. I am rebuilding a life that answers to my own rules, and the running is where most of the thinking gets done.
