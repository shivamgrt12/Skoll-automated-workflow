UA LOCAL 51 APPRENTICE REFERENCE - INTERNAL TEMPLATE
====================================================

Prepared by: Jessica Murray, RI Master Plumber MP-20-0847, RI GC GC-04-2391
Business:    Murray Plumbing and HVAC LLC, Elmhurst Providence
Purpose:     Reference letter template used when a UA Local 51 apprentice
             on Murray Plumbing crew moves to journeyman status or
             transfers to another shop. Kept in the shop binder and mirrored
             on the shared Box folder. Fill in the bracket fields per
             apprentice, then print on Murray Plumbing letterhead.

Background statement (paste at top of every letter):
----------------------------------------------------
Jessica Murray served as a UA Local 51 apprentice from 1996 through 2000,
sat for the RI journeyman exam in 2000, and holds RI Master Plumber license
MP-20-0847. She is qualified to speak to apprentice performance on rough-in,
service, and boiler work. She is not a member of the Local 51 training
committee and cannot sign competency exams; those go to the UA training
office directly.

Filing rules:
-------------
  1. Every issued letter is scanned and stored in Box under
     Crew Safety / Apprentice References / [YEAR] / [APPRENTICE NAME].
  2. Do not send the letter to the client, only to the UA training office
     or the receiving shop.
  3. If pulling hours for the letter, pull from the Airtable clock-in log,
     not from a summary sheet.
  4. Refresh the shop binder copy at year-end.
