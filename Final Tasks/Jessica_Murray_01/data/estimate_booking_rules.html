1. When estimates can be booked

Weekday late afternoon. Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday accept slots 16:00 to 19:00. Thursday and Friday accept slots 16:00 to 18:00.
Sunday is blocked all day. Sunday morning is Dorothy brunch, leave the shop by 10:40.
Thursday after 18:00 is blocked. Sophia LaSalle Academy family time.
Saturday morning is emergency only, no scheduled estimates.

3. Location and drive-time rules

Estimate visits inside a 25 minute drive from the Elmhurst shop are booked at face value. Anything farther gets a manual review before confirming. Do not stack two out-of-town estimates back to back.

4. Deposit and card link

Estimate visit itself is free. If the client signs, deposit invoice goes out via Stripe (product prod_estdep, Estimate Deposit $500). Card link expires in 5 business days.

5. Confirmations

  • SendGrid template d-confirm sends the appointment card at book time.
  • Twilio reminder text goes out the day before at 09:00, from the Job Reminders number (+1 401-555-0188).
  • Google Maps drive-time is precomputed and embedded in the confirmation.

6. Cancellations and reschedules

Clients may reschedule up to 12 hours before the slot without penalty. Inside 12 hours, offer the next available weekday afternoon and log the shift on the crew schedule board.

7. Reconciliation reminder

If a client asks whether an old estimate is still valid, check Baxter's and Atlantic current pricing before quoting. The Notion supplier page has not been refreshed since April 2026 and cannot be trusted for copper. Use the Atlantic statement or a fresh counter quote.
