# Memory: Carlos Reed

## Personal Profile

Carlos Reed is an American Black man raised in a tight, churchgoing family that moved from Columbus, Ohio to suburban Maryland when he was a small child. Cultural inheritance shows in specific places: the food, the gospel music, prayer cadence at the dinner table, deference to elders that he is translating into a parenting philosophy he can live with.

BS in Computer Science from the University of Maryland (2015). MS in Cybersecurity from Johns Hopkins (2018). CISSP passed in 2022, second renewal cycle. OSCP targeted Q3 2027 after paternity leave.

He thinks in attack surface, blast radius, and recovery time, and metabolizes large things by breaking them into smaller, knowable parts. The same instinct shapes pregnancy planning, the house hunt, and how he reads the family calendar.

His theology is shaped more by the songs than by the sermons. He prays daily and briefly while making coffee. Public displays of piety embarrass him. He is steady in a way that takes a while to notice and is funnier than people expect, with a dry delivery saved mostly for Ethan and Emma. Anxious current surfaces only when something he loves feels precarious; the response is research and silence, not withdrawal.

He believes most of life is preparation. He trusts his church, his family, Emma without qualification, and his Torchwood colleagues on technical decisions but not on personal life.
