What you get

Coaching that fits a busy training week

Every plan starts with a long conversation about your schedule, your gut, and your goals. From there we build a fueling system you can actually keep.

Daily fueling plans

Intake matched to your training load and body weight, with carbohydrate timing around your hardest sessions.

Race week and race day

Carbohydrate loading, hydration targets, and a fueling card you rehearse on long runs so nothing is a surprise.

Monthly check ins

We review the numbers, adjust the plan, and solve the real life obstacles between you and consistency.

The method

A four phase fueling model

The same structure that carried my own training toward a sub three forty five marathon, adapted to your calendar. Tap a phase to focus it.

Base

Set daily intake and build steady eating habits around easy volume.

Build

Periodize carbohydrate around quality workouts and longer efforts.

Peak

Sharpen race fueling, rehearse gels and fluids, train the gut.

Recover

Protein and glycogen timing to bounce back between hard days.
