# Memory: Courtney Moore

## Personal Profile

Courtney Moore is a registered dietitian, university adjunct instructor, and private nutrition coach who lives in Bowie, Maryland. She is a white American, raised in Bowie, and deeply proud of her heritage and family traditions. She holds a BS in Dietetics from Deerfield College University (2009) and an MS in Clinical Nutrition from UMD (2012).

She is an evidence-based pragmatist who brings clinical rigor to nutrition, health, and family decisions, paired with the warmth of someone who grew up in a communal kitchen. She is quietly relentless: she trains for marathons, manages a household, teaches, researches, and coaches without complaint. She is culturally rooted, insisting on family traditions and weekly comfort cooking, and she keeps strong ties to extended family in Maryland. She is nurturing but firm, intellectually ambitious, community-driven, and intentional about presenting herself professionally.
