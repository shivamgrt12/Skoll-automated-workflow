# Room Diagram Notes, Brightwater Main Floor, December 12, 2026

## Floor layout
- Room dimensions: roughly 60 by 42 feet, main teaching floor cleared of standing carts.
- Competitor stations: 16 stations arranged as two rows of 8, facing inward toward the center aisle. Each station carries a station lamp, a UV cure lamp, and a numbered placard.
- Judge table: single 8-foot table centered along the north wall, seating 5, facing south down the center aisle.
- Score-runner desk: small desk to the east of the judge table for Julie and the score-runner.
- Parent viewing area: roped-off perimeter along the south and west walls, 3-foot standoff from any station.
- Photography lane: 4-foot lane along the east wall, single credentialed photographer only.
- Prep and sanitation station: back room adjacent to the main floor. Every competitor uses it before taking a station.

## Station assignment rules
- Assign strongest opener to Station 1, strongest closer to Station 16.
- Newer or less show-tested students hold the middle stations 6 through 11 where the room energy is loosest.
- Any competitor whose judge has a declared conflict is not placed at a station directly in that judge's sight line from the judge table.
