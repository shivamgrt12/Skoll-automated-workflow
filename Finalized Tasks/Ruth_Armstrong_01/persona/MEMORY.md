## Work & Projects

- **Employer:** Ville de Montréal, Service de l'urbanisme et de la mobilité. Office at City Hall (Hôtel de Ville), Old Montreal.
- **Title:** Senior Urban Planner (Urbaniste principale). Joined the city in 2016. Promoted to senior level in 2022.
- **Key projects:**
  - **Projet Mobilité Plateau:** Lead planner for a comprehensive active transportation plan for the Plateau-Mont-Royal borough. Includes protected bike lanes, pedestrian priority zones, and transit stop redesigns. Currently in public consultation phase; contentious with some business owners concerned about parking loss.
  - **Parc-Extension Housing Equity Study:** Co-leading an assessment of displacement and gentrification risk in the Parc-Extension neighbourhood as new transit infrastructure arrives. Involves community surveys, demographic analysis, and policy recommendations. Sensitive; immigrant communities are wary of city promises.
  - **Montreal Climate Adaptation Framework, Green Infrastructure Module:** Contributing to the city-wide climate plan, specifically the green infrastructure component: urban tree canopy targets, bioswales, permeable surfaces, and cooling corridors. Cross-departmental collaboration required.
- **Schedule:** Office Mon-Thu. Friday WFH or field visits. Occasional evening community consultations (1-2 per month).
- **Professional development:** Member of the Ordre professionnel des urbanistes du Québec (OPUQ). Preparing a presentation on equitable transit planning for the Institut Canadien des Urbanistes (ICU) national conference in October 2026.
