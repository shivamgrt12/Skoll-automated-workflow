# Mama's pound cake, the original

From Mama, who got it from her mother, who said the proportions came from a church cookbook in Bessemer in the early fifties.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb butter (yes a pound), softened
- 1 lb sugar (one bag is just shy, top it up)
- 6 large eggs, room temperature
- 1 lb cake flour (sifted twice not once)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (not the imitation kind, do not start)
- 1/2 teaspoon almond extract (this is the secret)

Method:
1. Cream butter and sugar until very light, full eight minutes in the stand mixer
2. Add eggs one at a time, beat well between
3. Fold in flour in three additions, do not overmix
4. Add the extracts at the end, fold once
5. Tube pan greased and floured, three hundred twenty five degrees, ninety minutes
6. Cool ten minutes in the pan, then turn out

Notes: Mama says do not open the oven for the first hour. She is right.
