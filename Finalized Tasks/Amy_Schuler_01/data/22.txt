HOA Meeting Minutes - September 2026
Federal Hill Condominium Association
Meeting Date: September 15, 2026, 7:00 PM
Location: Community Room, Ground Floor

Attendees: 18 of 24 unit owners (quorum met)
Board present: Chair Martinez, Treasurer Williams, Secretary Park

Agenda Item 1: Lobby Renovation Proposal
The board presented three renovation bids for the main lobby area. The selected contractor (Harbor Renovations LLC) submitted the lowest qualifying bid at $102,000 for the full scope including new flooring, lighting, mailbox area redesign, and accessibility improvements.

Vote: Approved 15-3 with conditions. The three dissenting votes cited concerns about the timeline conflicting with the holiday season.

Assessment: A one-time special assessment of $425.00 per unit will be levied to fund the renovation. Payment due October 31, 2026. Units may request a 3-month payment plan through the management office.

Agenda Item 2: Holiday Decorations Budget
Approved $1,200 for common area holiday decorations, consistent with previous years.

Agenda Item 3: Parking Garage Lighting
Two residents reported burned-out lights in sections B and C of the garage. Management to address within 2 weeks.

Agenda Item 4: Package Delivery Security
Discussed the increase in misdelivered packages. Patricia Hale (4th floor) volunteered to coordinate with delivery services. The board approved installing a package room camera.

Next meeting: November 17, 2026, 7:00 PM
