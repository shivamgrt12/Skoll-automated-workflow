Annapolis Day Trip Ideas - Updated August 2026
(Weekend getaway options, no dates committed)

OPTION A: Maritime Museum + Waterfront Lunch
  - Maryland State House tour (free, 11 AM start)
  - Walk to City Dock and Ego Alley (people-watching)
  - Lunch at Carrol's Creek Cafe (harbor views, quiet, no loud music)
  - Maritime Museum afternoon ($15 admission)
  - Drive time from Federal Hill: 45 minutes via I-97

OPTION B: Naval Academy + Bookshop
  - USNA campus tour (need valid ID, guided tours at 10 AM)
  - Lunch at Iron Rooster (good brunch, reasonable noise level)
  - Old Fox Books & Coffeehouse (browse + sit)
  - Walk Main Street shops
  - Note: parking near the Academy is tight on weekends

OPTION C: Art + Architecture Walk
  - Start at Banneker-Douglass Museum (free, opens 10 AM)
  - Walk through Historic District (Georgian architecture)
  - Lunch at Galway Bay (Irish pub, unexpectedly good fish)
  - Annapolis Pottery (browse, maybe a gift for Miriam)
  - William Paca House and Garden ($12, beautiful in fall)

GENERAL NOTES:
  - Best months: April-May, September-October (avoid summer crowds)
  - Parking: Hillman Garage ($2/hour, walkable to everything)
  - Bring sumatriptan (always)
  - Miriam has been to Options A and C; Hanna would like Option B
  - Could combine with Eastern Shore if we leave early enough

Last visited: June 14, 2026 (Option A with Miriam, lovely day)
