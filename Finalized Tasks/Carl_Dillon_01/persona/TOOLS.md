# Tools: Carl Dillon

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Mail, Calendar & Meetings

- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Primary inbox for professional correspondence, apartment listing alerts, and race confirmations. Drafts stay formal and proofread.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Receives calendar invites from conference organizers and counterpart legal teams who live in the Microsoft world.
- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): The master schedule: runs, viewings, case deadlines, and family visits. Exact times, no approximations.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Booking link for apartment viewings with brokers and mentoring slots with Linnea.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Video calls with international clients and the standing video call with Elsa.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Joins meetings when external legal teams insist on it. Meeting links only, no firm files.

#### Documents, Notes & Files

- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Home of the Helsinki conference research document and the apartment viewing spreadsheet. Folder structure stays layered and traceable.
- **Google Docs** (`google-docs-api`): Personal document workspace for travel itineraries with Maja and private drafts she cannot see that hold the surprise birthday dinner logistics.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Receives and downloads secure document links from external counsel for case-related reference materials.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Apartment hunt tracker: viewings, avgift figures, and total monthly cost scenarios per property.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Personal knowledge vault for forensic methodology notes feeding the internal training program.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Drafting space for the training curriculum before it moves into firm channels.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): Database of sources and citations for the digital evidence presentation.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Apartment paperwork and personal contracts. Every signature request gets flagged for his review first.
- **Figma** (`figma-api`): Slide layout polish for the Helsinki deck, where Frida occasionally shares design feedback.

#### Messaging

- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Main channel for Lars, Maja, and Klara, linked to his phone.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Channel where the running club posts route announcements; he checks it before every club run.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Södermalm Löparklubb workspace for relay team and race-day coordination.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): A crime fiction reading community he reads far more than he posts in.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Sends SMS reminders and confirmations for viewing appointments when a broker insists on text.

#### Money, Banking & Investments

- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Links Handelsbanken accounts to track monthly spend against budget categories and savings progress.
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Personal expense categorization mirroring the budget he keeps. Clean books are a point of pride.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): He keeps Frida's freelance design bookkeeping in order here, reviewing her invoices and VAT coding each quarter.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Payment records for the running club race registrations he helps the committee administer.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Splitting dinner bills with Maja and paying foreign race entry fees.
- **Square** (`square-api`): Sandbox transaction exports he works through to show juniors how US point-of-sale records are structured and how they get falsified.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): US market data feeding the watchlist he reviews weekly to sanity-check his Avanza index fund allocations.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Pulls historical crypto pricing for fraud casework analysis and Helsinki conference deck preparation.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Pulls exchange pricing and order-history formats during fraud case research and methodology training.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Cross-checks exchange data against Coinbase and Binance whenever an investigation touches crypto; he never trusts a single source.

#### Projects & Professional Operations

- **Jira** (`jira-api`): Task board for building the internal training program, kept deliberately separate from firm case systems.
- **Linear** (`linear-api`): Lightweight milestone tracker for the Helsinki presentation, from abstract to final deck.
- **Asana** (`asana-api`): Family visit planning shared with Frida.
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): Shared board where the running club committee tracks race-season volunteer rosters and gear logistics.
- **Trello** (`trello-api`): Apartment search pipeline: researching, viewing scheduled, viewed, shortlisted.
- **ServiceNow** (`servicenow-api`): He studies enterprise control and approval workflows here as background for compliance audit work.
- **Salesforce** (`salesforce-api`): He explores CRM data structures to understand how client records get manipulated in fraud schemes.
- **HubSpot** (`hubspot-api`): Manages the signup list and reminder pipeline for the internal training program's November kick-off.
- **Zendesk** (`zendesk-api`): Support tickets for his own subscriptions and devices, nothing more.
- **Freshdesk** (`freshdesk-api`): Keeps landlord and broker correspondence threads sorted and searchable during the apartment hunt.
- **Intercom** (`intercom-api`): How he reaches Avanza and other service helpdesks through their chat widgets when something needs fixing.

#### Data, Evidence & Infrastructure

- **GitHub** (`github-api`): Small private repos of spreadsheet automation scripts for cleaning transaction data.
- **GitLab** (`gitlab-api`): Mirrors those scripts as backup. He treats redundancy the way he treats evidence copies.
- **Sentry** (`sentry-api`): Error logs from his automation scripts. He reads stack traces the way he reads ledgers.
- **Datadog** (`datadog-api`): He studies transaction monitoring dashboards as background for the digital evidence talk.
- **PagerDuty** (`pagerduty-api`): Alerts him if the automated apartment-alert pipeline fails overnight.
- **Okta** (`okta-api`): He works through identity and access-control logs while building the access-trail section of the digital evidence talk.
- **Cloudflare** (`cloudflare-api`): DNS and email routing for his personal domain, which carries the apartment-alert summaries and will host the methodology blog.
- **Kubernetes** (`kubernetes-api`): Background study of how containerized financial systems are deployed and logged, feeding the digital evidence presentation.
- **Google Analytics** (`google-analytics-api`): Speaker-page traffic stats the Helsinki organizers shared with presenters.
- **Segment** (`segment-api`): He maps how customer data pipelines move so he can follow money trails through modern systems.
- **Amplitude** (`amplitude-api`): He runs through cohort and retention reports to build the event-logging examples in his junior training sessions.
- **Mixpanel** (`mixpanel-api`): He builds small funnel examples here to demonstrate to juniors how event data reconstructs user behavior.
- **PostHog** (`posthog-api`): Self-hosted analytics instrumenting his apartment-alert pipeline so he can see which listing types he actually opens.
- **Algolia** (`algolia-api`): Search layer over his methodology notes so old material surfaces fast.

#### Running, Health & Food

- **Strava** (`strava-api`): Logs every run: weekday sessions with Klara, the Saturday club long run, the Sunday recovery jog. The half marathon plan lives here.
- **MyFitnessPal** (`myfitnesspal-api`): Training nutrition during the half marathon buildup. Consistency patterns, not calorie pressure.
- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Pre-run weather checks, especially before the Saturday long run.
- **Yelp** (`yelp-api`): Restaurant research with prices and locations for Maja dinners and visiting family weekends.

#### Travel, City & Apartment Hunt

- **Amadeus** (`amadeus-api`): Flight and rail options for Helsinki, Gothenburg, and personal city trips. Aisle seats, always.
- **Airbnb** (`airbnb-api`): Accommodation research for personal travel. Work trips stay in hotels near the venue.
- **Uber** (`uber-api`): Airport transfers and late arrivals. Daily transport is the tunnelbana on his SL card.
- **Google Maps** (`google-maps-api`): Commute timing, viewing addresses, and running routes in unfamiliar cities.
- **Zillow** (`zillow-api`): US listing and price-history data he pulls to stress-test the valuation model in his apartment spreadsheet against a deeper market.
- **Ring** (`ring-api`): Indoor camera covering the front door of the rental. He checks clips and delivery alerts from the office and while traveling.
