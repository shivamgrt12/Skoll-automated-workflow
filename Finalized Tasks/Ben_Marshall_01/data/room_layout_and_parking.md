# Hamilton Community Hall — Room Layout & Parking

**Address on file:** Hamilton Community Hall (committee venue of record for the retreat and the Spring Community Meal)
**Access window:** 8:30 AM for setup; hall must be cleared and reset by 1:30 PM
**Facilities contact:** hall office (booking under the outreach committee name)
**Prepared by:** Devon Pratt (Setup & Logistics), reviewed by Anthony Delgado (Accessibility)

## Main hall
- Capacity ~120 seated; the retreat uses only the front third (committee U-shape).
- Two 20-amp circuits on the north wall — **do not** run the PA and both coffee urns off the same one (past trips). Split loads: PA on north-east circuit, urns on north-west circuit.
- Stage-left has the only HDMI-reachable projection wall; the media cart must sit within 15 ft of it.
- Ceiling is high and echoey — always use the mic, do not rely on unaided voice.

## Accessibility
- Ramp entrance on the **east** side; two accessible parking spots adjacent — reserve both.
- Accessible restroom off the main corridor (signed).
- ASL interpretation available on request — note this on any **public** page (not needed for the internal retreat).
- Hearing loop is **NOT** installed — use the PA and speak into the mic.
- Keep the main aisle at least 36 inches clear for mobility devices.

## Kitchen
- **Warming-only** (no full cook line); plan catering accordingly.
- Two urns max on the north-west circuit; a third urn goes to the corridor outlet.
- Food-safety sign-off by Keisha Board (ServSafe) for anything served hot.

## Safety & security
- First-aid kits: hall closet A, kitchen, and office.
- Accessible entrance and restroom must be clearly signed before doors.
- **No overnight storage of cash or valuables** — office safe only, same day. Registration is free; the donation table is voluntary and reconciled the same day.
- Fire exits on the east and south walls kept clear.

## Same-day reset
- Return furniture to stored configuration; A/V logged back into inventory.
- Sweep the front third; wipe tables; bag trash to the dock.
- Confirm the hall office the space is cleared by 1:30 PM.
