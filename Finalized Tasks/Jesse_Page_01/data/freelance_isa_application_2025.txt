Freelance ISA application 2025. Personal benefits filing reference.

Joined the Freelancers Union ISA (Income Stability Account) program July 2025. This file is the application packet I submitted with the supporting documentation, kept here for renewal year reference.

Why I joined: Ravi suggested it at the Q2 2025 close call. I had a deferred revenue smoothing problem (cookbook milestones lumpy across the year, Stocksy unpredictable). The ISA lets me stabilize monthly income drawn against a 12-month rolling baseline. Not a replacement for cash reserves, a complement.

Application structure submitted Jul 11 2025:

Section 1, Eligibility.
- Solo freelancer, no W2 employees, not currently incorporated as S-corp.
- New York State resident, Brooklyn (Williamsburg).
- 5+ years of freelance income history (started April 2020).
- Average annual gross revenue past 3 years: $158,000 (2022 $142K, 2023 $156K, 2024 $164K).
- No bankruptcy, no liens, no open IRS disputes.

Section 2, Income documentation.
- Schedule C copies 2022, 2023, 2024.
- Form 1099 summary from Stripe, PayPal, Stocksy, Etsy, Amazon Seller, Oatly direct deposit.
- Bank statements 6 months from Chase Business checking and Chase Business savings.
- Q1 and Q2 2025 estimated tax payments confirmation (Q1 $5,400 paid Apr 15, Q2 $5,400 paid Jun 16).

Section 3, ISA terms applied for.
- Monthly draw band $9,500 to $11,000 (smoothed against rolling 12-month average).
- 90-day buffer requirement (waived because cash reserves above the threshold).
- Quarterly true-up against actual deposits.
- 12-month commitment with 60-day cancellation window.

Section 4, Acknowledgments.
- Read and accepted the ISA terms of service.
- Confirmed beneficiary designation (Mateo Rivera, partner).
- Confirmed tax pass-through (deposits to ISA are pre-tax basis, draws are taxable income in the draw year).

Approved Aug 14 2025. First draw cycle starts Sep 2025. Quarterly true-up scheduled for Dec 31 2025.

Renewal: comes up Jul 2026. Probably renewing. Working well so far. The smoothing made the Q4 2025 cash flow much less stressful than 2024 was.

Filing status as of now: active. Draw band held at $10,500 since Sep 2025. No deviations.
