# Studio branding notes

Working notes on the Jesse Page Studio identity. Mateo and I have been kicking this around since 2023 without committing. Filed but not finalized.

## Current state

- Wordmark: "Jesse Page" in custom-spaced serif (Crimson Pro Roman, 22pt cap-height, kerning -10 on Pa, -5 on ge). Used on the invoice header, the email signature, and the cookbook colophon when I get a credit.
- Mark: none. We tried a J-P monogram in 2023, killed it because it read too aggressive for the editorial work.
- Colors: black text on warm white (Pantone Warm Gray 1C as substrate when it matters). Single warm orange accent only on the storefront print labels (Pantone 1655C).
- Voice: short sentences, no exclamation marks, no "thrilled to share". Email-side voice carries the same lowercase tendency as personal writing.

## Active concerns

- The studio name itself: "Jesse Page" vs "Jesse Page Studio" vs "Jesse Page Photography". Different surfaces use different forms. Currently:
  - Invoice header: "Jesse Page Photography"
  - Email signature: "Jesse Page"
  - Website domain: jessepage.studio (not the brand name on the home page)
  - Stocksy + Etsy storefront: "Jesse Page Prints"
  - Slack workspace: "Jesse Page Studio"
- This is fine but I should pick one canonical form for the cookbook credit line. Talked to Mateo Oct 5, leaning "Photography by Jesse Page" full sentence for editorial, "Jesse Page Studio" for the storefront, no "Photography" in the mark itself.

## Decisions taken in 2025

- Email signature shortened to two lines. Was four.
- Invoice template updated to match the wordmark spacing (Q2 2025).
- Storefront print labels redesigned with the orange accent, came back from Print Partners BK in May 2025. Holding.
- Website footer credit line consistent: "Jesse Page Studio is based in Williamsburg, Brooklyn."

## Decisions deferred

- Whether to publish a logo mark separate from the wordmark. Mateo argues yes for the merchandise spinoff and Stocksy thumbnail. I argue no because every additional element opens up enforcement work.
- Whether to use a tagline. Tried "the slow shot in a fast kitchen" in 2024 outreach decks. Felt overworked.
- Whether to commission a hand-lettered alt-mark from Mateo for the cookbook colophon. Estimated $800 if I pay him scope. Pending.

## What stays the same regardless

- No social-media-first identity work. Instagram bio is just the name and the city.
- No vendor-logo lockups on the invoices. Clean header, clean footer, single account number.
- No emoji in any business surface. Personal Discord is the only place I use them.
- No serif-and-script combinations. Single typeface always.
