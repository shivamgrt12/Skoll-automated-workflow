# Q4 2024 review (archive)

Personal year-end review from late December 2024. Filed in the Notion vault, exported to flat-file Mar 2025. Reading it back a year later, mostly holds up. Notes on what hit and what missed.

## What worked in Q4 2024

The Bedford morning light study started Oct 6 and ran through Nov 18, walking out before dawn three days a week. Logged 14 walks total. The series became the foundation for the Tangerine fall issue spread plus three Stocksy uploads that have all licensed at tier 2. That is the highest return-on-time block of the year.

The studio buildout finished in February cleared its first real test in October when I did the Hatch Beverages short-form three-day shoot in-house. Saved roughly $1,800 in studio rental compared to what I would have spent at Sokol. The 220V circuit decision was vindicated.

Tomas signed me with Herrera Creative Management in April 2024. End of year results: he booked the Oatly retainer cold (signed Nov) and the Hatch Beverages cold outreach (signed Dec). Net commission to him for 2024 was $3,840. Worth every penny. Renewing for 2025.

The cookbook outreach to Salt + Press in October landed the first call. Rebecca Allenford warm intro through Tomas. By December we had M1 milestone scheduled. This is the start of what becomes the cookbook project in 2025-2026.

## What did not work

I overcommitted in November. Said yes to a corporate brand pitch I should have declined (NDA, did not convert, wasted three weeks of pre-production). Lesson: if Tomas does not source it, I do not say yes.

The October Stocksy submission was rejected (8 of 12 images). The 8 rejected images were the dim sum series, all tier 3 placeholder, art direction was inconsistent. Pulled them, redid the lighting setup, resubmitted in November, all accepted tier 2.

The Q3 estimated tax payment Sep 15 underpaid by $1,250 because the August Stocksy income was higher than projected. Caught at the Ravi call Sep 30. Adjusted Q4 estimate to compensate. Lesson: check Stocksy monthly, not quarterly.

## What I want to carry forward

- Block the pre-dawn walks weekly Oct-Dec for the next cookbook year.
- Stay disciplined on the no-yes-without-Tomas rule.
- Standing call with Ravi every 30 days, not 90.
- Stop testing new gear in production. Tested the Profoto B10X Plus second head on a live Tangerine shoot in November and ate two hours on a focus issue. Test in the studio off the clock.

## Numbers

- Q4 2024 gross: $42,800 (Stripe + PayPal + Etsy + Amazon + Stocksy + Oatly).
- Q4 2024 net after deductions: roughly $31,200.
- Year 2024 gross: $164,500.
- Year 2024 net: roughly $118,000.
- Tomas commission paid 2024: $3,840.
- New clients converted 2024: 4 (Oatly, Hatch Beverages, Edgewater Food Journal, Salt + Press warm intro pending).

## What I would tell myself if I could send this back to Q1 2024

Buildout was the right call. Studio rental was killing the unit economics. Tomas was the right call. Stop second-guessing. Do not say yes to corporate work that comes through cold channels. Walk the morning. Eat the egg tart.

End of 2024 review. Reviewed at the Q1 2025 planning session Jan 5.
