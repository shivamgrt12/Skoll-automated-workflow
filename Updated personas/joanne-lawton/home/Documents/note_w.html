Where we need hands

Tap a task to read what it involves. None of it is hard, and the company is good.

Harvest Fair setup, October 24 ▸

Arrive at 7:30 AM to help arrange vendor tables, the plant and herb table, and the raffle display. Coffee and cider provided. We finish in good time for the 10:00 AM opening.

Food drive sorting, November 14 ▸

Sorting and packing day from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We check dates, sort by category, and build the Thanksgiving baskets. A warm and satisfying morning.

Homebound basket delivery, November 22 ▸

Drivers and helpers from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM to carry baskets to neighbors who cannot get out. A short visit at each door means a great deal. Please bring a vehicle if you can.

Altar flowers and linens ▸

A gentle weekly rotation. We arrange seasonal flowers, press and lay the linens, and prepare the Communion vessels before the 10:00 AM service. I will teach you everything.
