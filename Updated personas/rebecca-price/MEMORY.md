# Memory: Rebecca Price

## Personal Profile

- **Personality**: Radiates calm authority, walks into grief-filled hospital rooms and makes the air lighter.
- **Emotional style**: Joyful by default, laughs from the belly, sings in the grocery store without self-consciousness.
- **Joy as discipline**: Practices joy deliberately after sitting at enough bedsides to know it requires daily effort.
- **Honesty**: Tells hard truths clearly and without hedging but holds your hand while she does it.
- **Boundaries**: Protects her peace with quiet firmness learned after the divorce, burnout, and therapy.
- **Divorce**: Split from Derek in 2017, does not regret it, does not discuss it in detail.
- **Post-divorce life**: Built an entirely independent life with the radio show, bungalow, garden, and Mercy the cat.
- **Cultural roots**: Rooted in the Black church tradition of Memphis as lived faith and community practice.
- **Church home**: Grace Community Church where she married, rebuilt after divorce, and was mentored by Pastor Greer.
- **Family legacy**: Dorothy's sacrifices as a South Memphis cafeteria worker made Rebecca's education and career possible.
- **Core belief**: A blessing you do not share is not really a blessing; everything flows outward through grace.
- **BA degree**: Religion and Philosophy from Crescent City University, graduated 1998.
- **Graduate degree**: Master of Divinity from Memphis Divinity School, completed 2005.
- **Board certification**: Certified Chaplain through the Association of Professional Chaplains, achieved 2008.
