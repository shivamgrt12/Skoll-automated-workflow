# Heartbeat: Sam Osborne

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- 6:00 AM wake. Feed Copper. Coffee. Check the Sheriff's Office dispatch log before the shift starts.
- 6:45 AM review the case board, scan email, queue priorities for the day.
- Evening bench time when home, 7:00 to 10:00 PM. Check commission deadlines and pick up the cuff in progress.

### Weekly

- Monday: detective day. Case board review, evidence requests, follow-ups with Eddie Tsosie.
- Tuesday: typically interview and field-work day. Bench time light.
- Wednesday: midweek admin morning, bench time evening if no active case is breaking.
- Thursday: case file write-ups and statement reviews; metalwork shipping prep if a gallery deadline is close.
- Friday: shift close-out, hand-offs to Captain Rita Benally, evening at the bench.
- Saturday: Kai's cross-country meet in season; otherwise hiking the Zuni Mountains, the F-150 restoration, or a fair table.
- Sunday: morning call to Dorothy in Window Rock, grocery run, meal prep, family time with Kai.

### Monthly

- 1st Saturday: Gallup Flea Market. Sells the occasional piece and buys rough stones for the bench.
- 15th: Kai's 529 contribution of $300 and Dorothy's $400 support transfer.
- First week: review Etsy orders, check gallery inventory, restock silver and stones if needed.

### Quarterly

- Department physical fitness assessment.
- Schedule C bookkeeping pass for Osborne Metalworks.
- Tune-up on the workshop ventilation hood and the bench torch.

### Seasonal & Variable

- Spring: cross-country season, gallery spring inventory ship, hiking returns to the Zuni Mountains.
- Summer: Gallup Arts & Crafts Fair booth (mid-July), Kai's college visits, summer BBQ at Martha's.
- Autumn: cross-country championships, annual physical with Dr. Chee, holiday gallery prep.
- Winter: short days at the bench, F-150 garage time, telescope nights when skies are clear.

### Annual

- Nov 4: Sam's birthday, kept quiet, usually dinner with Kai at home.
- Oct 12: Kai's birthday (16). Dinner of her choosing, usually Golden Dragon.
- Mar 22: Dorothy's birthday (75). Drive to Window Rock with cake.
- Jan 18: Ben's birthday (46). Call in the morning, family dinner the closest weekend.
- Feb 7: Sarah's birthday remembered quietly. Sam visits her bench tools and Kai's framed photo.
- March: annual physical with Dr. Michael Chee at Gallup Regional Medical Center.
