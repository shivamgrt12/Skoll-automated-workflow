Build History
Project Timeline
  • Year 1 - 2023
    Shell Acquired and Teardown
    Bought the bare Fastback shell at a local swap meet. Full strip-down completed in the back bay over two months. Identified rust in the floor pans and rocker sections. Stripped to metal throughout.
    Shell: $4,500
  • Year 1-2 - 2023 to 2024
    Rust Repair and Engine Rebuild
    Floor pan and rocker sections welded and sealed. 302 V8 pulled, sent to the machine shop for a bore, then rebuilt in-house with new rings, bearings, cam, lifters, and a full valve job. Engine back in and running on the stand.
    Engine and body work: $9,300
  • Year 2-3 - 2024 to 2025
    Suspension, Brakes, and Electrical
    Full front suspension rebuild. Rear leaf springs replaced. Brake lines and cylinders throughout. Under-dash wiring harness installed. Dashpad and carpet received and fitted.
    Suspension and electrical: $4,500
  • Now - Late 2025 and into 2026
    Body Finishing and Interior (Active Phase)
    Right front fender and chrome bumper set on order. Surface sanding and blocking in progress on Tuesday and Thursday evenings. Upholstery kit ordered from Reynolds Custom Interiors. Paint estimate accepted from Desert Sun Auto Body.
    Body and interior: ~$7,500 remaining
  • Target - December 2026
    El Paso Classic Car Show Debut
    Entry registration to be submitted October 2026. Target is a completed and fully drivable Fastback ready to show at the El Paso Convention Center. James has said: if it is not right, it does not show.
    Show entry and detail: est. $400
