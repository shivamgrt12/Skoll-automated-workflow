About Jake

Two Careers, One Belief: Nourishing People Is Everything

Jake Thornton is a Certified Nurse-Midwife at a Midtown Atlanta women's health practice and the founder of Jake's Table. He brings the same steady attention he gives to birth work into his kitchen - deliberate, generous, and grounded in the belief that good food and good care start in the same place.

Born in Baltimore, raised on big family meals, and trained in Atlanta, Jake's cooking pulls from Southern comfort food, his husband Marcus's family traditions, and years of teaching students to trust their hands.

  • Hands-on technique at a renovated prep island - no passive watching
  • Recipes sourced from the East Lake backyard garden and DeKalb Farmers Market
  • Small groups of eight so everyone cooks, not just watches
  • Saturday afternoon classes that end with a shared meal around the table

Backyard Grilling Intensive

The Process

Simple from the First Click to the Last Bite

01

Book Your Seat

Choose a class from the calendar, reserve your seat through the booking link, and get a confirmation with all the details including what to bring, where to park, and what to expect.

02

Cook at the Island

Show up Saturday afternoon. Jake demonstrates each technique, then students cook in pairs at the prep island. Real knives, a commercial-grade range, and hands that get in the food from the start.

03

Eat Together

The class ends with a shared meal. You take home printed recipe cards and enough knowledge to cook the session dishes again the following weekend. No performance pressure, just good food and good people.

What You Need to Know Before You Book

No prior experience is necessary. Jake's Table is designed for students at every level, from first-time cooks to people who already cook regularly and want to sharpen specific skills. The small group size means Jake can give individual attention to wherever you are starting from.
Just yourself and comfortable shoes - closed toe is safest in a working kitchen. All ingredients, tools, knives, cutting boards, and aprons are provided. If you have specific dietary restrictions or allergies, let Jake know when you book and the menu will be adjusted accordingly.
Home classes are held in the Jake's Table kitchen in the East Lake neighborhood of Atlanta, GA 30317. Street parking is available on the block and neighboring streets. The booking confirmation includes the full address and directions. Venue classes are held at partner community spaces in Atlanta - the location is always included in the booking confirmation.
Full refunds are available up to 72 hours before the class date. Cancellations inside 72 hours can be transferred to a future class date but are not refunded. If a class is cancelled by Jake's Table due to low enrollment or an emergency, all enrolled students receive a full refund.
Yes. Private classes for groups of four to eight are available for corporate events, birthday celebrations, date nights, or family gatherings. Send a message through the contact link or reach out on Instagram at @jakes.table to discuss dates, menus, and pricing for private bookings.
