What we do

Care that moves from the table to the field, with measurable checkpoints the whole way.

A

ACL and Knee Recovery

Structured rehab from surgery through clearance, with limb symmetry and hop testing that tells us when the knee is truly ready.

R

Return to Sport Testing

Force plate, hamstring, and movement screening that turns readiness into a number you can train against.

P

Performance Assessment

Baseline athletic testing for healthy athletes who want a clear picture of strength, power, and asymmetry.

C

Concussion Protocols

Baseline and graded return for young athletes in contact sports, led by our athletic training background.

S

Strength and Conditioning

Programming that bridges the gap between cleared and competitive, for the athlete who is not done improving.

T

Telehealth Follow Up

Remote check ins for traveling athletes so progress never has to pause between visits.
