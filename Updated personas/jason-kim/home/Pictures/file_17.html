About the program

Structured guidance, not a brochure

The program closes a gap in structured mentorship for people in their first three years in the field. Each participant is paired with a volunteer mentor for one on one guidance, joins group sessions, and gains access to the Columbus Trade Association network. Directors serve without compensation, and the two founding firms absorb the time as an investment in the regional pipeline.
