Front Desk Coverage, Week of the Sign-Up

A simple way for the desk team to claim a shift, trade with notice, and keep the morning chair from ever sitting empty. Coverage changes go through Elaine. No quiet swaps.

How Coverage Works

  • Monday opens with the 7:15 AM staff meeting before the desk goes live.
  • Alternating Saturdays run a 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM morning shift.
  • Night desk is held by Nancy Clifford. Amy Soto is training on days.
  • To claim or trade a shift, fill in the form below. Elaine Bradshaw confirms every change.
