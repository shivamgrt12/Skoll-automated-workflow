Phil Lane

Front Desk Manager, Birchwood Valley Inn, Brattleboro, Vermont

Thirty years at a hospitality front desk, fourteen of them at this inn and nine of those managing it. The work is plain: show up, do what you said, write it down. A guest remembers the clerk who fixed the heat at night, not the one who smiled widest.

About the Work

I run the front desk at a small New England inn and conference center: reservations, check-in and check-out, housekeeping coordination, and the logistics behind a meeting of twenty in the back room. I know the local roads, the closures, and the warm lunch in town well enough to advise a guest without checking. Reliability beats brilliance, every time.

Reporting to General Manager Elaine Bradshaw since 2012. Promoted to manager in 2017.
