# Memory: Phil Lane

## Personal Profile

Phil Patrick Lane (née Doyle) is a front desk manager at Birchwood Valley Inn in Brattleboro, Vermont, born in Springfield, Massachusetts. She lives in a 2-bedroom cape-style house in West Brattleboro that she owns outright (mortgage paid off in 2021). Widowed since February 2022, when her husband Dennis Lane died of a heart attack at 62 after 34 years of marriage. She speaks English and a handful of French phrases from growing up near the Quebec border. Holds an associate's in Hospitality Management from Green River Community College (1985).
