# Heartbeat: Henry Harmon

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- **Daily, 5:30 AM ET (school year)**: Henry is up. Dorothy calls every morning at 6:15 AM ET like clockwork. He is at school by 6:45 AM ET for P.E. classes and coaching prep.
- **Daily, 6:30 PM ET (school year)**: Family dinner at home unless game day or booster night moves it.

### Weekly

- **Monday (school year)**: P.E. classes, coaching prep, practice 3:15 to 5:30 PM ET, home by 6:15 PM, dinner at 6:30 PM. Film review 8:00 to 10:00 PM during game weeks.
- **Tuesday (school year)**: School day as normal, then barbershop chair 5:30 to 7:30 PM ET when possible. Pop Brennan covers the earlier block at Harmon's Heritage Cuts.
- **Wednesday (school year)**: School day, practice in the afternoon, Bible study at Grace Crossroads 6:30 to 8:00 PM ET when the football schedule allows. Surface a leave-by reminder around 6:15 PM.
- **Thursday (school year)**: School day, practice, barbershop chair 5:30 to 7:30 PM ET when possible.
- **Friday, 4:00 PM ET (late August to November)**: Pre-game routine begins. Game at 7:00 PM. Home by 11:00 PM at earliest. Rosie's Kitchen with the staff after home wins.
- **Saturday, 9:00 AM to noon ET (football season)**: Film review with the coaching staff. Barbershop chair 1:00 to 6:00 PM. Evening with family or college football on the TV.
- **Saturday, 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM ET (off-season)**: Barbershop chair through the day, occasional bass fishing on Hoover Reservoir with Keith in the morning before the shop opens, grilling in the evening when the weather holds.
- **Sunday, 9:15 AM ET**: Leave for Grace Crossroads Methodist. Service 9:30 AM to noon. Surface the leave-by reminder at 9:00 AM.
- **Sunday, 1:00 PM ET**: Family dinner. Dorothy or Karen cooks. Afternoon goes to rest, dominoes, home projects, or NFL during off-season.

### Monthly

- **1st of each month**: Barbershop rent payment of $1,100.
- **1st Monday, 6:30 PM ET (school year)**: Booster club meeting at Ridgemont. Surface the agenda the Friday before.
- **15th of each month**: P&L review with Pop Brennan at Harmon's Heritage Cuts.
- **3rd Saturday, 9:00 AM ET**: Elder board meeting at Grace Crossroads Methodist.
- **Last Saturday morning**: Round of golf with Pastor David Callahan, badly but enthusiastically.

### Quarterly

- **Each quarter**: OHSAA coaching compliance review. Block prep time the week before.
- **Each quarter**: Tax-cycle check-in with Maria Collins (CPA) on the shop books, scheduled the second Saturday of the quarter.

### Seasonal / Variable

- **Late August through November**: Football season tempo. Game weeks dominate, film review runs late, Saturdays open with staff time, Friday nights are non-negotiable.
- **December through July**: Off-season tempo. More hours at the shop, more time with Henry Sr., spring practice opens in March, summer conditioning begins in June, recruiting camps run in July.
- **June through July**: Summer conditioning Monday through Thursday at 6:30 AM ET. Recruiting visits and camps interleaved with shop coverage.
- **Visits to Henry Sr. and Dorothy**: 3 to 4 times a week, more on weekends and after a hard day. Home maintenance handled when he is there.

### Annual

- **January 12**: Karen's birthday. Dinner out at her pick, flowers from Henry, card from the kids.
- **January 22**: Annual physical with Dr. Nathan Caldwell at Ridgemont Health Partners. Flu shot rolled into the same visit because Karen insists.
- **February 4**: Keith's birthday. Brothers meet at Harbor View Pub or an early lake morning if the weather holds.
- **February 18**: Megan's birthday. FaceTime call from Henry and Karen, card and a check mailed to Indianapolis.
- **March 22**: Dorothy's birthday. Lunch at her favorite spot in west Columbus with Henry and Karen.
- **October 12**: Pop Brennan's birthday. Cake and coffee at the shop after closing.
- **October 22**: Henry's birthday.
- **October 30**: Brian's birthday. Phone call from the road, longer if his route runs through Ohio.
- **November 8**: Henry and Karen's wedding anniversary (married 2002). Dinner out, no kids, no coaching talk.
- **November 20**: Tyler's birthday. Family dinner at home, sometimes shifted around a Friday night game.
- **Late November (Thanksgiving)**: Thanksgiving dinner at home with Dorothy, Henry Sr., Keith's family, Megan in from Indianapolis, and Pastor David Callahan.
- **December 8**: Henry Sr.'s birthday. Visit at his house with Karen and Tyler; Dorothy bakes the cake.
- **December 25**: Christmas morning at home, afternoon at Dorothy's.
