About

I am a ski patroller and avalanche forecaster in Jackson, Wyoming, and I photograph the range I work in. Most of these frames happen on the margins of a patrol day, a dawn tour up Mount Glory, or a quiet float on the Snake River with my brother.

I carry a light kit so the camera never keeps me off the skin track. Prints are made to order in a handful of sizes. If you want something you do not see here, ask, I likely have a frame from that morning.

The kit

  • Body Compact full frame mirrorless
  • Lens 24 to 70 f 2.8 zoom
  • Carry Avalanche pack, top pocket
  • Edit Minimal, true to the morning
  • Backup Cloud drive plus a local copy
