# Heartbeat: Jeff Peterson

## Recurring Events

### Daily
- **5:00 AM (winter)**: Jeff checks overnight snowfall totals and the avalanche advisory before the patrol report, over two cups of black French-press coffee.
- **6:00 AM to 4:30 PM (winter weekdays)**: Jeff is on patrol. Report at 6:00 AM, route opens by 8:30 AM, off by 4:30 PM. Summer days run more flexibly.

### Weekly
- **Monday**: Reviews the week's patrol schedule and flags any coverage gaps at 7:00 AM, then strength training at 5:00 PM at Snow King Fitness. Cody usually has Mondays off, so it is their day together.
- **Tuesday**: Morning trail run or ski tour, dawn patrol in winter and an earlier 5:30 AM start in summer.
- **Wednesday**: Kyle comes over for dinner most weeks, year-round, often with fresh trout, so a reminder goes out to check whether he is bringing fish. Strength training at 5:00 PM at Snow King.
- **Thursday**: Morning trail run or ski tour.
- **Friday**: Strength training at 5:00 PM at Snow King, then post-patrol burgers at The Bird, the standing tradition.
- **Saturday**: Morning trail run or ski tour, and car-camping weekends with Cody through the warmer months.
- **Sunday**: Calls his parents in Bozeman at 7:00 PM, with a reminder at 6:45 PM that does not get skipped. Cody makes a big batch of chili or stew for the week.

### Monthly
- **1st of the month**: Jeff reviews the budget and savings progress toward the Denali expedition and the land down payment.

### Seasonal / Variable
- **Avalanche course season (December through March)**: Jeff finalizes the field plan and prints handouts for the Avy 1 public course on evenings and weekends.
- **October weekends**: Jeff and Cody cut and split three cords of firewood for the winter.

### Annual
- **October 5**: Kyle's birthday, marked with the standing Snake River fall float.
- **October**: Pre-season physical with Dr. Amy Oakes, and CPR and Wilderness First Responder recertification before the season opens.
- **November 12**: Linda's birthday, a call and a card to Bozeman.
- **November**: Patrol fitness test.
- **December 3**: Cody's birthday, with a planned night off the mountain.
- **February 11**: Jeff's own birthday, kept low-key by preference.
- **March 8**: Tom's birthday, the weekly call runs long with a visit if the passes are open.
