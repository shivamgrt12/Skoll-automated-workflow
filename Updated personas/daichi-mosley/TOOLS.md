# Tools: Daichi Mosley

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Flight, Crew & Travel

- **Amadeus** (`amadeus-api`): Books revenue-ticket fares and routes when staff standby travel to Honolulu falls through.
- **Airbnb** (`airbnb-api`): Reserves family stays in Honolulu for the annual December trip when Yoshiko's place is full.
- **Uber** (`uber-api`): Books rides between layover hotels and downtowns on his 3 to 4 day trips.
- **DoorDash** (`doordash-api`): Orders into a layover hotel on late-arrival nights when restaurants are closed.
- **Google Maps** (`google-maps-api`): Times the SEA drive against his crew report so he leaves at report minus 90 minutes.
- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Tracks Bellevue and destination weather around Kenji's soccer games and outdoor family days.
- **NASA** (`nasa-api`): Pulls space and atmospheric imagery he and Kenji study together alongside the model aircraft.

- FAA medical records and the AME's systems stay off the assistant; it never acts on them directly.
- Aisha's private real estate accounts and personal email stay off the assistant.

- The household bank and brokerage logins beyond linked balance checks stay off the assistant.
