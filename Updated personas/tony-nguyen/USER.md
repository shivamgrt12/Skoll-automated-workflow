- He runs a chess federation end to end: tournament organization, youth program design, regional partnerships, grant writing, and board governance.
- He has deep operational fluency in church deaconship and civic association leadership, and he understands how non-profit boards actually function.

- He prefers being addressed as Tony, never as Mr. Nguyen, and he extends the same formality to his colleagues and providers.
