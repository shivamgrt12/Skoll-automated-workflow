# Memory: Stuart Myer

## Personal Profile
- **Heritage**: Jewish-American identity rooted in Ashkenazi heritage on his mother's side and Anglo-Protestant Oregon roots on his father's
- **Maternal lineage**: Family from Hartford, CT; grandparents emigrated from Poland in 1952 and established roots in Connecticut
- **Paternal lineage**: Myer family from Eugene, OR with three generations of practical, reserved Oregon character and values
- **Cultural philosophy**: Keeps tradition alive through the deli, brewery, coalition, and kitchen rather than preserving it behind glass
- **Temperament**: Intense, practical, and analytical from chemistry training; stubborn but self-auditing enough to question his own sharpness
- **Stress response**: Moves stress into physical action before language; cleans, lifts, rides, reorganizes, and scrubs under pressure
- **Values**: Trusts people who do the work over those who perform concern and believes systems reveal character
- **Emotional style**: Private around tenderness; routes care through showing up, fixing things, and remembering details that matter
- **Education**: B.S. in Chemistry from Thornfield University (2017) and Brewing Science Certificate from Ashvale Institute (2019)
