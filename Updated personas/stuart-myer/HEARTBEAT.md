Cellar work (transfers, dry-hopping, tank cleaning) through midday; coalition admin, email, and water testing data entry in the afternoon. On the 1st and 15th of each month, review brewery P&L and personal budget.
- **Tuesday, 5:00 AM alarm / 5:30 AM start**: Brew day at Driftwood. Full day through early afternoon; clean up and prep for Thursday brew afterward.
- **Wednesday, 8 AM-2 PM**: Packaging and cleaning; taproom opens 4 PM. First Wednesday of each month: coalition board meeting at 7 PM at Evergreen Club downtown.
- **Thursday, 5:00 AM alarm / 5:30 AM start**: Brew day. Same schedule as Tuesday; early bed afterward.
- **Friday**: Brewery admin (orders, invoices, planning) in the morning. Taproom 3 to 11 PM; occasionally meets Lauren after close. Every other Friday: water testing data entry to keep records current before the next coalition board cycle.
- **Saturday, 7:30 AM**: Farmers' market for ingredient scouting and personal groceries. Taproom open 12 to 11 PM; busiest day of the week.
- **Sunday**: Visit Bubbe and Deb at Greenfield's Deli or Deb's apartment at 10 AM; call Bubbe if unable to visit in person. Afternoon bike ride with Nick Farrell or solo on the Springwater Corridor or Columbia River Gorge Trail. Evening: meal prep, coalition prep, wind down for the week.
