# Heartbeat: Craig O'Neill

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- 5:00 AM coffee, porridge, weather review on Vedur.is and Windy, ice and sea state check.
- Harbour walk by 6:00 AM on shore days, vessel check, gear review, hut coffee with Sean Fitzgerald.
- Daily check on Bridget near the harbour, in person or by phone.
- 10:00 PM target bedtime on shore. At sea, four-hour watch rotation with Patrick Sullivan.

### Weekly

- **Monday**: shore day. Vessel maintenance, gear prep, admin block in the morning, buyer and supplier work in the afternoon. Dinner at home with Shannon.
- **Tuesday**: shore day. Crew coordination, safety checks, provisions loading if a departure is planned. Visit Bridget. Quiet evening at home.
- **Wednesday**: shore or sea. Crew briefing and vessel checks if departing by tide. If ashore, maintenance and planning.
- **Thursday**: typically at sea during season. Full fishing operations, watch rotation, weather updates, position reports.
- **Friday**: sea or return. If returning, harbour approach, unloading at Vestfirsk Fiskur, crew debrief. Evening pint at Husid with Sean if ashore.
- **Saturday**: shore day. Household tasks and vehicle maintenance in the morning, time with Shannon and a Bridget visit in the afternoon, dinner at home or Edinborg Bistro.
- **Sunday**: weekly call with Liam in Reykjavik and call or video with Maeve in Akureyri. Sunday dinner of lamb stew or colcannon. Weekly plan, early bed.

### Monthly

- 1st of the month: financial review, personal budget, savings progress, vessel reserve check, quota balance review.
- Mid-month: gear and provisions stocktake against upcoming voyages.
- One Saturday a month: extended drive through the Westfjords with Shannon or museum visit.
- Monthly hurling or football match on RUV when the schedule allows.

### Quarterly

- Quarterly review against the 2026 fishing season plan, including quota utilisation by species and market price trends.
- Quarterly vessel safety equipment check beyond the monthly drill, including life rafts, EPIRB battery, and immersion suits.
- Quarterly catch-up with Kevin Murphy at Vestfirsk Fiskur on pricing and forward contracts.

### Seasonal / Variable

- Spring: spring fishing campaign, longer voyages, lambing-season family visits.
- Summer: longer daylight, electronic monitoring pilot equipment installation during August maintenance window, occasional Ireland trip.
- Autumn: October to early December fishing campaign, Fisheries Conference, autumn weather windows tightening.
- Winter: December engine refit window, reduced fishing, planning and admin focus, Christmas Eve as the main family celebration.
- Weather: closely tracked year-round through Vedur.is, Windy, and the harbour hut intelligence with Sean.

### Annual

- January: annual planning session, RUV catch-up, Craig's birthday on the 22nd, fishing year-end paperwork wrap.
- November: annual checkup with Dr. Brendan Walsh at Isafjordur Health Clinic, Shannon's birthday on the 14th.
- December: engine refit window, Christmas Eve gathering, year-end financial review.
- Annual physiotherapy and dental cycles handled across the year, plus Fishing Vessel Owners' Association renewal.
