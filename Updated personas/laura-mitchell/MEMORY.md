# Memory: Laura Mitchell

## Personal Profile

Laura grew up in Dearborn and has lived in the family house on Lexington Drive in East Dearborn her whole life apart from the four years of B.F.A. studio nights at the College for Creative Studies in Detroit. She is the only girl in the family and the youngest by four years. She has a working hand-lettering practice she inherited from her maternal grandmother, Florence, who taught her with brush pens, gouache, and a kitchen-table mat in Sarasota starting at age 11. She runs Laura Creates Studio as a sole proprietor and treats it as a real business, not a side gig. She is quietly building toward a hybrid in-house brand designer role at a Detroit or remote agency without abandoning the freelance practice or the lettering. Her relationship to faith is private and lapsed-Catholic in the family-cultural sense, and she does not raise it.

## Work & Projects

- **Laura Creates Studio**: sole-proprietor brand and identity practice, operating since 2022. Services span brand identity, logo design, packaging design, social-media template systems, and small-business marketing collateral.
- **Income**: $2,500 to $3,500 per month from the studio, four to six active clients at any given time, mix of one-off projects and quarterly retainer relationships.
- **Tooling**: Adobe Creative Cloud (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign), Figma for collaborative work, Canva Pro for client-managed templates, HoneyBook for contracts and invoicing, Zelle and PayPal for payment, Stripe for the booking form.
- **Portfolio**: lauracreates.studio, currently hosted on Webflow. Annual relaunch each October with the year's new case studies. Considered Squarespace in early 2026 and stayed on Webflow for the design control.
- **Hand-lettering practice**: paid bridal signage at $250 to $400 per wedding, paid event lettering at $150 to $250 per event, individual commissions at $40 to $80. Seasonal income $400 to $800 per month, strongest from October through February. Sells letterpress prints and watercolour originals on Etsy.
- **Instagram**: @lauracreates for studio work, @lauracreates.lettering for the lettering practice at 5,200 followers and growing slowly and deliberately.
- **Workshops**: quarterly hand-lettering workshops at the Dearborn Community Arts Center. Twelve seats at $75 per attendee. Curriculum is the brush-pen fundamentals her grandmother taught her, retooled for adult beginners.
- **Quiet plan**: short list of in-house brand designer roles at Detroit and remote agencies, tracked in a private Greenhouse view. Has not told family. Lena Park is the one person she has talked it through with.
- **Rate history**: lifted the lettering individual-commission floor from $45 to $55 in early 2026 after a podcast episode and a candid talk with Lena. Studio rates revisited each January with the retainer renewals.
