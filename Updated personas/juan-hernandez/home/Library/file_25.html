About

I spent my career as a policy analyst working on environmental regulation and federal land management. Since retiring I have stayed close to the work that mattered to me: writing on conservation for the local press, lecturing on the history of environmental stewardship, and tutoring adult learners at the regional library. I read widely in history and policy and keep a steady, unhurried pace.

Federal environmental stewardship

Local writing on protecting the forested buffers and water quality of the Chesapeake watershed.

Adult learning

Volunteer tutoring in reasoning and language arts for adult learners working toward the high school equivalency.
