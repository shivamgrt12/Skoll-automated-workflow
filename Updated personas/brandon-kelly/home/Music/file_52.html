The club

Wednesdays, half past six

We meet every Wednesday evening, six thirty to nine, second floor of the Ridgemont Community Center. Doors at six fifteen. Skittles when you arrive, a rated ladder game most weeks, and once a month a themed night, an opening lecture, a tactics session, or a simultaneous exhibition.
