Open questions to settle

Each one needs an answer before an application goes in.
Does the clinical block fit a Monday through Thursday schedule?
Northgate keeps campus time to two days, which is the easiest to fit around clinic hours at Lakewood. Riverside runs weekends, which would compete with vendor meetings and Sunday cooking. Worth confirming the exact campus calendar before deciding.
Will Dr. Kim write the supervising letter Lakeshore asks for?
She has already offered a reference for expanded clinical pathways in the last review. The Lakeshore route is the only one that needs the formal supervising letter, so confirm the format she is comfortable signing.
Can the monthly Heartland volunteer Saturday continue during the program?
Northgate and Lakeshore both start after the wedding year and keep weekends mostly free. Riverside weekends would force a pause on the volunteer shift, which is a real cost, not a small one.
LEANING TOWARD

Northgate College, Detroit

Lowest tuition, shortest timeline, lightest campus footprint, and it starts cleanly after October 17, 2026. The plan is to talk it through with Emma on a Wednesday planning night once the wedding budget is settled, then request the reference from Dr. Kim.
