# Memory: Jessica Taylor

## Personal Profile
Jessica Marie Taylor was born in San Jose, California, and now lives and works in Los Angeles. She holds a B.F.A. in graphic design from Pacific Coast School of the Arts in San Francisco, earned in 2017, and built her freelance practice in the years since. She speaks English natively and conversational Spanish picked up from family, with limited reading and writing. She is single; her last relationship ended in 2025 after eight months, and she is not actively looking but not closed off. She cares deeply about how things are made, treats taste as a form of respect rather than a status game, and carries an ambient hum of freelance uncertainty that she has learned to sit with. Her affection tends to come out as craft: a designed birthday invite instead of a card, a curated playlist for a friend in a hard stretch.

## Work & Projects
Jessica runs Taylor Visual Studio as a sole proprietor, doing freelance brand identity, packaging, digital design, and occasional print for small businesses, startups, and nonprofits. Her typical workflow runs discovery call, proposal on Drive, mood board, initial concepts, two revision rounds, then final deliverables, with client calls usually over Google Meet. She charges $75 an hour for project work and $3,500 to $8,000 for brand identity packages depending on scope, and she is working to raise rates with Ray Mitchell's encouragement. Gross revenue is about $57,600 a year, averaging roughly $4,800 a month and varying seasonally, with busiest stretches in January through March for new-year rebrands and September through November for holiday packaging. Recent active projects have included a Verdant Provisions brand refresh, a Pinnacle Collective logo and signage package, and a seasonal packaging update for Nina Okafor's skincare line. Her tools are Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign as primary, Figma for UI and web, and Procreate on the iPad for sketching. The portfolio lives at taylorvisualstudio.com on Squarespace, and the business email is jessica@taylorvisualstudio.com, managed through Outlook desktop.
