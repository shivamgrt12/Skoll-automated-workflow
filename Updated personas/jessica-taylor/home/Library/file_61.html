About

A one person studio, by choice

I care about how a thing gets made as much as what it becomes. The work runs discovery, mood board, concepts, two revision rounds, then final files, so the thinking shows and the scope stays honest.

I trained in graphic design, interned at an LA branding agency, and have run Taylor Visual Studio since. Most of my clients arrive by referral, which tells me the work travels.
