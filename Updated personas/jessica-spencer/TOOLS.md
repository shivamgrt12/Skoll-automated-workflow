# Tools: Jessica Spencer

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Sourcing, Furnishings & Commerce
- **Etsy** (`etsy-api`): Vintage Eames and Noguchi finds and one-of-a-kind artisan pieces for client rooms.
- **Pinterest** (`pinterest-api`): Mood boards and reference imagery for mid-century projects and tablescapes.
- **Amazon Business** (`amazon-seller-api`): Trade-account procurement for accessories and staging fillers, where Jessica places and tracks the studio's own orders.
- **BigCommerce** (`bigcommerce-api`): Restoration Hardware trade portal for case goods and lighting.
- **WooCommerce** (`woocommerce-api`): Smaller artisan-network shops for custom textiles and ceramics.
- **Square** (`square-api`): Point of sale for occasional sample-sale purchases from local makers.
- **Instacart** (`instacart-api`): Green Harvest Market grocery delivery on Mondays.
- **DoorDash** (`doordash-api`): Olive and Ivy or Joe's Pizza on busy studio evenings.

#### Project & Studio Management
- **Asana** (`asana-api`): Task tracking across the Hendricks, Martin, and Desert Vista projects.
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): Master timeline boarding for all active projects and milestones.
- **Trello** (`trello-api`): Sourcing and procurement board Sophie keeps for sample tracking.
- **Linear** (`linear-api`): Punch-list items and close-out tasks shared with Natalie.
- **Jira** (`jira-api`): Issue tracking for the studio website and client portal build.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): Vendor directory, fabric archive index, and project budget rollups.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Studio planning hub for design briefs and meeting notes.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Private notes vault for design philosophy and project observations.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Shared knowledge base for studio process and vendor terms.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Client intake questionnaires for new design engagements.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Signatures on design contracts, vendor agreements, and change orders.

#### Documents, Drive & Design Files
- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Project folders, mood boards, presentations, and contracts.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Backup of large photo shoots and CAD exports.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Secure storage for client contracts and high-value invoices.
- **Figma** (`figma-api`): Design boards, presentation decks, and signage layouts.
- **Webflow** (`webflow-api`): The Spencer Interiors portfolio site.
- **WordPress** (`wordpress-api`): The studio blog with project case studies.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): Structured content for portfolio pages and project pages.
- **Algolia** (`algolia-api`): Search across the portfolio and the fabric-archive index.

#### Communication, Calendar & Scheduling
- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Primary email for clients, vendors, and personal correspondence.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Secondary address for vendor receipts and order confirmations.
- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Client meetings, site visits, tennis, and family activities.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Client presentation and walkthrough booking.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Quick threads with Kevin and close friends.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Backup messaging with the artisan network.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Automated delivery and appointment reminder texts.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Virtual client presentations and remote vendor calls.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Calls with the Acacia Homes development team.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Studio channel with Natalie, Sophie, and Janet.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): A design-community server for mid-century collectors.

#### Client Outreach & Marketing
- **HubSpot** (`hubspot-api`): Client and prospect CRM for the firm.
- **Salesforce** (`salesforce-api`): Records for past clients and referral sources.
- **Mailchimp** (`mailchimp-api`): The seasonal studio newsletter to clients and trade.
- **Klaviyo** (`klaviyo-api`): Segmented campaigns announcing completed projects.
- **ActiveCampaign** (`activecampaign-api`): Follow-up automation after consultations.
- **SendGrid** (`sendgrid-api`): Transactional email for proposal and invoice sends.
- **Mailgun** (`mailgun-api`): Backup bulk sender for larger trade mailings.
- **Intercom** (`intercom-api`): Website chat for prospective design clients.
- **Zendesk** (`zendesk-api`): Inquiry tickets routed from the portfolio site.
- **Freshdesk** (`freshdesk-api`): Backup support desk for client questions.

#### Finance & Payments
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Spencer Interiors books that Janet maintains.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Backup bookkeeping view of recurring studio expenses.
- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Links the Chase business checking so Jessica can see the studio's cash position during the monthly budget pass.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Online client deposits and milestone invoice payments.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Small artisan and vendor payments.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Tracks the household's small Bitcoin holding, which Jessica folds into the monthly finance review.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Watches the small Ethereum position Kevin opened, checked alongside the rest of the household money each month.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Pulls coin prices when Jessica reconciles the household's small crypto holdings.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Tracks the small taxable brokerage account that sits beside the emergency fund.

#### Website Analytics & Performance
- **Google Analytics** (`google-analytics-api`): Traffic on the portfolio site, checked to see which projects draw the most visitors.
- **Mixpanel** (`mixpanel-api`): Engagement on the project case-study pages, showing Jessica which portfolios hold attention.
- **Segment** (`segment-api`): Routes site events into the studio's analytics so the portfolio numbers stay in one place.
- **Amplitude** (`amplitude-api`): Funnel view for consultation inquiries, showing where prospects drop off before booking.
- **PostHog** (`posthog-api`): Self-hosted analytics on the portfolio site, used to see which case studies turn into inquiries.

#### Developer & IT Infrastructure
- **GitHub** (`github-api`): Tracks the outside developer's commits on the studio site so Jessica knows when new case studies and portfolio updates go live.
- **GitLab** (`gitlab-api`): Holds the portfolio site's code, where Jessica pulls release notes before announcing site updates to clients.
- **Kubernetes** (`kubernetes-api`): Surfaces hosting status for the studio site and client portal so Jessica knows the storefront is up before sending a client to it.
- **Datadog** (`datadog-api`): Uptime and performance on the portfolio and client portal, flagging slowdowns before a client presentation.
- **Sentry** (`sentry-api`): Surfaces errors on the studio website so Jessica can have the developer fix anything breaking the client portal.
- **Cloudflare** (`cloudflare-api`): DNS and security status for the spencerinteriors.com domain, watched so the site and email stay reachable.
- **PagerDuty** (`pagerduty-api`): Alerts Jessica if the studio site or client portal goes down during a launch or a big presentation.
- **Okta** (`okta-api`): Single sign-on for the studio's staff accounts, keeping Natalie, Sophie, and Janet's logins in one place.
- **ServiceNow** (`servicenow-api`): Tracks IT tickets for studio hardware and accounts so nothing for the team slips.

#### Staff & Operations
- **BambooHR** (`bamboohr-api`): Staff records for Natalie, Sophie, and Janet, where Jessica keeps start dates, contact details, and time off.
- **Greenhouse** (`greenhouse-api`): Tracks candidates for the junior-designer role Jessica plans to open as the firm grows.
- **Gusto** (`gusto-api`): Studio payroll and contractor payments.

#### Home, Travel & Local
- **Ring** (`ring-api`): The Grayhawk home doorbell and security cameras.
- **Zillow** (`zillow-api`): Comps for Kevin's spec listings and neighborhood values.
- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Forecasts for site visits and tennis mornings.
- **Google Maps** (`google-maps-api`): Navigation and timing for studio, sites, and errands.
- **Yelp** (`yelp-api`): Finding restaurants for client lunches and dinners out.
- **Uber** (`uber-api`): Rides to client dinners and airport runs.
- **Airbnb** (`airbnb-api`): Stays for Palm Springs modernism week and design trips.
- **Amadeus** (`amadeus-api`): Flights for East Coast family visits and design shows.
- **FedEx** (`fedex-api`): Shipping fabric samples and small furnishings to clients.
- **UPS** (`ups-api`): Tracking vendor deliveries and trade orders.
- **Shippo** (`shippo-api`): Printing labels for sample returns and artisan shipments.

#### Wellness & Movement

#### Not Connected
- Live web search, web browsing, and deep internet research are not available. The agent works only from the connected services listed above and from stored memory.
- Houzz Pro, the studio's project-management system, is separate and not connected to the assistant.
- Kevin's Spencer Development Group systems and his private accounts are not connected.
- Personal banking apps and the household's private investment accounts are not connected.
