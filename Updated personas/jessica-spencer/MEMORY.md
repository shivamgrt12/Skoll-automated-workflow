# Memory: Jessica Spencer

## Personal Profile
Jessica Spencer is the owner and lead designer of Spencer Interiors, a boutique residential design firm she built around a mid-century modern aesthetic warmed with comfort. She holds a BFA in interior design from Hudson School of Design in New York, and she designed her own Scottsdale home as a living showcase of her taste. She believes minimalism without soul is just empty rooms, and she runs her firm and her home with exacting standards, generous warmth, and a love of hosting. She grew up in New Jersey, keeps close ties to family on the East Coast, and carries her mother's Sunday-dinner tradition forward in her own household.

## Home & Living
A custom 4,500 sq ft home in Grayhawk, built 2018, contemporary desert modern, with the entire interior designed by Jessica as a showcase for clients. The entertaining hub is a pool and outdoor kitchen with a string-lit patio and custom fire pit. A converted casita serves as her home studio with a sample library, mood-board wall, and fabric archive. The guest room is periodically refreshed before Diane's visits. Landscaping is desert native with mature saguaros on a timed irrigation system. A 12kW solar system installed in 2022 covers about 80 percent of electricity. The smart home runs Lutron lighting, Sonos in every room, and Nest thermostats, with a Ring doorbell and cameras for security. A new Wolf range and Sub-Zero fridge were installed in January for $18,500. Pool maintenance is Desert Pool Pros at $180 a month, with a filter replacement to schedule. The 3-car garage holds Kevin's tools and storage on one side and Jessica's Lexus and overflow samples on the other.
