What we do

Support built around your child

Every plan starts by watching how your child moves, plays, and responds, then meeting them exactly where they are.

▣

Sensory processing

Support for children who are over or under responsive to everyday sights, sounds, and textures.

✎

Fine motor and handwriting

Playful, graded activities that build the hand skills used for writing, cutting, and self care.

☆

Feeding and oral motor

Calm, no pressure strategies that help cautious eaters widen what feels safe.

♡

Parent coaching

Simple home programs so the progress made in session carries into daily life.

About the practice

A solo practice with a personal touch

Long Pediatric Therapy is led by Jennifer Long, a pediatric occupational therapist who has built her practice around team based, family centered care. She reads a child the way a good teacher reads a classroom, noticing the small changes that matter.
