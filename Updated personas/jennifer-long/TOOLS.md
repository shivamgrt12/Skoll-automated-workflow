# Tools: Jennifer Long

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Practice Operations & Scheduling
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Parent and client session booking around the 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM practice hours.
- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Practice schedule, IEP meetings, kids' activities, and family events.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Intake forms and parent feedback surveys for new client families.
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): Caseload and IEP-season planning through the fall review stretch.
- **Asana** (`asana-api`): Sensory play manuscript tasks, working back from the December target.
- **Trello** (`trello-api`): Board tracking the 40 curriculum activities, 18 drafted and 22 remaining.
- **Linear** (`linear-api`): Manuscript sub-tasks shared with illustrator Helen Carter.
- **Jira** (`jira-api`): Issue tracking for the practice website and intake workflow, filed for the website vendor.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): Therapy activity library with materials lists and developmental targets.

#### Documents, Notes & Knowledge
- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Manuscript drafts, curriculum chapters, therapy activity templates, and IEP documentation.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Planning hub for the curriculum project and CE study notes.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Personal vault for clinical observations and activity ideas.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Reference knowledge base for protocols and intervention notes.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Backup of large scans, assessment files, and manuscript versions.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Secure storage for client-related documents kept separate from general files.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Signatures on the she-shed contract, insurance paperwork, and consent forms.

#### Communication & Email
- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Primary email for schools, pediatricians, insurance panels, suppliers, and personal correspondence.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Secondary account for insurance panel portals that require it.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Quick messages with Brian, family, and Meg.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Backup messaging for the running group.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Group chat with Ethan's robotics team parents.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Occasional virtual school IEP meetings.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Virtual parent consultations and telehealth check-ins.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Outbound appointment SMS reminders sent to client families.
- **SendGrid** (`sendgrid-api`): Transactional email for intake and registration confirmations.
- **Mailgun** (`mailgun-api`): Backup bulk-send service for parent updates.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): Observer access to Ethan's FIRST LEGO League team server.

#### Finance & Billing
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Practice books and the household budget Jennifer reviews monthly.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Backup accounting for the practice's CE and supply spend.
- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Bank links for the monthly financials review with Marta.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Online cash-pay session payments and future manuscript sales.
- **Square** (`square-api`): Point of sale for in-office cash-pay clients.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Supplier payments and Helen Carter's illustration invoice.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Tracks the small Bitcoin position Jennifer opened as a long-term experiment and surfaces the balance during her monthly savings review.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Pulls global crypto price references she compares against the Coinbase holding before deciding whether to add a little each quarter.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Watches staking yields on that same small holding and flags when a rate moves enough to be worth a look.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Monitors the family's long-term brokerage positions alongside the 529 and retirement savings and alerts her to swings worth raising with Linda Tran.

#### Continuing Education & Research
- **Google Classroom** (`google-classroom-api`): Hosted continuing education courses toward the 20-hour requirement.
- **OpenLibrary** (`openlibrary-api`): Catalog lookups for clinical references and reading.
- **NASA** (`nasa-api`): Space imagery for sensory and visual-motor therapy activities with kids.
- **ServiceNow** (`servicenow-api`): Files and tracks building-services requests for the leased Proctor District suite, like HVAC repairs and after-hours access with the property manager.

- **BambooHR** (`bamboohr-api`): Holds Marta's employment record, time off, and onboarding notes, ready to expand when the second OT joins.

#### Health, Fitness & Running
- **MyFitnessPal** (`myfitnesspal-api`): Loose tracking around the 20 to 25 weekly miles, consistency over calorie pressure.

- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Forecasts for early runs and garden care.

#### Home, Garden & Errands

#### Website, Publishing & Analytics
- **WordPress** (`wordpress-api`): The Long Pediatric Therapy public website.
- **Webflow** (`webflow-api`): A landing page for the sensory play guide.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): Structured content for parent handout pages.

#### Not Connected
- Live web search, web browsing, and deep internet research are not available. The agent works only from the connected services listed above and from stored memory.
- SimplePractice, the practice management system, is separate and not connected to the assistant.
- The Nest thermostat and Google Home speakers are not connected; the agent cannot see or control them.
- Personal banking apps and Brian's private accounts are not connected.
- No connected finance, brokerage, or crypto tool may move money or place a trade without Jennifer's explicit approval.
- Client family private accounts and school internal systems are not connected.
