The colophon

Made on a kitchen table

Thread & Bone is edited, laid out, and folded by Jennifer Williams, a poet and barista in Fox Point. Each run is printed locally at sixty copies, sold from a tote at the open mic for five dollars cash, and listed online for eight. Guest pages come from friends and first-time contributors. The tin does not make change with grace, so exact change is loved.
