# Tools: Jennifer Williams

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Writing, Publishing & Submissions
- **Substack** (`wordpress-api`): Drafts and schedules the "Marginal Notes" newsletter to its 340 subscribers, never sending without approval.
- **OpenLibrary** (`openlibrary-api`): Catalog lookups for the 3 to 4 library books Jennifer reads each month.
- **Mailchimp** (`mailchimp-api`): Holds the backup subscriber list and sends the zine announcement to readers who are not on Substack.
- **Klaviyo** (`klaviyo-api`): Segmented announcements for zine releases and reading dates.
- **SendGrid** (`sendgrid-api`): Transactional sends for zine order confirmations.
- **Mailgun** (`mailgun-api`): Delivers the larger quarterly newsletter blast when a release goes wide.
- **ActiveCampaign** (`activecampaign-api`): Follow-up sequences for readers who buy the Thread and Bone zine.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Reader sign-up and feedback forms for the zine and open mics.

#### Zine Storefront & Sales
- **Etsy** (`etsy-api`): The "Thread & Bone" zine shop, the main source of writing income.
- **Square** (`square-api`): Point of sale for zines at release parties and bookshop pop-ups.
- **WooCommerce** (`woocommerce-api`): Direct zine sales page linked from the Substack.
- **BigCommerce** (`bigcommerce-api`): Hosts the wholesale order page where local bookshops buy Thread and Bone in bulk.
- **Amazon Seller** (`amazon-seller-api`): Lists Thread and Bone for the occasional Amazon order and keeps its stock count in step with Etsy.
- **Shippo** (`shippo-api`): Prints labels for mailed zine orders and bookshop distribution.
- **FedEx** (`fedex-api`): Occasional rush shipment of a zine batch to an out-of-state reader.
- **UPS** (`ups-api`): Carries the bulk zine cartons to Symposium Books and other shops.

#### Communication & Calendar
- **Gmail** (`gmail-api`): Primary email for submissions, appointment confirmations, and personal mail. Draft only, never send without approval.
- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): Holds a handful of editors who only use Outlook; she checks it each week for submission replies.
- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Work shifts, submission deadlines, open mic dates, and friend meetups.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Booking link for readings and mentorship calls with Professor Wentworth.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): Quick messages with Reese and Dex.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Day-to-day chat with the local zine and open mic crowd.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): SMS reminders for zine release parties and reading slots.
- **Zoom** (`zoom-api`): Virtual readings and the occasional remote grant interview.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Used for the community arts grant committee call.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): The Alchemy staff channel for shift swaps and shop notes.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): Small poetry and zine-maker servers where Jennifer trades feedback and event news.

#### Documents, Notes & Drafts
- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Poetry drafts, zine layouts, cover letter templates, and the budget spreadsheet.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Backup of finished zine PDFs and high-resolution scans.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Archive of older manuscripts and past submission records she pulls from when assembling a chapbook.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Submission tracker and reading-list hub.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Personal vault of poem fragments, lines, and notebook scans.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Shared notes for the Providence Poetry Festival volunteer team.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Signing publication agreements and reading honorarium forms.
- **Figma** (`figma-api`): Zine cover and layout design alongside Canva.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): Structured content store powering her poetry portfolio site.
- **Webflow** (`webflow-api`): The author landing page linking the zine and newsletter.
- **Algolia** (`algolia-api`): Search across the archived Substack posts and zine back issues.

#### Money, Budget & Banking
- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Links the Navigant Credit Union accounts so the monthly budget check pulls real balances.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Processes zine sales and reading honorarium payouts.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): Splits the Spotify bill with Dex and collects small zine payments.
- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Light bookkeeping for zine income and submission-fee expenses.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Keeps the year-end ledger for writing income at tax time.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Pulls Bitcoin prices as raw material for a poem sequence on speculation and value.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Tracks global coin prices for the same money-and-value poems, setting hype beside a barista wage.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Cross-checks coin prices against the other exchanges so the poem numbers stay honest.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Pulls stock-market data for the speculation poems, putting market noise next to an hourly tip jar.

#### Open Mics, Events & Local Life
- **Eventbrite** (`eventbrite-api`): Ticketing and RSVPs for the zine release party and festival slots.
- **Ticketmaster** (`ticketmaster-api`): Show tickets when a favorite act comes through and the budget allows.
- **Yelp** (`yelp-api`): Finding cheap eats and meeting spots around Fox Point.
- **Google Maps** (`google-maps-api`): Bus and bike routes around Providence and to Warwick.
- **Uber** (`uber-api`): Books a late-night ride home from a reading when the buses have already stopped.
- **Airbnb** (`airbnb-api`): Cheap stays for the monthly Boston visits with Reese.
- **Amadeus** (`amadeus-api`): Flight lookups for a possible out-of-state writing residency.
- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Deciding bike versus bus on a given Providence morning.

#### Health & Wellness
- **MyFitnessPal** (`myfitnesspal-api`): Loose tracking of vegetarian meals, B12, and Vitamin D3 without calorie pressure.
- **Strava** (`strava-api`): Logging daily walks and bike commutes, Jennifer's primary movement.
- **Ring** (`ring-api`): Checks the shared front-door camera at the Fox Point house when a package or a guest is due.
- **Zillow** (`zillow-api`): Checks Providence rents each month in case the housemate situation changes.
- **Instacart** (`instacart-api`): Orders a grocery run when a double shift runs long and the budget allows it.
- **DoorDash** (`doordash-api`): Brings a hot dinner to the house on a deadline night, charged against the dining-out line.

#### Music, Reading & Media
- **Spotify** (`spotify-api`): Indie folk and lo-fi for drafting, classical for editing, split with Dex.
- **YouTube** (`youtube-api`): Recorded readings, poetry craft talks, and Canva zine tutorials.
- **Vimeo** (`vimeo-api`): Hosts her archived open mic sets and festival readings for sharing.
- **TMDB** (`tmdb-api`): Looking up arthouse films for movie nights with Nia.
- **Reddit** (`reddit-api`): Poetry, zine-making, and frugal-living communities for ideas.
- **Pinterest** (`pinterest-api`): Zine layout and cover-art inspiration.
- **Instagram** (`instagram-api`): The @jennifer.writes poetry account, around 1,200 followers.
- **Twitter** (`twitter-api`): Follows the poetry community and Providence arts news, and posts the odd reading announcement.
- **LinkedIn** (`linkedin-api`): Profile kept current for grant and residency credibility.
- **Twitch** (`twitch-api`): Drops into a few writing-stream channels for company on a long drafting night.
- **NASA** (`nasa-api`): Image archive mined for ekphrastic poems about deep space.

#### Outreach, Support & Analytics
- **HubSpot** (`hubspot-api`): Keeps the contact list of editors and presses for the Providence Poetry Festival outreach she helps run.
- **Salesforce** (`salesforce-api`): Updates donor records for the Providence Poetry Festival during her volunteer outreach shifts.
- **Zendesk** (`zendesk-api`): Answers the occasional zine-order question from an Etsy buyer.
- **Intercom** (`intercom-api`): Runs the chat widget on the author landing page so readers can ask about zine orders.
- **Freshdesk** (`freshdesk-api`): Catches zine customer questions that come in outside the Etsy inbox.
- **Mixpanel** (`mixpanel-api`): Tracks which festival pages readers open for the volunteer team's post-event recap.
- **Segment** (`segment-api`): Routes the festival site's sign-up events into the tools the volunteer team reports from.
- **Amplitude** (`amplitude-api`): Builds the festival's sign-up-funnel report she shares with the volunteer organizers.
- **PostHog** (`posthog-api`): Watches how readers move through the festival's site during event week.
- **Google Analytics** (`google-analytics-api`): Tracks traffic on the zine shop and the newsletter archive to see which issues land.

#### Work & Shop Operations
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): The Alchemy staff board for shift swaps and supply notes.
- **Asana** (`asana-api`): Zine release party planning with Joon.
- **Trello** (`trello-api`): Personal board tracking poems out on submission.
- **Linear** (`linear-api`): Logs the small fixes she requests for the author website the volunteer dev maintains.
- **Jira** (`jira-api`): Files and follows bug tickets for the festival volunteer site she helps test.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): The master submission database with deadlines and responses.
- **BambooHR** (`bamboohr-api`): Pulls her own Alchemy pay and time-off details when she plans days around readings.
- **Greenhouse** (`greenhouse-api`): Watches the part-time arts postings the festival shares for steadier seasonal work.
- **Gusto** (`gusto-api`): Where Alchemy paystubs and tax forms land.
- **GitHub** (`github-api`): Keeps the zine-distribution scripts she and Joon use to push bookshop stock lists.
- **GitLab** (`gitlab-api`): Mirrors the author site repo as a backup she can restore from.
- **Kubernetes** (`kubernetes-api`): Restarts the festival site's hosting service when it stalls on a high-traffic event night.
- **Datadog** (`datadog-api`): Watches the festival and author sites for slowdowns during event week.
- **Sentry** (`sentry-api`): Catches errors on the author landing page and forwards the details to the volunteer dev.
- **Cloudflare** (`cloudflare-api`): Manages DNS and caching for the zine and newsletter domains when she swaps a page.
- **PagerDuty** (`pagerduty-api`): Carries the festival site's on-call alerts during event week so outages get answered fast.
- **Okta** (`okta-api`): Signs into the festival's volunteer tools through one shared login.
- **ServiceNow** (`servicenow-api`): Files venue facilities tickets for the festival, like a broken mic or a locked door.
- **Google Classroom** (`google-classroom-api`): Runs the poetry workshop Jennifer occasionally co-teaches.

#### Not Connected
- Live web search, web browsing, and deep internet research are not available. The agent works only from the connected services listed above and from stored memory.
- HotSchedules, the barista scheduling app, is phone only and not connected.
- The Navigant Credit Union banking app is phone only and not connected for transactions.
- Jennifer's personal social media on her phone is hers to post from; the agent does not publish on her behalf.
- The crypto and brokerage tools are used for price data and poem research only; no account holds trading authorization.
