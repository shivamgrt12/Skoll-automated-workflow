#### Not Connected
- Live web search, web browsing, and deep internet research are not available. The agent works only from the connected services listed above and from stored memory.
- Keystone Legal Aid work email system is a separate internal system, not connected to this workspace.
- Signal stays the channel for sensitive client communications, but it is not connected via API.
- Personal banking apps live on Grace's phone and are not connected through the agent.
- Court electronic filing systems, including ECF and PACER, route through Keystone Legal Aid directly and are not connected here.
