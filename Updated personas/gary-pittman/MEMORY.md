# Memory: Gary Pittman

## Personal Profile

- **Full name**: Gary Pittman, born and raised in Aberdeen, South Dakota. Has never lived anywhere else.
- **Education**: Graduated from Aberdeen Central High School in 1974. Worked maintenance and handyman jobs around Aberdeen before taking electrical and plumbing courses through WITCC in 1978.
- **Language**: Speaks English natively with a few German phrases from his mother and grandparents, mostly food words and prayers.
- **Personality**: Quiet, steady, and private. Does not share easily, but when he does it carries real weight.
- **Routine**: Values daily structure because it holds the shape of his days together, especially after losing Marlene.
- **Faith**: Lutheran, lived rather than spoken about. Shows up, helps out, and does not make a fuss about it.
- **Philosophy**: Do useful work, take care of your people, and leave things better than you found them.
- **Community role**: Volunteers chair-and-sound logistics for the Aberdeen veterans memorial events in honor of his father Robert Sr.'s WWII Navy service. Gary is not a veteran himself; he helps as a community member.

## Key Relationships

- **Marlene Pittman (late wife, born January 8, 1956, d. December 2023)**: The center of everything for 43 years. Her absence defines Gary's current life. He keeps her photo on the kitchen counter, tends her garden in summer, and sits in their shared pew at church. He is not looking to move on, just learning to live in the house without her.
- **Karen Pittman-Dahl (daughter, born March 15, 1982)**: Lives in Sioux Falls with husband Roger and two kids (Nate, born January 9, 2010; Emma, born March 22, 2013). Calls Gary every other day. Worries about him being alone. She set up the assistant on his phone and visits once a month, sometimes more. ICE contact, medical proxy, and power of attorney holder.
- **Jeff Pittman (son, born October 22, 1984)**: Lives in Bismarck, ND. Works in oil field services. Divorced, one son (Tyler, born February 18, 2016). Calls less often than Karen but shows up when it matters. Gary and Jeff are similar: they do not talk about feelings, but they understand each other.
- **Roger Dahl (son-in-law, 45)**: Karen's husband. Network engineer in Sioux Falls. Built the small home server in Gary's basement that keeps the thermostat and Ring camera monitored.
- **Robert Pittman Sr. (father, born February 7, 1928, d. 2003)**: WWII Navy veteran, Pacific theater. Worked at the Aberdeen post office for thirty-four years until retirement in 1991. Quiet, blunt, and steady. Gary inherited his practicality and his habit of showing up for veterans' memorial events without making a fuss.
- **Margaret (Mueller) Pittman (mother, born November 24, 1930, d. 2011)**: German Lutheran on both sides of her family. Bethlehem Lutheran's church organist for thirty years. Gary's German phrases came from her kitchen. Buried beside Robert at Riverside Cemetery.
- **Siblings**: Only child.
- **Pastor Linda Sorenson (58)**: Senior pastor at Bethlehem Lutheran. Gary's employer and pastor. She checks on him gently without being invasive. Gary appreciates that she does not push.
- **Barb Kleinsasser (church secretary, 52)**: Bethlehem Lutheran administrator. Logs maintenance requests, runs the bulletin, and hands off the Friday newsletter draft for Gary to send.
- **Dale Gustafson (best friend, born December 3, 1953)**: Retired postal carrier. Friends since high school. They share morning coffee at the Koffee Kup Kafe and talk about weather, sports, and town gossip. Neither talks about hard things, which is how they both prefer it.
- **Elaine Hoff (neighbor, 68)**: Widowed, lives across the street. Brings Gary a casserole once a week. They watch out for each other's houses. She lost her husband in 2021, so there is an unspoken understanding.
- **Dr. Robert Strand (primary care, 60)**: At Avera St. Luke's, Aberdeen. Gary's doctor for 20 years.

## Work & Projects

- **Current role**: Part-time custodian and handyman at Bethlehem Lutheran Church in Aberdeen, roughly 15 hours per week.
- **Work schedule**: Part-time mornings three days per week at the church (Monday, Wednesday, Friday, 7:30 AM to 12:30 PM CT).
- **Responsibilities**: Building maintenance, minor repairs, event setup and teardown, and seasonal groundskeeping for the church property.
- **Supervisor**: Pastor Linda Sorenson is his employer. Barb Kleinsasser is the church secretary who logs maintenance requests.
- **Building committee**: Gary sits on the second-Tuesday building committee, presents the month's maintenance log, and owns the multi-week renovation projects (basement classroom refresh, sanctuary lighting upgrade) currently in flight.
- **Previous career**: Head custodian at Aberdeen Central High School for 38 years, from 1980 until retirement in 2018.

## Finance

- **Total income**: Approximately $3,250 per month from Social Security, church part-time work, and school district pension combined.
- **Social Security**: Approximately $1,850 per month, the largest single income source in Gary's fixed-income household budget.
- **Church pay**: Approximately $600 per month for part-time custodian and handyman work at Bethlehem Lutheran Church.
- **District pension**: Approximately $800 per month from the Aberdeen School District for 38 years of custodial service.
- **Monthly expenses**: Approximately $1,320 per month covering property tax, utilities, groceries, insurance, Medicare, medications, and household essentials.
- **Monthly surplus**: Approximately $1,930 per month remaining after expenses, directed to savings, grandkids' gifts, and home repairs.
- **Savings**: $34,000 in Dacotah Bank savings account. Marlene's life insurance paid off the house and all medical bills.
- **Debt status**: None. House paid off, truck paid off, no credit card debt. Gary lives fully within his means.
- **Banking**: Dacotah Bank checking and savings with one Visa card at $2,000 limit, credit score approximately 800.

## Health & Wellness

- **COPD**: Mild to moderate, diagnosed 2019. Former smoker who quit in 2005 after 25 years of smoking.
- **COPD management**: Daily Spiriva inhaler and albuterol rescue inhaler as needed. Flares in cold weather and with exertion.
- **Osteoarthritis**: Progressive condition in hands and knees, worse in cold weather. Managed with acetaminophen and staying active.
- **Medications**: Spiriva daily, albuterol as needed, acetaminophen daily, lisinopril 10mg for blood pressure, and vitamin D daily.
- **Primary care**: Dr. Robert Strand at Avera St. Luke's in Aberdeen. Gary's doctor for over 20 years.
- **Pulmonologist**: Dr. Christine Agard at Avera Pulmonology in Aberdeen for COPD monitoring and pulmonary function tests.
- **Dental care**: Dr. Keith Olson at Aberdeen Family Dentistry for checkups every six months for routine dental care.
- **Vision care**: Wears bifocals with annual exams from Dr. Amy Richter at Prairie Eye Care in Aberdeen.
- **Exercise**: Daily walks of 20 to 30 minutes, weather permitting. Church work and gardening provide additional mild activity.
- **Sleep pattern**: In bed by 9:00 PM, wakes at 5:30 AM. Sometimes wakes at 3:00 AM and reads the paper.
- **Mental health**: Grieving but functional after Marlene's death. Manages through routine, Dale's friendship, and staying busy with work.
- **Dietary restriction**: Doctor advised watching sodium intake. Gary tries to follow the guidance, though not always strictly.

## Interests & Hobbies

- **Gardening**: Tends Marlene's garden in the warm months, growing tomatoes, cucumbers, and beans alongside her perennial beds. The off-season is for planning the next year's beds at the kitchen table.
- **Reading**: Reads the Aberdeen American News daily. Enjoys Louis L'Amour westerns and Clive Cussler novels from the library.
- **Television**: Watches evening news, Wheel of Fortune, Jeopardy, and westerns on broadcast TV most evenings before bed.
- **Sports**: Follows Aberdeen Central loosely out of old loyalty. Watches the Minnesota Twins on TV during baseball season.
- **Handyman work**: Does small repairs for neighbors, fixing leaky faucets and stuck doors. More longtime habit than active hobby.
- **Bible**: Reads the Bible on Sundays out of lifelong habit. Faith is part of routine, not performance.
- **Veterans memorial volunteering**: Helps the Aberdeen veterans organization with chair setup, sound equipment, and parking at the Veterans Day ceremony and the Memorial Day observance.

- **House style**: Two-bedroom, one-bathroom bungalow built in 1952 with a small front porch and detached single-car garage.
- **Interior layout**: Kitchen, living room, Gary's bedroom, and Karen's old room used for grandkid visits. Basement with laundry, tools, and a small home server rack Roger installed in 2024.
- **Backyard**: Marlene's garden with perennials and a vegetable plot. Garage has a workbench and basic repair tools.
