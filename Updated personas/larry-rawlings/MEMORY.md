# Memory: Larry Rawlings

## Personal Profile

Larry Michael Rawlings is a Black man from the South. Born in Raleigh, moved to Charlotte at 25. Glad of the distance from his parents even as he loves them; geography runs deep for him.

Runs warm and high-frequency, talks with his hands, laughs easily at his own missteps. Under the buzz, a more deliberate person who second-guesses himself privately, especially about money and long commitments. Aware the slow-yes pattern is not purely virtue.

His brain is a pinball machine of ideas and creative impulses; the restlessness is fuel, not avoidance. Fiercely independent to his own detriment, default "I'll figure it out myself," but getting better at delegating.

Photography is how he processes; he notices light, color, composition in everything. Told he is "a lot," and fine with it. His work lives in a specifically Black Southern visual tradition he has only recently started naming.

Carries the inheritances of a respectable, modest, save-the-receipts household, kept and discarded deliberately. Believes the work is what is real, not its announcement; in showing up for people he committed to and being honest when he cannot; and that grown men should have therapists, which is why he keeps attending sessions even when he resists the work.

**Education**: B.A. in Communications, UNC Greensboro, 2015. Minor in Studio Art.

**Languages**: English (native). Conversational Spanish, practiced with friends and in an occasional class.

**Relationship status**: Single. The last relationship ended about a year ago. Not actively dating; he calls it intentional space.

## Interests & Hobbies

- **Photography** as the through line: started in high school with his uncle's film camera, shoots mostly digital now, has returned to film for personal work. He likes the way you slow down when every frame costs you.
- **The Porch Light series**: photographs of porches across Charlotte made at the same hour every evening. He is conscious of the Black Southern visual tradition he is contributing to and where he wants to push against it.
- **Travel as creative fuel**: came back from Morocco with frames he is still processing, from Portugal with a hard drive he has not fully looked through. Next big want is Japan. After that, Colombia. He goes deep instead of collecting countries.
- **Cooking**: enjoys the process more than the result. Sheet pans and good intentions most weeknights. Real meals when Simone comes over.
- **Yoga**: not for the philosophy, for the shoulders. The studio teacher does not talk too much, which he appreciates.
- **Vinyl record stores** for the browsing, not the buying. Museums in the quiet ten minutes before the rest of the visitors find the room.
- **Reading**: photography monographs, creative memoirs, currently rereading *On Photography* by Susan Sontag.
- **TV**: limited. Docuseries and travel shows. Currently into *Abstract: The Art of Design*.

## Preferences

- **Music**: R&B, neo-soul, indie. Spotify playlists tagged by editing project. Always on while editing.
- **Reading**: Photography monographs, creative memoirs, photography blogs.
- **TV**: Docuseries and travel shows, a small slice of his time.
- **Travel**: Loves it. Budget travel, Airbnbs, walking until his feet give out, then sitting in the nearest cafe. One camera body, two lenses, three changes of clothes. Cities you have to sit in for three days. He cooks at least one meal in any apartment he rents.
- **Clothing**: Earth tones, well-worn denim. Brown, olive, rust, cream. Vintage when he can. Leather jacket from a flea market in Lisbon he will wear until it falls apart. Pays attention to fit more than brand.
- **Food and drink**: Oat milk latte every morning at the NoDa coffee shop, same order. Eats semi-healthy with comfort-food detours. Wine with Simone at brunch, occasional cocktails out, no alcohol kept at home. Snacks: trail mix, dark chocolate, apple with peanut butter.
- **Shopping**: Online mostly. Amazon for basics, local boutiques for clothes occasionally, slow art purchases (small prints from photographers he admires).
- **Aesthetic**: Likes a clean white wall behind a subject. Likes a window that throws diagonal light. Likes warm-bulb light at home. Cares about the feel of objects: weight of a camera body, texture of a leather notebook, the give of his preferred pen.
- **Sensory world**: Notices light first, always. Sensitive to refrigerator hum, fluorescent whine, and a particular kind of urgent-sounding pop music. Headphones for cafes that play the wrong thing. Loves a cold morning; the South wore him down on heat.
- **Comfort and decompression**: Editing with the playlist on and overhead lights dimmed. No-shoot walks around NoDa at six in the evening with the camera and no plan. Brunch with Simone. Phone calls with Terrell that have no agenda. Sundays where he refuses to feel guilty about doing nothing.
- **Social cadence**: Small intentional circle. Texts and voice notes over phone calls (except Clarice and Simone). Responds within a day, not within an hour, no apology. Selective about non-work events. Will not go to network.
- **Communication style with him**: Casual, warm, observational. Short when short will do. Practical help first when he is stressed.
- **Dislikes**: Being negotiated down on rates. Flash photography in restaurants. Group texts above six. Performative busyness. A microwave running past 90 seconds. Movie-narrators. Condescension dressed up as concern. Hot weather, which he resents annually.
