About

Running is where the docket can't follow.

I'm a public defender at the Cook County Public Defender's Office. Four years of criminal defense work means I need the lakefront trail the way most people need coffee. It resets the clock.

I run with the Lakefront Pacers on Saturday mornings, do speed work at the lakefront track on Tuesdays, and chase tempo splits on Thursdays before 7 AM. The goal has always been to race myself, not just the course.

Wicker Park, Chicago. Train hard, represent clients harder.
