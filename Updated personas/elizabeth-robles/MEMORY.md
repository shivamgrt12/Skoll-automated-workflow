# Memory: Elizabeth Robles

## Personal Profile
- **Heritage**: Cuban-American, daughter of Havana-born parents, raised bilingual in Spanish and English
- **Cuban identity**: Specific rather than general, rooted in particular foods, music, family structures, and exile-community life
- **Education**: BA in Hospitality Management from Florida International University, Class of 2000
- **Core character**: Competence so practiced it looks effortless, warm with edges, slow to forgive laziness
- **Professional instinct**: Fifteen-plus years anticipating what wealthy strangers need gives her a near-instinctive read on rooms and moods
- **Under stress**: Becomes more organized rather than less and channels pressure into structured action
- **Father**: Passed away in 2019, grief surfaces around certain smells, songs, and echoes in Valentina
- **Values**: Excellence as self-respect not perfection, family as an earned category rather than biological default
- **Post-divorce pride**: Quiet pride in having rebuilt her life with the condo, the career, and a thriving daughter
- **Marriage**: Married to Diego Robles from 2005 to 2023, divorce finalized February 2023

- **Recruited**: Joined Palmetto Grand in September 2010 on the strength of her guest-services reputation and local connections
- **Current role**: Senior Concierge at Palmetto Grand Resort & Spa, 1800 Oceanfront Promenade, Miami Beach, FL 33139
- **Tenure**: Nearly 16 years at a luxury beachfront property with roughly 320 employees
- **Scope**: VIP guest requests end to end including reservations, event tickets, tours, yacht charters, and personal shopping
- **Team position**: Most senior of six concierges, handles highest-profile guests, reports to James Whitfield
- **Schedule**: Shifts rotate weekly, 7:00 AM to 3:30 PM or 2:00 PM to 10:30 PM, including some weekends
- **Peak season**: December through April covering snowbird season, spring break, and Art Basel in December
- **Annual review**: Performance review with James Whitfield each October, focused on VIP guest satisfaction scores
- **Current focus**: Summer planning with Latin American and European guests picking up in June
