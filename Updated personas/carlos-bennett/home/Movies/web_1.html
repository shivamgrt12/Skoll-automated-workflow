What I do

Bench services

Thirty years of saltwater humidity have taught me what a guitar needs to stay playable down here. Every job leaves with a fresh setup.

Fret level and crown

Leveling, recrowning, and polishing to clear out buzz and dead spots, finished with a full action and intonation setup.

Neck resets

Steaming and refitting the dovetail, resetting relief and geometry on instruments that have given up on a comfortable action.

Full restoration

Crack repair, bridge plate work, refinishing where it earns its keep, and color matched touch up that respects the original wood.

Electronics and wiring

Pickup swaps, pot and cap work, and clean solder joints on vintage electrics, with original parts kept where they belong.

Mandolin and banjo

Setups and repairs for the local bluegrass players, from head tension to fretwork and bridge fitting.

Seasonal checkups

Humidity here moves wood. Bring it back in a season and I will chase the relief and the saddle height for the new weather.
