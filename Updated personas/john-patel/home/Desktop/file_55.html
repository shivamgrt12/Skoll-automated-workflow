Research

Current manuscript under review.

Point of Care Ultrasound in Early Trauma Assessment

John Patel, MD, and Amara Okafor, MD · Journal of Academic Emergency Practice

A prospective look at whether a short, fixed bedside ultrasound protocol moves the disposition decision earlier. Median time to decision fell from 42 to 28 minutes, with no rise in missed findings.

Manuscript JAEP-2026-0142 · under editorial review
