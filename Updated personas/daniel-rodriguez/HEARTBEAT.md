- **Friday, 8:00 AM**: Morning run, 3 miles, Battery waterfront loop.

### Monthly

- **1st of each month**: Budget check. Transfer savings to the food truck fund and review expenses.
- **15th of each month**: Inhaler refill check. Confirm there is enough albuterol for the month.

### Annual

- **Around January 15**: Annual physical with Dr. Sato at Palmetto Primary Care.

- **March 14**: Daniel's birthday. Low-key celebration, usually dinner with Willa and beers with Danny.
- **Around November 18**: Friendsgiving potluck with Danny and the Charleston restaurant crew on a Monday off.
- **Around December 20**: Holiday care package from Rosa with dried chiles, spices, and tamales. Family call with Houston and Austin.
