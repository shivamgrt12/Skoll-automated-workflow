About

I run the hot line at Linden & Rye, an upscale Southern fusion kitchen in downtown Charleston, where I lead a prep team of four and develop seasonal dishes. I grew up in Houston, trained at the Gulf Coast Culinary Institute, and I cook the food I love best: honest, fermented, charred, and built with care.

Linea Taco + Torta Co. in build

A tight menu done right. Al pastor off a real trompo, slow carnitas, a rotating smoked special, and pressed tortas on good telera bread. Salsas made and fermented in house. The name is the kitchen line, the through line from family table to restaurant to the street.

  • Base. Shared commissary kitchen in West Ashley, the setup the city requires for a mobile vendor.
  • Route. Weekday office lunch, brewery lots in the evening, weekend markets and events.
  • Timeline. Permits and equipment through 2026, target open in the fourth quarter.
  • Salsas. Salsa macha, fermented hot sauces, lacto pineapple, all made ahead.
  • Signature. Pecan smoked duck taco with garlic honey glaze and curtido.
  • Crew. Year one is me on the line with one helper, and Danny consulting on menu.
