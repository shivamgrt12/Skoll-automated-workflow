About the work

A carpenter who thinks with his hands. Clean joints, level cabinets, and furniture built to last.

I am a finish carpenter working toward journeyman and a small shop of my own. By day I build kitchens, trim, and cabinets. By night and on weekends I build furniture at a bench in the garage.

The approach is simple. Mill the wood true, cut the joinery tight, and finish it so it feels good in the hand. Mortise and tenon over screws. Dovetails where they count. No shortcuts that show up in five years.

Most of what I build starts as a sketch and a conversation. If you have an idea for a table, a cabinet, or a built in, I would rather build the right thing than the fast thing.
