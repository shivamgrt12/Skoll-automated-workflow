About

A short bio,
in case you skipped the homepage.

MSN, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 2024 Fox Valley Women's Health Clinic, Green Bay Quality lead, since Q4 2025

I went into women's health because a clinician believed me when I described my own period pain. Six years and one stage III endometriosis diagnosis later, I do the same job for other people.

My clinic work is the day job. The writing and the peer mentorship are how I share what I have learned with patients and with new NPs who are still finding their footing.

APRN-NP, Wisconsin AANP Women's Health WNA member ACOG patient advocate
