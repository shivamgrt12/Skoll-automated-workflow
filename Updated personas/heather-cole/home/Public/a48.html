FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to be a women's health NP to join?

Not strictly. About a quarter of past mentees were family NPs with a women's health focus, or RNs in NP programs. If your weekly work overlaps a lot with mine, you will get value.

How do you handle confidentiality on shared cases?

We talk about cases in de-identified form only. Names, dates, and identifying details get stripped or changed before any conversation, including the Signal group.

What if I have to miss a session?

You get one make-up slot per cohort, scheduled in the same month. Async questions are always open in the group.

Can my employer pay for this?

Yes. Sponsored placements are how about a third of mentees join. I will invoice your employer directly and provide quarterly outcome notes.

When does cohort 6 start?

October 5, 2026. Applications close October 1, 2026 or when the four open slots fill.
