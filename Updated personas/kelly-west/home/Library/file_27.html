How I can help

Students

A quiet place to think out loud, sort a worry, or plan the high school transition. No problem is too small to bring by.

Families

Help with course selection, the 8th grade timeline, and connecting to the right support when something at home gets heavy.

Teachers

A second set of eyes on a student you are worried about, and a fast path to referral when it is needed.

Quick resources

Call or text 988, Suicide and Crisis Lifeline, 24 hours Text HOME to 741741, Crisis Text Line Dial 211 for housing, food, and utility help in Virginia Weekend Backpack Program, food support for families

A note on confidentiality

What a student shares with me stays with me, within the limits of safety. If I am ever worried that a student is at risk, I will involve the right people to keep them safe, and I will be honest about that from the start.
