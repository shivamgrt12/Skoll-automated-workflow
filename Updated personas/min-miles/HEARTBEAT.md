# Heartbeat: Min Miles Park

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- **Daily, 5:30 AM**: Wake. Cafe con leche at the kitchen counter. During harvest (November to January), shift to 4:30 AM.
- **Daily, 6:30 AM**: Grove walk with Pedro. Inspect trees, check irrigation lines, note pest signs.
- **Daily, evening**: Estate perimeter walk, same path, same direction. No phone.
- **Daily, 10:30 PM**: Lights out target. Earlier during harvest.

### Weekly

- **Monday, 6:00 AM**: Weekly grove inspection block with Pedro. Walk the groves, check irrigation lines and tree health, and log anything that needs attention.
- **Tuesday, 4:00 PM**: Buyer correspondence check. Review pending emails from Javier Romero and export contacts. Soo-Jin runs the Baena market today.
- **Wednesday, 10:00 AM**: Professor Castillo visit every other Wednesday for the soil and pest check. Prepare grove notes the day before, then record-keeping for the east grove organic field diary in the afternoon.
- **Thursday, 4:30 PM**: Cooperativa Oleicola Sierra Morena meeting every other Thursday at the hall in Baena. Review agenda and prepare motions in advance.
- **Friday, 6:00 PM**: Weekly review with Soo-Jin on sales, market schedule, and supply needs. Family dinner at Meson La Almazara at 8:30 PM.
- **Saturday, 9:00 AM**: Recreational football with the village men, errands in town after, then family time and a grove walk with Hana when she is around.
- **Sunday, late morning**: Korean BBQ at the cortijo, Min cooking on his father's half-barrel grill. Soon-Hee visits or the family visits her, then week planning with Soo-Jin in the evening and an early bed.

### Monthly

- **1st of each month**: Cooperative dues payment. Verify processed. Review euro account balance and the monthly budget.
- **15th of each month**: Organic certification log update. Field diary and input records for the east grove.

### Seasonal / Variable

- **November to January**: Harvest season. All hands on deck, seasonal workers scheduled, pressing rhythm dominates the calendar.
- **March to May**: Pruning windows, blossom monitoring, and pest watch for olive fruit fly precursors.
- **September**: Chuseok lunch with Soon-Hee, David, Jenny, and the family. A quiet ritual, long lunch with old photographs.

### Annual

- **Annual, January**: Annual checkup with Dr. Alejandro Fuentes at Centro de Salud Baena.
- **Annual, November**: Annual skin check with Dr. Pilar Vega in Cordoba.
- **Annual, December 3**: Min's birthday. Family dinner at the cortijo.
- **Annual, December 8**: Soo-Jin's birthday. Quiet dinner, gift from a Korean artisan researched 4 weeks ahead.
- **Annual, December 11**: David's birthday. Tools or a single bottle of good wine.
- **Annual, December 14**: Hana's birthday. Family dinner at the cortijo, book gift chosen carefully.
- **Annual, December 17**: Jenny's birthday. Card and shared family meal with David's household.
- **Annual, December 19**: Ethan's birthday. Football gear and a cake at the cortijo.
- **Annual, December 22**: Soon-Hee's birthday. Long lunch in Baena, miyeok-guk in the morning.
- **Annual, December 25**: Christmas at the finca with family.
