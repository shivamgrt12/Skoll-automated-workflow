Our story

A small estate, by choice

Finca Miles sits four kilometres outside Baena, in the hills of the Subbetica. The family has farmed Picual and Hojiblanca here for two generations. The east grove is finishing its conversion to certified organic, with certification expected in the first quarter of 2027. We press a smaller harvest well rather than a larger one adequately, and we keep the welcome personal.

Picual first

Green, aromatic and peppery early-harvest oil, cold extracted and high in polyphenols.

Traceable

Every premium lot traces to its parcel and its pressing, with a certificate of analysis.

Korean and Spanish

A table that sets aceitunas and banchan side by side, the way the family eats at home.
