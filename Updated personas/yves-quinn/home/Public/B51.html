FAQ

Things people ask first

Do you cook in a commercial kitchen? +
Cuisine du Nord operates out of a licensed home kitchen in Beaverton under an Oregon Domestic Kitchen License. For larger volume jobs we rent time at Culinary Crossroads, a shared commercial space in Murray Scholls.
What is your minimum order? +
25 guests for the Classic and Premium tiers. The Reveillon and holiday menus start at 40 covers because of the multi-course structure and longer prep window.
How far ahead do I need to book? +
Two weeks for standard tiers, four weeks for Reveillon, eight weeks for anything over 100 covers. December books out by early November in most years.
Do you handle dietary swaps? +
Gluten-free crusts, dairy-free filling, vegetarian tourtiere with lentils and mushrooms, and a kimchi fusion variant developed with chef Sienna Park are all standard.
What is the deposit and cancellation policy? +
50 percent deposit holds the date, refundable up to 14 days before the event. Inside 14 days the deposit becomes a credit toward a future booking within 12 months.
