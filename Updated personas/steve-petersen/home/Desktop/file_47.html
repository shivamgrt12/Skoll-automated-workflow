About the Maker

Steve Petersen Jr. learned the trades on a Pittsburgh mill floor and now builds furniture by hand in his South Side garage. Hand tools hang on the pegboard above the workbench his father built in 1968. The radio stays low, the rags stay in the can, and every rocking horse is built one at a time. Proceeds from the parish pieces support the St. Michael's craft fair on December 13, 2026.
