Current Issue

The 2026-2027 Contract Fight

Management has put a proposal on the table that would increase the average member's healthcare costs by over a thousand dollars a year. Here is where things stand.

Management Proposal

12% Premium Increase

+$1,044/yr

A jump from 8% to 20% employee share of healthcare premiums. In plain terms, that is roughly an extra $87 out of every member's monthly paycheck.

Union Counter

3% Maximum Increase

+$261/yr

The negotiating committee is holding the line at a 3% maximum increase, bringing the employee share from 8% to 11%. That is where we intend to stay.

Key Date

Contract Expires January 31, 2027

The next negotiation session with management is set for October 28, 2026. The committee is doing the homework between now and then - costing every proposal, checking it against the master agreement, and building the case.

Status

Grievance Caseload Active

The local currently carries between eight and twelve active grievance cases at any time. The highest-profile matter involves a 20-year employee whose wrongful discharge is headed toward arbitration.

About Terry Dawson

TD

Shawn "Terry" Dawson

Northwest Detroit, MI
Irongate Motor Assembly Plant
Local 247 - Allied Auto Workers
Union Steward Deacon Coach Detroit Native

Fourth-generation Detroit. Thirty years on the chassis line.

I started on the trim line in 1996 and have been a union steward for the last nine years. My job is to make sure every worker on that floor gets what the contract says they are owed - no more waiting, no more runarounds.

Outside the plant, I serve as a deacon at Greater Hope Baptist Church on Pembroke, where I have been for seven years. I also coach the Westside Wolverines in the Detroit Metro Parks League - four seasons of scorebooks and a team that wins on contact and defense.

I have been offered a regional union role in Indianapolis twice. Turned it down both times. Detroit made me. The work is here.
