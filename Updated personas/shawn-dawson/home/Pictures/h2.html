Head Coach Notes

Approach and Strategy

We win on contact and defense. These are the principles we carry into every game.

Lineup Philosophy

  • Lawson sets the table at leadoff - speed and bat control
  • Bishop follows with contact and patience
  • Robinson and Mendez drive them in - best contact hitters slot 3 and 4
  • Okafor at 5 is the RBI man
  • Marcus slots in at 7 when home from school

Situational Rules

  • Late and close: pinch run Brooks for Carter
  • Against a lefty: move Sanders up, nobody sits
  • Double header: rest Okafor in game two, Bishop catches
  • Never swing for the fence - make contact and let defense work

Practice Schedule

Practices run Tuesday and Thursday evenings, 6:30 to 8:30 PM. Raymond Okafor handles drills and practice logistics. Coach Dawson focuses on game strategy, lineup decisions, and matchup preparation for each Saturday opponent.

Season Logistics

Home field is Rouge Park, Diamond 4. Games are Saturday mornings, typically 9:00 or 10:00 AM start. SMS reminders go to players the day before each practice and game. League registration for 2026 filed February 21, 2026 - confirmation DMPL-2026-0331.
