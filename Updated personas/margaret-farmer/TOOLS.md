# Tools: Margaret Farmer

## Tool Usage

### Connected Services

#### Studio & Document Management

- **Google Drive** (`google-drive-api`): Primary cloud storage. Portfolio masters, kiln logs, exhibition contracts, scanned glaze recipe pages, gallery PDFs.
- **Dropbox** (`dropbox-api`): Pulls the installation photos and catalogue PDFs that Jean-Luc shares for the Galerie Terre "Earth and Hand" show so she can check display and crop choices.
- **Box** (`box-api`): Her connected account where she uploads signed loan paperwork and pulls the one-off file shares museum and large institutional partners send during commission handoffs.
- **Notion** (`notion-api`): Master studio notebook. Kiln firing log, glaze test database, exhibition production trackers, gallery and collector CRM.
- **Obsidian** (`obsidian-api`): Personal commonplace book. Reading notes from Yanagi, Wendell Berry, and the ceramics journals. Margaret maintains this herself.
- **Airtable** (`airtable-api`): "Seasonal Table" 30-piece production tracker. Pieces mapped by season, form, glaze, and firing status.
- **DocuSign** (`docusign-api`): Gallery consignment agreements, commission contracts, museum loan paperwork. She reviews every document.
- **Figma** (`figma-api`): Daniel's design files for exhibition posters, business cards, and catalogue layouts, where she leaves comments and marks up crop and copy changes before he finalises each layout.
- **Contentful** (`contentful-api`): CMS layer for the portfolio site. Add new work, edit exhibition pages, manage the press archive.
- **Webflow** (`webflow-api`): Live portfolio site at margaretfarmer-ceramics.jp, built by Daniel. Edit copy and exhibition pages; publish only after Margaret's review.
- **WordPress** (`wordpress-api`): The Higashiyama Craft Collective blog. Margaret occasionally cross-posts kiln stories with the collective's permission.
- **Confluence** (`confluence-api`): Collective shared documentation. Kiln scheduling protocol, safety procedures, member resources.
- **Monday** (`monday-api`): Her seat on the Galerie Terre board for the Paris show, where she updates her own crate-ready dates and ticks off shipping milestones as the eight pieces move toward install.

#### Email, Messaging & Voice

- **Outlook** (`outlook-api`): A working secondary mailbox she keeps for the Shibui Gallery and forwarded museum threads, replying to Akiko and filing consignment correspondence from here.
- **WhatsApp** (`whatsapp-api`): The line Jean-Luc and the Paris crating firm reach her on for quick shipping confirmations she answers from the studio.
- **Telegram** (`telegram-api`): Carries the group thread Jean-Luc runs for the five potters in the Paris show, where install timing and crate handoffs get sorted.
- **Slack** (`slack-api`): Her account in the Galerie Terre coordination workspace, where she posts crate handoff times and confirms install-schedule and shipping details for the Paris pieces.
- **Microsoft Teams** (`microsoft-teams-api`): Kyoto Craft Museum invites her to commission status calls via Teams.
- **Discord** (`discord-api`): Keeps her in a wood-fire potters' channel where she follows anagama loading discussions and the occasional shared glaze result.
- **Twilio** (`twilio-api`): Backup SMS for material delivery confirmations and gallery courier alerts.
- **SendGrid** (`sendgrid-api`): Transactional email for portfolio site contact-form responses and exhibition RSVP confirmations.
- **Mailgun** (`mailgun-api`): Backup sender for the collector newsletter when SendGrid hits send limits during exhibition launches.
- **Mailchimp** (`mailchimp-api`): Collector and gallery mailing list, approximately 240 addresses. Open studio announcements twice yearly, exhibition invitations.
- **Klaviyo** (`klaviyo-api`): Segments the collector list so that people who actually use the work hear about open studios separately from gallery contacts and press.
- **ActiveCampaign** (`activecampaign-api`): Runs the follow-up sequence to collectors who inquired through the portfolio site, nudging them toward the autumn "Seasonal Table" opening.
- **Intercom** (`intercom-api`): Powers the live-chat widget on margaretfarmer-ceramics.jp so collector questions about a piece reach her queue for a considered reply.

#### Calendar, Events & Travel

- **Google Calendar** (`google-calendar-api`): Master calendar. Studio days, kiln firings, gallery deadlines, mother's Wednesday call, Sunday off-day.
- **Calendly** (`calendly-api`): Studio visit booking link for serious collectors and curators. Margaret approves each booking before confirming.
- **Eventbrite** (`eventbrite-api`): Open studio RSVPs and craft fair ticketing for the spring and autumn collective open studios.
- **Ticketmaster** (`ticketmaster-api`): Holds tickets for the Nils Frahm and chamber concerts she and Ethan catch in Osaka or Tokyo when one tours through.
- **Google Maps** (`google-maps-api`): Studio routing, gallery directions, the temple-path morning walk, Shigaraki trip planning.
- **Uber** (`uber-api`): Backup transport for moving fired work to Shibui Gallery on opening days when the regular courier is booked.
- **Airbnb** (`airbnb-api`): Shigaraki visits to Toshio (twice yearly), Mashiko Pottery Fair lodging, the postponed Portland trip.
- **Amadeus** (`amadeus-api`): International flights. The October Paris trip for the Galerie Terre opening, plus the postponed Portland family visit.
- **DoorDash** (`doordash-api`): Late-night delivery during firing weeks when she cannot leave the kiln. Higashiyama radius only.
- **Instacart** (`instacart-api`): Stocks up on the heavier pantry staples and Carol's beef-stew ingredients ahead of weekend cooking with Ethan, so the bike trips stay light.
- **Yelp** (`yelp-api`): Gallery-area restaurant research when meeting curators or collectors at unfamiliar venues.
- **OpenWeather** (`openweather-api`): Anagama wood-fire planning. Humidity affects glaze behaviour, and she watches the forecast before loading.

#### Galleries, Sales & Storefront

- **Etsy** (`etsy-api`): Watches the potter friends she trades with, sourcing a specific tea cup or small bowl when she wants a handmade gift she cannot make herself.
- **Square** (`square-api`): Open studio in-person sales. Card reader at the studio table during spring and autumn open events.
- **Stripe** (`stripe-api`): Portfolio site checkout for direct online orders, connected to the JPY business account.
- **PayPal** (`paypal-api`): International collector payments, particularly Galerie Terre clients who prefer USD or EUR.
- **BigCommerce** (`bigcommerce-api`): Hosts the small seconds-and-test-pieces shop she opens after a firing to clear shelf space and recover material costs.
- **WooCommerce** (`woocommerce-api`): Runs the storefront on the Higashiyama Craft Collective blog where the four potters list shared open-studio inventory.
- **Amazon Seller** (`amazon-seller-api`): Lists her digital "Seasonal Table" exhibition catalogue and a small print run so distant collectors who cannot attend can still buy it.
- **Typeform** (`typeform-api`): Commission inquiry form on the portfolio site. Captures budget, timeline, and intended use before she reviews.

#### Finance, Accounting & Markets

- **QuickBooks** (`quickbooks-api`): Her sole-proprietor books, shared with the accountant, where she categorises studio expenses, records gallery payments, and runs her monthly P&L.
- **Xero** (`xero-api`): Tracks the Galerie Terre euro invoices and FedEx crating costs in their original currency, which the accountant reconciles against the yen books at filing.
- **Plaid** (`plaid-api`): Aggregates the JPY business and personal accounts for the monthly budget review on the 1st.
- **Alpaca** (`alpaca-api`): Holds a small index position where she parks above-average commission months, a slow second track toward the personal-kiln fund alongside her cash savings.
- **Coinbase** (`coinbase-api`): Converts the occasional crypto payment an overseas collector insists on into yen and moves it straight to the business account.
- **Binance** (`binance-api`): Checks the live exchange rate when a Galerie Terre client proposes settling a piece in a stablecoin, so she can quote the equivalent yen price.
- **Kraken** (`kraken-api`): Cashes out the rare crypto-settled overseas payment here when its rates beat the alternatives, sweeping the proceeds to cash.

- **UPS** (`ups-api`): Backup carrier for domestic Japan and US-bound shipments when FedEx scheduling slips.
- **Shippo** (`shippo-api`): Label generation for direct studio sales. Tracks fragile-ware insurance and signature-required deliveries.
- **Zillow** (`zillow-api`): Scouts neighbourhoods and short-term rentals around her parents' Portland area for the trip she and Daniel are planning after the autumn shows.
- **Ring** (`ring-api`): Watches the collective's shared courier door so she gets an alert when a clay delivery or an outbound gallery crate is picked up during a firing week.
