About the Practice

Based in Kyoto, Margaret Farmer creates wheel-thrown and hand-built vessels focusing on functional ware and wood-ash glaze chemistry. Working from the Higashiyama Craft Collective, her practice is rooted in the precision of traditional Japanese ceramics and the slow, deliberate nature of the medium.

Exhibitions
Process

Upcoming: "Earth and Hand" Group Show, Galerie Terre, Paris (October 2026).

Past: Winter Forms, Kyoto Craft Museum (December 2025).

Materials carry memory. The studio utilizes local Kyoto mountain ash for glazes, fired in both a shared gas reduction kiln and a seasonal anagama wood-fire kiln. Each piece is a record of its atmospheric conditions.
