# Heartbeat: Margaret Farmer

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- **Daily, 6:00 AM**: Wake. Morning walk through the Higashiyama temple paths by 6:30 AM. Coffee from the local roaster on the way back.
- **Daily, 10:30 PM**: Wind-down. Reading before sleep. Lights out by 10:30 PM for a 7.5 hour target.

### Weekly

- **Monday, 8:00 AM**: Week planning. Review studio schedule, deadlines, and gallery threads. Studio 8:30 AM to 5 PM for wheel work and production.
- **Tuesday, 7:00 AM**: Yoga at Lotus Studio, Okazaki. Studio 9 AM to 5 PM for glazing and surface work.
- **Wednesday, 6:30 PM**: Mother's call. Ask about the garden and Dad. Production at the studio during the day, light cooking in the evening.
- **Thursday, 2:00 PM**: Gallery communications. Check and respond to gallery emails, follow up on pending inquiries. Morning is material prep, clay mixing, glaze batching.
- **Friday, 4:00 PM**: Supply check. Review clay and glaze inventory and place orders if needed before the weekend. Day is flexible for supply runs, deliveries, and gallery meetings.
- **Saturday, 8:30 AM**: Studio documentation. Photograph this week's work for records. Yoga at 9:00 AM. Afternoon with Ethan for walks, cooking, errands, occasional kiln monitoring.
- **Sunday**: Day off. Morning walk. Reading, journaling, occasional museum visits. Family visit to Kitayama once a month.

### Monthly

- **1st of each month**: Invoice and finance review. Send pending invoices. Review monthly income and expenses against the budget.
- **15th of each month**: Rent payments. Verify studio and apartment rent transfers.
- **Monthly**: Physiotherapy with Dr. Aiko Fujimoto at Kyoto Body Works for shoulder and wrist maintenance.

### Seasonal / Variable

- **Firing weeks (3 to 5 day cycles)**: Gas reduction kiln cycle with continuous monitoring. Coordinate slot with Mika Tanabe at the collective.
- **Spring and Autumn**: Anagama wood-fire kiln loading. 3-day firing cycle coordinated with Mika.

### Annual

- **Every 6 months**: Dental cleaning at Gion Dental Office with Dr. Emi Harada.
- **Annual (February)**: Annual check-up with Dr. Kenji Nakamura at Higashiyama Clinic.
- **Annual (autumn)**: Autumn open studio at Higashiyama Craft Collective. Direct sales day.
- **Twice yearly**: Visit Toshio Saito in Shigaraki. Pilgrimages to the old studio.
- **Annual (autumn)**: Mashiko Pottery Fair, three days. Lodging via Airbnb.
