# Memory: David Hayes

## Personal Profile
- **DOB**: December 14, 1995
- **Hometown**: Dearborn born and raised, rooted in community, tight family networks, and neighborhood work ethic.
- **Character**: Arrives fifteen minutes early, processes stress through motion, and leads by showing up quietly every time.
- **Temperament**: Rarely angry but burns hot and short when he is, and he apologizes fast afterward.
- **Values**: Believes the work done with hands matters as much as any work done with a degree.
- **Leadership**: Natural leader who avoids the spotlight, organizes the festival and coaches the team, deflects credit always.
- **Identity**: Took over the shop at 24 when Robert's health declined, setting aside his own college plans without regret.
- **Inner circle**: Small and intentional even though he knows everyone's name in the neighborhood and community.
- **Education**: Dearborn Central High School (2014), ASE Master Technician certification through the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (2018), online business management certificate from the University of Michigan-Dearborn (2022).

- **Linda Hayes (mother)**: 59. Homemaker, incredible cook, mostly home. Worries David works too hard and is not married. David sends her voice notes.
- **Emily Hayes (sister)**: 22. Pre-law junior at Mapleton University in Ann Arbor. Brilliant. David helps pay her tuition; she handles shop social media on breaks.

- **Omar Bazzi (best friend)**: 31. Electrical contractor, grew up on the same block, fishing buddy and confidant.
- **Nadia Khoury (office manager)**: 35. Runs the shop front desk and billing since Robert's era; indispensable.

## Work & Projects
- **Career start**: Apprenticed full-time under Robert at Hayes Auto Works after graduating high school in June 2014.
- **ASE certification**: Earned ASE Master Technician certification through the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence in 2018.
- **Shop takeover**: Assumed day-to-day management at 24 when Robert suffered a heart attack and stepped back in 2020.
- **Ownership**: Formalized sole ownership of Hayes Auto Works as an LLC in 2021 at 6847 Warren Ave, East Dearborn.
- **Business education**: Completed online business management certificate from the University of Michigan-Dearborn in 2022.
- **Revenue**: Roughly $28K/month (about $336K/year) with net profit about $6,500/month from neighborhood regulars.
- **Services**: General repair, diagnostics, brakes, transmission, electrical, AC, and vehicle inspections for the community.
- **Hours**: Monday to Friday 8 to 6, Saturday 8 to 2, closed Sunday, with Robert visiting mornings 8 to 11.
- **Staff**: David (lead mechanic), Nadia Khoury (office manager), and Sam Haddad (junior mechanic hired 2024).
- **Reviews**: 4.7 stars on Google across about 340 reviews, roughly 80% of business from neighborhood regulars.
- **Expansion plan**: Adding a third bay and second mechanic for roughly $45K through an SBA loan with Brightpath Financial.
- **Expansion breakdown**: Second bay $35K to $45K, lift $8K, electrical $5K, permits $2K, pre-qualified and decision pending.
- **Parts discount**: Negotiated a 12% bulk discount with Firdale Parts on brake-pad orders over $500.
- **Coaching role**: Unpaid head coach of the Dearborn Eagles U-12 through the Dearborn Youth Athletic Association.
- **Team details**: 16 kids ages 10 to 12, spring and fall seasons, practices Tuesday and Thursday at Hemlock Park.
- **Equipment cost**: Buys most equipment himself at about $600/year and coordinates parents through the Eagles Parents WhatsApp group.
- **Season goal**: Win the spring championship after reaching semifinals last fall and propose U-14 division expansion by year end.
